Organ Trail: Directors Cut. The popular edutainment game about the impending zombie apocalypse, for iOS, Android, PC & Mac.

Can't view the video? You must be on a phone. Click here to watch it on YouTube

- - We reached 5K! As promised, we will add Android and iPad version of the game to the pre-order rewards. Any money we get beyond this point will be used to pour more love into the game and support our team. - -

No, that isn't a typo. Organ Trail is a zombie-parody-remake of the classic game, Oregon Trail, for the Apple 2.

This game currently exists as a playable flash game at our website:http://www.hatsproductions.com/organtrail.html

We released the game about 14 months ago and the game exploded beyond our expectations with over 600K players so far. So we've decided to listen to the fan's comments and turn Organ Trail into a proper game for iPhone, iPad and Android. We want the PC/Mac version to be something that you would buy on services like Steam or Desura.

Totally fake story behind the game:
"Originally developed many years ago as a teaching tool for students, schools across America used the game to prepare children for the impending zombie apocalypse, and dysentery. But what the public doesn't know is that the released version does not follow the program director's original design. Before they tried to have him killed, he stole all the code and it has taken him 40 years to program the game in his own vision. Unfortunately no one uses the Apple-II anymore, so he's putting it on those newfangled phones."


This is a Director's Cut. We want to give a proper treatment to this game and include all the ideas that we didn’t get to the first time around. The story will get a slight re-arrangement and it will play better than the one you can play for free on our site. Plus, you will be able to play it on your phone! (Hot Damn!)

The largest task will be to overhaul the interface to fit a touch interface in addition to the mouse and keyboard.

As you can see from the rewards, the community also has a chance to contribute to the design of this game. It’s all so very exciting/frightening!

We will be adding a new conversation mechanic, where you will meet other survivors and interact with them.

We are also adding tombstones written by the community to the random encounter system.

We will be redoing the entire soundtrack, for a commercial license. And I’m glad to announce we will be giving that soundtrack to anyone who donates 20$ or more.

We will finish all the art. (Did anyone even notice that we didn't finish the art last time?)

And... Other changes!

We don't make any money! All the operating costs of the Hats has been out of pocket so far. We really want to make a cool game and that takes more than pocket change. Here is a breakdown of where the money will go.

There are 2 tiers of goals we have planned for.

For $3,000 - We will be able to buy the development licenses we need to publish the game, bare minimum.

For $5,000 - We will be able to purchase necessarily development hardware for iPad and time-saving development tools for the project.

Any amount beyond our goal will really help us polish the game into something great. It would also be cool to pay people who are sort of doing work for us on a, “good faith,” basis. We currently net no profit from the Hats and all the money we get will be be a great seed for all future projects.
