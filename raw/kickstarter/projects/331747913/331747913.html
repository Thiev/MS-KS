A perfectly blended top down action/puzzle video game that will make you laugh, cry and potentially bang your head against a wall.

Thanks for checking out our Phil The Pill Kickstarter Campaign! We’re excited to introduce you to our game, our villain Hank The Stank, our very own Princess Polycorn, the pillbug inhabitants of Pillville and, of course, our hero Phil The Pill. Our goal for this campaign is to raise the necessary funds to complete and launch Phil The Pill on iOS and Android. We look forward to hearing your feedback, thoughts, ideas and suggestions. Let us know what we can do to earn your support!
 

Think Bomberman meets Pacman meets Temple Run. Phil The Pill is an immersive, surprising and mildly addicting action/puzzle video game. Players will experience fits of laughter, tears of joy, racing hearts, fits of frustration and above all else, pure fun!
 

Welcome to Pillville!  Ruled by the fair Princess Polycorn, Pillville is home to a peaceful colony of kind hearted, fun-loving pillbugs. One such joy filled pillbug is our hero, Phil The Pill! Phil is a pillbug who spends his days rolling ‘round the kingdom, relaxin' with friends and devouring delicious sugary snacks. Pillbugs love their sweets.

Unfortunately, the peace, quiet and serenity of Pillville is under attack!  Hank The Stank, a downright dirty rotten stink bug and his brood of Stenchmen have infiltrated Pillville and are spreading stench across the land.  The poor, defenseless pillbugs have no choice but to retreat into their shells to escape Hank’s nauseating smell. 

Pillville is no longer the joyful paradise it once was. It's a wasteland consumed by vile stink bug stench. Even the once bright and happy Polycorn Palace has been converted into a filthy, pungent bowling alley. Hank The Stank, a fierce, competitive bowler now spends his days on the lanes using frightened, rolled up pillbugs as bowling balls.

Captured and locked in an airtight bowling trophy, Princess Polycorn’s magical powers have been rendered useless and there is nothing she can do to save her kingdom! All hope would be lost, if it weren’t for the severe allergies and clogged sinuses of Phil The Pill! Even Hank The Stank’s aromatic stench cannot infiltrate the stuffed nasal passages of Phil’s nose!

Pillville and it’s sugary snacks have begun to wither and rot.  Phil The Pill, immune to the stench, is our only hope! Be a hero, support our Kickstarter Campaign and help Phil The Pill save Pillville!

At its core, Phil the Pill is a top down action-puzzle maze explorer game. Using intuitive swipes, taps and multitouch gestures you control Phil as he navigates a series of mazes riddled with hazards, traps, tricks and baddies. As you might expect, Phil's main mode of transportation is rolling. Swiping in any direction, Phil takes off, rolling at a brisk pace, till he hits an obstruction. A quick tap stops Phil and brings him to an upright action stance where he can punch or fire off a variety of weapons to bust through walls or take out baddies. Don’t stand around for long though, you gotta keep on rolling on to stay ahead of the creeping cloud of stench that chases you towards the finish of every level!

SAVE THE PILLBUGS
Each maze has 3 pillbugs to save. Saving all the pillbugs isn't required, but come on, have a heart.

SWEEP THE COINS

Coins are scattered generously throughout the levels. Collect them for extra points, equipment upgrades and to use in Bert's Shop.

SEARCH FOR CRUD
Pillbugs love random crud. Many levels have a hidden piece-o-crud. Find them all for bonus loot. 

FIND THE EXIT

Like the ancient proverb says, In order to begin the next level you must first exit the level you are in… Make it to the exit portal to unlock the next level.

Puzzles become less forgiving and more intense as you travel deeper into the game. Choosing a strategic path, conserving your weapons and managing hazards are key to your survival. The following level feature overview will prove useful when strategizing your way through the levels.

WORM HOLES
These holes can be used as secret underground transport paths but beware, there’s no telling where you’ll popup after entering one. You’ll also want to be cautious of the dwellers that occasionally pop out of them! 

LASER GATES & SWITCHES

Each laser gate has a color coordinated switch that must be pressed before passing through it. Running into the gates isn't a good idea, take our word for it!

CONVEYOR BELTS

Conveyor belts are a one way street. They’ll get you moving in one direction, but don’t even think about trying to go back!
 
 

STEEL BLOCKS
Steel blocks are impassable. Don't punch or waste your ammo on them. No matter what you do, they won't go away!

GRASS BLOCKS
They might slow you down but they'll never stop you. You can punch right through these blocks!

STONE BLOCKS
These blocks require a little more firepower. Stock up on bombs to clear stone blocks out of your way.

WOOD BLOCKS
Wood blocks are a little too tough for Phils' fists, but any other weapon will blast right through ‘em.
 

A variety of destructive weapons will be available when you need them most.
 

Don’t underestimate the power of an insect's fists. Phil can punch out enemies, and through dirt walls. Watch out for stone though, it can be a little rough on the knuckles.
 

As you may have guessed, bombs go boom! And take out most things in a close vicinity. Bombs can destroy dirt, stone, wood and enemies.
 

A basic semi automatic projectile weapon, the pea shooter is handy for ranged shots as well as blasting through dirt, wood and enemies rapidly.
 

The missile launcher turns a bomb into a ranged weapon! Use missiles to blast blocks and baddies from a distance.
 

Sometimes you gotta fight fire with fire; or in this case, stink with barf. The barf cannon sends a glob of barf over an obstacle where it splats down with nasty force taking out whatever it hits.
 

It may be pretty, but the rainbow ray should be used with caution. It has the ability to take out every destructible item in its path in a high-powered laser-like fashion.
 

Designed for the most dire situations, the smart bomb has the ability to clear an entire level, leaving virtually no chance for Hank’s Stenchlings to beat you. They’re rare so use ‘em sparingly.
 

Life in Pillville has become very difficult and even the strongest Pillbug needs an occasional “pick me up.” As Phil fights his way through level after level, he’ll need power ups to lift his spirits and keep him alive. 

Power ups can be found throughout the game, or are available for purchase in Bert’s Shop using coins you’ve collected along the way. Bert can be a bit of a slimy guy, but he has a knack for being there when you need him most.

HEALTH REFILL
Runnin’ on empty? Fill 'er up and bring Phil’s health back to 100%.

GAS MASK
Gas masks lessen the debilitating effects of the creeping stench cloud. You're nostrils will thank you.

STINK REPELLENT
Take your time, fumigate and relax! This repellent completely removes the stinky creeping stench from the level you’re on.

INVINCIBILITY
The name says it all! This one renders Phil invincible for the remainder of the level.

CRUD FINDER
This handy gadget reveals where hidden crud can be found. Nothing hit's the spot like a little crud!
 

If obstacles are opportunities, there are a LOT of opportunities in Pillville! Rescuing a princess, defeating a stink bug and setting a colony of Pillbugs free is pretty hard work when danger lurks ‘round ever corner. Keep on your toes and watch for these hazards.

CREEPING STENCH
This green gaseous cloud is found on every level of the game. As Phil races to collect coins, nab weapons and free his fellow pillbugs, the creeping stench wafts further into the maze, pushing Phil higher and higher on the screen. If Phil can’t complete his mission prior to the level being fully engulfed…  All hope is lost.

SPIKED WALLS
Avoid these sharp spikes at all costs! Nothing ruins your morning like impaling yourself on a wall!

MOVING SPIKES
Avoiding a stationary spiked wall can be fairly difficult. Avoiding a moving spiked block is downright hard!
 

Some baddies are Hank The Stank’s henchmen and others are mutated bi-products of the stench that has rolled into Pillville. Either way, they’ll do anything in their power to derail Phil’s quest.

STENCHLINGS
These stink bug larvae are foul little beasts that leave an offensively odorous trail of stink that’ll burn the retina right outta your eyes! Steer clear or you might find yourself the victim of a Stenchling “crop dusting."

SLUGLIES
Sluglies sluggishly slug around Pillvile depositing a slimy snail trail wherever they go. Watch where you roll, the Sluglies sludge will slow you down to less than a crawl.

WORMURS
These toothy wormhole inhabitants don’t take too kindly to our hero trespassing in their deep, dark, and dirty corridors. Watch your fingers, toes, arms, legs and face anytime you’re near a worm hole!  You never know when a Wormur might pop up!
  

As the stink continues to rise along with the difficulty of each new level, you’ll find that Phil requires new and improved gear to keep him fighting for the Pillbugs of Pillville. Use the coins you've earned to keep Phil up to code. 
 

Makes Phil tougher and less vulnerable to various level hazards.
 

Helps Phil punch through grass tiles and take down enemies with fewer punches.
 

Makes Phil faster! Swipe once for normal speed and a second time on a straightaway for a super sweet speed boost!

Curious Media is a small-ish studio based in Nampa, Idaho. Nampa, Idaho you say?! Where the heck is that!? Well, Nampa is a beet farming town about 15 miles west of Boise, Idaho. Risks and challenges Learn about accountability on Kickstarter

One of the biggest challenges with Kickstarting an iOS/Android app is distribution of the game itself. This will play heavily into how we decided to monetize the app initially, whether its Free to Play or a Premium app. Our thoughts are that we are going to need to initially release the app for free. Beyond that, we are really not sure how we should monetize the app for non-backers. We'd like to have an open conversation with our Kickstarter backers about this and try to come up with the preferred approach of the community.

There has been a lot of controversy lately regarding free to play games and understandably so. Many free to play games are designed around the monetization mechanic and pay walls rather than around what makes for the best possible game. We can assure you that this is not and will never be the intention of Phil the Pill. The indie game industry on mobile is in a state of disarray due to free to play models and we'd love to help push it back in the right direction before its too late. We think it will require a collaboration between players and developers because without the players the developers won't have anyone to make games for! Please help us determine the best way to distribute our game in a way that is good for the players and gives us the financial success we need to continue making more games.
