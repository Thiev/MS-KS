Do you remember the days when games were hard and made you think? Lore is our idea of bringing that back to the gaming community.

Story rewrite by the Lore community and supports there standing together to make a different's

Lore is a game where you struggle against Gods and Demon's. The game takes place in many different locations across the globe. You will visit legendary places such as the Labyrinth of Minos where you must chase down Jason, his Argonauts, and Hercules! You will explore ancient worlds and civilizations. In Greece you must fight the Greek pantheon! In Rome you will fight alongside the 300 Spartans! In Hades you will explore tolerance itself (?)! In Egypt you will raid the tombs of the Pharaohs in search of their lost treasure! You travel to the country of Japan where you face Japanese legends such as Nobunaga Oda and other demons. You fight tragic battles in the realms of Raga, going up against all mighty Gods such as Thor, the God of Thunder. You come across battles with frost giants, Loki and other Gods from Viking history.

Â Lore begins in Hades. You find yourself stranded on a boat in the river Styx, not knowing who you are or how you arrived in such a dark, ancient place. You aren't even sure if you are alive or dead. You proceed forward, knowing only that you must escape Hades. But the freedom of your escape is short-lived; you discover that the mortal world has been conquered by Gods and Demons! A great evil has taken over the minds of the Gods, corrupting and twisting them until their only desire is domination over the mortal realm! You must find the source of this evil, remove the corruption, and bring order back to the world! Only YOU can prevent the total destruction of the mortal world!

This project is not just about making the game its also about the process of making a game from start to finish. This is to give you an inside look of everything it takes to put a video game together. Also, to show the transformation of a concept into a fully functioning virtual world. My hope is that by the end of this project you will have new respect for developers and you will have a greater love for the world of Lore.

Do you remember in the good old days when games were complicated and actually made you think? This game is Dark Souls, Diablo 2 And Minecraft all in one. This game will give you the depth of Dark Souls combat with more options to use your mind and skills to over come traps, monsters, puzzles and bosses. The Diablo 2 factor is when you die trying your best to make it back to your body because it holds all your items you had on you. Within the Minecraft factor there is a ledge you can reach with materials given such as a rope and a old hook in your item bag. Combing the two gives you a way up. I have designed certain obstacles to make gaming more exciting. Lets say your running and you trip a trap wire and a big boulder comes right for you . Thankfully you survived that but now your trapped on a dead end level with a big boulder in front of you blocking your way up. You check your tool bag and the easiest way out would be a rope, No fun right ? So instead of a rope you are equipped with a Pickaxe. Now this is no ordinary Pickaxe it has the strength of 30 super strong men in only 1 swing. Chip the bolder away with 3 good swings and now that boulder is your stairway out and up to the next level! Another way of getting out of dead end levels would be body steps. As your eliminating monsters one by one you can get your hands dirty by plying up the dead body to create your body steps and walk your way out of there! Risks and challenges Learn about accountability on Kickstarter

We are making an action RPG game name Lore. This is a game that consists of a few elements from other great games. Our first goal is to get a full team because it is only I and one other person at the moment and we lack numbers to give our project full life. I am currently finding other great artist so that I can complete my team. Our second goal is to start a website to give a visual and details about our creation. This website will also allow us to build a relationship with our supporters and help us start a community for Lore where they can gain full access to in game videos, game music, concept art, updates, game demos and much more. The next goal is to start working on the game itself the story line has already been worked out as well as the setting of the world.
This game is based on Lore from the Greek Gods, The Sphinx and Pharaoh, The Minotaur and the Labyrinth, and these are a few characters we want to bring to life in our video game. We are taking stories from the past and giving them life in a video game world. We are also focusing on fun and challenging combat such as being able to hit and strike matters for example to slow him down you would be able to focus on a monsters leg to decapitate it. You will also be able to strike his eyes to blind him so he can no longer see you this are just a few things we will be putting into Lore.
The greatest risk this project faces is it never getting to see the light of day but I am driven to overcome that. When I am passionate about something I donâ€™t let anything stop me from reaching my goals. I have gained a lot of friends and followers on social networking sites such as YouTube and Face Book, and some that I game with on The Dark Souls forms and Twisted Metal forms who are ready to support this project because they believe in me and what I am creating. There will be things this project will face but we have a vision and we know what we have to do to overcome it and we will continue to be open to constructive criticism and looking to bring people together and invest in great material.
