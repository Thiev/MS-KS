Play a stimulating strategy game with your friends in a high-adventure, sci-fantastical military setting. Playstyle for your lifestyle.

Greetings all!

Timeline Pirates are trying to change the course of history, with only the MultiVersal Corps standing in their way!  You follow the main character Ezzy as she gets recruited by the MVC from our timeline into their alternate universe.  While Ezzy comes to grips with the dazzling array of 'Verses, she learns some startling secrets about her own past... 

The gameplay is a fusion of the best of several genres:

  • Great strategy and replay value from Collectible Card Games
  • Gripping storyline and evolving characters from Role-Playing Games
  • Cooperative teams and scripted boss fights from MMOs
  • Real competition and fast PvP from Real-Time Strategy

In addition to an exciting in-game world, we've also created an accommodating real-world game:

  • Playstyle for your Lifestyle, made for busy people. You can play in short snippets, adjourn, and pickup games at your convenience
  • Real Appeal, to both women and men, adults and teenagers.  Though each play session is short, the story and complexity grow over time
  • Genuine Accomplishment.  We aim to give players a feeling of mastery and success in addition to engaging entertainment

We've been busy beavers the last several months and have already created a lot of material. Here's some great stuff for you:

  • Our Wiki is the best place to learn more about 'Line Pirates.  We've created a Kickstarter Tour on the Wiki to showcase the game.
  • The first stop on the tour are answers to Frequently Asked Questions (which we'll duplicate below for your convenience).
  • Get a bird's eye view of 'Line Pirates on the aptly named Start Here page.  This page also contains a roadmap of the rest of the site.
  • If you want to dive right in and test the card game, you can download our Playtest Kit. This contains the Starter Deck and will give a basic feel for the card game portion of 'Line Pirates. Afterwards, please give us your feedback.

With any great venture, the devil is in the details.  We've been as thorough as possible in keeping track of the nitty gritty.  Here's some more information at a greater level of detail.

We've made several compelling innovations in 'Line Pirates:

  • Real-Time Turns, where every player takes their turn at the same time.  This enables much faster games and larger multiplayer events
  • UpClose Matchmaking, where competitive players and teams are matched by both Player Rating and Deck Power
  • Balanced Diversity, we've ensured lots of viable deck types will interact in a fair manner.  Each card is guaranteed to be useful in some Format

See how we would wisely use your pledges in our Finances spreadsheet. 

  • Essential items are necessary to complete the project, which is around $252K Project + $123K Overhead = $375K Goal
  • Desired items are high value, low cost enhancements, for an additional $281K Project + $84K Overhead ~ $740K Goal
  • Awesome items are the ideal dream version of the game, adding on a $691K Project + $235K Overhead ~ $1.7M Goal

We outline our (ahem) game plan on Ways to Help.  We need your help to:

  • Partner with mid-sized content creators and use their characters and stories in our game (specifically, in their own unique 'Verse)
  • Network with potential employees, namely flash developers, animators, and illustrators with styles compatible with the 'Line Pirate theme
  • Spread the word about 'Line Pirates, through enthusiastic word of mouth

Our Official Rules are for serious competitive players, not for the faint of heart.

  • Mathematically Designed, the rules are defined precisely which not only ensures fair balance but also provides a clear Framework for resolution
  • Many Formats, guaranteeing that deck building and card use always remain fresh and interesting
  • Teams and Leagues provide the structure for high-prestige long-term competition play.  They also offer the best Prizes!

We've got some great Pledge Rewards for you and have added a new feature to the typical Kickstarter fare.  We have Tier Bonuses in addition to the regular pledges.  Here's how they work:

  • There are five tiers at $25, $50, $100, $250, and $500. 
  • Tier Bonuses are added to every other pledge that amount or above 
  • So, don't select the Tier Bonus rewards.  You'll automatically get them!

And yes, the Tier Bonuses are cumulative.  So if you were a U.S. backer selecting the Souvenir Bundle ($70 + $20 shipping), you'd automatically receive the $25 Player and $50 Gamer Tier Bonuses. If you tacked on another $10 to your pledge then you'd also receive the $100 Recruit Tier Bonus. You could also add on another Sticker, Poster, or T-Shirt for their respective values to reach the next tier, which would ship together.

Another way to reach the next Tier is to combine a BUNDLE with any other pledge (which might itself be a bundle).  So, for example, you might add the $100 Recruit Bundle to the $150 Signed Original Artwork to get to the $250 Angel Tier.  We're pretty flexible and want to make sure you get exactly the rewards you like the most, so if you want to mix and match, just send us an e-mail.

Thanks for making this a wonderful journey.  Now go pledge!  :-)

FAQ

  • What is 'Line Pirates?

    'Line Pirates is the most Simulating Strategy Game on Facebook, with a high-adventure, sci-fantastical military theme. Timeline Pirates are trying to change the course of history, with only the MultiVersal Corps standing in their way! Battles happen at critical junctures in history called http://linepirates.wikia.com/wiki/Start_Here#Premise and there are many known alternative universes (called 'Verses for short).

    Last updated: Thu, Jul 19 2012 12:14 am EDT
  • How does the cooperative gameplay work?

    It's similar to Wing Commander II. You alternate between story and missions, culminating in Finale Events. http://linepirates.wikia.com/wiki/Category:Missions are grouped into http://linepirates.wikia.com/wiki/Category:Campaigns with a complete story arc. Each mission you complete you get a http://linepirates.wikia.com/wiki/Category:Components… used to craft a new card; for more powerful cards, you have to group with other players to tackle a hard boss fights.

    Last updated: Thu, Jul 19 2012 12:16 am EDT
  • What is the gameplay like?

    'Line Pirates is a blend of the best of several genres: Collectible Card Games, Role-Playing Games, Massive Multiplayer Online, and Real-Time Strategy. There's many ways to play, depending on your preferences. You can play cooperatively or competitively, solo or in teams.

    Last updated: Thu, Jul 19 2012 12:16 am EDT
  • How does the competitive gameplay work?

    You can compete against other players for prizes and prestige. There are several formats and different cards are more and less useful depending on the http://linepirates.wikia.com/wiki/Category:Formats. You can compete singly or in teams, arena-style or tournaments. The team competition is very similar to sports, with teams in leagues building up to the season finals. As long as you keep competing, you'll gain http://linepirates.wikia.com/wiki/Category:Components… to craft more powerful cards.

    Last updated: Thu, Jul 19 2012 12:18 am EDT
  • What are Real-Time Turns?

    In 'Line Pirates, every player takes their turn at the same time! Each http://linepirates.wikia.com/wiki/Players gets to select one http://linepirates.wikia.com/wiki/Order_of_Play#Actions, and they are all http://linepirates.wikia.com/wiki/Resolution simultaneously. This allows us to have much faster games with many more players than other comparable games.

    Last updated: Thu, Jul 19 2012 12:18 am EDT
  • Can I play a villain?

    You begin the game as a recruit in the MultiVersal Corps, who are constantly battling against the 'Line Pirates. Clearly the MVC are the good guys (trying to preserve history) and the 'Line Pirates are the bad guys (trying to change it). But as the plot unfolds things become less clear... you may get to play a hero or villain, if you can figure out which side is which. :-)

    Last updated: Thu, Jul 19 2012 12:18 am EDT
  • Where could I learn more specifics about the card game?

    Check out http://linepirates.wikia.com/wiki/Start_Here and then http://linepirates.wikia.com/wiki/Innovations_in_%27Line_Pirates.

    Last updated: Thu, Jul 19 2012 12:18 am EDT

    Alpha begins no later than February 2013, Beta no later than May 2013, and Release is no later than the end of August 2013. But since we plan on releasing content continuously, we hope that it really is never finished... but just keeps getting better. ;-)

The main character in the training missions is Ezzy, and she'll be featured throughout the subsequent campaigns. Ezzy begins as a normal everyday person like you and me, and then undergoes a series of (sometimes painful) transformations into a genuine heroine.
