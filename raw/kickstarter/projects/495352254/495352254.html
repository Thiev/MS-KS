You can create new realms on and add custom artifacts to the game! Your characters can build kingdoms, and YOU are the storyteller!

The Oceania is a limitless fantasy world which is based on the vast expanses of the ocean. Land is represented here as a set of separate surface and flying islands of various shapes and sizes. From time to time new islands or even continents appear rising from the depths of the ocean.

Features

1. No limit

This game has no limitations. Users have total freedom in their choice and modifications of the gameplay. They can affect Oceania's universe, co-creating and redesigning it as they see fit. The character class system is also different from the conventional one. The character is not obliged to choose a particular class immediately and develop within certain limits. Instead, it is possible to reach new levels at the skills belonging to various classes.

Race Transformation
It is worth noting that not all the races are going to be openly available in the beginning of the game. New races may appear in the game, depending on the player's actions. For example, vampire character will only be available for you to play as, only after a vampire bites your human character. The process of â€œturningâ€ will take place next and will transform your character into a vampire. The â€œturningâ€ into vampire process will vanish after certain amount of time and you will have an opportunity to switch back to human race. That is of course if this is what you want, as our immense focus on enabling your creativity has another present for you. You could choose to stay vampire and embrace the DARK SIDE. Who are we to take this choice from you?
Underwater class
The races belonging to the Underwater class have abilities to change their anatomy, which could be handy when you need to quickly grow a pincer in order to protect yourself from an upcoming danger or grow a tentacle to reach a prey. In this manner, the character's entire appearance can be modified, effectively turning him or her into a totally different creature. It is somewhat similar to the pirate crew of the Flying Dutchman in Pirates of the Caribbean: Dead Man's Chest who grew on their bodies various parts of undersea animals.

3. Craft

In Oceania, crafts are an important and well-developed part of the gameplay. A player can learn several trades, which, if desired, can be reset in order to begin learning others. At the beginning, a character has an initial skill level at all crafts. Player can upgrade all of them, but itâ€™s not possible to reach the top at all of them at once. One can become a master at one or several chosen crafts but not at all of them. There are no strict standards. Every object created can be totally unique. Using different resources and materials in the creation of a particular artifact gives the artifact different properties. Thus, two artifacts may look quite similar yet be significantly different from each other.

4. Trade

Players can open shops and stores on their own land or rent market stalls in cities or on kingdom territories, which is usually more profitable. The trading venues continue working and bringing income even when the owner is offline.

5. PvP & PvE

Each player can prove himself as a lone warrior or demonstrate mastery of battle command both in the vast expanses of the Oceania and in the arena. In the Oceania there is an ability to deploy guild wars where much depends not only on the forces of the rivals,Â Â but also on the tactical choices. Any player can raise an entire army by hiring mercenaries. MERCS can help capture castles and towns, resolve guild war conflicts, explore dungeons, etc..

6. Clans, guilds

Unlike many other games, Oceania offers total freedom of choice. Members of one clan or guild, rallied around a common cause, may belong to different races. This is somewhat similar to the situation in real life, adding diversity and wider gameplay opportunities.

7. Private Territories
Players can take possession of private islands where they can do whatever they want: build a tavern, develop a farm with crops and livestock, or simply decorate the territory. Large groups (guilds, clans and others) may acquire larger areas to create their own cities, kingdoms, and other things.

8. The live world
The Live World essentially enables players to introduce new objects/artifacts, plants, animals, and other stuff which is initially nonexistent in the game universe.
This is Oceania's main distinctive feature. It enables players to introduce to the gameplay custom artifacts and objects they create or invent. It is up to the player to decide how exactly the new item may be acquired â€“ bought, for example, at a neutral store or at the player's own shop, for in-game currency or real-life money (yes, this is a fun money-making opportunity). Or, perhaps, the item might be won as a trophy, found in a secret place, etc.
Thanks to this endless stream of new things, the world changes and evolves.

9. In-game knowledge base

From now on, you won't need to spend a lot of time and effort searching for the descriptions of skills, monsters, weapons and armor, formulas and other things on the official game website or fan sites. Most of the knowledge base content is now available for your perusal from within the game. All you have to do is click 'Knowledge Base' to see the list of topics with detailed descriptions.
