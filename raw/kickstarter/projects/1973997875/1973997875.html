A project to complete character art for a modern, yet traditional 2D fighting game called BRUTAL CARNAGE made for the OUYA

A modern yet traditional 2D fighting game made in Hi-Def realistic fashion created for the OUYA. Brutal Carnage will consist of savage graphics, hardcore video game music, and barbaric character art. This 2D 1080p game will feature 16 characters, 1-2 players, 1-2 player online play capabilities, content download and monthly updates. Extremely unique fighting styles, surreal combative sorcery, and intense stage interaction will be the DNA of Brutal Carnage. 

Our reason for this project is to utilize the opportunity Kickstarter provides and to maximize our projects potential. The primary focus of this project is based on the creation of 9 Brutal Carnage warriors. This project is all about 1st stage concept/character art work. Character art is usually produced in the beginning of every video game production to serve as a guide for the rest of the games development. There are several stages of art involved with the making of a video game. For example; you have concept, character, texture, background artist etc… 

Concept/character art is generally a small part of the artistic process for most big budget video games. Our character art means a lot to us. Brutal Carnage is a fighting game. What’s the most important ingredient in a fighting game? The characters! This project is one of importance considering what’s being produced. Without the benevolent contribution of a backer there can be no Brutal Carnage created. It is that simple. The creation of our final 9 characters can only manifest if optimistic Kickstarter backers pledge their allegiance. 

An additional 9 characters will equal a total of 16 warriors you can choose from to brutalize your opponent with. This art is necessary to assure the games vision is followed. The 7 characters we have were methodically assembled, and creatively constructed. We can assure that anyone who backs this Kickstarter project will not be disappointed. We can also assure that anyone who backs this project will become a little brutal as well.

Brutal – Definition: Grossly ruthless or unfeeling.

Carnage – Definition: Great and usually bloody slaughter or injury.

Constant deception by The Host has lead to a violent battle of Brutal Carnage between 16 gruesome warriors on earth. Each fighter has a motive. Although every warrior fights with deadly purpose, one thing can be agreed upon… They all want The Host destroyed! Check out the first 7 Brutal Carnage barbarians we have professionally illustrated!

An ageless warrior turned evil sorcerer born in the depths of earth’s darkest corner. What is known about The Host is very little. It has been said that the blood of an insidious warlock and savage vampire runs through his veins. He is believed to be immortal because of his sinister ancestry. The practice of black magic enables him to place a spell on any human or creature of his choice. Voracious betrayal and deceit against mankind provides The Host with his most prized possession of all… 

 A soul. 

Control and sacrifice motivate his existence. The Host searches the world with an insatiable yearning to find those who are miserable, vulnerable, and in need. A simple promise to humans of the things he despises, tricks them into taking part in gruesome battle and fighting for their lives as well as their families. The promise of love, peace, and happiness helps The Host obtain mind-control of those who crave it. His persuasive nature and clear proposition aids him in finding the perfect humans and creatures to participate in Brutal Carnage among each other. 

No one truly knows where, but it said that The Host has vowed to quench his violent blood thirst for Brutal Carnage by orchestrating gruesome death matches on earth. 

Prepare for brutality!

To create Brutal Carnage we will assemble a small collection college interns with one primary focus in mind. The development of a great 2D fighting video game for the OUYA. As we have stated in previous paragraphs, this will not be a very difficult game to create. Art work and programming are the 2 major components for the completion of this fighting video game. 2D graphics will provide that nostalgic video game feeling once again.

Here are a few of the options we would like to work on and have incorporated into Brutal Carnage:

Realistic stage interaction

Realistic character damage

Broader range of 2D character movement

Weapons

Realistic damage done by weapons

Gore

Better character “punch-lines” and “phrases”

More profanity

Warrior’s special moves

Easy to perform combos

Unpredictable “finishing moves”  

Brutal Carnage will be designed with a vibrant hi- def modern look and all original American art. Every single piece of art for Brutal Carnage will, and has been designed from scratch. We also feel like the immense detail we plan to incorporate into Brutal Carnage will be a huge factor. The look of our art work will be a primary focus for us. We cannot stress this enough

As we have stated before we would like Brutal Carnage to be created with the traditional style of 2D game play. We want Brutal Carnage in 2D for several reasons. One being because we feel a great video game can still be created without a “humongous video game budget” and another being we understand the game availability state of the OUYA. We all can remember how much fun it used to be playing 2D fighting games like; Eternal Champions, Bloody Roar, and Mace: The Dark Age right? Well now it’s time for a modern fighting video game with some of the same traditional values those games held. Although we expect tradition there are certain cons in vintage 2D gaming that we do not want to see.

Here are a few examples:

We do not want Brutal Carnage fighters to have to jump over their opponent in order to get on the other side of them.

We do not want Brutal Carnage fighters to have the ability to perform one single move over and over again and defeat you.

We do not want Brutal Carnage fighters to take internal organ-crushing hits, and hard skull-shattering blows then recover like nothing happen.

These are the type of cons to us that can be speculated for modification. And they are just the beginning of some of the 2D fighting game cons we plan to get rid of.

WHY DO WE CHOOSE THE OUYA TO CARRY BRUTAL CARNAGE? There are plenty of reasons why we choose the OUYA. The most important reason is that it is an open source platform that doesn't require the purchase of an expensive developer’s kit to create games. By OUYA eliminating fees this provides independent developers an equal chance to compete in the gaming market, and primarily focus on the creation of the game. The OUYA allows developers to create without boundaries.

WHY CREATE A HIGH-QUALITY GAME FOR THE OUYA? To date OUYA has released with 200 games. Although many of these games are very good we do not see a game that utilizes the OUYA’s hardware. Therefore we will create a game that does, and more. We intend to develop a game that will make people go out and purchase the OUYA. Just as Nintendo has Mario, Xbox has Halo, or PS3 has Little Big Planet. 

We seek to make Brutal Carnage a elite game for the OUYA.

OUYA PLATFORM: NVIDIA Tegra 3 processor, 1GB of RAM, and 8GB of internal storage (expandable via USB), Wi-Fi/Ethernet and 1080HD. (Brutal Carnage will be developed specifically for the OUYA)

The creation of Brutal Carnage concept/character art is essential. As we have mentioned before, character art is a large part of the foundation for most video game development. It’s a very big part of the foundation for Brutal Carnage in particular. Therefore the backing of this Kickstarter project ensures the 1st stage concept/character art completion and a copy of the game when completed

Throughout the development process we will make sure that all backers are informed of the current status of Brutal Carnage. We will do this by releasing a monthly progress update. This progress update will consist of Brutal Carnage development info and anything else associated with it. We are dedicated to making Brutal Carnage the video game a reality. This is an opportunity for all fighting video game lovers to have a massive role in the production of a new classic American fighting game called “Brutal Carnage”. Honestly… that sounds cool enough to tell anyone. LOL!

Project risk and challenges: We believe the risk of this project well be minimal. Our concept/character art work can certainly be completed.We do not possess a huge budget or have a giant video game development team supporting us. What we do have is a talented, dependable, professional artist that has proven he can handle all concept/character art work. We have previously worked together and created the 7 Brutal Carnage warriors you see now. We are fully confident all 1st stage character art can continue to be developed. Only challenges we could possibly face, would be hitting a creative brick wall. And this definitely will not happen! We are 100% dedicated to the development of Brutal Carnage.

FAQ

  • How much will Brutal Carnage the Game cost when completed

    Brutal Carnage the Game will ONLY be available to the people who back this project.

    Last updated: Thu, Jul 18 2013 10:26 pm EDT
  • How do you guys plan on developing Brutal Carnage the Game at $3,500 when before you were asking for 465K?

    Before this decision to make this a real project Brutal Carnage the Game was designed from a business perspective based on research with everything having a COST. We never intended to benefit financially from its existence and it seems that profit is a VERY big issue with games being made. We have come to terms that its more important to see this game made than to attempt to create it on a professional level. By making it project based on internships it eliminates the seriousness of the project making it a fun process. It will also provide college students a chance to gain experience and participate in a really cool project. We want Brutal Carnage the Game to be fun and not something that is ruined because of a dollar sign.

    Last updated: Thu, Jul 18 2013 9:21 pm EDT
  • Before you guys were asking for a budget of 56K just to complete the artwork? How is it you can complete the artwork with a budget $3,500?

    The $3,500 will be used to create concept artwork for future artist/interns to use as a blue print when it comes to our standards concerning detail and design. We intend for Brutal Carnage the Game to have a solid inspiration. Our goal of $3,500 will be used to fund more concept artwork. The 56K stretch goal we asked for prior was based on employing artist to create all the artwork needed to create a game such as levels, weapons, special moves, etc, etcc. Basically what we are doing now is seeking funds to create the blueprint for the characters.

    Last updated: Thu, Jul 18 2013 9:24 pm EDT
  • How long will take to create Brutal Carnage the Game with it relaying on internships as workforce?

    Since this is a project that is not based on profits we intend to reach out to a few college professors in our area and see if there are any students that they may know of that are currently looking for internships for college credit. Usually department chairs know exactly who the good students are and hopefully they will be able help us. We have attended college and are very familiar with organizing a project on this level. If unsuccessful there we will then get more aggressive and and go through the career centers of these schools. We are looking to organize a group of students that will work together on campus in there free time. Therefore we estimate that the time period needed to develop may slightly be extended a few extra months. We estimate that we will be able to complete this game with in 1.5 years.

    Last updated: Thu, Jul 18 2013 10:01 pm EDT
