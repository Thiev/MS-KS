These games will be for people of all ages from causal gamers to Hardcore gamers it will be cheap to buy and look nice to the eyes.

I want to thank all that came and looked at our kick starter video; it means a lot to us. ((For more updates on these games check out the update tab Also Check out the video about the concepts of both game.)). We are going to use  the money to make these games look nice, and to have them ready and in stores by December, and ready for download on our website that we will be working on. We will be using the money to hire voice actors, CG cut scenes, make the games longer to play by adding more levels to them, HD characters that are fully detailed and are wonderful to look at and play. If we raise more than our goal, we will be porting these games to the Wii, PS3 and the Xbox 360. Also we want to make these games playable on the iPhone, iPad and the Android phones. We also want to make games that are cheap to the buyers pockets, but look nice and fun to play at the same time. With your help we can achieve this. Also we will be making better looking videos of the games and posting new videos every week so that you can have a better description of the games and to see how well both games are turning out.

These are the basic Concepts for both games Since my team is to busy doing music, animations, character models and sound for the games. I decided to sketch what each game will look and play like to further shine some light on what is to be expected. The games will look a lot better than what your going to see these are just the basic concepts of both games.

This adventure game that we are working on will be filled with puzzles that the player or players has to solve in order to complete the level. Here is a brief summery of the game. Scientist made some animals that they wanted to be super strong and able to adapt anywhere but there experiment was a success with unforeseen problems. The Animals they created didn't follow there orders and instead they turned to there animal instinct and started to destroy things. After the Scientist saw what they had created they tried to turn the animals back to normal. You Join Lipsy and two other characters in an adventure of escaping to enjoy your new found life. By destroying floors and floors of the building and enemies that get in your way, as they try to catch you and your friends you must solve a series of puzzles while trying not to get caught by the scientist. There will be power ups that will come to you at random and it will be your job to get these power ups and use them to help you pass a level. It wont be easy with scientist and cute furry dogs chasing you. You will have to think quickly and use only the power ups that will help you win that level. This game will be fun for all ages and if you like PAC-man and Mario then you will love this game. Credit goes out to Mr. Eugene Chu for doing the 3d character model for this adventure game.

This game will take place in a distant future. You and your brother have not talked in years but after a strange note and the amulet of your brother's in the note. Its up to you to get your brother back because deep down inside even though you and him don't see eye to eye he's your brother and no one messes with your brother but you. You and 4 other characters go on a mission to get your brother back. After getting your brother back you find out that's only the beginning of your quest. This game will have action in it the whole time, it will be filled with twists and turns that you never saw coming. With cut scenes and 100% fast paced action you will be leaning out of your chair for more. We are going to put some brand new elements in that has not been done to any beat em up game to date. This will be a fun game and for hardcore gamers that love beating people up in there way all for a good cause, Loveable characters and tough bosses to beat. You will love to play this game again and again. Credit goes out to DMITRIY LOGUNKOV for the character models and animation.

You play a very important piece to the success of both games. I have been funding the 3d beat em up for a year now but I have ran out of money. I can no longer do this game with the money I was making. With your help you will be helping us to get these games out to the public by December and you will also be helping us to add more depth to both games so that they will be both fun and appealing to the gamers who will love to play these games for hours and hours over and over . You will also be helping us with obtaining licenses that we will need. You will be helping us with hiring voice actors, CG cut scenes and composing more loveable tunes for these games. You will be helping us to hire programmers that will be programing the engines for both games. If  we exceed our goals these games will get ported to other consoles like the Wii, X-box 360, the PS3 and the iPad, iPhone and the Droid. This is why we need your help. We ((Promise)) to get this completed with your help. You will get Big Bangs for your Bucks as the rewards are small but come with a big packet. These games will be fun, have lots of action in them. These games will also be cheap and affordable. With your help we can make our dreams come true and share them with the world.

Here are some of the music that will be in these games. Grahf677 did a wonderful job and so did Jsweezy productions with these tunes so sit back relax and enjoy.

Q: What Platform will these games be coming out on?

A: Initially, the platform of these games will be available for the PC. But if we raise more than our goal you can expect these games to arrive on the Wii, iPad, iPhone, Android Phones and the X Box 360.

Q: When can we be expecting Game play footage?

A: Currently both games are still in their early stages but we will be working hard on this so we can present more to the public.

Q: Will the animated comics be on the same CD as the games?

A: No the animated comics will be on a completely different CD. The reason for this is because the animated comics will have more bonus features, concept art , bloopers and more.

Q: I want the posters not the desktop wallpapers do I have to pay extra for that?

A. No but if you can spare extra cash that would help us to print the posters and ship them. If you can't don't worry about it we will be able to cover it. That goes for everything that's being mailed including international shipping.

Q: Why Do you need $10.000 to make these games?

A: That's an easy one. We will be hiring programmers to program the AI and the game's engine since we're making the engine from scratch. Voice actors as we currently do not have any. We are also going to be making the CG cut scenes look great with professional quality . Hiring people to do the Sound and make more music for the games. We will also need Money for printing the boxes of the games and getting CDs for these games to have in stores. We are also paying every artist and sound maker which gets expensive. We will need more software and software license's. We will also use the money to do our website so people who want to buy the games digitally can buy them.

Q: Will both games have multiplayer capability?

A: Yes both games the player can either play it themselves or with a friend. If we can raise more than our goal we will try to add online play to these games so you can play with anyone in the world.

Q: Why does the world need these games?

A: We are trying to do what not a lot of game companies do. We are making games that will not hurt the buyers pockets but look nice and be loads of fun to play. So the buyer is getting the best of both worlds. That is our goal and with your help we can achieve that. We want to make a puzzle game that is going to be fun but challenging at the same time, the player will be learning and having fun. Our beat beat em up game will have brand new game play that to my knowledge no other beat em up game has. This will bring a new set of strategy to the table that the player will have to exploit.

Q: I have a question or questions how can I contact you.

A: If you have any questions about the games or other stuff please contact me at Thomas@le-swanns.com or you can just PM me.

FAQ
