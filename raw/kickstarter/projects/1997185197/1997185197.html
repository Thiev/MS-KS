Miner Stats:

We would like to deepen the strategy involved in the game by giving different stats to all of the miners unlocked. Interstellar Combat:

Interstellar combat would also enhance the Team vs Team experience, but would be a new aspect of the original Co-Op gameplay as well. Wade (Vindicator) has been using the StarCraft 2 editor since the day it was released during beta in 2010. By treating custom game making as a full-time job since then he has become one of the most competent users of the editor in the community. He currently works on two #1 rated games in different StarCraft 2 regions around the world (Squad TD (North America and Europe) & City of Tempest (Korea)) on the side while working on Mineralz Evolution. While there may be some levels of risk involved while figuring things out and potentially hitting temporary roadblocks, the risk minimal.
The other lead developer, David (SyCo), has been making custom games since StarCraft 1 and has even longer term experience with Blizzard tools under his belt.

We have proven ourselves in the past as being capable of delivering fun, high quality games with NO budget and very limited donations, and we'll only be raising the bar even further if funding can be achieved. You can rest assured we can get the job done. Money is truly the only limiting factor in this situation, so with your help we would love to improve the quality of Mineralz Evolution for everyone who already enjoys it and many many more in the future! Thanks so much for your support! We hope this is a life changing endeavor.
