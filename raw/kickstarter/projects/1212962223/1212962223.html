About Bumpin' Uglies

Bumpin' Uglies is a Freemium physics-based puzzle game for iPhone, iPad and iPod Touch (as well as Android later this year).

(...as well as a dab of recessive genes for good measure!)

We Need Your Help!

We've been hard at work since late last fall to bring Bumpin' Uglies to life and we think it has the potential to be a surprise hit once it's released early this fall.

Our path to getting where we are has lead to evolution and refinement of concepts that have only made the game better, but have also resulted in a couple extra months of work that we hadn't planned for!

We need YOUR help in the final push towards the finish line!

We're seeking $65,000 to finish, which will allow us to:

  • Finish production on the artwork and level design for the World Packs needed for launch
  • Fine-tune the economy and transaction mechanics in the game
  • Complete a sweet game trailer to help market the game prior to and after launch
