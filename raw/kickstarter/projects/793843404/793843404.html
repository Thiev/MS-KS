Who is Phantom Games Studios?

Phantom Games Studios is an independent game Company who founded by Craig Walker in 2008 and the studios is locate in New York City. Phantom Games Studio focuses primarily on indie games.

StoryLine

The story is about evil a scientist name Professor Livers Dies who wants to rule the world by turning everybody into zombies. The reason why Professor Livers Dies wants to rule the world because as a kid he was bullied by bullies all his life. Professor Livers Dies made a device called “turn into a zombie ray” aka T.I.A.Z.R. Professor Livers Dies did not realize that you cannot control the walking dead. So Professor Livers Dies begin to work on the most genius device ever in mankind history to control the undead. He create the zombie can recognize their leader device aka Z.C.R.T.L.D. He hacks the news station and represented himself. Professor Livers Dies said I am going to turn everybody into zombies and rule the world and be my slaves. Professor Liver Dies don’t realize today is April 1, 2013. And everyone is starting to laugh at him!!! So Professor Livers Dies kidnap a little boy name Mason Disick. He hack the news stations and he tells his life story when he was a child. He uses the T.A.R.Z and Z.C.R.T.L.D on Mason. Mason turned into a zombie. So Professor Livers Dies said I am going to rule the world by turning everybody into zombies. Professor Livers Dies uses the T.I.A.Z.R and Z.C.R.T.L.D and starting to turn people into zombies. Calvin Johnson is a 25 years old police officer and he is a gamer. He likes plays video games and he’s a momma’s boy. Calvin Johnson is the main Protagonist. He is the only hope can stop Professor Livers Dies.

Power Ups

Location

So far there are 7 locations in the game but more will add in development. The locations are Farm, Strip Club, Airport, Franklyn Town Prison, Gas Station, Lighthouse, Train Station. These picture just concept arts.

There are many items in the game, like acid bomb, bomb, freeze grenade, and mines. More items going to add in the game like sticky grenade, dynamite, flamethrower, bomb eggs, and many more.

There are many different types of zombies in the game. Each Zombie represents the levels in game.

Zombii Outbreak has many different levels. The levels so far are strip club, farm, light house, prison, Train Station, and Gas Station. Each level tells a story. First you have a time limit. When the time limit runs down, the round is over. The game will tally how many points you earn. Each point you earn you can upgrade your weapons. The rounds are endless. There are many different types of zombies. Each zombie has different abilities. Some of zombies can shoot, run fast, pick up weapons and counter attack. You have a health bar, if your health bar runs out Game Over. 

How to play

You have a bar of items on the button of the screen. The items you use are acid bomb, bomb, firecracker, freeze grenade, land mines, sticky grenade, machine gun torrent, and many more. You press the button item on the screen drag the item on the playing screen. For example, you press the freeze grenade button and drag on the item next to the zombie. The freeze bomb will activate and freeze the zombie. When you finish use the items, you have to wait the items recharge. 

What Systems Zombii Outbreak is coming?

Zombii Outbreak is coming to Wii-Ware, Windows Phone 7.5/8 and possible PS Vita. I am still learning to make games on the Vita but Vita is still possible.

Why Wii-Ware not the Wii U?

The reason why because Wii have a large install base. The Wii U Just came out and the install base is not their yet especially a small developer like me. Nintendo is improving on their indie game support. WiiWare is also improving also. Recently 2 games came out on WiiWare – la mulana, and retro city rampage and all of them have critical acclaim. This style of game is perfect for WiiWare. Also you can play zombii outbreak on the Wii U in Wii mode.

Is the 40mb is a problem to make the game on WiiWare?

No, the 40mb is not at a problem. Zombii Outbreak going run in 2d; so it’s not a problem to develop the game on wiiware.
