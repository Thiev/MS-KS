Story:

After the samurai era...A meteor entity fell again in the new millennium. However,instead of people competing with weapons in this era, the most competitive items are sportsballz. The entity became attracted to what humans love to be competitive about...sports...which gave life to the Meanballz.

As you conquer the app with your meanball of choice, the story will be unlocked via video clips of the past. (The story of Tanaka & Tenchuo during the samurai era). For more information on the back-story, click here.

Meet some of the MeanBallz and their Athletes. 

MEANBALLZ are sports ballz that were brought to life by magical dust particles from an outer space meteor in a galaxy 10,000,000 light years away, which turned them into creatures personalities of their own. They have tele-kinetic abilities and have the power to control (Talented athletes or Humans) with a type of hypnotic mind control causing enigma within their thoughts. When the meanballz confront or are in close proximity of one another they start glowing and get ready to battle. They all want to be the most dominating of their kind. Each one has a unique power move, ability and "mean-streak". As the ballz fight against each other the humans will also jump in to fight. They too have their special moves and powers all unique to each individual … To watch these characters in battle is something you don’t want to miss out.

THE ULTIMATE MOBILE GAMING APP!!!!!!

BREAK THROUGH TECHNOLOGY PHASE I:

This game will have something NO other mobile gaming app has. This will be a 2 player game offensive and defensive or both and you can play against the computer. Filled with levels and sub levels this game has plenty of puzzles for you to try and breakthrough to defeat your opponent. A ranking system in place that allows you to be at a higher level by using your gaming skills to climb your way to the top. If the Meanballz are to collide in the air, both players must tap the screen as fast as they can. Whoever reaches ten-first will knock out the opponent and keep cruising through the sky to cause damage to the other party's home base or character. 

ALL IN REAL TIME GAME-PLAY!!

BREAKTHROUGH TECHNOLOGY PHASE II:


We will have something in place that will blow your mind I cant really give out to much information on it but here is a quick view on it: (Player search) If there is another player in the area with the same Meanballz game a small pop-up menu will arise or you can tap the search button. It would ask you if you would like to challenge or decline. The user name would show up and also the opponents rank and mean streak level. No mobile game has this and this will be a trendsetter for all of the mobile gaming apps that will come out in the future. Also you would be able to search for your opponent through their user ID, no matter where they are in the world, you can challenge them as long as you have their user ID. Our team has put a lot of hard work and effort into making this game exceed all expectations and simple enough for anyone to play for Sports Fans and Gamers around the world international and domestic ,we finally have your game.

RELEASE DATE:

We will be releasing the (v.1) in March 2013 we are still doing some extra beta testing for the player search and real time gameplay to make sure that there are no flaws. This is a very complex staging part of the app. We want to make sure the 2 player runs on real time touch screen and the player search needs to work with all iOS servers as well as our own. We will also have the apple id search where you can search by apple id and challenge someone anywhere in the nation or internationally. These are very difficult tasks but we will make sure we do extra beta testing to get it fit where it needs to be that's where Kickstarter comes in.

Risks and challenges Learn about accountability on Kickstarter

Most of our work is completed as far as the app development. We already have a working test model of the app. The real work will be making sure the app runs on real time. When on 2 player mode. We also have to make sure the coding we have for the player search is on active at all times through our servers, when you are around a challenger. Another challenge will be advertising this across all countries throughout the world. We plan to utilize many of the advertising strategies that the most successful games have used in the past (Angry Birds, Temple Run, Fruit Ninja etc.). Though there is no real way to make sure that any mobile application is widely used and downloaded, we have devised a way to help the mobile gaming project get in front of the eyes of thousands of people at launch. Much like every business there are risks that come with starting something new, some of these include: unable to capture the proper clientele, getting the app/product sold/disbursed and making sure the innovation of the technology we implement stays on course and Completing the proper BETA Testing within the time frame necessary to have a proper launch. We have done all the research to make this a success and will no doubt be successful once we attain the proper funding. We plan to capture clientele by mass advertising through numerous web-sites, local businesses, ads,commercials and many more. In order to ensure that the app is downloaded and purchased, we have created a free "demo" version (V.001.1) that will be released in February 2013 as a teaser as well as free power-ups for the games and other ways to obtain some bonuses. As far as our time-frame march 2013 the app should be on full course, this all depends on the funding, once we have this, we will be able to complete the testing of the real time and player search in the project and enhance our app to where it needs to be.
