Incredibly fun and addictive, Commander Flag brings a new style of puzzle packed with action to mobile platforms.

Commander Flag: Puzzle mixed with strategy and full of action

Commander Flag is a free game coming initially to iOS and Android devices, both cell phones and tablets, designed and developed by Fire Horse Studio. The game has a quite simple mechanic however can be very hard and challenging. It is aimed toward both the hardcore and casual gamers, with fast levels that can be played on the go, where Flag, the main character, must protect his people from the terrible hedgehogs.

The Game

The idea is very simple. Flag is an anteater, sworn to protect his kind. The whole anteater nation lives underground as the only way to survive against the hedgehog onslaught, however, there needs to be a way to the surface where the anteaters can gather resources and food when necessary. Unfortunately, the only way out is at the same time, the only way in, and the hedgehogs know it. They will literally rain down soldiers on your city`s entrance and it falls in the hands of the most talented anteaters to protect their beloved nation.Â

As mentioned before, the game will initially focus on mobile devices, and Â taking that into consideration we want to make a game that is at the same time simple and enjoyable for, well, basically everyone from regular to casual play-on-the-go players. As you can see on the image above, enemies constantly fall and bash their heads on your line of defense, demonstrated here as your sandbags. You must eliminate your enemies by firing at them, cutting em to pieces or even using one of the many possible helpers you might have. However, there is a catch: if they are still wearing their helmets and holding their weapons, they might attack you as their last act of vengeance! You know, hedgehogs are vicious creatures.

However, after being fired off of a cannon and having your head bashed against the ground some times, your grip won't be as strong as it used to... and after a few bounces, they will lose their gear and be susceptible to fire. You must wait until they are disarmed and then use your weapons against em.

But dont be fooled: things can get real messy. The hedgehog army is composed of many kinds of enemies that respond differently to everything. The hedgehog marines, for example, drop over your base wearing their parachute and if they manage to land safely, they will tear your defenses apart. If you shoot em, they will damage your defenses with their weapons. However, if you cut their parachute, they will have their heads bashed against the ground and be disarmed just like the others.

Hedgehog soldiers are so relentless that sometimes, even the dead come back to life to eat your defenses away. Bullets cannot kill what is already dead, and your only hope is to cut em to pieces before they are able to break through your barrier.

There are many, many other enemies planned to be on the game, fearsome, gruesome soldiers... and some others that might not look that bad, but can be a hell to fight against.

But you are not left alone in this fight. Using enemy resources, you can hire specialist anteaters and their machinery to grant you great bonuses against the hedgehogs. For example, Ralbert Reinstein is capable of blowing up the entire battlefield, eliminating every hedgehog nearby. Saint Drummont can use his machine to make the hedgehogs float just above your base, vulnerable to your fire, keeping your barrier safe.

There are many other specialists you can hire, and you can also become a specialist! Player will constantly see their name and will most certainly appreciate their help in times of need.

Between levels the player can also access the anteater capital, where he will find a way to access the item shop for some epic upgrades, and even upgrade the city to gain access to new levels, better prices, and many more advantages.

And all of this... everything... depends on you! We need your support to make this idea come to life! And... who can say no to these cute little fellas?

Features

  • Fast paced gameplay, requiring smart use of temporary buffs and resources to achieve victory.
  • Play-on-the-go, levels are fast and fun, and sometimes furious too, and since enemies are generated randomly, you will always have a different challenge.
  • Dozens of different enemies, ranging from soldiers to zombies to football players to about anything you might like, as you can help us create new enemies, providing you with unique gameplay every time.
  • Endless mode, how many hedgehogs can you kill before your base is overrun? Can you beat your friend`s score?
  • City customization, with every update to the game, expect new ways to customize your city to look just like the way you want it to look.
  • Many possible specialists, build your team and get ready to defend your base the way you see fit. You can even become one of the specialists! How cool is that?

The Team

Fire Horse Studio is a new company composed mainly of three professionals: Allan Michael Smith - our programmer, Danilo Franco - our 2D artist and Fernando Colombo - our 3D artist. Our team has worked together for years now, we have developed games using both Flash and Unity 3D mostly for web. We`ve won the Brazil Game Jam 2010 which challenged teams from each university in Brazil to develop a game in 48 hours. We`ve been invited to participate again in 2011 but unfortunately due to personal problems we could not go to the competition.

We know we can make something great if we work together, completely focused on the project. And this is what we are trying to do now, with Fire Horse Studio, and this is the reason we need Kickstarter.

Why Kickstarter?

We believe in this idea. We quit our previous jobs recently and been working with little projects that have been taking most of our time to have some income, however that is not what we want. We want to make this game come true. We believe we can bring a fun, addictive experience to everyone, something cool and enjoyable, see our ideas gain life. We had planned to develop this game using our own resources and this is how we got here, however this game is an ever evolving idea, from a simple, but cool mechanic, we began to expand and find new ways to make gameplay deeper, more meaningful, to truly create a great experience that goes much farther then we expected when we started. But we need your help to make this game come true.

Creating a game is extremely time demanding, it involves not only a lot of programming, 3D modeling, concepts, sketches, design, soundtrack, it requires a lot of balancing and fixing to create a truly smooth and enjoyable moment for the player. Making games only on the free time, almost as a "hobby", is hardly something viable and unfortunately we are running out of resources.

Your generous pledges will be used to pay for the sound design, and we will be able to put ourselves into the project full time, creating a more complete, fun and enjoyable experience. This game should take at least six months to be completely developed, as we still are in a very early stage of development. If we can get more then we asked, we will implement even more fun stuff! More enemies, more achievements, more assistants, more customization, better music, everything. We even have some crazy ideas that might come to life depending on the values we might get that can turn this game into something beyond awesome!

Our most sincere thanks!

I think we can`t possibly thank you enough. Thank you for reading, for watching the video, for sharing the idea... and thank you for your pledges! We still have no idea if we are going to make it, but anyway thank you for the opportunity of being here!

We will be sure to keep you all posted on the updates the project might receive and rest assured that we will do our best to bring you the best experience possible.
