Anime Poker is a free to play RPG style implemented online poker game playable on facebook, iOS, and our website.

Cloudmonster is a new game company!

We’d like to introduce our first project: Anime Poker, an RPG-style poker game playable on Facebook, iOS, and our website.

Cloudmonster will be the leading destination for games on iPhone, Facebook, and the web. Please follow us for updates.

Founded in the spring of 2013, California-based Cloudmonster, Inc. is a new game company that specializes in mobile, Facebook, and online games. This year, our team has been hard at work developing several different game titles. Our team is comprised of three artists, one game designer and sound engineer, and three game developers. We are a young, highly motivated group of people, working remotely to make our dreams come true. We have number of projects in the works from many game genres, including MMORPG, Tower Defense, TCG, and more. All of our games will be free-to-play online games in an anime style, which will be playable on Facebook, iOS, and the web.

Our first game, titled Anime Poker, is an online poker game that incorporates RPG elements with an anime visual style.One of our goals in creating this game is to create an environment that offers more to gamers than a simple tabletop poker gaming.Anime Poker will include a colorful world map, customizable avatars, an-game chat system, and unlockable destinations and opponents.

We chose Anime Poker to be our first game title, a project which we can develop and release from our current working situation.At the moment, our team is spread over several cities and holding down various side jobs; we will need to be working together in one location in order to tackle greater projects.So while we will continue to develop Anime Poker remotely, in the future we intend to move into an office in Los Angeles, California, so Cloudmonster can come together to work face to face.

For now, we are working tirelessly and the project is about half way done. We are very confident with the results so far!Even though we do have other games in the pipeline, we are dedicated to finishing Anime Poker before turning our focus away to our future projects.

Our game takes place in a small anime town.At the start of the game, you simply start as a poker player who dreams of becoming the world champion poker player. Anime Poker’s map is still under development, but the end product will include a variety of locations such as hotels and apartments.You can even utilize your hard-earned winnings to buy and decorate your own apartment! We will keep you updated with any ongoing changes as we implement them.   

As a beginning player, you start small.With Anime Poker implementing RPG elements, you will unlock better venues and bigger tables as you grow. Each of the different hotels and casinos will host different dealers and sport different themes. Of course, if your dream is to be the best card shark in town, nothing can stop you from playing through the circuit on your way to the top!

Anime Poker is an online player vs. player game which will be playable on popular platforms such as iOS, Facebook, and our website.Additionally, you will be able to customize your avatar with accessories and visual upgrades to impress your friends at the table, or in the in-game chat system.

We've been working as Cloudmonster since March of this year, so of course we are a young game company just starting out. We want our first game to do well and we'd like to add all the great features we have in mind.And though we’ve been able to get to this point on the resources we’ve invested so far, we still need to finance our programmers and artists as they work to finish the game.The goal of this Kickstarter campaign is to raise the funding to get Anime Poker ready for release as a product we can be proud to put our names on. It's our first game, and quite simply, we want it to come out perfect.

Starting a game company is a huge challenge. We want you to be part of our company and we will never forget your help. No matter how much you pledge, your name will remain on our website… forever! 

We've been thinking about what we should offer our backers and hopefully you'll all find our pledges interesting and exciting!

Anime Poker has two main NPCs. They will help you throughout the game and teach you everything you need to know, so please be nice to them! You cannot purchase them in-game but there are other characters for you to unlock and use as your own avatar. Each character has a unique in-game story behind them, and we hope you’ll have fun meeting them all!

Main NPC1
Name : Emi
Age : 21

Birthday: August 1 (Leo) Creative, idealistic, ambitious.Born leaders, Leos attract followers with their warm, courageous personalities. But they can sometimes put themselves in charge and their pride won’t allow them to take no for an answer.Leos aren’t afraid to gamble, confident that no matter the hand they are dealt, they are sure to come out on top.

Lore:Emi comes from a large family of over-achievers.Her grandmother, head of the household, made the family fortune with a series of inventions, setting the bar for her children and grandchildren.Emi’s father became a professor of archaeology at a prestigious university, and her mother is one of the nation’s foremost names in bio-engineering.

But Emi, the baby of the family, has never wanted to go to medical school or become a concert pianist or win a gold medal at the Olympics like her brothers and sisters have done.Of course she wanted to follow in her grandmother’s footsteps and make a name for herself, but finding an area of expertise unclaimed by her family proved to be quite the challenge.

While on summer vacation with some friends, Emi came to the Casino looking for fun and excitement.She found all that and more – by the end of the day she had discovered her calling in life.Right away she packed her bags and wrote to her family that she had decided to pursue her new dream of becoming a Poker World Champion.

At first her family didn’t know what to think.But once Emi’s grandmother gave her blessing, there was no talking her out of it.Her grandmother offered these words of wisdom, “When I was your age, I took a gamble on my dreams.Don’t let anybody tell you not to try.”

So now Emi works at the Casino, living her dream.Even though she is still new to the game, she knows that with hard work and determination she can become the best in the world.She sees every day as a chance to learn something new.With a smile and a can-do attitude, Emi is always willing to offer a helping hand to those following dreams of their own.

Catch phrase:“Follow me to the top!”


Main NPC2
Name : Hiyoko
Age : 21

Birthday:November 11 (Scorpio) Curious, intuitive, passionate and motivated. Determined to succeed, Scorpios don't know how to quit; even total failures tend to be seen as minor setbacks.Often secretive of true feelings, Scorpios can be manipulative and hold grudges against those who cross them.

Lore:Hiyoko has always known what she wanted.More importantly, she has always been able to play people in order to get what she wanted.Mastering the art of the bluff at an astoundingly young age, Hiyoko has used her intuition of others’ motives to gain the upper hand in cards and in life.

The youngest entrant in the Poker World Series a few years ago, Hiyoko dazzled veterans of the game with her unfaltering skill.They sweated under her steely gaze; she seemed never look at her cards, but rather straight into her opponent’s soul.She checked, called, and raised until only one player remained between her and the victor’s spoils.

The last hand was dealt, and a tense silence stretched across the table.Her cards were duds, but she barely saw them.In the game of poker, the bluff was king, and she its queen.She danced around her opponent card after card, reading his every move, until she knew she had him.Going all in, she waited for him to fold under the pressure.

Instead of fame, fortune, and eternal glory, Hiyoko faced a royal flush.In disbelief, she was forced to fold and concede victory to Sai, the world’s newest Poker Champion.

Since that disastrous defeat, Hiyoko has left the pro circuit – but only temporarily.She currently works as a dealer at the casino, studying the players that come and go, absorbing every tip and trick she can in order to become better.Her goal is to challenge Sai to a rematch, and this time, she has every intention of beating him at his own game.

Catch phrase:“Work hard, play hard.There’s no difference to me.

Character001
Name : Sai
Age : 24

Birthday: October 10 (Libra) Charming, fair, and easy-going.  Libras are all about balance and harmony, and they put a lot of effort into keeping everyone happy.  As such, they are excellent diplomats, able to read others’ inner thoughts for often strategic ends. 

Lore:  Sai doesn’t like to make waves.  He likes to sit back and observe the world around him, understanding personal interactions as if they are puzzle pieces fitting into place.  He can practically see the tick and tock of the minds at work around him, and always knows what people want to hear.

Lukily, he found that he could use this skill for profit, playing poker professionally.  For a long time he lived the easy life, people flocking to him because of who they thought he was – a suave, rich playboy who loved the limelight.  Still, he never let anyone come too close.

At the highest point in his career, he found himself competing for Poker World Champion along with several other top tier players.  Along with a young girl named Hiyoko, he easily shredded the competition.  Seeing her as the only one at his level, Sai focused on reading her plays alone.

Finally it came down to the last hand between them.  Though Sai had been dealt a strong hand, he could tell from Hiyoko’s maneuvers that hers had to be stronger.  Curious to see his opponent’s winning cards, he matched her gutsy all-in bet, showing his royal flush.  But he would never know what she held, as she immediately folded in defeat.  Stunned, Sai was announced Poker World Champion.  For the first time in his life, he had entirely fallen for a bluff.

Since then, Sai has drifted away from the attention of the pro circuit.  The thought that there are still people out there whose minds are closed off from him is too unsettling.  For now he seeks a way to perfect his skill at reading his opponent, and wonders if he’ll ever get another chance to match wits with the girl who almost won it all.

Catch phrase:  “The true spirit of the game lies beyond the cards.”
