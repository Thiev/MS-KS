GrifballHub is raising funds to purchase a booth at RTX 2014, complete with tips, swag, and playable Grifball!

Grifball is the world's greatest Sports-Game-Played-Within-A-First-Person-Shooter, originally created by Rooster Teeth's Burnie Burns. RTX is the Rooster Teeth convention, where over 10,000 Rooster Teeth and Halo fans gather to celebrate gaming and the internet. It's a match made in heaven!

Last year, GrifballHub was able to purchase a booth at RTX 2013, thanks entirely to the help of the outstanding community that donated over $4500 to our Kickstarter. We were able to provide RTX attendees with three days of Grifball tournaments, contests, and prizes, as well as mountains of free Grifball swag, including lanyards, wristbands, buttons, stickers, cinch sacks, custom box art, and t-shirts!

This year, we want to do it all again...but bigger and better!

Our goal is set at $4500, which is enough to ensure that we can provide the same awesome Grifball experience as last year. $4500 will allow us to buy:

  • A double-sized booth of 20' x 10'
  • 1000 GrifballHub wristbands
  • 500 GrifballHub and RTX buttons
  • 350 GrifballHub lanyards
  • 100 GrifballHub cinch sacks
  • 50 Grifball t-shirts
  • 50 GrifballHub posters
  • 1000 copies of custom Halo Grifball boxart
  • 1000 GrifballHub at RTX stickers
  • All of the equipment necessary to provide playable Grifball at the booth!

After the smashing success of last year's RTX, we would not want to return with anything less than what was accomplished last year.

But why stop there?

We're dreaming big and only want the best for our burgeoning eSport. RTX will be the largest gathering of the world's best Halo players and Grifballers. They deserve the very best, and we want to provide them with a truly awe-inspiring experience!

We are partnering with Iron Gaming to provide Grifballers with the greatest time of their life. Thanks to a generous donation from GAEMS, the GrifballHub booth will include 16 GAEMS stations! This will allow us to have up to 16 Grifballers playing two matches at the same time every hour of every day of RTX!

But we need space for all this awesome! If we are able to raise enough funds, we can double our booth space again so we can have 16 playable Grifball stations at the booth. We will be able to run legitimate eSports tournaments at our booth, complete with real prizes for the victors. The championship matches will be played at the Iron Gaming Main Stage, where the action will be live-streamed for Grifball fans around the world! With 16 Grifball stations running all the time, it'll be the greatest Grifball tournament in the history of the sport, and one of the biggest Halo events ever.

All we need to make this dream a reality is your support. $4500 is a solid floor, but it's just the beginning. Further fundraising goals will include more and better swag as we amp up our RTX presence even more. The dream of a 400 square foot booth with 16 playable Grifball stations will cost a bit more, but we know that our community of supporters are willing and able to make that dream a reality.

Stretch Goals

If $4500 is raised through this Kickstarter, we'll be able to once again have a booth at RTX, complete with playable Grifball and loads of free swag. But Grifballers are never satisfied unless we're moving onward and upward!

Help us reach these additional goals to make RTX 2014 a truly legendary experience!

If we raise $8000, we'll have the most awesome booth at RTX. Not only will we have all the swag from hitting $4500, but we'll be able to double our booth space again! Last year we had 200 square feet, but hitting this goal will allow us to have 400 square feet! What's more, we'll have room for all 16 Grifball stations at the booth, so we'll have multiple games of Grifball going on all day, every day!

This is where the extra swag starts. At $9400, we'll be able to fully embrace Grifball's role as the Sport of the Future by including Grifball sweat wristbands with our swag! Every donor who gives $115 or more will receive one, and we will also give them away as special prizes at the booth.

At $10,500, the swag just keeps coming! If we hit this goal, we will be able to ramp up our merchandise to the level where we can have additional GrifTees for sale at the booth! We'll have our classic "Vintage" shirt, our "Grifball World Tour" shirt, and our "Grifball Pro" shirt!

And for our Kickstarter donors, everyone who receives a physical reward will get a shirt of their choosing! So the folks who donate $115 or more will receive both the Grifball Pro shirt and an additional shirt, while the shirt count for our $75 donors will go from zero to one. You get to pick the style and sizes!

If we get up to $11,800 we'll have even more swag! We'll DOUBLE the number of sweatbands and give them away to our $75 donors, too! We'll also print a run of GrifballHub bumper stickers for our $115 donors!

Hitting this goal will also allow us to have internet access at the booth, so teams at RTX can go right to GrifballHub.com and sign up for the leagues!

Grifballers gotta stay hydrated, right? At this level, all our donors at $115 and above will receive an exclusive GrifballHub water bottle!

Hitting this goal will also allow us to DOUBLE the number of cinch sacks ordered, so we can give away even more at the booth!

Over $14,000? Are you kidding me?

If we hit $14,300, you will have tapped us dry. This will allow us to print more GrifTees for RTX, but it would also allow us to...

...offer a $1,000 prize pool for Grifball tournament champions at RTX!

Who ever said playing video games wouldn't pay off?

  • All the Thank Yous

Our $115.00 donors will get:

  • All of the above
  • A "Grifball Pro" t-shirt in addition to the other t-shirt of your choice
  • A Grifball bumper sticker
  • An exclusive GrifballHub poster
  • A stainless steel Grifball water bottle
  • A physical copy of our Cards Against Grif set

And our booth will have:

  • Twice the space as last year's
  • Sixteen playable Grifball stations
  • A live-streamed Grifball tournament with a huge cash prize
  • Mountains of free swag
  • More GrifTees for sale
  • And more!

Risks and challenges Learn about accountability on Kickstarter

Although we completed a Kickstarter campaign last year and purchased and successfully ran a booth at RTX 2013, setbacks can always arise. We learned a lot from last year's experience, but additional merchandise problems or unexpected costs are always a concern.

Our experiences last year give us the expertise we need to overcome any additional hurdles that may arise between now and RTX. GrifballHub has been a very successful (and growing!) enterprise for almost six years now, and the staff of the site and the Grifball community have always found success, no matter the problems.
