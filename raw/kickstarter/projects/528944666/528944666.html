An adventure game following Alice and Dorothy through Wonderland and Oz as they try to stop the evil Red Queen of Hearts

The Red Queen of Oz: Two Fates is an exciting new point and click adventure game based on the graphic novel "The Red Queen of Oz".

Play as Dorothy and Alice as they revisit curious locations in Oz and Wonderland and quest to find a way to stop the Red Queen of Hearts from destroying both lands!

Navigate strange and beautiful settings, solve curious puzzles and interact with an amazing cast of characters spanning two fascinating lands!

Alice and Dorothy aren't alone! As they venture, they will meet characters who will join them on their quest to stop the Red Queen of Hearts' rampage before everything is lost!

Making games as a part-time job more often than not ends up with production ceasing completely and much work being lost. In order for us to ensure a timely release and a high-quality game we need to focus our complete attention on it to give the game the love it deserves.

The backing of enthusiastic fans drives us to push harder and deliver more. Nothing provides more excitement for us than knowing what we're doing is anticipated and we have the opportunity to make great things for great people!Â

Risks and challenges Learn about accountability on Kickstarter

With "The Red Queen of Oz" graphic novel still in production when this project begins we have slight overlap of Simon's time but nothing conflicting since Simon's role on the game comes in after the graphic novel is finished.

My time on the graphic novel ends in December, then I have some product manufacturing to oversee in February, again, not enough time to cause a conflict in the development schedule for the game.

Should the campaign exceed expectations, we are prepared to bring in additional artists to take up the extra work and maintain a tight development schedule and release date.
