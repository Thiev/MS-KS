Mean Mallows (iOS) - Help Choco, the last brave candy bar, battle against Mega-Graham and his vast army of evil marshmallows.

ABOUT MEAN MALLOWS:

SYNOPSIS: As darkness falls upon a dim-lit campfire, the last brave candy bar (Choco) fights to save a campsite from his evil nemesis (Mega-Graham) and his vast army of evil marshmallows. Help Choco battle against the Mean Mallows and protect the campgrounds from Mega-Graham’s sinister plot to kill all chocolate-kind to use for s’mores.

GAME PLAY DESCRIPTION: Mean Mallows is a survival game (3rd person shooter) played from the perspective of a warrior candy bar fighting to save his kind from extinction. Armed with a bow and arrow (fabricated from a rubber band and tooth pick) he brawls cute, yet incredibly wicked, marshmallows. Players maneuver choco via joystick/button controls to avoid hazardous game elements, shoot arrows (toothpicks,matches,etc), collect weapon upgrades (matchboxes containing an array of items to shoot), and save captured prisoners. There are several difficulty modes to accommodate anyone from the casual gamer to the mobile gaming addict. Mean Mallows is an exciting, fun-filled game with undeniably rad characters you’ll quickly fall in love with.

Platform: iPhone (other platforms TBD)
Operating System Specs: iOS 4 tested
Device Orientation: Horizontal
Application Type: Game

WHY SHOULD I DONATE MONEY TO MEAN MALLOWS?

Don’t merely think of your pledge as a donation. Your support is viewed as more than just money to us and will really help us take strong steps in the right direction as an independent studio. We genuinely love what we do and want to share that passion through the games we make.

Mean Mallows is destined for great things and we’re proud to be developing such a unique game of this caliber. Be a part of the Mean Mallow movement and we will forever be grateful.

DON’T FORGET ABOUT THE REWARDS!!!

Please spare a moment to check out some of the Mallow-tastic rewards that come with your pledges! KickStarter backers have the opportunity to get their hands on limited release/collector’s series items. Most of which, will not be released to the public outside of KickStarter. This experience is unique to us and we’re creating some cool rewards just for you!

NOTE ABOUT PLEDGING – if you back a project, you don’t pay anything until the END of the campaign. You can pledge any amount and lower/raise your pledge at any time during the campaign. If we fail to reach our funding goal, all pledges are cancelled and no one pays a cent. For more info, see the Backer’s FAQ here.

KICKSTARTER BACKERS TO THE RESCUE!

It is well known that game production can be an expensive process and we have hurdled many obstacles to get where we are now. Though we have been approached by potential investors, larger financial institutions have only shown interest based on possible dollar signs. Their willingness to water down Mean Mallows for the sake of profit simply won’t do and we are choosing to take a different path. There are many hidden agendas, stipulations, and risks when getting tied to bigger investors, so we’re turning to the KickStarter community for help. Thus far, by financing most of the project ourselves, we’ve been able to develop Mean Mallows without creative limitations or sacrificing quality. So here we stand with the finish line ahead of us and we are determined to see this project through. Your donations say more than you know and our gratitude for your support goes beyond what words can express.

WHAT WILL THE MONEY BE USED FOR?

Funding will be used toward overall development costs, necessary software tools, production assets, and promotional materials. Any excess funding will be used to add polish to the final product (level updates, character add-ons, scene upgrades, etc) and to beef up the advertising campaign (game trailer updates, online ads, website updates, guerrilla marketing assets, print assets).

THANK YOU!

We want to express our deepest appreciation for stopping by and supporting Mean Mallows. Find out more at MeanMallows.com and don’t forget to follow us on Twitter and Facebook! (this is where we’ll be updating the public on the latest and greatest Mallows news)
