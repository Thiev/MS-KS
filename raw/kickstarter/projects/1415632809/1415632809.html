Help María defeat the evil treasure and rescue King Bling from a scurvy pirate invasion.

The music was composed by the very talented Eric Skiff of http://ericskiff.com/music/.

I have a degree in Game Design, and I'm using my tendencies of producing thoughtful controversy to push a subtle message of equality with an otherwise straightforward, colorful, fun, and charming gaming experience.

This is a potentially very long game (see stretch goals), but it is by no means the endless or unbeatable saga many mobile games tend to be. This game will ship with 50 levels, with perhaps more on the way based on stretch goals and general success.

Check out these varied environments boasting their own gameplay mechanics. (footage represents a work in progress)

 More gameplay mechanics and environments will be unlocked based on successful stretch goals.

There are many things to pay for with a project like this. At the $3,000 baseline goal, you'll help cover the Google and Apple licenses, the cost of producing the rewards, and the remaining time spent on the game.

I currently create everything on my PC, but in order to publish to the iOS market, I will need to consider buying a device running mac OSX, which isn't cheap. I'm all for borrowing a friend's to make due, but the convenience is always desired....

Every dollar will be used to make this game shinier and more fun.

New stage environments will incorporate new game play mechanics, just as the water level brought in new swimming mechanics, the desert level brought in the sinking sand element, and the ice level brought in skating with momentum. You're not only getting new environments, but you're getting new ways to play!

Risks and challenges Learn about accountability on Kickstarter

Shipping prices are currently an unknown and the cost of getting your rewards to you is somewhat daunting, but I will make it happen! As a single developer, it stands to reason that if I too were abducted by evil pirates, or struck down by a rogue warrior wielding a pick, the release of this game could be hindered. Everybody pledging will get an email from me, month to month, on the status of this game's development, along with the latest BETA versions to play while you wait.

As with any game design project, a delay can happen if an unforeseen addition to mechanics are added too late. These additions will be transparent and available in the beta testing releases.

FAQ

  • What if the goal isn't reached?

    I have faith that it will, but your pledge will be refunded if the project falls short. Kickstarter does not keep your money. I have a noticeable presence online, so you can always contact me or disparage my name in front of my online crowd regarding your rewards. Pledge with confidence.

    Last updated: Sun, Feb 23 2014 9:00 pm EST
  • What can my NPC or Enemy Design be?

    NPC is an acronym for non-playable character. This character can look like you, or a character of which you hold full rights to. No NPC can be of an offensive/disparaging nature. Feel free to message me if you feel as if your idea treads on this territory.

    As far as enemy designs, it must do these two things:
    1. Be killed or attack in a unique way (all enemies do something different. I can work with you to ensure your idea is different).
    2. Related to treasure in some way. From its design, to its name, something about it must involve treasure.

    If you have further questions regarding this, please send me a message and I will respond as soon as I see it!

    Last updated: Sun, Feb 23 2014 9:00 pm EST
