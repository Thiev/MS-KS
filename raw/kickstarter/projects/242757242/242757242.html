FEATURES

  • Command a customized crew which must survive the entire game
  • Each unit has unique attributes, skills & upgrades
  • Choose your own path across the planet's surface
  • Randomized story elements for each region to explore
  • Procedurally generated maps, each game is unique
  • Full voice acting for captains. Our trailer was voiced by voice actor Scott McNeil (League of Legends, Warhammer 40,000, Dragon Ball Z, etc...)
  • Permadeath! Once they die, they're gone for the entire game
  • Build, repair and customize structures for defence, offence & resource collection
  • Open Source Art for the game development community. On release, all our custom-made art will be release under a Creative Commons license so everyone can join in the game-dev fun!

Hextraction is a real time strategy game for PC / Mac / Linux and OUYA where you create your own unique Hextraction Mercenary Crew - harvesting resources, building structures, fending off enemies and exploring dangerous territories.

Hextraction was developed in Unity and was born from the 48 hour Global Game Jam competition in early 2014. In those 48 hours, we created a game that really took us by surprise and got us very excited! While the content you see today is just a glimpse into the overall experience, we believe it is able to convey the epic, action-filled adventure that the final game will take you on.

The core of the game is built on the Real-Time Strategy (RTS) genre by offering units to manage, resources to harvest, enemies to fend off, and worlds to explore. However, the game incorporates elements from the Role Playing Game (RPG) genre, allowing you to put together a unique team of mercenaries, each with their own set of attributes, abilities and personalities.

You play as the Mission Commander for a mercenary crew tasked with recovering a dense concentration of phantom energy from a planet teaming with hostile life-forms. As Captain, you decide which corporation to work for, who makes up your crew, which path to take and ultimately how to conquer each territory so you can secure the planet's resources.

You want to be the best crew in the galaxy, able to secure as many resources as possible while surviving the harsh, hostile conditions of uncharted planets. In order to prepare for your mission, you must perform three steps:

Step #1 - Choose your Corporation. There are many corporations in the galaxy to choose from and your selection will have a large impact on your progression throughout the game. Various upgrades, abilities, wealth and how well a corporation can negotiate are all major factors which come into play.

Step #2 - Choose your Captain. The Captain is the leader of your team on your team and powerhouse on the battlefield. There is great adoration and respect for the position which allows a captain to influence the crew, allowing them to gain benefits and bonuses when in their presence.

Step #3 - Choose your Crew. The heart and soul of every operation is the crew and you are responsible for picking the team that can get the job done.

Crew members come with a variety of skills and are split into three tiers: top, middle and low. Potential candidates in the lower tiers are numerous and can be hired for fraction of the cost, but are lacking in experience & skills. Whereas middle and top candidates will come equipped, but at a hefty price. Each crew member has a unique personality, gameplay attributes and the opportunity to gain experience which directly affects their skills.

How you select your team is completely based on personal preference and play style. Grab a couple of top ranked characters to accompany your captain, or a multitude of weaker characters in order to manage more tasks and roles. No matter who you choose, they will be responsible for exploring, constructing & repairing structures, engaging hostiles and most importantly, staying alive! Because if they should perish, they are gone for the entire game.

Your mission as a Hextraction Mercenary Crew is to conquer alien planets and strip them of their resources. Nobody said you had to play nice... sorry aliens, but this is our rock now and you're getting in the way of our rockets.

Complete one of two main goals. Either retrieve a specific amount of resources for your corporation from each territory, or conquer the territory by annihilating all alien presence. Basically you either build a mercenary stronghold or blow everything up, it's your choice.

Of course, every now and then you'll encounter a random story event where you'll be faced with an un-expected scenario! Perhaps you must rescue a stranded crew member, or maybe you find yourself amidst an alien breeding and their none too happy you're messing up their groove. With a massive collection of these unique, randomized story elements throughout the game, you'll never be sure what to expect.

 As you complete missions, your team will grow stronger and you will be able to venture into more difficult territories. Exploring each new uncharted planet means bravely venturing into the unknown, which is why Hextraction is completely procedural. Every new game you'll be given a new crew, varying territories, randomized maps, and new stories to explore.

As you progress through the game, you'll advance through three main territories: geologic, biologic and crystallic. Starting in a barren wasteland of rocks, desert and minimal life, you'll make your way through lush alien forest full of diverse (and dangerous life) before finally moving on to a truly foreign landscape filled with terrifying, nightmarish creatures and landscape.

The highly unstable nature of the planet itself means not only will you be battling the aliens which inhabit it, you'll have to deal with earthquakes and asteroid strikes! No one told you stripping a planet of it's resources was going to be easy.

A successful Hextraction operation relies on well-built and maintained offensive, defensive, & mining structures.

Once a structure has been completed, crew members are able to add buffs in order to increase it's stats & effectiveness. What kinds of buffs you are able to apply and how they affect the structure vary based on it's type & function.

Operating a Hextraction mining operation doesn't come cheap, which is why structures are constructed on site by the crew with a single purpose in mind, often using the resources collected from the planet. Structures can be recycled in order to regain a small percentage of the original resources invested.

Your biggest challenge will be dealing with the endless waves of "filthy bugs" who seem to take pleasure in making operations difficult. These creatures come in a wide variety of types, each with their own unique brand of chaos.

Risks and challenges Learn about accountability on Kickstarter

Making a game is no easy task. We must face the reality and recognize the variety of risks associated with such an arduous goal. Here’s how we are dealing with each:

FINANCIAL RISK: We have invested in Hextraction ourselves and are prepared to do what is necessary to reach completion. Living on a bare minimum living allowance is the reality we have accepted and have been living while funding early production from our own savings. We wouldn’t be asking for support without having already invested in the game ourselves. We can complete it with your help!

SCHEDULE RISK: The completion date for the game is an estimate based on our experience completing similar multimedia projects in the past. If certain features take longer than expected to implement, there’s a chance the completion date will slip. If such an event happens, we will be vigilant to keep you, the backers, informed along the way. We plan to be releasing early alpha & beta builds to the backers who choose to participate, so even if final completion date moves, you’ll still get hands-on with the game before then.

FUN RISK: Let’s face it, we’ve all played something someone was excited about and it ended up being less fun than anticipated. This is why we believe in early access testing and a rapid prototyping development schedule. If something isn’t working, we will find out early on in our schedule and fix it, long before the game ever launches. We strive for quality, and fun is the key ingredient!
