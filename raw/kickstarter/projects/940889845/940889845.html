Update #1 - Stretch Goals Added

Keepers of Grimoire: Trading Card Game combines the best features of a real-life trading card game, or TCG, with the convenience of online and mobile gaming. Players will be able to experience Keepers of Grimoire TCG absolutely free and can grow their collection just by playing the game and trading with friends.Â 

Players can:

  • Build unique deck strategies
  • Play against friends
  • Compete in online tournaments
  • Participate in competitive elo ranking
  • Explore a rich, in-depth fantasy world of lore
  • Play wherever you want, for as long as you want

Keepers of Grimoire combines the best attributes of a real TCG with all the perks of the digital world. While many digital TCGs are highly simplified, Keepers of Grimoire will provide an authentic player-versus-player TCG experience in digital format. This means players have absolute freedom to build decks to fit individual tastes, strategies, and gameplay.

We enable all of our players to participate in all the positive aspects of a physical card game, such as trading, collecting, chatting, and making new friends, as well as customizing card decks with sleeves and playing mats. With Keepers of Grimoire, we make trading an integral part of the game because cards are sold in packs, never individually. This means if players want specific cards, they have to trade for them, buy a new card pack, or keep playing to earn new card packs.

What Makes Us Different?

Players Earn Packs
With Keepers of Grimoire, players can earn packs of additional cards just from playing the game, instead of only having the option to purchase more. Participants can play anytime, with anyone, and we provide a wide range of trading options. We have features to maximize customization options, such as saving multiple decks to individual player profiles. We also strive to make each and every card meaningful and useful.

NOT â€œPay to Winâ€
In other TCGs, whoever spends the most money has the best deck, but here at Keepers of Grimoire we strive to keep our playing field equal for all players. Newer sets will not make the older cards obsolete. This will help prevent massive spending each time a new set comes out - the same outpouring of money that often discourages players of other TCGs from continuing to play. We want a meaningful game that players will love. To us, that means making it possible for players to keep using their favorite strategies, no matter the card edition. Weâ€™ve dedicated a lot of time to making each card balanced and fun to use!

Simple Gameplay
Basically, our â€œresource systemâ€ is hand dependent. Players, referred to as â€˜Keepers,â€™ must pay with the cards in hand in order to play high level minions. Keepers may attack their enemy with one minion at a time, which the opponent may choose to defend, or take the hit directly. We also have a variety of card types including terrain cards, ability cards, and gambit cards. To see the full rulebook, please visit:Â http://www.keepersofgrimoire.com/official-rule-book/

No Card Transformation
In Keepers of Grimoire, we donâ€™t believe in cards that â€œlevel upâ€ or evolve. This does not make sense for a real TCG. Instead, we have special music to go with rare cards. We believe our music, composed entirely in-house, alongside our lore, can make the cards come to life while maintaining the integrity of the game as a TCG.

Special Digital Collection
Here at Orca Bear Studios, we realize that a lot of players put a high value on the rarity, or collectible qualities, of trading cards. We feel that gaming companies have overlooked the worth of â€˜first edition cardsâ€™ because current digital TCGs simply do not have them. In order to maintain the excitement of first edition cards, we have designed a unique 'time stamp' method that will distinguish our first edition cards from cards obtained at a later date. This method tracks the date that the card packs were purchased or earned and all the cards within the pack will have the exact time and date of the original acquisition. For collectors, this will deeply enhance the game and add value to their cards. It also ensures our Kickstarter backers receive a handful of first edition cards, because everyone who pledges $35 or more receives 5 free packs .

Play Anywhere, Any Time
Apart from PC/Mac/Linux clients, Keepers of Grimoire is designed for mobile play. We plan to release a Keepers of Grimoire TCG app for both iOS and Android. Players can take the TCG experience with them anywhere they go. Waiting for an appointment? Challenge or make some friends by playing a game! Bored but with limited time? Explore the world in the single-player campaign. Waiting for a friend? Finalize a new strategy. Play Keepers of Grimoire TCG in any situation, any time frame.

Customizable Strategy
Keepers of Grimoire has all the depth and customization of a real TCG with the bonus option to create multiple decks. This means players can try multiple decks and strategies while easily saving past decks, exploring limitless card combinations. Keepers of Grimoire will also have a single-player campaign mode available.

Single Player Mode
Players wishing to play alone (PvE) want and need a different digital campaign mode than competitive play (PvP). We will add meaningful quests and opportunities for single players to earn achievements and rewards, while making decisions that will impact the story as it unfolds.

Free to Play
Keepers of Grimoire is completely free. As mentioned before, players can earn packs through gameplay to improve their decks without paying a dime! We have decided to incorporate an Elo (ranking system) within the game, in order to allow people to play with others close to their skill level. In order to keep track of skill levels, this ranking system is more complex than the simple win/loss ratios traditionally seen in online TCGs. Keeping our ranking system in mind, we will also include alternative playing modes so players can test their decks without â€œtarnishingâ€ their rank.
