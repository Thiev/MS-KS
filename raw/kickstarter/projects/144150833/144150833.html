Battle of Cultures is TPP MMORPG available by browser, set in a new fantasy world developed by Rage Games with help of Magdalena Raubo.

Battle of Cultures

The story - in brief :

It takes place in fantasic universe where the four fractions (Pirates, Vikings, ronin - samurai Persians) suddenly been transferred to another dimension due to curse of magical stones that have been found over the years by the leaders of these factions. As a result of what we all now are forced to struggle for dominance and survival of the site's own nation. Players actions have dynamic influence on political situation in the world and every week there is change of alliances due to voting made among users.

Please listen to our official soundtrack ! 

 What makes the Battle of Cultures a unique game?

  • The game does not require installation, because it is fired in a browser, thanks to which you can log in to the game on each computer.
  • Innovative strategic game mode. Each player in the first tab, see the map, which refreshes every 30 seconds (this means that Live sees the changes Diplomatic), while in the second tab takes the gameplay (camera view characters and gameplay similar to SMITE), which affects the change in the overall strategic map .
  • Changing allies (fractions) by voting. Player once a week gives voice to who would be in the alliance, and the game engine collects the voices of all players from each fractions and changes diplomatic relations between them. For comparison, imagine WoW and the fact that you can just be in alliance with the elves, and another time with the orcs. This leads to rotation and reduces the dominance of one site so the game is very balanced and does not allow for complete dominance of one of the factions.
  • The influence of the guild on players. Players in the city have the opportunity to buy their own home for real currency, where they can hide gold, equipment, decorate an apartment, then decide whether they want to join a guild. If you decide to join the guild will have to pay to the bank guild. The collected monies leader is purchasing needed power ups which are granted to all members on a permanent basis * (* until you leave the guild).

GAMEPLAY :

By creating this amazing adventure game we tried from the beginning to give the greatest possible margin of maneuver for player.

Players have the ability to play PVE game (against monsters) or PVP (against players) in addition player can take part in the voting with who you want to be (in that week) in the alliance. Then the engine based on the number of votes selects 2 pairs of allies for the next week.

In this way, the game is continuously dynamic and not constant as is the case in most competing production.

Strategic dynamic mode of land takeover in real time is something you do not have in ANY GAME!

Well, imagine you're playing just against other players (for example, in the arena) and goes to you to win! Suddenly you realize that after every won game you contribute to changing the diplomatic game and it immediately! Changes are seen in worldmap in instant.

Therefore each player will have an impact on the fate of the game and it's on a massive scale!  

In addition:

- Each player has the opportunity to establish their own clan, adding to your friends, chat in real time (online) and offline play by yourself or in a group of friends.  

- We intend to implement a concept that will allow players to earn real money when tournament is won by you! 

- Each month we want to constantly add new maps and develop the game providing players with new, irreplaceable experience.
