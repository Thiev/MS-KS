ABOUT THE GAME:

TECH EXECUTIVE TYCOON is a business management Simulation/Strategy game that puts you in the role of the CEO of a tech company. Can you build this company into a Tech Giant? Develop Hardware that will wow your customers and make their lives easier. Build custom devices, peripherals and products that will make your competitors envy you and simultaneously worship you?

This game will be a deep strategy/simulation game. You will hire employees, delegate responsibility, manage the budget, monitor business relationships, upgrade office equipment, rent new offices, set prices, outsource and more...  

If you enjoy management games, you will definitely enjoy this game! Stay tuned for more information! ***ALL images and video is unfinished quality and placeholder art assets, final quality will be on display soon***

Planned for PC, Linux, possibly more... stay tuned

The release of the game is planned for December 2014.

FAQ

  • When will the game be released?

    We are aiming for a December 2014 release. At the stage of development we are currently at, things could move fast or things could slow down, so as a result, nothing is yet written in stone. You can be sure that we are working tirelessly to get it completed as soon as possible, but not too fast as to compromise quality. Stay tuned for more updates on this in the coming future!

    Last updated: Wed, Dec 25 2013 3:43 pm EST
  • What will the $7,000 be used for?

    The primary focus of the $7,000 is to cover specific expenses. I have purchased a ton of equipment and software myself already, but I need help in securing Unity Licensing ($1,500), the engine I am using to develop the game. Also, I will be moving to 3dsMax ($3,650) for art asset creation and Adobe suite (roughly $1,000). These products are quite expensive, but I am very familiar with them and they are industry standard. I will have legal fees for various items such as copyright registration and other legal advice. If there is more than $7,000 after the Kickstarter, I will use the excess funds to secure better hardware for production. There are other things I will need as well, but I am covering those expenses and have covered all other expenses 100%.

    Once again, thanks for your time and donations!!

    Last updated: Sat, Dec 28 2013 9:13 am EST
  • How will we receive a copy of the game

    Currently that will be through multiple online avenues.

    - Steam (if greenlit) - you will receive a steam key to secure your copy of the game
    - other online avenues including our website. To name a few, Desura, Gamersgate, Amazon.com and more... stay tuned for more details on this.

    There are plans to create a limited number of "hard copies" to guarantee all backers get their copy. More details on all of this is soon to come.

    Last updated: Sat, Dec 28 2013 1:10 pm EST
