The Realms of Creation is a MMO RPG/RTS. Where you can be a Mayor, Farmer or a tradesmen. You control the game not us.

Watch our new video and give us some feedback on our comments

The Realms of Creation is a Massive Multi-Player RPG/RTS that allows a player to decide what kind of character you want to be. You can be a Farmer, Tradesmen, or Mayor (see more detail farther down in this document). Please also see our extended goals at the bottom of the document.

This is a game where the players control the outcome of the game. You level up by learning trades and skills from other players and/or NPCs. The higher the level of your character in a certain field the faster your character can make buildings. If you’re a mason, armor-smith, weapon-smith, farmer or the mayor, you can get the approval of your work much easier. The more you invest into your character the more output you see. The whole point of the game is to help your city grow as a community.

·Style of the Cities and Information about Infinite City: The Realms of Creation takes place in a medieval/ fantasy setting with an English style town. When you enter the World of Creation you have the option to create a new town or search for openings for a job similar to what you want to become.

·Free to play game: In our game you will be able to play the game for free. You can get extra items from our store or you can get subscriptions to get special deals with recurring in game money and items. You will be able to play the game to its fullness without a subscription.

·Subscriptions/Money: We will offer special deals for players wanting to subscribe to our game to gain more items and special privileges. It is free to play our game, but if you want a subscription you will have to pay for it. We will be adding subscriptions to the game with four different levels. The levels are bronze, silver, gold, and platinum.

In this game we will set the stage and the players will grow the world and control it. It is up to you what becomes of the game. If you’re mayor, you will change the face of the game. You could become a tyrant that makes towns and villages shake at the sight of your banner or the great king that all his/her people love. Farmers can become a farming lord with the power to own special crops that maybe no one else knew about to become as powerful as a king. Tradesmen create the items and things needed by both farmers and mayors from the lowly shovel to the great towers of a main keep; without the tradesmen the others would not be anywhere.

What separates Realms of Creation from all other RPG/RTS??

“This game is the start of a revolution. Why play multiple games to be different characters when all the characters are in one game that is in complete 3D.”

“Have you ever wanted to be able to play a game everywhere you go? This game will be coming out on Facebook, Xbox 360, PS3, Wii, Wii U, PC, Mac, Linux, and all App Stores. All of these systems will transfer all information.”

Mayors – Mayors start off with an empty land where the road system and town hall placement is mapped out for you by your engineers.

You get the building materials to build a few of your buildings you need to start out with.

You build your town hall and it releases vacancy slots for you to build different types of buildings and opens up land for farms to come to your town. Remember to build shops to bring in the tradesmen.

You will be able to set your taxes

You can set taxes on NPC town people, farmers, tradesmen, as well as outside tradesmen and farmers; you can also add ones for individual players or groups of players

Raise armies to protect and acquire new lands.

You first start off with a base unit of the different types.

After they do work for you the unit gets stronger and adds to attack or defense as their skills grow.

The next stage of the NPC of the unit will become SGT. With this he/she will get status and skills that will increase the units’ skills and strengths.

As the SGT. grows you can promote him/her to different ranks and even a general of one of your armies. But the loss of this person will hurt the morale of your army, so be careful.

Other NPCs of note (all noted NPCs will be able to gain in rank, skills and powers to help as you use them)

You will get Town advisor – He/she will let you know if you town needs something and how the health of you populous is doing. The town advisor is your right hand man/woman.

You will get a Sheriff - He/she will help keep crime down in the city and will help in the defense of the city if necessary.

You will get a Master of Secrets – He/she will run any spies, assassins, or other hirelings in that area. He/she will inform you of any upcoming attacks, if known.

You will get a town crier – He/she give out notifications to town populace and can be used to go to other towns to try to steal important NPC or population from them but be careful. You can be caught.

There are many others we will talk about soon.

Tradesmen – tradesmen are the ones that build the items and needs of the realm

Some of the items you make are for normal every day use, but others will add bonuses to different things like increase defense or attack with special sword and shields. Tradesmen’s items can increase the productivity of crops.

You start off with a small shop in Infinite City or if you have a player you know or meet that is a mayor you can start in their town.

You have a limited supply of raw materials to build items and level up with them.

At level 3 you get an apprentice that will help you build items. This apprentice can grow and learn new skills and recipes. You can also promote them to higher ranks.

After that you will get more apprentices and bigger shops.

You can also open a market stand if there is an opening in the city where your shop is located.

You can make trade agreements with other merchants and farmers to get materials and items that you need to create stronger and better items.

Mayors, farmers, and other tradesmen may hire you to do jobs or quests, you can build them items they may need or want.

There are mysteries that are out there in the world.

You can learn through trial and error. Learn how to make things that do not exist in the world currently.

If you are first to learn this you get to name it and control it, but be careful your ideas can be stolen by hired thieves and spies. So hire guards or maybe even a thief or spy of your own to protect your secrets.

This can make you a very important and rich merchant but you might need a strong mayor to help protect you because you will be very sought after.

As a tradesman you have many different skills you can choose to learn and hone to great levels

Jeweler, weapon smith, armor, teacher, master guildsman and many

more

Farmer – We want to give you a more detailed look into the world of a farmer. All the different kinds of farmers need give variety of food or resources to keep the towns’ people and the mayor happy.

You start off with a farm either at Infinite City or if you know a mayor or meet one you can start there

First you have a choice:

Crop famer – a small sack with a few cleared areas to farm and some seeds

Animal Breeder - a small sack and a small barn and starter animals

Logger – a small cabin and tools, and seeds for new trees

Miner - a small cabin and mine and tools, and excavation things

Fishermen – a small sack and fishing tools, small tanks for raising fish.

At level 3, you can get a apprentice farmer, apprentice miner, apprentice logger, or apprentice fishermen.

If you start owning more farms in other cities you can make a title for yourself like if you own a farm in every city an emperor has then you will grow your name to become an estate farmer. For a crop farmer you will start with the ideal concept of one plant and it’s up to you learn how to plant other crops and once you do that you can teach it to your NPC advisers. Once you learn different concepts you can start messing with their genetics of the plants through trial and error to create a new secret item that will be a plant blueprint that we added to the game.

Miners will start out knowing how to mine either ore or onyx gems. From the start of the game you need to invest into your miner to be able to learn how to mine out new materials like bronze, gold or gems like amber or moonstone. The more materials you learn, the more you can mix the minerals or gems with chemistry, trial, and error to unlock new secret gems or minerals like platinum or diamonds.

For animals you will start off with basic farm animals that you can raise. You can take your knowledge of animals to the next level so you can raise dragon hatchlings, dragons, wolves, or other exotic animals. You can crossbreed certain animals to unlock a whole new breed that only you get to raise and sell, making you a powerful person in the game that every mayor or king will try their hardest to get you to join their kingdom.

You can also, be a woodsman that will start off knowing how to plant a limited amount of trees. From there you can teach your character to learn what different trees are needed in order to plant and take care of them. The more you know the more money you can make and the higher the chance for you to become powerful. There are also secret trees that can be unlocked that only you get to plant and raise, which will make you a fortune.

Updates for Teachers and Master Guildsman – As a teacher going into the game you have a few options. You can join Infinite City or go to a player’s realm where you start off completely different than any other tradesman. You will become a paid apprentice for either the Academy or Barracks that is under the head teacher or trainer to do all their dirty work to earn the skills.

You can develop your teacher into becoming an Academy Professor or a Guild Master, or you can become both and have the master guilds become a secret throughout the lands. You can train NPCs for other players to rent and/or buy from you.

At level 3, you can get a NPC that is an Professor or a specialized  Guild Master advisers that work beneath you.

At the academy you get paid from NPCs that want training in a certain subject. The other way you get paid as a Professor will be by other players, if they just hired a bunch of NPCs and they don’t have the time to train them, he will send all of them to you to train. You can train them up to your set training skills over a course of time.

Now if you go down the Guild Master path. You will be training Thieves, Soldiers, Policemen, Spies and Assassins. You will be hired by the mayor or players to train guards or soldiers to help protect the city or private property. You have to decide what matters most in your skills to train. You have to pick, is it better to learn nothing but skills to make sure they don’t get caught or is it better to max out their abilities to not talk under torture, so they don’t ever release any information about who sent them or who paid for it. The choice is yours.

Lastly you can be a Professor and a Guild Master.It’s up to you what skills and options are best for your character.

Updates for Secret items – Secret items in the game are blueprints that we put into the game to be unlocked by one person and it’s up to that one person if he wants to sell it to others or keep it for his/her own gain. With you keeping the secret item, you will be the only person in the entire world to have this secret recipe.Once the recipe is found we will deactivate it so if others in the game try to put mix the same ingredients they won’t unlock anything. Also, once you unlock a secret item you get to name that item or if you want you can leave it as the current name in the real world. There will be some secret items that will need multiple people to come together to make it. Things like invisible cloaks and guns will need people from all different trades to come together to make these items and they will be very difficult to make. This is why it is so important to get as many players in your town so your town can grow together and become the greatest empire the world has ever seen.The other key thing to remember is when you unlock a new item to hire guards or soldiers to watch your land from thieves that people can hire. The other thing to keep in mind is if someone steals your current recipe you can send thieves to go destroy a copy version of a recipe but you cannot send a thief to destroy the original copy.

Farmer example

Farmer: In our game a farmer is not just a crop farmer.It is a person that gets all the raw materials that all the players and NPC (non-player Characters) need. You can be a miner that specializes in different types of minerals, ores and gems. You can be a dairy farmer or plant farmer. You can raise and train animals and breed new faster and stronger ones. You can be a logger and learn how to make the different types of trees and even wild herbs and other unique plants which you can grow and harvest them. You can learn how to breed and make your own variety of fish and other marine life so you can sell aka fisherman. This is only the beginning as we go deeper into how we what to evolve the game.

Tradesmen example

Tradesman: As a tradesmen you can create items and become an expert in building them so that Kings and Barons will fight over you to get your products.If you work hard you might unlock a mystery that you will be able to control and name so that no one else will be able to have it unless they buy it off you.There are times where a tradesmen can be more powerful than his King maybe even an emperor.Learn my friends that Knowledge is power and power is everything!!! These are a few of the trades skills.

Armor-smith * Alchemist * Engineer * Jewel Crafter * Leather-worker * Tailor * Weapon-smith * Blacksmith * Woodworker * Mason * Culinary * Enchanter/Enchantress * Guild Master.

Mayor: In game if you pick to be a mayor, the mayor has a lot on his/her shoulders.You build the infrastructure of the town.If you make the NPC towns people mad they will not come to your town.You need to attract players to your town to help you build it faster and better.You must make the choices that will affect how your towns runs and defends itself and conquer new lands.It is all up to you how your realm will run and how to affect other realms around you.You might want to try to build a Kingdom or empire.To do that you have two options: Convince others mayors to join your alliance or you can attack them try to beat them down to give them the choice join your alliance or start over and make a new town in another land as you take over their current lands.If you don't team up with other mayors or take over other mayors then your tradesman and farmers can't expand, they can't open up new farms or stores in other cities increasing their revenue. In turn, they may decide to leave your city to a different area because they have that option. But you have to remember mayors might join you and remember what you did to them. They may ally with others behind your back to over throw you.So choose wisely.

No, the game is completely free to play and will allow every person to be able to work on their character on any device whether it is a cell phone, a console or a computer.

The second question you may ask: will it cost to play this game on my Xbox or my PS?

The answer to that question is no. It is completely free and we will have it where you can buy Xbox live points or PS points and purchase a certain amount of in game money that will be added to the game, so you can buy furniture or even your choice of fertilizer.

  • What is the permanent dragon? And is it decoration? Or does it have a impact on game play?

    A dragon is a animal that you can use to protect your property, town and other assets. Dragons can be used by the military to battle with other towns/cities. The permanent means if the dragon dies in battle or protecting your crops it will re-spawn after a while rather than having to get another dragon which can die while protecting your crops. No its not a decoration and it will have a huge impact on the game either being a guard or a massive threat in the military.

    Last updated: Tue, Jul 9 2013 2:12 am EDT
  • If you make a farmer will you be able to learn to harvest more than one resource? Or will you be limited to only one? On a similar note, will tradesmen be able to learn one or multiple trades.

    Yes you will develop your farmer to be able to expand what you can farm. If you raise animals you can overtime learn how to breed dragons to sell or other mystical animals. If you are a Miner you can learn to harvest all different kinds of resources. You can become a farmer that grows many different types of plants. You can even crossbreed plants, metals, and animals to make a whole new type of plant/metals/or animals. Once the plant has been unlocked you will be the only person in the world that can have that blueprints unless you sell it because once an item has been discovered no on can crossbreed to make that again. Yes tradesman can have multiple different kinds of trades. Tradesman just like farmers can mix things to create a special sword that only you have, armor, or other specialty items.

    Last updated: Tue, Jul 9 2013 2:12 am EDT
  • You create a Mayor. Will someone be able to take over your city while you are logged off?

    We have been talking about this because we don't want to make a mayor lose everything just because he isn't online. I know people don't want their city to be trusted with their adviser and they lose their city just because of that. What will most likely happen if you are offline and you are getting attacked by another city. They can only attack you so far and they aren't allowed to attack any more than that and when you get back online you will be notified who attacked you. That's why we will have special defense units that will do a great job defending your city. If you both are online then you can fully take over the other city completely. The other option which is least likely we will put in where you can't be attacked at all while the mayor is offline since that would limit the RTS side of the game.
