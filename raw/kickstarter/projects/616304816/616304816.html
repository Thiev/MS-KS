Survival Games Is Being Put On Hold For The Time Being

Your support of Survival Games has been outstanding and overwhelming, and it has meant a lot to us at Gazido Gaming. Unfortunately, due to extraneous circumstances, this project is being put on hold. This is NOT the end of Survival Games, as it has been my passion for over two years. We will return to finish what we've started. Once again, thank you so much for your support and input, as your excitement for the project has given it new light. See ya later, survivors. 

 -Hayden Allred

FAQ

  • Why are you using Unity instead of Unreal or Cryengine?

    I know that saying I'm using the Unity Engine definitely turns a lot of people away, but trust me when I say that it is the best choice for the project. Using Unity gives me access to a lot of work that I won't need to do on my own (Mostly some engine stuff that would normally take me 1-3 months to do myself). Unity has a great asset store that can help greatly throughout the process that can help me deliver the game quickly I know Unity gets a lot of crap for those horrible games that are made with it. The thing is, Unity has a free version that is available to EVERYONE! This gives everybody the is illusion that they can create a game through their drag and drop editor. Once they get to scripting, they quit. What they release is horrible, and THOSE are the games that Unity is known for. Even though Unity has made some really incredible games, people focus on the bad ones. It just depends on who's developing the game! Just look here: http://www.youtube.com/watch…

    Last updated: Fri, Apr 19 2013 1:24 am EDT
  • Won't you be sued for this game?

    No. This project is staying far enough from Hunger Games to avoid copyright issues. Yes, Survival Games features 24 players in a ring fighting to be the last one standing, and they also receive items during the game based on the Scavenger Points they earn, but that's it. There aren't any districts, they aren't children, there isn't any reference in Survival Games to Hunger Games. So Survival Games will definitely be okay.

    Last updated: Mon, Apr 15 2013 10:41 pm EDT
  • Is this game DRM free?

    The game is 100% DRM free. You will have an account that you can use anywhere! It's similar to how Minecraft handles accounts.

    Last updated: Tue, Apr 16 2013 3:06 am EDT
  • Can you set up your own server?

    There will be official servers hosted by Gazido Gaming, but you can also download the standalone server exe that will allow you to host your own server.

    Last updated: Tue, Apr 16 2013 3:09 am EDT
  • Is the Server component MS Windows only or will there be a binary service that can run on a Linux server (or MacOS server)?

    The company that creates the SDK for the standalone server says that it will support all three operating systems.

    Last updated: Tue, Apr 16 2013 3:09 am EDT
  • Is the game free?

    Nope. Survival Games will cost $15. It is a one-time fee! There will be a demo/trial mode where you can play with your friends, although the limitations are still being determined.

    Last updated: Tue, Apr 16 2013 3:09 am EDT
