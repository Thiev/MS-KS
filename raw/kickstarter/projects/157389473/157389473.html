OUTRAGEOUS Interactive DVD Board Game, 2 – 6 players play as six zany fun characters playing against each other & Lord Charles.

What makes OUTRAGEOUS so different from the rest?

Outrageous has FOUR different Game Play Options

1.. Outrageous can be played just like any other common board game i.e - Monopoly without any of the interactive elements

2.. Outrageous can be played combining the Board Game and DVD movie featuring Lord Charles Lancelot III

3.. Outrageous can be played combining Board Game and IVR or Interactive Voice Response

4.. Outrageous can be played combining Board Game, Interactive Voice Response and DVD movie

Outrageous does NOT follow the controlled elements of your typical DVD Board Game in the market place where generally the game is not practical without playing game using the included DVD movie.

Introduction

The Outrageous Organisation was originally formed by Leonard McQualter & Radio & Television personality Gavin Wood in Australia in early 1992. Both creators have a wealth of experience working in television production both in front and behind the camera, sales and marketing in music industry and Gavin a number one breakfast radio presenter on FM Radio.

Today Gavin continues with his radio and television work both in Australia & USA, now based in L.A., Leonard McQualter is managing a Web Design company in Melbourne, Australia and both friends remain in constant contact, a friendship and partnership not diminished by time or distance.

Originally the Outrageous Organisation main goal was to deliver fun family games which involved entertaining people, make them laugh, creating tears of joy via participation with and against their fellow game players. Create role play, perform silly stunts which generally have their opponents rolling on the floor in laughter and testing general knowledge via outrageous trivia questions with three possible answers.

To entice people out of their comfort zone, to excite a broad age group and see families enjoying personal time together. 

Outrageous certainly does this and more, a game that has four different player options.

Why Kickstarter?

Outrageous was originally licensed to an Australian Company in 1992 who while working with us suffered some internal issues; the eminent release of our game was put on hold and then locked away for three years.

Fast forward 20 years - What many classed as an exciting original interactive game then we believe is just as or even more relevant today.

We are seeking your support to help us return Outrageous to life and face your scrutiny and love of video and board games. We must digitally remaster our Outrageous 90 minute video movie to DVD, digitally remaster our original soundtrack, add support level credits for our valued supporters to DVD, Game & CD Booklets, update about 20% of trivia Rage Cards, update packaging, manufacture game, upgrade our website to feature bonus video elements of game, re-program the IVR element and update some visual elements on board.

With AU$120,000+ already spent on Outrageous, creating original artwork, characters, game content, packaging, writing, producing, shooting, editing a 90 minute Game Play DVD to support one of the four player options of how this game can be played, trademark, recording original soundtrack and focus groups.

Initially we created Outrageous as a Video Board Game and the Lotto board Game which was an award winning game - both being interactive games.


Game Play Basics:

Outrageous features a ninety minute game DVD to support only one of the game and can be played three other alternative ways, very different from the original video games of the 90’s that only played using the included movie. The game requires 2-6 players who play the characters of the game to attempt to collect Character Cards of that character they had chosen to play while trying to beat the hour glass of time which separates game commands during the DVD game movie.

Above video shows our slightly 'over the top' multimedia character near completion of game - 1 of 35 appearances within game of our crazy feudal host Lord Charles Lancelot III.

At random intervals the game is interrupted by Lord Charles Lancelot III who would appear to either command various players to perform silly stunts which generally had the whole room in stitches, or taunt, reward or punish the players in a variety of crazy ways.

As each player travels around the board they participate answering trivia questions, barter Character Cards from their opponents, land on various board spaces obeying instructions at all times, fight to stay out of the Lord’s Dungeon, Mad House etc. - if playing with the DVD movie – obeying Lord Charles outrageous demands on you. The game is won by collecting seven of you own Character Cards of the game character you have chosen to play. The six player characters within game encompass many of today’s important lifestyles ... and all in a game that thrills and entertains players from 12 to 80.

Throughout game the Lord’s Rage Card’s are drawn by players which are based on general knowledge and outrageous events that were drawn from our days of reading trivia books, each trivia question has three possible answers.

Answering a trivia question correctly does not guarantee any bonus moves as Lord Charles Lancelot III could command you to move back 64 spaces for being answering a question correctly ... this is an added element as at no point in the game are players sure whether to answer any of the trivia questions correctly or not.

Outrageous combines a fun, outrageous frivolous time with elements of movie, collecting cards, crazy trivia, bartering, karaoke, interactive voice response and ultimately keeping this crazy Lord Charles contained behind his castle walls.

The first player to return to the Lord’s Table is bestowed the title of the most outrageous character playing the game and declared the winner. At all times during game play the influence of Lord Charles is at a peak no matter which of the four different options you select to play before commencing playing game.

Original Game Board

Intro to our Game Host

LORD CHARLES LANCELOT III

The game stars Lord Charles Lancelot III a mildly crazy English Lord and six wildly funny player characters that represent individuality, combing topics such as our environment today, greens elements, sport, gay, sex, royal family, music, movies, karaoke and outrageous general knowledge trivia spanning from the 50’s till today.

Our host, Lord Charles Lancelot III comes from a very long line of Lancelot’s dating back to the earliest days of the 16th century. His great, great, great grandfather Lord Rupert Lancelot VI was a powerful but very crazy feudal lord who plundered and pillaged small English villages up and down the coast.

Lord Rupert Lancelot VI was finally captured and after a royal decree he and his family, all future descendents and heirs to the Lancelot title were sentenced to be imprisoned in the family home, Lancelot Lodge forever.

As the centuries passed, so did the Lancelot family, sadly leaving one remaining member of this once proud family, Lord Charles Lancelot III.

Intro to Game Characters

Lady Maree Lusting - Wildly vivacious, flaunts her sexuality, known to have broken many hearts and loves rich older men. She was at one time a B grade horror movie actress until she met and married 80 year old Texan beef barren, Tex Stallion who passed away four months after they married of physical exhaustion.

President Woody Pine - This "jack of all trades" has at one time been a Lumberjack, truck driver and an ex navy commando who was highly decorated for his clandestine political missions around the world. President Woody Pine is now leader of a little known movement called P.W.P.S.T.P.T.F - PRESIDENT WOODY PINE SAVE THE PINE TREE FOUNDATION. Never seen out of uniform and loves to discuss his favourite pass time, planting pine tree plantations.

Prince Plaster of Paris - Is very much the gay blade of Paris, seen with the right people and at the right places? Always on the arm of much older and particularly wealthy women, an outrageous dresser and always in bright colours. Father was a Polish Prince and his mother a peasant girl who worked in one of his many textile factories.

Princess Sandy SandTrap - Ever smiling, bubbly, and very loud, supposedly secret second cousin to the queen, has never been married and somehow has been able to keep her private life and family a secret. It is rumoured that our Sandy is an International golf groupie and is certainly seen at most golf tournaments around the world. The Royal Family denies any connection or blood relationship with this self proclaimed Princess.

Count Lenny the Legend - The only son of Count and Countess Erik Von Hinkler, incredibly rich, a little arrogant, a story teller, sadly he inherited all the family money the day his parents were killed when their hot air balloon burst and fell into the sea. Some people say his title is fictitious and other believes his father won his title Count playing backgammon.

Contessa CarmenGetit - Famous for her outrageous stunts in public, a beautiful and popular Spanish socialite who has for many years raised vast sums of money for charity. Her father was a very famous bull fighter until unfortunately he died in the ring; mother is a highly respected Gynecologist. This never married Contessa owns and manages the infamous pleasure parlor chain called "Scarlets".

Why a jigsaw?

Why you may ask? While setting up our Kickstarter campaign we tried to think outside the square about "point of difference", packaging and freight, enemies of any business today. So we hit on the idea - design and convert our Outrageous game board into an actual jigsaw of maybe 20 pieces.

Why you may ask? Well this idea becomes another outrageous element of the game, effects packaging, reduces packaging size by 50% - less shelf space required, reduces printing costs and the weight element eliminating part of deadly freight/shipping cost at all levels.
