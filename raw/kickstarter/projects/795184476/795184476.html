We have built five unique racers for players to choose from, each with their own musical style and abilities.

Â  Â  1. The Steampunk Racer as seen in the video, has the ability to create a smokescreen blinding the other players. It features a victorian and classical musical style.

Â  Â  2. The Modern Racer has the ability to create a shield around itself protecting the player from objects both on the track and from other players. The musical style of this racer is Rock/Electronic

Â  3. The Cartoon Racer has the ability to drop bananas on the course causing players that collide with them to swerve into the walls. The musical style of this racer is Jazz.

Â  Â  4. The Dark Racer has the ability to cause spirits to stun its enemies for a short period of time. The musical styling of the Dark Racer is Orchestral.Â 

Â  Â  5. The Glitch Racer has the special ability of CopyCat. It can steal any of the other players abilities, but there is a catch. The ship has the chance to end up glitching, teleporting itself to a random place in the race. I.E (1st to 5th, 2nd to 4th, etc) The musical style of the Glitch Racer is Chiptunes.

The cool thing about the musical styles of the game is that on any given race, the music will adapt based on who is in first place. We have created songs for each race, however certain characteristics will be altered to fit the racing style of the person in the lead. We feel that this is something that can create a new racing experience for players.

So far we have developed 15 unique levels for the player to experience, each with its own music composed in house. And we plan to have 30 levels total by the end of development.

Maestro Interactive Games was founded to focus on the audio aspect of game design. We felt that gamers have gotten tired of the same old experiences that have come out lately. Everyone has become accustomed to Halo, Call of Duty and the rest.Â At the time, those were NEW experiences and they were amazing. We've had beautiful experiences from games like Limbo, Super Meat Boy, and acoustic experiences like Journey, and we've had new adventures from games like LA Noire, but we wanted to let players explore something new. We wanted players to re-experience audio in games.

This is a game designed to be fun, to be engaging, and to push the boundaries of what players expect video games to be. Most of all, it is meant to be a new experience for gamers.

Maestro Interactive Games is a team of developers with experience in the "AAA" and "Indie" game space. Our teams developers individually have some impressive titles such as Donkey Kong Country Returns, and companies like Microsoft and Nintendo under their belts.

The answer is no - this is a game for everyone, the primary objective of the game is to create a fun gaming experience for all gamers and enjoy the audio as they go. We hope that gamers will feel that the acoustic merits of games is something that should not be discounted when they play Cosmic Highway.

We've put a lot of effort into the stereo mode so we hope you will experiment with the way audio plays in your own gaming environment with this title. On the Wii U, you will use the Wii U GamePad to move left and right, as well as select everything using either the touch screen or the diagonal pad. We anticipate a second screen experience as well, as well as the TV off option that the GamePad supports. We are also developing extra features in the multiplayer splitscreen component for the player using the GamePad.Â

Risks and challenges Learn about accountability on Kickstarter

Cosmic Highway has an aggressive schedule and healthy ambitions. As with any other game project, the greatest risk is that deadlines might get missed and at worst the game might fail to finish.

Thanks to previous experience in the game industry shipping games of all sizes, we've insulated Cosmic Highway against this regrettably common risk. Our core strategy is focusing on complex audio design, which means that we focus heavily on tools and being able to test changes quickly. This also allows us to develop the game at a much faster pace without compromising the quality of the title.

Additionally, Cosmic Highway core mechanics "scale" well, which means that they can be represented very simply but also with high levels of polish. We can also develop new levels quickly, while still retaining the high quality should the stretch goals be met.
