Features:

BINARY FLOW 2 is a new kind of multiplayer game that will test your skills. You have to be quick, smart and brave while you race against your friends in real time through Binary waves. Customize your character with over hundreds of thousands of accessories, unlock new levels/game modes (survivor, race, kingOfTheHill, and much more...), and achievements.

Allow its own leader board where you will access to the full list of the worldwide ranking. You can now download the FREE DEMO version available for PC and MAC at http://enrickdev.com/bf2/download.

To start off, my idea was to place a circle inside another circle and last the longest without falling outside the other circle. This has been the first version of Binary flow that I have posted exclusively for iphones and ipads (ios). This game got the in the top 11 arcade games in china with over 13k downloads on the release date. 

Now, Binary flow 2 will be the next big game that will be more complex. I love how this game just differs from other and how the multiplayer function will outstand its experience. It is why this new game will release only in multiplayer but I will eventually put up the single player mode for those of you that want a solo experience. You will have the ability to make yourself your own account to be able to access your friend list and the leader board.

I will block access to some functions since this is the DEMO (free version). This test version is available at this moment to be downloaded. If you like the concept and would like to participate in helping me in this project, do not hesitate to pre-order on the official website at http://enrickdev.com/bf2/store. 

Start testing new map and new game modes as they are release with further updates (ALPHA only). Blow up your friends with the new power-ups! Be sure to share this page and talk to your friends about it. For more information be sure to leave a thread in the forum section. 

If the game becomes a success, I will publish a version for XBOX ONE and PLAYSTATION 4.

 ENRICK LAMBERT  (GAME DEVELOPER)

I'm Enrick Lambert from Canada. I do writing/programming, marketing, arts, animation, design, 3D modeling and some other things. I'm an Indie Game Developer who needs projects to live. I create, I design and I innovate.

I currently work part-time as an analyst programmer and as a computer technician in Drummondville, Qc.

I have created “Pandoori”, “Binary Flow 1″ for iPhone / iPad. The first version of “Binary Flow” have reached top 11 arcade games in china AppStore.

I have started to learn programmation by myself in 2007 and since that time, I create games. I became officially an Independent Game Developer has the release of “pandoori” in January 2013. 

JASON DIONNE (MUSIC COMPOSER)

Hello, my name is Jason Dionne and I am the music composer of BINARY FLOW 2.

I am a big fan of video game music. In the old school video games like pokemon, tetris and super mario where the music is so nostalgic makes me want to make something just like it.

I have made metal and acoustic video game music covers on youtube which allowed me to learn even more about what a video game music is.

This is my oportunity to show you how my music will make this game even more awesome.I am currently composing exciting and vivifying music to let the players like you enjoy the next generation of gaming you have all been waiting for.

For more informations, you can contact me at jason@enrickdev.com.
