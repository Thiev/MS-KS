KnowNote.com brings music lovers and gamers together to compete for high scores playing today's hits and creating original music.

Ever wonder if you would be good at playing a musical instrument you can’t play now? KnowNote (http://www.knownote.com) lets you learn music rapidly by using something very familiar: the computer keyboard.

You learn to play music simply by playing the game. As you type the songs, your brain will automatically learn which notes are higher and lower. As you continue to play, you will learn the rest of the music paradigm without ever studying archaic sheet music.

You play, practice, and compete by typing out popular songs. You earn KnowGold, Items, and Awards in the process. These allow for a personalized profile page that customizes your achievements. With the knowledge you gain from playing the songs we provide, you can use the advanced studio to create and publish your own original songs.

These days, almost everyone knows how to type. Because of this skill, you can learn to play music almost instantaneously without investing the time and money to learn to play an instrument.

We have already entered into agreements with Universal Music Group to use their music in the game, and we already have several instruments ready to use in the recording studio.

However, the more variety we can offer, the better the KnowNote will be, and so we’re trying to record more songs and more instruments. We have a studio and musicians, and we’re very pleased with their work. We just would like to be able to buy more of it.

Risks and challenges Learn about accountability on Kickstarter

The biggest challenge associated with making KnowNote successful is the programming. Jason has the skills, management, and drive to develop anything necessary for KnowNote.

Jason's focuses have been and are to insure that: the servers must be able to handle heavy load, the game and studio must be robust and fast, the software is scalable allowing new features, the code is simple and direct, and the software must be easy to convert to apps for tablet devices. These are explained in more detail in the video.

Music licensing is another challenging aspect of developing KnowNote. As copyright infringement becomes a more prominent issue in today’s world, we had to be extra cautious about obtaining our music the right way. We have signed agreements for the music that we have and are fully protected by our agreement.

The foreseeable challenges right now are getting enough users to test our beta version and raising enough capital to continue adding more music to the site. That’s where we need and appreciate your help.

FAQ

  • What languages is this kickstarter and web page/game/create music available in?

    Right now, we only support the English language with a standard US, Dane, and Swede keyboard. To activate Dane and Swede support, at the beginning of the game, press either the Æ (Dane) or the Ö (Swede) key. Support only works in Firefox, Internet Exporer, and Chrome. It will not work in Safari or Opera due to incomplete control of trapping keys.

  • How long before I get my virtual items and awards?

    It will take time for us to properly link your KnowNote account and kickstarter together. Due to this fact, people will obtain the items and awards at different times. We will link the goodies as fast as we possibly can.
