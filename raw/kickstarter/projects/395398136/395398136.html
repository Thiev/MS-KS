"Belief is as deadly as reality... and it's infinite."

Combining an incredible story with the edge-of-your-seat skill of an action game;  Believe is an episodic psychological action adventure thriller. Game play and story meld beautifully as you uncover one shocking discovery after another. Your world is shaped by what you Believe.

 

Sisters Fyuri and Meya have lived a difficult life. Being forced to raise themselves after a sweet mother went insane and their father left inexplicably. It seems as though anyone close to Fyuri becomes mentally unstable. It’s as if her beliefs become their reality. A fleeting hallucination in a moment of fatigue becomes a permanent resident in their mind. Compounding incidents build and consume the victim as their unfortunate soul is lost to a world of nightmares.

And that is only the beginning...

Clipped from Fyuri's journal:

“It seems wholly possible that I am meant to be alone. The rest of the world was built as an ecosystem but I am the pollution. I’m not trying to be down on myself and I’m not one for drama. I’m just being logical.  

All that I’ve been through makes me wonder, what is my role in this world? I only want to live a nice life and be around people I love. I long to leave some positive impact on this world. A small stream only wishes to provide nourishment and life but after a time the erosion becomes clear as it gouges into the earth. What level of erosion is acceptable before our role in this world becomes catastrophic to the people we love?”

In each episode you will assume the role of Fyuri’s victims. You will see the world through his/her eyes as you attempt to survive the unique terrors within. From your twisted perspective a straightforward path may turn into a deadly maze, an innocent girl might be the demon who ends your life. Race to learn the truth before it’s too late.

So I do what?

You are under Fyuri’s influence. Meaning you are at some stage of mental deterioration. In each episode you play as a different victim with his/her own personality, strengths and fears. You experience everything this person is thinking. Hearing each thought and seeing every hallucination it’s up to you, the player, to decide what you believe.


Each of these victim’s traits will show through as he/she responds to situations differently. One victim might be cowardly and unable to face a fear, forcing you to run and hide (it’s unfortunate that you're clumsy too isn’t it?). Another victim might be courageous and able to control his emotions long enough to dispel a hallucination. But maybe he should have run, there’s more than one way to die.

What about live actors?

As the player you learn about the victims by playing through their eyes. Being under Fyuri’s influence has its effects though. Her power isn’t as straightforward as you might expect. Such an intimate connection is prone to anomalies. You will learn more about the Du’ress sisters as their memories tear through your world in the form of speech and video.

                                                Lost

Excited and optimistic, the sisters begin college. Meya fits right in. She loves the parties and makes a lot of new friends. Fyuri on the other hand, is happy to spend all her time working or relaxing in the privacy of her apartment.


Life is full of surprises and an always cautious Fyuri meets a great guy named Troy. He is witty, loves the outdoors and is most of all, not an idiot. Everything is going great at first, but things aren’t always as they seem.

Gameplay examples from Part One:

(in-game story elements have been removed from each segment)

Season 1 will be split into 3 Parts or Episodes. The game-play portion (not counting "Discover" mode) of each part/episode will be approximately 2-3 hours long and provide a lot of replayability. The game is brutally difficult but accommodates for this by automatically scaling in difficulty each time you lose. It will be a real challenge for any player to complete an episode in one attempt.

I have been developing Believe for a little over a year. Gameplay for part one is already playable from start to finish. I am in the process of adding additional story elements, polish, tweaks, and balance. The plan is to release part one in April 2014, part 2 in late 2014, and the season will wrap up with part 3 in early 2015.

In addition to the Game Edition, each episode will also have a Movie Edition. Each Movie Edition is a full length live-action movie in glorious full screen high definition! With the Movie Edition you will see exclusive footage and get a deeper look at each of the characters. Be a part of their lives and become engrossed in everything the game didn’t show you. The plot of each episode is similar to (and expanded from) the game. Allowing you to relive the experience with story in the front seat.

The Game Edition of Part One is limited to roughly 12 minutes of live footage for pacing and story reasons. Whereas the Movie Edition of Part One is 80 minutes long. To get the full experience I highly recommend getting the Movie and Game editions of each episode. The run-time of each Movie Edition is approximately 70-120 minutes depending on the story being told. They are detailed and thorough but won’t be “fluffed” up with meaningless content just to add more length.


Please Note: The Movie Edition does not include any gameplay video, it is entirely live action.
 

 Me

My name is Mitchell Sorenson, I’m just some guy who decided he wanted to make a video game. I created a demo and nearly the entire first episode before posting a Kickstarter because I realize my name doesn’t carry any credibility yet. Working on ‘Believe’ has been my full time job for the last year. I am passionate about this project and hope you all enjoy it as much as I do.

And some volunteers


I am fortunate enough to have some good friends who were willing to help me with acting, filming and production. One day I hope to be able to pay them in dollars instead of high fives.

Risks and challenges Learn about accountability on Kickstarter

I believe (no pun intended) that the biggest risk is my inexperience. I know that’s not what a backer would want to hear, but it’s the truth. This will be my first released product and that’s quite a feat. By creating the entire first episode I hope to have proven my dedication. I believe (ok pun intended this time) the hardest part of development is complete.

Please keep in mind that this is an Indie game and an Indie movie. I’m not trying to complete with AAA anything. My goal is to create an engaging, unique experience that you can’t find anywhere else.

I know it’s going to be a great journey ...if you can survive it! Remember, your world is shaped by what you believe.

--Mitch
