President Forever 2016 is the most in-depth, intense political election game ever made. Using realistic electorate modeling techniques, an unprecedented number of options, and detailed candidates, this is big time! So far it’s only been available on Windows. This Kickstarter is about getting President Forever 2016 on Mac as well.

We want your Kickstarter vote! Going cross-platform is expensive. We’re going to invest a lot in the Mac version, but 270soft is a small developer and there are a lot of up-front costs. This Kickstarter is about accelerating development to make the Mac version happen in early 2014.

Small developer. Big software.

The goal is simple: win enough Electoral College votes to win the election. You are responsible for all aspects of your candidate’s election strategy: planning and buying advertising, conducting fundraising, giving speeches, recruiting high-profile and grassroots supporters and spinning news.

Our aim is to convey the experience of what it's like to be in a campaign to gamers of all ages and backgrounds.

Creating games for political enthusiasts is a core part of why 270soft was founded over 10 years ago.

The game already includes:

  • 2016, 2012, and 2008, with 34, 10, and 28 candidates to choose from respectively - in 2016, there are the Republican, Democratic, Libertarian, and Green parties and a hypothetical Bloomberg Independent bid to boot!
  • Primaries and General Election.
  • Fully featured Campaign Editor – modify the candidates, add new ones, or even insert yourself and run for President! You can also modify or add endorsers, surrogates, parties, and more, all with the click of a button. The control is in your hands. 
  • Leaders with detailed attributes (Leadership, Experience, Charisma, and so on) and campaign strengths (Fundraising, Polling, Advertising, Command and Control, and so on) that mimic the strengths of real life campaigns.
  • Surrogates, who barnstorm, fundraise, or spin for you – tell them where to go and what to do. Will Christie provide more muscle than Ryan? O’Malley more than Cuomo?
  • Build up state-by-state command-and-control, and synergize it with your Footsoldiers to swamp your opponents’ Get Out the Vote operations in swing states!
  • Barnstorm, give speeches, fundraise to boost your campaign finances, and much more – you decide. Or perhaps it’s time to hold massive rallies to accommodate the thronging crowds and boost your reach?
  • Work to get on primary ballots if you’re not. Stymied by primary ballot rules which keep your candidate off the ballot in Virginia? Now you can change that.
  • Which primaries are coming onto the radar, and which swing states look the most appealing to target? See political data at a glance and and arrange states at a click with the Strategy Screen.
  • Debates. Call out specific opponents in debates to stop their momentum, or boast about your own achievements.
  • Craft ads to target specific opponents and issues, or tailor them to specific regions to gain extra bonuses when you run them.
  • A large universe of possible endorsers, who give momentum or financial boosts. You can also turn endorsers into surrogates - they will then campaign for you after endorsing.
  • You have 270+ Electoral Votes in your column for now, great – but how deep is your support? Now you can see at a glance with new electoral vote display. Secured your party’s nomination?
  • Pivot to the general election, including choosing your Veep running mate at any point up until the convention.
  • and much more.

The game will continue to be expanded leading up to the 2016 election, including new campaigns, graphics, features, candidates, and more.

The big Mac question.

“Is there a Mac version?”

That was one of the first questions asked when President Forever 2016 came out on Windows in 2012.

Now, changes in development technologies relevant to the Mac have made it possible for us to add Mac, with your help.

On Mac in early 2014.

270soft is a small, independent software developer. Making products like President Forever 2016 is expensive.

The large majority of the Mac version will be funded by 270soft. This Kickstarter campaign will allow us to purchase the essential hardware and development tools needed to create the Mac version, and then launch full development by allocating coding resources to the Mac version.

That’s where we need your help.

This Kickstarter gives us the boost we need right now to launch full development so that President Forever 2016 for Mac comes out early next year.

When exactly next year? We’re aiming for March.

The point is that you’re not backing an unknown project – President Forever 2016 already exists. Gamers and political geeks are using it everyday. You know what you're getting into.

This is about giving Mac political geeks the game they deserve.

A quick note on exchange rates

So, we’re based in the Victoria, Canada. But interest in the U.S. election is international, and of course primarily in the U.S.A. However, Kickstarter won’t let us show our goal and tiers in $US, so they’re all displayed $CAN.

If you’re from the US this means the CAN value will be converted to US dollars when you pledge. The good news is that $1 CAN is approximately equal to $1 US. So, a $20 CAN pledge is about $20 US, and so on. This may fluctuate depending on the state of the two countries' economies.

If you have any questions at all about pledges from the US or other countries, please do contact us.
