Long Night is a new third person survival horror game for the PC, Linux and Mac, and later PSN and XBL. It will be released in English and French on May 2014.

Long Night WANTS YOU to reconnect with the atmosphere of classic Survival Horror like Resident Evil 1, Silent Hill 2, Haunting Ground and Obscure, in a new intrigating way!

To do so we re-develop the well known mechanisms we enjoyed in those games as Exploration of strange territories, puzzling Enigmas and Bosses to fight against.Then we add our ideas about the psychological roots of fear, our originality in story tellling, and our artistic touch, to make Long Night a unique gaming experience in the world of Survival Horror.

We are on our way to bring back psychological fear in your life, but we need your help to really make you shiver !

For any updates on Long Night and the Trickster Face team, join us on Facebook :

https://www.facebook.com/TricksterFace

This black tale takes place in an American holiday wood camp, at the end of summer 1998. The main characters of the game are a group of four teenage friends, accustomed to meet there every year. Spending their last evening together telling horror stories for fun, they find themselves plunged into a real nightmare, bringing their darkest secrets to life, tormenting them. All of a sudden they loose contact with one another, and the real game begins:

Becoming David, you will try to find and help your friends. But while doing so, you will have to face the monsters lurking in the darkness of their hearts (and maybe in yours, too) without losing your sanity.

David will discover not only all kinds of family secrets, but also Native American folklore, urban legends, Jewish myths and Voodoo magic, learning more about himself and the duplicity of the human mind through these different cultures.

Long Night is a story between reality and supernatural. The fear comes from realistic family drama. The paranormal is used to give life to those fears and force the characters to face their own unconscious problematic.

Long Night will be a wholly original game with an unique gameplay.

So, if you, as we do, miss the complexity and challenges of the Old School Survival Horror, and if you want to take part in the development of their natural son and successor: Long Night... this is your chance to contribute !

We are a bunch of old mates who grew up, next to each other, with a gamepad in our hands since nearly our birth. After video game studies and work experiences in various studios on both sides of the Atlantic Ocean, we have quit our jobs and gathered last spring, in our home country (France) to realize our dream: creating our independent video game studio: Trickster Face

We will work hard and have long nights in order to plunge you into THE Long Night, our first game, during spring 2014!

Risks and challenges Learn about accountability on Kickstarter

Long Night is currently 50% complete and will be finished and launched, whatever the results of the Kickstarter campaign might be.
We will use all the funds raised through the campaign to make your game become an experience even more frightening, more beautiful, more puzzling, more well-rounded and polished.
The only risk goes with the challenge. We want to create the best game we can, so if Long Night next spring does still not fit with our ambition of quality, we will have to adapt the release date. We prefer to give it one more month of development,and be sure to give you the Long Night you deserve!
