Frustration with micromanagement leads to a NEW take on Real Time Strategy Genre! Shadow Heroes: Vengeance in Flames


Fifty years ago, in an attempt to save the kingdom from an ancient threat, the Sorcerer-kings nearly destroyed us all. Now the fledgling nations of shattered Hendrika try to rebuild what we have lost. But no nation thinks alike, and conflict soon ensues. Is magic too dangerous to exist or is it a tool to be harnessed? Is technology the next big thing or just a fad? Are the people fodder for the next war or the leaders of the future?

You are the captain of a military company trusted by your nation to raise your own troops and fight for glory, fame and power. Yours is the power to answer these questions. Yours is the power to decide.

Shadow Heroes is a real-time strategy game that focuses on the strategy instead of micromanagement. You are the commander of an army, not the hand manipulating every single peon. Your role is to train and hire your troops, craft and assign equipment, then select what units head out in each wave of attack against your enemies. But don't expect to wait with your arms crossed while your men and women die for ideals! As your troops clash with the enemy, you will have orders to give and decisions to make. Should your paladins use their inferno diamonds now? Should you send cherubs to support your weakened front line? What about those erratic thaumaturgic machines that look like they are ready to blow up?

Shadow Heroes brings you:

  • A non-traditional fantasy world: You want something different, and so do we! Hendrika mixes technology with magic and the spice of real world conflicts.
  • A new form of RTS: Hire troops and send them out to battle, affect the battlefield with minute decisions and make moral choices on how to handle allies and enemies alike.
  • A Single-player campaign: You are Garrison Captain Armas Vilhelmi, reluctant governor of a frontier town in the Republic of Three Towers. Your job: dealing with rioting slaves, hunting pro-magic terrorists and making the hard decisions no one else can!
  • A Multi-player campaign: You are the leader of your own army, serving any of Hendrika's 8 nations in a living online campaign! Join forces with other commanders and lead your nation to victory!
  • The chance to make History: Nations will rise and fall, alliances will be shaped and wars will be declared. Ancient secrets will be sought and revealed. Ultimately, it is player's successes and failures that shape the History of Hendrika.

Once upon a time there was a young girl named Hendrika. Hendrika was born with amazing powers, far greater than any of the mages of the Eight Cities who ruled the land north of the Velvet Sea. Realizing they could use the saint-girl to their advantage, the kings of the Eight Cities made her the Queen and pretended to bow before her while using her religious influence to manipulate their enemies. One day, young Queen Hendrika learned the truth, and the Eight Cities paid the price.

For generations the Kingdom of Hendrika had been the envy of the Known World. Ruled first by the benevolent and wise Queen and after her passing by her council of Sorcerer-kings, the Kingdom grew to become the most successful of nations. In Hendrika's kingdom even the poorest peasant had access to the magic that would heal the sick and enrich the soil.

Then came the Great Rift. Few understand what it was or why it happened. Some believe the Sorcerer-kings were driven mad with power, others that they were just trying to protect their beloved kingdom. The fact is that in a flash of light the Kingdom of Hendrika was ripped off of the Known World and lifted into the sky, away from the dangers that surrounded the land.

The Great Rift saved the people of Hendrika, but doomed the kingdom. The capital city fell. The Sorcerer-kings vanished. Magic was twisted in horrible ways. From the ruins of the Eight Cities new nations had risen. Where magic was the strongest the people and the land were changed, giving birth to the ndras, creatures born of magic.

How does a world shattered by magic treat those born with it? Do they fear magic or learn to accept it? Do they hunt them down, enslave them or use them for their own gain?

In a shattered world learning to live again few are the people willing to take the hard decisions, willing to do what is necessary. They don't care about glory or fame. They are heroes acting in shadows. They are the people who get the job done, no matter the cost.

Risks and challenges Learn about accountability on Kickstarter

Developing a video game is no simple feat. It involves many parts and processes that may be challenging to the untried. Fortunately we have a team of experienced developers, designers and managers who will bring their expertise to the table, ensuring delays and other unexpected issues are dealt with swiftly and efficiently.

We have been studying other crowd funding projects for months now, especially the usual challenges like maintaining an open communication channel with backers, projecting expenses and planning a viable deadline. With that in mind, we are very confident we are ready to develop and deliver this game with your support.

Sometimes things do happen though and with this in mind, we would rather have the game working properly than rushed out the door. Because of this, we can't guarantee our launch date but will try our very best to meet it.

FAQ

  • Is this game Pay to Win?

    We have added the equipment system in order to allow players to customize their play style. With all the items comes many different strategies on how to actually play a game. The in game store can help when you start out but it can only get you so far.

    Equipment follows a QLevel system. This means there are 11 versions of each item of varying potency. in the store, you can buy items from QLevel 0 to QLevel 5. After that you have to earn better items. So at lower levels of play you get a boost to your performance with better items but that will only fast track you to an area where people have the same level of equipment. Even so, people earn items every match so just playing will get you there almost as fast. We take a strong stance against Pay to Win. If you pay, you get a short cut but that's it. We made sure people could not buy the best things in the game.
