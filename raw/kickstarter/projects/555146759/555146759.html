A humoristic full inertia space simulator for the elite pilot. PC, Mac and Linux.

Hi Kicky's and Kicker's! I'm Eric and welcome to my video game project.

STORY

By accident, you ejected your shuttle from a "Papaship"

during a transsubstellar transport. Doing so, you broke the space time fabric and you are lost in a galaxy.Â  Alone, in your small ship, you'll have to find your way back.

GAMEPLAY

You are piloting a small range gem gathering shuttle, the F-29, a vessel with a six axis control capability. This desktop game is meant to be played on a full sized keyboard, no mouse is used.
Donâ€™t worry, both hands will be put to contribution with instinctive controls for anyone who understands this : WASD, Left Shift, Space bar, Arrows and Numpad +.

The â€œ29â€ absorbs the Space Gems who float in a gem racetrack leading to the sun. Depending on their colors, they give you gas, air, repair and shield capability. Be careful and don't touch the red ones, they give damage depending on the speed of the impact and the time of contact with your ship.Â 

Your piloting skill is put to the test in this full inertia space simulator.

A majority of red gems defines the way to go, which is... into the sun. This is where you will find the Mega Gate Star System which teleport you from a level to another. To go in the sun, you'll need to find 10 yellow gems in the race track without running out of gas, missing air or losing your structural integrity.

Those yellow gems are the energy for your sun shield, which is important for going in the sun without burning in seconds.

A single level is completed between 10 and 45 min, depending if you go right to the sun or decide to clean up the entire level from every gems.

To help you in you venture, you have :

  • five speeds;
  • a space brake;
  • a rotation brake;
  • a super cool light that turn and is used as a proximity visual sensor;
  • the ability to compress and expand time;
  • the database of every absurd audio log of Extra Star Command to listen and pass the time;
  • no minibar.

"A hasted astronaut is a dead astronaut."
-Subcommander Khayto

This is a â€œseek and absorbâ€ mission.
In the full inertia environment of SGT, speed is your worst enemy and patience your savior.
The "29" is a black hole for gas, so watch your green bar carefully.
It is a play of what I like to call â€œMovement Strategyâ€.
This game will tilt your body when you want to turn left or roll right.
You have no guns and don't need it.

SPECIAL INGREDIENT

There are two key gameplay elements that make SGT special.

First, the camera system is integrated with the gameplay. You have to switch frequently your camera to find your resources, if you don't, you will pass very precious gems without even knowing about them. You can Zoomergize and have the ability to zoom out for a limited, renewable, period of time. You control the camera system with the numeric keypad keys.

Second, there is no HUD, every information you need to know is on the ship. A system of glowing colored bars and numbers clearly show, in a glance, your situation. To see those bars, you'll have to switch your camera systems to the basic view (numpad 0).

COMPLETED STUFF

A 10 levels + 1 training level fully functional pre-alpha prototype is ready.

A difficulty structure is placed changing many parameters like the gas consumption, the numbers of gems to grab, the power of your thrust and more to create progressive challenges, suited to your talent.

A statistical engine will analyze your piloting run and inform you on your gas efficiency, the numbers of gems collected, the time you did the level and many more data. This give you an overview of your performance and hints on how to improve yourself.

VISION

I intend to deliver a simple, challenging and humoristic microgravity piloting game. As my means are limited, I keep a realistic scope for the game. I use a basic visual style, even cubic, to put most of my energy in the gameplay mechanics. Because this is where I want to shine.

Would it be a small, medium or a big bang, I will prioritize the feel of the game to give you instinctive controls, numerous missions and a ton of fun. I'll focus on bringing the gameplay to life with jokes and references about the sci-fi pop culture universe. Nothing or nobody is safe, I will make fun of many movie, TV show, books, comic books and all that it is nerdy and related to space.
