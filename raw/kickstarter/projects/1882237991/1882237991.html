Middle of Nowhere is a game with a beautiful yet deceiving reality inspired by generations of survival horror games. With Middle of Nowhere we're aiming to create an amazing, unique survival horror experience that stays with you forever, here's some features:

  • Middle of Nowhere is a single player, first person survival horror game.
  • You will play a unique, story-driven game and encounter various creatures.
  • The game will be developed in the Unity engine for PC (Windows).
  • The game will be distributed digitally(Possibly from Steam and other similar options).

Here's a brief summary of our story:

Dr. Aleister Newman receives a letter seeking his aid. A young girl has fallen ill in a small town in North America. Scared for a her safety and curious about her condition, he offers his expert assistance...

Aleister never returns, and soon after, is declared missing. His brother, Isaac Newman decides to travel to the town in search of answers, but what he encounters is a place filled with horrors that far exceed the confines of reality. His sanity is tested as he ventures further into the middle of nowhere and unravels the mystery of his brothers' disappearance.

Middle of Nowhere is based in a beautiful, yet troubled North American town in the year 2020.

Brave the fog, explore hidden shacks, trek through dense forestry, journey into the catacombs, tread the swampy waters, search abandoned schools, churches and farms...

As you'll discover... this is no ordinary town.

ISAAC NEWMAN
A broken man with little self regard. He was once a man of faith, but ever since his wife and child were killed in a fatal car crash he has lost his way.

ALEISTER NEWMAN
Brother and polar opposite of Isaac. He's a highly skilled and sought after doctor and finds pleasure in helping the needy.

EMILY CROWLEY
A young North American girl. She is the reason why Dr. Aleister Newman traveled to the town.

This is just 3 of the creatures we have in the game, after the Kickstarter we'll show more progressively but don't want to give away too much at this stage. That would ruin the surprise :)

STITCHES
What was once human is now a deformed nightmare.
Stitches is an unforgiving organism that screams in agony as it stumbles towards you and tries to tear you apart.

CREEPER
Found lurking in the depths of the catacombs. Creeper is a genetic mutation that waits silently in the darkness. Stumble into her web, into her territory, and you may never escape.

SCREAMING NURSE
Crazed, mutilated and decayed. The Screaming Nurse is far from dead and has only one thing on her mind, to inject you with her syringe.

Middle of Nowhere will have a strong emphasis on story, survival, puzzles and horror. We are creating a beautiful world ready for you to discover.

In the beginning, you'll start with nothing and have the opportunity to explore and find a number of different items. As you venture into Middle of Nowhere you'll have a number of survival elements to consider like building fires and building shelter to recover your health. There will be various different puzzles to solve, some simple and some hard. Rooms that you enter showing you the true essence of this game in its element; horror. Combat will be just as important as running away. Weapons being either very expensive or very hard to find and as you progress you can even progress some skills such as run-speed.

Don't just take our word for it, try it out yourself. We've taken the time out to make a demo especially for Kickstarter cram packing many of these different elements into 1 compact level. We wanted you all to feel safe with what your buying into so the goal of this demo is to try everything out while solving the puzzle of finding 4 parts in order to build a bridge. The demo has the option to try out skills, some work in progress combat, guns, a puzzle and to see and feel the world we've created. Nothing here is final and we would love to get your feedback on features you would like to see in the game. Have fun! (PC only).

Will the game be more jump-scares or more survival-like?

Middle of Nowhere isn't going to be just about jump-scares, that's not to say it won't have it's moments and be scary but we believe in pacing in horror games, it's going to be a mix of both, just the right amount.

Will this game be heavily story based or random based?

Middle of Nowhere won't be a random game, it's going to have a well thought out and detailed story.

Will the game have a melee/combat system? if so, is it an easy FPS shoot em up?

The game is going to have a combat system but not your average first person shooter. As fun as it is to run around gunning we are keeping the survival aspect in mind the whole time while developing this game. Remember the classic survival horror games where ammo was
limited and it was really difficult to defeat the enemy sometimes? that's what we're going for.

How many hours will it take to complete the game?

As the game is still in development, we aren't sure on the hours especially since Kickstarter could completely change our capabilities but definitely plan on it being lengthy with lots of different places to explore such as shanty-like shacks, caves, graveyards, farms, dungeons and a complete town/world.

What is your influence for this game?

Our influence for this game is mainly classic survival horror games like Silent Hill and Resident Evil's game series. If we could sum up Middle of Nowhere we'd say it's a mix between both of those games.

When will the game be released?

We are working as hard as we possibly can on the game and with that said, those who have been following us, you've seen how quickly we are progressing the game. We think it's important not to rush the game though, we want this final outcome to be as best as possible so it's estimated for around May 2014, though that isn't final.

Are you looking for any help with the game?

We are happy to consider people with certain skill-sets and if you think you have something we need, don't hesitate to go to contact us. We are all set in the audio department as the developer of Middle of Nowhere is a professional composer/sound designer for film and game seen at www.AaronWildeMusic.com. We also have a very talented story/screenplay writer.

Where will you sell this game, Steam?

We plan to hopefully be able to sell it on Steam through Steam Greenlight. We plan to also sell it through sites like Desura.

Is the game free or does it cost? if so, how much?

The game will cost but we don't plan to overcharge. We want the game to be as fair priced as possible and are leaning towards the price of around £12.00 (around $19).
