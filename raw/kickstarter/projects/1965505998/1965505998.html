OUYA Gaming Sourceisa one-stop destination for OUYA game news & updates,withweekly content which carries into a weekly video series calledOUYA Radar. OGS also gives you direct access to OUYA game developerswith Dev Team Insider, a series of livecommunity interviews with developers on their latest projects.Our goal is tofind and showcase the very best OUYA projects out there,helping gamers find awesome games they might otherwise miss, and help developers find a way to get their game noticed amongst the crowd.

If we can reach our funding goal, we want to officially launch immediately after we complete our Kickstarter campaignin mid-March,highlighting and showcasing the best OUYA games upon the official OUYA launch to the public.This funding will allow us to produce12-weeks of content, which will include 12 weeklyepisodes ofbothOUYA RadarandDev Team Insider.

YouTube Channel:http://www.youtube.com/ouyagamingsource

Risks and challenges Learn about accountability on Kickstarter

What happens if OUYA fails and no games worth covering come out?

This seems very unlikely. Hundreds of developers have already committed to releasing games for the system, and there's nothing to stop them from doing so. But, nothing's a sure thing! We love OUYA because it's bringing a whole new range of awesome and innovative games to the TV, but the OUYA isn't the only way to play those games on a TV. OUYA is going to be our focus, but it's the games that matter. In the end, our team just wants to showcase games that do something amazing, but don't show up on consoles and end up easy to miss. If another platform like Gamestick or Steam Box (or even PC) brings excellent games to the TV that aren't getting the attention they deserve, we'll cover them, too; we'll follow the games, wherever they are.

Our challenge will be to find the best quality games that will be released on the OUYA on a weekly basis and to present these games on a regular weekly schedule. It will be our team's focus to search through a lot of static to find the best undiscovered gems. Our team is fully prepared for this and has already spent months of research with our beta website and early testing with the video series and Google+ Hangout videos with several game developers. We're fully prepared to take the time that is needed for this and have no doubt that this will be an excellent project if we can reach our funding goals.
