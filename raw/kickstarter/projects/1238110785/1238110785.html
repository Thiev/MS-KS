In a remote desert, an unidentified object falls from the skies. A research organization known as G.E.R. has tracked and found the mysterious meteor: a capsule of organic material that brings an unknown life-form to our planet from the depths of space. Here begins the story of this impossible being, as it attempts to escape and survive on this strange, hostile world that we call home.

In Paradise Lost you will take control of an alien plant that has been hidden in a secret bio-engineering facility. A security breach in the system allows the plant to escape from imprisonment and discover the secret purposes of the research organization which put it in a jar.

References and gameplay

Our adventure takes inspiration from classic games like Metal Gear, Metroid, Castlevania and the Oddworld series, focusing on action/stealth gameplay with a dash of platforming and puzzle-solving elements.

The game incorporates nonlinear, Metroidvania style mechanics, in which the player at first finds many areas inaccessible. To continue the exploration and escape from the facility, the player has to use specific objects and unlock new abilities.

There is also an RPG component: during the course of playing the player gets to direct the evolution of the alien plant, adapting it to their playing style with different skills that will be discovered as the game progresses. These enhancements will allow players to develop their own gaming strategies as the story unfolds.

Current and planned features

  • Explore a huge and complex map full of secret projects and situations that will keep you hooked
  • Enjoy a rich gameplay experience based on stealth and platforms, mixed with modern mechanics and puzzles
  • Face different ways of progressing through the getaway, using stealth and action skills like mental manipulation, decoys, organic bombs and more
  • Exciting platform challenges. Climb different surfaces and defy gravity laws!
  • Amazing scenes full of immersive dialogues and situations
  • Find rare items and expand your game experience collecting them
  • Epic boss battles!

We are really looking forward to make Paradise Lost a unique and exciting gaming experience for the players. Our goal is to find the right balance between the different aspects that shape the gameplay and build an open map full of secrets, surprises and hair-raising situations.

Regarding the storytelling, dialogues and cut scenes are the cornerstones of the game. This means that not everything will be what it seems to be: twists, mysteries and double-crosses will be a constant during the aliens escape. Also, the adventure will be filled with references and hidden winks to our sharp-eyed players.

We also believe that the audio is an essential part of any immersive experience: not only it gives life to the story but it also intensifies its personality and atmosphere. Games have been my passion since I was a child and I have always dreamed of making one someday. I grew up drawing adventures set in the games I played at the local arcade, where I spent the money I'd earned, fascinated with the imagination and beautiful sprites of classics like King of Fighters, Dungeons & Dragons and Midnight Wanderers.

Since then I have dedicated myself to becoming a graphic designer and animator, and I want to put every fiber of my soul into developing an inspired visual experience for our players.

When I was young I fell in love with Spielberg's movies. The way he tells stories and mix fantasy with reality touches my heart. I want to take this experience into the world of videogames, because I think it is a great medium for personal and artistic expression. In a game you become the hero and live intensely all sorts of events and adventures. My goal at Asthree Works is to make awesome and passionate stories and share them with you <3

I took my first steps into the world of computing when I began programming at the age of 6. I enjoyed the golden age of pc gaming and I especially love adventures with deep storytelling and great characters, games like The Dig, Broken Sword, Monkey Island, Final Fantasy and The Elder Scrolls saga.

It has always been my dream to devote my life to the creation of Videogames, and there is no better time than now to join my best friends on a journey to do exactly that.

I started making music at the same time that I started writing and imagining journeys, and for the very same reason: to tell stories. Since then I have written music for theatre plays, documentaries, short films, shows and, yes, video games. However, for me "Paradise Lost: First Contact" is more than a video game: it is a world in itself, it is a compelling story and an engaging experience, and I am truly honored to give it sound. In short, my goal is simple: to make music that is worthy of your time.

I have been in public relations for a long time, it has been a dream of mine to work with indie video game developers to help their projects become a reality.

Developing a game of this nature requires a great creative effort, tons of hours of programming and writing and a huge amount of art and animations. These are the reasons that lead us to quit our jobs and devote full time to it, to create the best possible experience for the gamer community. We carefully planned everything and if we reach the production milestone, we will strive to create a deep and compelling microverse (or macroverse, who knows!).

One of our biggest risks is that we have adjusted all the stretch goals very tightly, to reach as many people as possible so they can enjoy the game on different platforms. All of this takes a great deal of time and money, and we hope you will support our campaign to achieve this together.
