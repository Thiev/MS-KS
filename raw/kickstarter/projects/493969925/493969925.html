Tiny Diggers 2 is an open-world, sandbox construction truck game for kids and parents.

I created the first Tiny Diggers almost 3 years ago for my first son who was 3 at the time. 

It was an awesome experience to be able to create a game that my son could play and enjoy. Tiny Diggers is a popular educational kid's 2D game I first created by myself for iOS, Mac and Android. At the time I saw that there were no decent construction truck games on the mobile market for kids age 3-5 and parents. A lot of kids around this age love trucks of all kinds, so I went ahead and created the game and it received success in a crowded app store. I continued on making more kid's games for my audience, my own boys and for clients internationally. 

The Twitter and Facebook messages from parents I received were very rewarding and after 3 years, we still get feedback. I noticed that the parents are a lot more involved with their kids in Tiny Diggers than I expected, so I wanted to create a successor that brought them closer together in involvement and participation. 

Early Prototype Gameplay Video - Tiny Diggers 2

Tiny Diggers 2 is a HUGE leap forward from the first title; It's taking the likeness of the characters, trucks and experience and putting that into an entirely new setting. Eight months ago I started working on prototyping Tiny Diggers 2 in Unity 3D while I worked on other kid's games for clients, toiling away on evenings and weekends. I experimented with so many options for everything from: How the trucks handled, the perspective, what's the goal of the game - and so on. I'm very passionate about this series I've created, and I hope you can see that in the prototype. I bootstrapped a lot of what you see in the early prototype videos, and now I've run out of funds. 

I have an immense passion for developing kid's games, I love the feedback I get watching them play my games, and I hope I can fulfil this experience with Tiny Diggers 2. 

Tiny Diggers 2 is one of the few games that are not only great for kids but for their parents, too! Parents will be able to join in with their kids in co-op mode, assisting in construction jobs throughout the map or take control themselves.

Tiny Diggers 2 is an open-world sandbox game for kids and parents who love trucks and tools. In Tiny Diggers 2, the player will take control and operate Trucks and Tools in an engaging, brightly coloured cartoon world. The player can operate and drive an Excavator, Dumptruck, Forklift or Bulldozer and many other popular trucks. The player begins the game with one Truck (Excavator) and one Tool (Shovel). Explore the world as the player in 3rd-Person either driving in the Truck or on-foot with your tool at hand. 

Here are some fun features of the game:

Construction Jobs

Numerous job areas will be present all throughout the map where you can use your truck to dig or dump dirt while manipulating the terrain in real time, move and transport pipes, wood or many other objects. With the tools, you may be instructed to dig holes in a certain area manipulating the terrain, hammering nails, sawing wood, operating a jackhammer or painting road lines. As you reach a job area, typically marked off with construction tape, you can head to the local Foreman hanging out in that area. He'll give you quick (cinematic cut-scene style) instructions on what you're supposed to be doing there. Well avoid using words for directions and instead use iconic imagery.

Side Quests

Assisting the construction of a house, gas station jobs, pipe fitting and many others. 

Helmet Rewards

Once a ‘Job‘ is completed, you will earn construction helmets as ‘rewards‘ for your actions. Collect as many helmets as possible to earn and unlock more trucks and tools throughout the map. The player will have the choice to perform any number of jobs for the given truck or tool at any time. Each Truck and tool will have their own set of ‘Jobs’. 

Throughout the game, players will gradually earn the status of ‘Foreman’ or Chief of Construction World. This gradual status rank will be visible via Badges and clothing colour on the character. 

Customize

Choose your gender, colour of clothing or modify your truck in the Trailer-Shop. Here are some of the fun things you can unlock by playing and earning helmet rewards:

Age-Specific Modes

In Tiny Diggers 2, the player can choose from 2-modes. The 1st mode is aimed at kids age 3-5. In this mode you can only drive your truck forwards and backwards on rails in a side-camera view. This allows much easier controls for the age range and more time spent enjoying the experience. In this mode you can still perform jobs with trucks and tools. 

The 2nd mode is aimed at kids 6+ who are more comfortable with 3D games and can move a character or truck in full 3D space. This mode is also geared towards the parents who want to join in. 

Trailer (Shop)

Inside of a construction site trailer will be where you can choose your jobs on a map, customize your truck and the options for the game. 

Easy Gameplay Mechanics

One of our key goals in creating this game for kids is gameplay mechanics they can get behind. For example - when controlling a truck like the Excavator, you only need to press one button to operate the arm and shovel in one motion. There's no complicated button pressing at key times. This will go for any platform the game is released on.

TouchTilt Games is a small studio based in Halifax, NS founded 1 year ago by Shane Whitehouse. We strive to make high-concept kids games for multiple platforms. Our educational games and simulations focus on the child and parents’ wants and needs in an interactive game without being a babysitter toy. We emphasize simple play, fun and effective yet positive experiences solving everyday problems at the core. We’ll focus on creating games that are ‘sticky’ and not just a one-off experience for the Family.
