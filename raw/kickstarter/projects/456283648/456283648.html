FAQ

  • What is Recyx economic model?

    Recyx economic model design for you to be free in the best as possible to be, as in the game itself.

    You have to buy the game, that is the only thing you are oblige to do. That will be the full digital copy of the game. No limitations, no features locked of any kind.

    There is optional subscriptions, that will give bonuses that will only ease the task of the player. Mostly design for casual gamer, who want to follow the hardcore ones, or simply for those who want to go faster, safer.

    Optional Copex purchase: Copex is one of the currecyx in the game, you can earn those by doing daily objectives and in many other ways.

    Use mostly for parts warranty, outdoor services, and some utility objects. No weapon or any gamebreaker can be purchased with it.

    MMO have high server costs(with high population of course), that is why most of them have subscription, or stronger means to start as free-to-play, what we can't do. We have no much choice then to have another income system to insure the long life of the game.

    There is great game like Leagues of Legends that have a fair economic model that isn't a gamebreaker (last time I saw it at least). We tried to create one like that in our way.
