20th Anniversary of Chrono Trigger for the Fans to play.in 2015

 Be apart of a Chrono trigger legend. 

The 20th anniversary of Chrono trigger is coming forth and I would like to celebrate this by assisting my efforts in a 2d game by RPG Maker vx Ace.

In this I have already played and beat the game in Super nintendo version, but I felt cheated as to what to do and where to go in the story. There was no clues as what to do at times and my character was very confused. Well Chrono looks confused most the time but well that is another story. So for the fans,

 Here is a video of me just getting a new character to look like Crono. You should never try to exactly do a character exact. Try to come up with something of your own. Something like this for a remake.

I hope a remake can bring us closer to this very entertaining game that was the starting point from the dream team. Yes those great programmers that spent countless hours. If you beat the game to find one of the endings is a dream team ending.

I graduated in 1995 and Chrono trigger also came out about this time. so this should be the 20th Anniversary coming up. I have had a great and awesome pleasure of working with Squaresoft company, now called Square-Enix. I worked for them as a beta tester for some of their MMORPG type games and came up with brilliant ideas for their MMORPG progressions. 

I wanted the chance to try to please fans with this remake for newcomers and for Chrono trigger players already. So in this remake I wanted to work on more of the story and have some magna Artist look at the characters and story. I think that we can also do voice overs on some content and bring new content to the 2d game. With the help of todays RPG makers, I think that some of this material is possible with a team and dedicaton.

Here is the site on Facebook that has the most information about the project. We would love your feedback and fully intend to finish this. So long as we can for the fans and enjoying this great RPG.

https://www.facebook.com/groups/1482912225256300/

If we can not get funded for this project. We cannot go on to the next stage. Right now we have RPG Maker VX lite from Steam. We need to get licensing from the real version. So we can work on new parts of the mapping, sound, and overall story.
