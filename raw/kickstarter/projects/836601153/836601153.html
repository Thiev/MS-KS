We hope to raise $5,500 within 45 days for the developing of our video game's demo, to obtain funds for full development.

Will you be ready?

New Breed Games is embarking on a venture.  "We are at War with the Big Four". This includes games such as: "Darksiders" / "Darksiders II" and "Gears Of War".  Why such high competition?  Because this is one for the records.

We intend on making this a cross-genre experience for all to enjoy.  Though we have entered development, production is far from over.  Help us move it along by pledging (check the right side of the screen and see your reward(s).

About the project:

"PRECIPICE" is a cross-genre video game, as well as cross-platform (PC will be standard; XBOX 360 and PS3 licensing may be possible so it may end up on consoles, also).

Cross-genre means: we are not ONE particular style of gameplay. This is a game of all trades: this is Real Time Strategy taken to a new level; this is a "hack-and-slash"...this is a Third Person Shooter, and this is a Role Playing Game.

You play as the Undead Prince of Darkness: OMERTA (Latin for: "A Code of Silence"). Controlling the Undead, you will be using numerous weapons straight from Hell, including demonic swords and bows.  You may also use guns found on nearby corpses...as well as numerous spells (from balls of fire to meteor showers).

And your mission is simple: annihilate the human race.

The year is 2032; the world has fallen under the Apocalypse and only the damned remain. A resistance group has formed, known as the UNITED FRONT, and they will be the enemy. Though enemy or not, what fun it will be to kill the innocent, as well! 

Your overall objective is to gather the many "Divisions Of Darkness" that inhabit earth.  These "divisions" are cults of Undead that have set up camp in the parallel universe they now inhabit.  Seven out of ten cults are required to win the game, so you must impress them on a series of mixed missions.

You have Undead Minions at your disposal.  They are the player's "helpers" and as the game progresses, the players will find themselves leading armies of 30 Minions (upward of that, if possible).  Using an interface called "Command Mode" (based on Real Time Strategy elements), the player will be able to control the Undead Minions to aide in combat.

Game Features:

1)  Play as the Undead Prince Of Darkness!  Hack, slash, shoot, and blast your way through the human's reign and take over cities one-by-one.  Protect your investments by positioning "NODES" that allow for health and spell regeneration

2)  Raise the dead!  Control armies of Undead "Minions", lead them into battle, and raise them from human corpses via your "Grave-Robber" spell.  The dead will literally rise from graves and dead bodies to assist you in battle.  Use the "Command Mode" interface to form a stratagem before entering battle

3)  RPG elements!  Every item, weapon, and piece of equipment within the game has a strong Role Playing Game influence. Add "points" to further equip yourself in times of battle.  There are also plenty of RPG-based missions within the game, including collecting of Undead Shrubbery (and then applying its poison to your weapon!)

4)  Take over the world!  The world is at your disposal!  Summon seven out of ten Undead armies that belong to a total of ten Divisions Of Darkness (Cults of Undead soldiers that have set up camp on earth's premise).  Then embrace the final battle between the humans, and the damned...
 

How Kickstarter will help us:

For those not familiar with Kickstarter's fundraising process: on the right side of the screen, there are Pledges and Rewards .  Pledge as much as you want - $10 donations can add up. Though remember: the total pledge income is only paid to us / debited from your account when we reach the goal, which is $5,500.  Every dollar counts!  Help us get going!

What will he funds be used for?

The total cost of developing this game runs at (minimum) 3 million.  Obviously we are not pursuing this via Kickstarter, though we do intend on using this funding to further develop our demo, which will be pitched to publishers within the video game industry.  Once the demo is completed, we can submit to our publisher connections and get the game funded for its estimated 3 million. 

In exchange, we promise to live up to our pledge rewards (check the right side of the screen) and deliver an epic game for all to enjoy.  The money is used to pay individuals such as 3D modelers, 3D animators, and programmers for their time spent developing the pitching piece.
