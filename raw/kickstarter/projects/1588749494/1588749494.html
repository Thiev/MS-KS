From the Community for the Community!

The purpose of Origins of Malu is to bring back the exploration and wonder to the MMO genre. Our unique blend of features along with a fresh and simple methodology allows you to uncover the dangers and rewards of this world. Sound fun? Excellent! Keep reading.

  • Large Open World - You are not stuck on railroad tracks with Origins of Malu. You are free to roam our open world with no zones. The world acts as a dynamic, living creature itself so not only will you have to be constantly aware of your surroundings but you also have the power to change your surroundings for better or worse. You must think about what you attack or alter; a ripple effect will follow your actions wherever you go. Our indie plan is to launch with 300 square kilometers of land plus water space. With Kick starter, we will expand on this as much as we can!
  • No Class Restrictions We didnt want to limit players with a traditional class system, but we still appreciate its form and function. Origins of Malu provides classless game play where you can customize your character free of most limits. Dive into different specializations that tailor your personal fighting style. Still crave traditional class systems? No worries, you can still build characters to fulfill certain roles and specialize them to the fullest extent.
  • Open World Structures Ever find the perfect plot of land and want to build a house there? While other games say no, Origins of Malu offers you unmatched freedom in housing creation. We devoted a significant amount of time into ensuring players can take full advantage of our sandbox housing. Our system creates the least amount of restrictions while providing you with the inventive tools to build your house the way you want to build it.
  • Powerful Crafting System - Story lines and battles are important, but we didnt forget about crafting! The importance of crafting will range from day-to-day purposes; such as building unique types of materials for housing and tweaking magic spells, as well as include managing your crafting skill set to cultivate and maintain your environment. Since the world of Malu is directly affected by your everyaction, investing resources into crafting can lead to a prosperous and influential character. Crafting will be as integral as you make it. Though crafters can be an important resource for factions, if you have no desire to craft, then you dont have to craft. But if you are a die-hard crafter, you can create an entire world of possibilities as well as make a game with just this skill set alone.

First and foremost, you get the biggest bow from the team here at Burning Dog! Your support takes this project one step closer to completion.

We know this is a huge undertaking. We also know we are asking for a lot of money, yet we dont take that lightly. We have spent three years developing this project on an almost $0 budget. We have had to reduce features in the game to meet tight deadlines. As proud our work as we are, we cant do it alone anymore. With your help, we can expand our scope and bring all our creative goodness to life.

So what will your support do for us? Here is what we want to improve!

  • Super Large World - Bigger is better and very important for a sandbox world. With your help, we would be able to expand our world to 500 square kilometers.
  • New Playable Race -We will add another playable race to the mix of our current diverse line up of Qulinta, Toljon, and Jolah.
  • More Items and Weapons - We have lots of big plans for weapons and armor. We want to introduce more biotechnology and out of the ordinary equipment that we guarantee you have not seen in a MMO before.
  • Enhanced Crafting System - We want to take our crafting even further by expanding the amount of options available to players. Some of these options will include more materials, greater manipulation of abilities, and more ways to create unique items that tailor to your style.
  • Licensing Technology - If we want to play with the big boys, we need to have the big boy tools to get the job done. This includes the engine and tools that improve the game experience.
  • People to Contribute One of our main obstacles in completing this type of project is losing critical talent to AAA studios. While we have AAA ideas, we currently lack AAA resources to retain valuable members since this is such a time intensive project. The freedom to hire more team members would allow our creativity and content to explode. If we can achieve so much with scant resources, imagine what we could do with your support!
