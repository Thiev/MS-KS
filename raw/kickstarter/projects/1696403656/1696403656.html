Unlock the mysterious secrets of The Midnight Grindshow™ in this epic dark-fantasy, role-playing game.

Imagine waking up in a misty, desolate meadow. With no idea who or where you are, you come to realize your memories are stolen, leaving nothing but a burning curiosity in their wake. All you see are the twinkling lights of tents in the distance. You are quickly accosted and attacked by a grotesque gang of clowns. Your goal - defeating them and taking their weapons and trinkets. You then proceed to The Midnight Grindshow, to engage in a harrowing battle that leaves your own soul hanging in the balance. Oh, and you also get to do some fun things, like battle a ferret and operate a cotton-candy chainsaw.

Main Idea

The Midnight Grindshow is a role-playing action/adventure, and puzzle game. It focuses on your exploration of a mysterious traveling sideshow that begins at midnight, and disappears at dawn. The Grindshow itself consists of dark and twisted tents, each an axis to secret dimensions ruled over by powerful, corrupt sideshow performers but occasionally you run into some surprisingly humane characters that want to help you along your journey. In each episode you learn their back-stories by traversing their various stylized worlds; from an ancient Assyrian temple, to 1920s New York, to the dry African-esque plains of a land called Drought, and a finally, a funhouse where all your darkest fears come to light. By defeating these characters, using wit or hand-to-hand combat, you discover a piece of the overall puzzle; the motivations of the Grindshow, all while getting closer to discovering your checkered past within it. No, you were not part of any kind of prostitution ring or drug scandal - the truth is much more interesting than that!

YOU-

You begin the game with no memories of your past, your only clue to discovering who you are is a mysterious grindshow in the distance. You quickly disguise yourself and infiltrate The Midnight Grindshow™, using your wits or battle expertise to survive five riveting episodes. Each episode lets you live through and experience the backstory of one of the other main characters. To survive, you must triumph over them, because learning their secrets and sorrows is the only way you can possibly understand the darkness of your own past.

*Appearance subject to change based on your customizations and fashion sense.

EMILY SUTTONCREST-

Emily is a prim and proper lady from Victorian England, as well as the other main character. She is searching the twisted tents and hidden worlds of The Midnight Grindshow™ for her lost child, but not having much luck.

Will you help her in her quest for a reunion, or abandon her to walk the dark and ominous grindshow alone.

STRYDE-

At the height of his career, Daniel Stryde was an anthropologist and theologian in 1930's Africa. He had his reputation, his family by his side, and a rewarding career studying an indigenous tribe of lion-worshipping pygmies. Then, a heart-rending betrayal led him down a dark path from which he has never emerged. He was the sole survivor of that expedition, although he often wishes he weren’t.

Will you help him find his faith, or throw him to the lions?

MADAME ASTAR-

Madame Astar is the resident fortune-teller of The Midnight Grindshow. She was born a princess in ancient Assyria, but was quickly cursed and deformed by a dark djinn summoning ritual that left her in the service of demons.

Will you follow the guidance that this misunderstood mystic provides, or will you trust nobody but yourself?

SLAUGHTER-

Joseph Slaughter is the brutish leader of the cut-ups, a troupe of wild clowns that enforce the codes of The Midnight Grindshow. In the late 1800s, he was just a lonely orphan on the streets of London, trying to hide in the shadows because he was born cursed with four arms. Then a rash of murdered prostitutes left him in the middle of an inner city turf war between two crime bosses, with all fingers pointed at him.

Did he fall prey to his accusers, or fight back and raze all his enemies into the ground?

MANX-

As daughter of the mayor of New York in the roaring 20s, Georgina Cercone lived the high life as an international socialite, until a doomed romance with a son of the mafia threatened to tear her whole world apart. What followed was an intricate game of political intrigue, broken alliances, and petty larceny ending in a disastrous fire that reduced her entire world to cinders.

Was she the one that lit the match? You decide.

 THE FUNHOUSE

In this episode you are thrust into a narrative that switches between the darkened streets/sewers of London in the 1880s, and a psychedelic fun house of horrors. You navigate a back story that involves prostitutes, pimps, constables, and innocent children to solve a rash of political murders, and reunite a lost child with her parent. This episode tears away the mask of The Midnight Grindshow™, reveals its true intentions, and turns the mirror on you, forcing you to face the darkness within yourself.

THE DEN OF MISFORTUNE

In this episode you travel back the gilded halls of Ancient Assyria, in the temple where Astar lived with her mother, the Queen. You have to solve the riddles of their relationship and uncover the secrets behind their centuries-old rivalry in order to defeat the demons that haunt them both.

THE MIDWAY

This episode unlocks a pathway of puzzles and mini games, to be played and beat at your leisure. Defeating the games unlocks all kinds of cool stuff like extra character stories and secret items as well as replenishes your health and time meters. There are also more than a few hints in the midway about the characters and themes of the sequel.

Main Goal

The main goal of the game is to discover your identity and reclaim your memories. You do this by conversing with the mysterious Grindshow characters encountered, who may not be willing to share what they know, at least not without a fight. You may need to defeat them before you learn anything about your past.

Gameplay

You must constantly adapt to survive; conquering The Midnight Grindshow depends a lot on your own intuition. Each obstacle you face requires a different swiping/tapping/touch technique, which you only discover by trying it (or using a hint, like a wuss). The game is free, and you will be able to play the entire thing without spending a dime, if you are patient enough. It just depends on how badly you want it.

Platforms

This first installment of three planned games that will be released for iPad, iPhone, and Android. We want you to play The Midnight Grindshow everywhere and anywhere, all day, and all night.

Photo-graphics

Photo-graphics is a term we coined to describe the artistic style of using 2D photos to create a 3D atmosphere. We want the entire game to read like a beautiful visual novel, where each scene looks like a fashion spread ripped from the pages of Vanity Fair (but with much more action and gore, obviously).

Special Items

To survive until dawn you have to adopt various disguises and collect special items, and can level up these items to travel through the episodes undetected. We have partnered with several fashion designers to add fun, customizable items and outfits to increase the individuality of your character. Many of these items will also be available for purchase in the real world, which makes for some pretty great cosplay, if you ask us.

Choose Your Own Fate

There are several various outcomes for each episode of the game, and the ending depends entirely on the choices you make. Since their are so many possible outcomes you will have to replay the game several times to experience all that it has to offer. Everybody loves replay-ability, right? We loved “choose-your-own-ending” books as children, and have adapted that feature to this game. Each character you encounter will try and convince you to believe their version of the story, and whether or not you choose to engage in these "activities" can directly impact everything down to who you fight and defeat at the end of each level.

Time & Health

There are two forms of currency in the game. Health, which is measured by a meter, and time, which is measured by sands in an hourglass. The game only gives you until dawn to defeat all the characters and make your way to the final episode, but you can both earn or purchase more currency via gameplay or in-app purchase, and have the choice to use either to purchase items within the game.

Puzzles & Minigames

Each episode contains puzzles that help you unlock certain special items or discover secrets. Halfway through the game you unlock the midway, a dark plaza lined with booths where you can play mini games, gain experience, level up, and unlock special features. Rewards for solving puzzles and playing mini games range from gaining free access to short stories about the Grindshow, to unlocking secret weapons and gameplay modes.

Social Media Integration

The Game will be fully integrated with social media platforms via the Grind Cam so that any point during the game you can share your discoveries, interesting characters, or anything else you encounter. Since the journey varies for each player the Grind Cam will allow you to share your unique experiences with the world. It will also allow you to tease your friends for their bad decisions, because that’s what social media sharing is about, right?

Artist Collaboration

We are collaborating with everyone from fashion designers to visual artists to create various parts of this game. For instance, some animation sequences showcase watercolor paintings done by outside artists, and many of the weapons you can purchase in-game (such as powerful rings) are actually artisan-created hand-crafted pieces. Our goal is to involve as many artists as we can to create a product that is a true collaboration of a collective of creative minds. Say that 10 times fast, we dare you.

Release Date

The game is scheduled for release in Spring 2015. Obviously our goal is to finish it as fast as possible, but it is more important to us to create a quality game.

The $130,000 will go to hiring a developer to program and animate the game. We have found a great developer, and for a game of this magnitude, $130K is a steal.

Donations

Donations are always great in any amount! We firmly believe that our project embodies the essence of Kickstarter, and it is a truly artistic project, spearheaded by two guys who just want to create something memorable, that will change the face of video games forever. Not too much to ask, right? Any amount you can give is unduly appreciated!

Social Media Sharing

We know firsthand that it’s hard to make it out there in the real world, especially for artists! If you aren’t able to donate, please take the time to share this video on your social media networks, like Facebook, Twitter, and even Instagram. The more shares and likes we get, the quicker The Midnight Grindshow will come to life, so please never underestimate the power of copying/pasting a link!

Give Us Feedback

We welcome any and all feedback, including ideas on how to make this game better. If you have a great idea that you think would mesh well with this project, feel free to email us! Unlike some of the characters in the game, we don’t bite, and we love the magic that can come from like-minded artists working together for a greater good.

Any support, contributions, or feedback would be greatly appreciated!

Risks and challenges Learn about accountability on Kickstarter

Our biggest challenge will be smoothing out any potential bugs in the app code, but we have sought out a top-notch mobile-app developer so any challenges will be quickly resolved.

It bears mentioning that we have lost many friends as we've been sequestered in a dark, cave-like environment making The Midnight Grindshow™. But, let's hope they will forgive us when they see how amazing this game is.
