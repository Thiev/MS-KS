A dedicated 3-person studio seeks to create a cute and memorable choose-your-own-adventure game.

Dream of Stars (DoS for short) is a visual novel project first conceived more than three years ago by a small group of friends with little more than a handful of ideas and a dream. Drawing inspiration from both Western and Eastern media, Dream of Stars seeks to tell a meaningful and thoughtful science fiction adventure that places emphasis on character growth and interpersonal relationships. Our characters help one another to sort out the complicated web woven from the threads of their past, all while facing off against the first humanoid species ever discovered in space!

We are Jam Toast Studios, a crew consisting of a writer and composer as well as a newly-added freelance artist. With a simple dream, a boatload of talent and energy, and a complete Madoka Magica fangame under our belts, we hope to create a touching and memorable experience to rival those of professional visual novel companies.

The production of Dream of Stars will be a very public experience in which our supporters are able to look on as DoS takes its final shape, and even, via our various stretch goals, determine the content and bonus materials that ultimately will appear in the final production.

Now that you're all very excited about all of this, let's dive into the universe of Dream of Stars and experience the amazing world of twenty-second century Japan!

Set well over 100 years into the future, Dream of Stars follows the story of Tomohiro Ishikawa, a sarcastic and reclusive young man who has spent the last three years of his life locked within the confines of his tiny apartment, whiling away his days hacking into various computer systems for fun and profit. These talents draw the attention of Hiroaki Abe, the head and founder of the mysterious and secretive government organization known as “Hoshi.” Offered a spot on Hoshi's roster as a computer engineer, Tomohiro finally finds a compelling reason to leave his self-imposed prison and once more rejoin the outside world that he had scorned so many years ago.

The young hermit soon learns of Hoshi’s aims: to seek out the secrets of the universe and pave the way for humanity's ongoing ascent into the galaxy and beyond. Joining him in this noble quest are four young women who all possess unique traits and talents that make them invaluable to the organization and its mission. Together, in a strange and dangerous new world, they must push themselves to their limit to ensure that the dreams of the human race are not lost.

Will Hoshi accomplish their mission, or will humanity remain bound to the earth, content only to gaze at the night sky and sigh as they imagine what might have been? Will Tomohiro be able to come to terms with his own issues and work alongside his crew mates? What secrets lie in the hearts of the rest of our colorful cast? And just how will the mysterious aliens react to human curiosity?

All of these questions and more will be answered in Dream of Stars, an original visual novel coming soon from Jam Toast Studios!

Besides depictions of fantasy violence, Dream of Stars will be an all-ages game! There will be NO adult content whatsoever!

Various Concepts

Without your support, it simply would not be possible to produce a visual novel of the quality we seek to achieve with Dream of Stars, and especially not within a reasonable time frame. Each member of Jam Toast Studios loves and is devoted to the project, but monetary compensation is necessary to ensure that substantial time to focus on the game can be set aside by each teammate. That is why we are humbly asking for assistance in creating a visual novel that reflects the high standards to which we at Jam Toast Studios wish to hold our productions.

Because we are not (yet) professionals with set wages for game production, each team member has determined a price they personally feel is fair for the amount and quality of work they are putting into the project. Here is a breakdown of how your funding will be used, based on our initial fundraising goal. All amounts are approximate.

Risks and challenges Learn about accountability on Kickstarter

1.) TIME
We're only human here at Jam Toast Studios, so naturally there will be some risks going into producing a top-notch visual novel experience. Foremost among these being the fact that, while each individual member of the studio is an experienced artist within their own field, this is our first big production as a group that is not a fangame. Because of this, due to the sheer number of original resources we will be working with for Dream of Stars, including backgrounds, sprites, a completely original soundtrack, and a host of other things, the game may not be completed within the estimated 9-month time frame.

But fear not, loyal supporters, for we will give you updates on our devblog and Kickstarter page all the way through the development cycle. You will always know where your funding is going and able to see the quality that it has helped to finance. All team members are devoted to completing the game and any funded side stories at all costs, even if it ends up taking longer than anticipated.

2.) REWARD PRODUCTION AND SHIPPING
Rewards may take up to several months to send out between designing, revising, producing, packaging, and, of course, working on the game itself. All rewards will be lovingly designed and shipped by the Jam Toast Studios Visual Artist in between creating content for the game. There is a minor risk of erroneous/misprinted products, but the visual artist has several years of experience self-printing and shipping products of all kinds, so that is unlikely.

3.) A NEW CREW
Dream of Stars has been a visual novel idea for several years. Originally it was thought up by the (returning) visual artist, who previously had to leave due to personal monetary concerns. DoS has also gone through an attempted production once with a larger crew of over 5 people, which was difficult to manage with many differing opinions, needs, and time zones of members across the country. We have learned from past mistakes, which is why we come to Kickstarter now with a small, tight crew of similarly-minded friends who each have their own specific duties within the scope of the project.

FAQ

  • What is a visual novel?

    A visual novel is a digital program that presents windows of story text while illustrated backgrounds and characters appear to bring the events to life. Most visual novels (Dream of Stars included) allow you the opportunity to make decisions that shift the outcome of the story, much like a choose-your-own-adventure book.

    Last updated: Mon, Mar 17 2014 8:33 pm EDT
  • How long will it take to make Dream of Stars?

    We estimate a production time of 9 months.

    Last updated: Mon, Mar 17 2014 8:33 pm EDT
  • How much will Dream of Stars cost once it is finished?

    We estimate $16 for a digital download.

    Last updated: Mon, Mar 17 2014 8:33 pm EDT
  • What operating systems will DoS be compatible with?

    Dream of Stars will be available for PC, Mac, and Linux! There is also the possibility of an Android build, but this is not for certain yet.

    Last updated: Mon, Mar 17 2014 9:41 pm EDT
  • How long will the game be?

    We are aiming for at least 10 hours. Parody routes and the bonus Excellion storyline, if funded, will increase that by 3-6 hours, possibly more.

    Last updated: Tue, Mar 18 2014 3:20 pm EDT
  • Will Dream of Stars be DRM-protected?

    No.

    Last updated: Mon, Mar 17 2014 8:33 pm EDT
  • What are the animations for?

    The animations seen in the Kickstarter video are intended to be used for a fully animated opening sequence in the game itself. Animations will likely also be a part of in-game events.

    Last updated: Mon, Mar 17 2014 8:33 pm EDT
  • Is there any adult content in Dream of Stars? Is DoS a dating sim?

    No, absolutely not. Dream of Stars is an inspiring story about all kinds of love and relationships--above all, friends and family. The "heroines" are girls that the player forms a strong bond with and helps to achieve their dreams; DoS is not a "dating sim" and the objective is not to romance the different characters.

    Last updated: Tue, Mar 18 2014 3:20 pm EDT
