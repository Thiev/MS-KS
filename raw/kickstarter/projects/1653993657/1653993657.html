Final Quest

Long ago the peaceful world was ravaged by a fierce conflict. King Edward, Sepcom Emperor torn apart by envy and bitter hatred, battled to decide the fate of the world. Ralph and his team are mercenary's true epic warrior's who in the past was quest hunters, look to seek out the magical Gem Power and for those who are trained to use it. For nearly a decade the Sepcom, Emperor and his followers have held the world under their Leadership , but now its time to fight back, take back what's rightfully there's and bring peace, tranquility and order to the kingdom. But a time of change is coming. One small boy, the last of a long line of Gem Warrior's, will soon discover his magic, power and leadership and his heritage. In so doing, he will embark upon a Final quest to free the people and to find his long lost father.

Final Quest Part II The Rise of Dormai

Character bios:


Name: Ralph
Age: 18
Type: warrior
Summary

Employed by the king and is a mercenary warrior who does jobs for king as and when needed who joins the 4 warriors of light , Ralph is big headed and has a way with the ladies , he has a long sword for combat given to him as a special gift by the king he has wavy brown hair and blue eyes and he also has a short fuse and does not take in all the information when given to complete a mission. He also learn how to cope with his anger issues and learn to become a true warrior for the king and has a lot of potential in doing so.


Name: Bennett
Age: 19
Type: Magic
Summary

Bennett is a childhood friend of Ralph and a skilled Magic Artist having been instructed by the King. He is one of the lead members of the 4 warriors of light and it is he who initially convinces Ralph to join them in their rebellion against the Sepcoms. He ultimately helps to unlock Ralphs missing memories when they fall into the planets core together, and she is the only character who knows of Ralphs past.

Elena's Theme

Name: Elena
Age: 14
Type: Magic
Summary
Is a junior member of the 4 warriors who obtained her position as the newest recruit after Ralph formed the Team . Elena takes her work more seriously than Ralph and Bennet and tends to become frustrated with their laid-back and arrigent attitudes. During Final Quest Elena is captured with Queen Adel by the Sepcoms, and Ralph and the party team up to save her. She has a crush on Ralph, and temporarily holds Ralph and the party accountable when he is injured while investigating the The Dungeons.
