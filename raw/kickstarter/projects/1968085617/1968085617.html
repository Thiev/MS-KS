A detailed Boxing Manager/Trainer Game. Train and Tailor fight plans, take on contenders lead your boxers to titles. Be a RING KING!

Connect with Ring Kings on Twitter and Facebook

INTRO

Our goal with RING KINGS is to create the best Boxing trainer/manager game ever. Build a stable of boxers, Negotiate contracts, Train fighters, set training regimes, develop fight plans, climb up the rankings, challenge for World Titles, get inducted in to the Hall of Fame. Fight in a multitude of venues around the World in this ground breaking Boxing Simulation.

ROAD TO GLORY

Imagine a game that puts you as head trainer and manager of a stable of fighters at your very own Boxing gym. Your star pupil is a middleweight boxer who joined your gym as a local fighter at age 18 and Turning professional at 21. Through tailored training regimes you have developed his physical gifts and worked on his technical flaws through thousands of hours of hard work in the gym. 

Coming up as a prospect you have navigated his career through the vast boxing landscape. From his debut, to fighting Journeymen, gatekeepers, fellow prospects until eventually your fighter became a contender and defeated a rival contender in a 10 round unanimous decision. Creating a potential title shot against the World champ. During this path you have come up against relentless brawlers, pure boxers, defensive counterpunchers and heavy handed KO artists and in each case you have developed fight plans to give your fighter the best chance of winning. So far he's undefeated 24-0(14 Knockdowns) and ranked number 7 with the World Boxing Council.

You have just received a email from the WBC notifying you that your fighter is now the mandatory opponent for the title holder. You have been given 28 days to negotiate the fight on behalf of your fighter. You need to ensure your fighter gets a career high purse, a split of the PPV (if applicable), negotiate a lucrative rematch clause in the contract and set out a training camp to ensure your fighter is in the best condition to defeat his opponent come fight night. Hiring sparring partners that mirror your opponents style/stance will better prepare your boxer for the fight ahead. Giving him better chance at success.

In the pre fight press conference when addressing the media, do you A. Question your fighters opponents resume and credentials (The trash talk approach- potentially angering him and taking him off his fight plan). B. Pay respect (The humble approach- potentially causing doubt to your fighter) C.  Make a prediction (potentially giving your fighter confidence) this all has a bearing on the psychology of fighters. How all this effect your fighter will depend on there mentality, and every fighter in the boxing game has a different mentality. Mike Tyson once said he won most of his fights before the bell ever rang.

The scene is the Garden Arena Las Vegas the title on the line is the WBC Middleweight Championship of the World. The opponent is a known fast starter with true KO power in his right hand, and explosive speed but has a suspect chin and fading stamina, a game plan might be to take it slow through through rounds 1-4 and keep moving to your left away from that powerful right hand, working the body to take away some of that speed away, and round 6 you instruct your boxer to get a bit more aggressive and take the fight to him.

 The fight is taking place in glorios 3D so you can see that your tall rangy fighter with superb and superior technical skills is beginning to win the battle against the  aggressive, shorter, stockier, more crude opponent. The bell for the end of the 7th round rings your fighter sits on the stool you instruct him to increase the punch output and look to employ the left hook to the body, to get your opponent to lower his guard and then look for the straight right to the chin. You also call on motivational shouts to get your fighter in the zone for this round. Your fighters adaptability is high, he executes 8th round KO WIN! and the NEW....

Champion

You are at the post fight Press conference, your new world champion boxer has his own unique personality he may call out a rival belt holder in the same division for a unification fight, he may want to move up divisions to take on the huge money fights at the weight division above. That depends on his motivation is it legacy? or money? the safe fight? the hardest fight? every fighter in the game behaves differently. You will be asked questions by the media about your fighters next step on his journey. Its difficult to win a world championship belt, its even harder to keep it. The journey of him defending his belt now begins....How will you guide him.

Game Database

The Retail database engine will be completely fictitious, there will be a 3rd party community website where fans can share custom databases, skins, graphics, and game saves. Including databases that reflect the current boxing climate and will be updated twice per year. This enables us to not pay licensing fee's to boxers and governing bodies and instead focusing the budget on the development of the game. 

Summary

In this boxing manager game there are a whole host of features that can be explored, heres a brief overview of a few.

True Boxing Landscape (Style) Brawlers, Boxers, Boxer- Punchers, Counter-Punchers, Sluggers.  (Class) Rookie, Young fighters, prospects, top prospects, journeymen, gatekeepers, fringe contenders, contenders, champions, fading legends. Boxers have ring tendencies and are rated in 37 attributes. Inside fighter, outside fighters. Move up or down divisions, fight at catch weights, unify titles. An authentic Boxing world.

Boxing Politics- Rival promotional companies, Interactive news feed, Press conferences, Fighter rivalries and rematches, a high depth Boxing world.

Gym Finances Your gym is a business and those books have to balance

Stable Harmony If you have 4 of the top 10 boxers in the world all fighting out of your gym, disarray may quickly set in, fighters need time and special attention too much world class fighters and you may see a drop in morale, and losses creep in. Set out your plans and goals with each fighters career and see them through.

Training Camp Set out training camp duration and regime, do you want to take a scientific approach, a old school approach, balanced, cardio heavy or boxing heavy, find and hire sparring partners that resemble your opponents fighting style. Presets available for quicker play. 

Rankings  Rise up the rankings of the 4 boxing governing bodies, as well as the linear True rankings, and eventually break in to the Pound 4 Pound top 10. This replicates the real boxing world.

Tactics- Give your fighter the motivational and tactical shouts to help him win

3D Fight Action- Glorious 3D fight engine, view the intricacies of your boxers fighting style as oppose to your opponents. Every fight looks different, some are brutal Bloody wars, some resemble patient tactical chess matches.

Animation- Each fighter moves different, Boxers, brawlers sluggers boxer-punchers, tall, short, muscular, slim, fast, slow, orthodox, southpaw. Every single fight(er) will feel unique.

Contracts- Negotiate Purses, contracts, Pay Per view split, ring size etc.

Judges Scoring - Lifelike 10 point boxing system,based on ring generalship, effective aggressiveness, clean punching and defence, occasional bad judging as in real life adds to the drama and controversy that exists in boxing.

Choosing Opponents- Research opponents for your fighters, put your young gun in with a relentless KO artist too early and he may suffer a bad loss that will derail his career and see him never fulfil his potential. You have to know how to space out your fighters opponents to bring him along nicely. If hes had 3 successive 12 round hard battles he will need some some time off.

Awards-  Fight of the year, prospect of the year, round of the year, trainer of the year, promoter of the year, KO of the year, Hall of fame inductees etc

Referees/Judges Database of referees and judges some judges may favour different styles this all adds to the authentic feel of the game.

Injuries/Suspensions  Failed drug tests, Broken jaws, broken wrists, Broken orbital bones, Concussions etc.

Game Evolvement rookies become Contenders, contenders champions, champions become veterans,  retirement, comebacks, vacated titles, fighters move up divisions as they get older and find it harder to make weight. Fighters and Trainers get inducted to the Hall of Fame.

Watch Videotape Scout your next opponent through 3D video tape

Trainers/Cutmen/Strength & Conditioning coaches-  All have their own styles and attributes.

Commentary/Ring Announcers-  Superb commentary add electric atmosphere on the night of the fight.

Tell of the Tape/Keys to victory/Broadcast Style Slick presentation- Gives you that Las Vegas big fight feel when your fighter gets into the big league.

3D Arenas(stretch goal only)- We will build all the major arenas and venues in Las Vegas, California, York Hall (London), Boardwalk (New jersey), etc. approx 16 unique arenas planned.

Find talent- Scout new fighters and try to attract other top fighters to your gym, a fighter on a bad run of form is more likely to be unsettled and you could get a great boxer to join your stable

Promotion Companies Play a big part in arranging fights, negotiate with them and build good working relationships. There are promotional rivalries and intricate politics which can shape a fighters career path.

WHY KICKSTARTER?

We want to make the most in depth game possible without compromising or bending to the constraints that a traditional publisher would put upon us. This is why so many boxing games end up being very arcade style, easy to pick up and play, with very little depth. No one is catering for fight fans who enjoy realism and strategy in their gaming. We have spent a considerable amount of our own money developing the general concept and laying the groundwork.

Going Independent we would need at least £300,000 ($455,373) to make this game. Our promise in return for your pledges is that we will make the best quality game possible and deliver it on time.

Additional fundings allow us to make Ring Kings even bigger and better.   Every penny counts towards making RING KINGS a reality. We humbly thank you for your support.

What is your pledge is going towards

The bulk of the pledge amount is going on programming the fight engine, game engine and the AI. This is a huge task as our programmers have to build this from scratch; there is no blueprint to a Boxing manager game, what we want to create is ground breaking. The rest of your pledge is going towards building the game database, artists, Interface designers, and researchers.  Production time is going to be 14-16 months.
