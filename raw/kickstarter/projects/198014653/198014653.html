Battle for glory, love, and freedom in this Unreal® Engine 4 open world Steampunk RPG inspired by your favorite classics.

     Two friends, River and Ollie, set out to chase destiny when they sign on to travel the air and seas as initiates of the Skylarks -- an organization that protects the alliance between the 7 countries of Pleone.

     Meanwhile Annette (Ant) Howland inherits her late parents' fortune and commissions a ship. As captain of the ship, Ant joins the quest to become a Skylark, hoping to exact revenge on the pirates that killed her parents and sister.

    As the adventurers fight their way deeper into a web of mystery and intrigue, they begin to discover that the lines between good and evil are not as clearly defined as they once believed. When secrets threaten to tear them apart, they must weigh their dedication to their cause against the cost of battling a conspiracy which could ultimately destroy the fabric of their civilization.

     With your choice of two main characters to play, unique quests that interweave throughout the game, and a conflict which will change the face of the world, Sky Bridge: Fallen Stars promises to deliver hours of immersive playing time and an experience worth enjoying again and again.

                                                                   Presenting:

 JAZ Games is an indie development company with online reach to amazing talent, and local roots that keep them grounded in the community. Local employees and interns are excited about the opportunities that a local game development company opens for technical education and work in Arkansas. JAZ Games creates fantastic cult classics and works to make a huge difference in the video game market, and in shaping and changing future titles to bring what players want, and will need as games evolve. Their eye is on the future of game development.

             

 Over ten years ago, in a house overlooking the Arkansas river, Jacob, Allyson, and Zachary Siler formed JAZ Games. Their goal has always been to bring quality, games driven by characters and story to the video game market. They have worked jointly and independently on numerous game projects over the last ten years. (The LOST Fangame, Darkwood (2002), and Alagaesia Online.)

Jacob enjoys writing, programming, jewelry making and challenging himself. He is dedicated to bringing quality games to the market for the whole family. 

Allyson loves puzzles, writing, singing and creating art. She loves to travel and explore new places. 

Zachary is a people person, a fine carpenter, a blacksmith and knife maker. He enjoys bringing the SkyBridge art to life and spending time with his wife and son.

Growing up in a creative family, they learned that they could make anything they put their minds to. This often led them to searching through hardware stores, salvage yards and thrift stores and the occasional chase around their home town in the Arkansas River valley to find just the right part or materials. These searches gave them tenacity and taught them to work hard. 

 The three siblings bring this creative spirit and work ethic to Sky Bridge Fallen Stars and are dedicated to seeing it launched to the gaming community on many platforms.
