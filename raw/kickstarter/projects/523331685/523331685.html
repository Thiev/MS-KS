Build & Destroy to take over the world! This is a Mobile game where you challenge other players to DESTRUCTURE battles.

With Destructure you can battle other players in challenges to destroy their creations.

This is a mobile, turn based game that lets you hold multiple challenges with multiple players at the same time.

Do you have what it takes to become a Master Chief Destructor?

The majority of the players will start playing as Apprentice Destructors. As you play along, you can move up the Ranks by challenging, accepting challenges, winning battles, recruiting, etc. 

Each different rank will have access to different building blocks and different weapons.

One of the main ideas for this game was to allow the players to be creative and build, so no single battle will ever be the same. 

If you are in a hurry to destroy, you can use a pre-existing structure, all you need to do is add your defense systems and go to battle!

I.- Challenge players around the globe to destroy your structure.

Before challenging anyone, you can always test your structure and adjust it so that it withstands any attack. 

II.- Accept challenges and destroy other players' structures.

III.- Play weekly Destructure challenges.

At the end of each battle, the challenger can see a video of how his structure withheld the attack, or how it was destroyed.

The more you battle the more ranks you earn!

How would you destroy? Weapons! You will first have access to a set of basic weapons. You can earn points to upgrade or buy more advanced weapons.

Please note that with your funding support the final design of the weapons will most likely change.

Points. 

  • Each time you destroy, you will earn game points. 
  • Each time your structure witholds an attack, you will earn more game points. 
  • Playing weekly challenges will also earn you points.

Depending on your rank, game points can be used to buy or upgrade weapons, buy defense systems, and/or buy special building blocks.

How would your structure hold an attack?

I.- Building strong. This is where your creativity needs to kick in.

II.- Defense Systems. There will be a defense system for each available weapon. The tricky part is that when creating and positioning your defense systems, you won't know what weapons other users will use, so you will have to plan for the worst.

Start building your crew and challenge other teams to group battles.

Can you recruit right away? No, you will have to win battles and become a Master Destructor to start building your team.

Pledge $200 or more and you can recuit your crew right away.

Destructure is currently on a prototype phase.  Before seeking funds we wanted to created a prototype that will work on an actual device. 

We wanted to make sure that the principal components of the idea that Carlos Jr. had behaved the way we wanted, and that we were not pursuing something unachievable. 

The prototype was also intended to have different people test the BUILD & DESTROY process and see if this was something that generated interest and was attractive from a game point of view. 

From an implementation point of view my main goal for the prototype was to be able to:

  • Build a structure 
  • Battle (with weapons and defense systems)
  • Upgrade weapons and defense systems
  • Record and Show Battles

I choose to use Cocos2D as my 2D Game Framework, Box2D for my Physics Engine and Kamcord for Game Play  Recording. 

At this point we can say that the prototype exercise was successful. All of the 4 bullet points above were met, and the kids can't stop testing it. Friends have also tested it and we have receive good feedback. We have also gotten comments with very good suggestions that will help us get this game to the level we want and players may expect.

The following set of videos show the prototype in action.

Currently the building process is simple and efficient.

As part of the initial tests we wanted to make sure that the different building blocks reacted in a "normal" manner when a weapon was used.

It wouldn't be fun if you can't protect yourself, which is why you can use defense systems to protect your structure and hold an attack.

When I started this project with Carlos Jr., I thought we can take it on our own, all we need was time! Yeah sure, just time! Well, we quickly realized that if we wanted this game to thrive and have the quality we envisioned, we were going to need help.

When we started exploring Kickstarter we didn't even look anywhere else, this had to be the place to promote DESTRUCTURE. We loved the community involvement, the transparency, and definitely feel the obligation to deliver a good quality and entertaining game.

We are reaching out to the community (YOU) to help us make this game a reality. 

Without your HELP, we cannot fund all the different components that are needed to make this a real world class game.

How would your money be used?

Enhanced Graphics

Visual elements are key to a successful game. We want to have a single theme that will unite all the components of the game and make it one that people would want to keep on playing.

With your help we can work with a professional Illustrator that can use his magic to make this a real world class game.

Sounds

Special effects and background track.

Development costs

Many pieces still need need to be developed, and only with your help can this be done.

  • New weapons, defense systems and building blocks.
  • Framework to manage the entire crew/team system, i.e ranks, Challenges, Team challenges.
  • Framework for game points system.
  • Weekly Challenges originated from the Destructure team.
  • Structure of the week. Any player's structure can make it to this challenge and will be visible in the home and to the entire player community.
  • Social Integration.
  • In-App purchases.

Destructure Website

Thankfully, I have experience in website development for both front and backend components, but we want to work with a web designer to make the website look the way every player deserves.

The website components are:

  • Game features
  • Leader boards and challenge standings 
  • Game Play Recordings for past battles

Infrastructure costs

I plan to use AWS to host all the back end functionality. If funded, I eventually want to make this a multi-platform game, and because of this I cannot tie down to platform specific components that may already be available.

Nothing is set in stone and there will always be risks, but with a correct time management, resource planning, and with my previous experience the risks can be controlled and resolved.

If funded we will be on top of having our rewards shipped ASAP, if something out of our control should occur there will be clear communication and prompt resolution to any problem.

Please feel free to contact us with any questions or concerns you may have before pledging, We will try to address them immediately and hopefully earn your trust.
