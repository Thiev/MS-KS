What is Winged Sakura: Mindy's Arc?

Winged Sakura: Mindy's Arc is a fast-paced stunning HD 2D Anime themed strategy defense game with RPG mechanics, based on telling a story about Mindy and how "it" all began. Players can level up, use skills, summon spirits to defend against waves of enemies.

The story is set in a modern fantasy world where Mindy unconsciously foresees the future. In this era, Sakura, or Cherry Blossoms, are known as flowers that give humans the ability to become a Contractor. If a human chooses to enter into a contract with a Sakura, they will gain abnormal powers. As a trade off, the human's life is the Sakura's life. The Sakura's life is the human's life. If one dies, the other one dies.

Core Game Features

  • A Brand New Innovative Defense System: Unlike tower defense games, the units you place on the field only last for a few seconds. You have complete control over each character; however, they can't move (unless you have a Teleport item). They "die" if an enemy touches them.
  • Leveling Up System: Similar to RPG games, every time you successfully finish a level, your characters will level up and get stronger.
  • Skill System: There will be a small skill tree for each Spirit and a slightly larger one for your main character. You get skill points after every x levels and you get to distribute them to make your characters stronger. Most of these skills are passive, but we have at least two active skill planned for each Spirit and at least 8 for the main character.
  • Shop, Equipment and Upgrading System: Sell loots you obtain from playing a level, upgrade the shop to buy better items, enchant your gears to make your characters stronger, and craft them into a higher tier. There's a total of 6 tiers ranging from the lowest grade, C, to the highest, SSS. 
  • Survival Mode (End game content): Use your story mode Spirits to defend against waves of enemies to see how long you can last. The enemies will slowly get harder as the number of wave increases. The equipment you've obtained and levels of your character will play huge a role in this. This mode gives you a reason to continue playing after beating story mode, offering a great amount of replayability.
  • Marathon Mode (End game content): This mode is a counter design to our Survival Mode for players who doesn't want to invest time to level up and spend time to collect gears for Survival Mode. Example of how it works: After every 5 waves, you get to shop, distribute your skill points, buy items (randomly generated), buy new Spirits (randomly generated), and continue playing. The items and Spirits you get to buy are random. If you lose, you have to start over again. Since the waves, items and Spirits are randomly generated, it'll feel like a new game every time.

Most of the gameplay art is already done, so we got this part covered. If by any chance we get over funded, we will use the money to produce more gameplay art where necessary. Some examples would be more unique levels. If we meet the funding goal, more options will open to us. 

As for programming, I do all of them myself. I dedicate myself full-time to this project.

Project Status

  • Development is 90% complete. We're still missing some features, such as upgrading equipment and crafting. Marathon mode is still incomplete due to it's difficult design which requires heavy balancing. We'll wait until we balance story mode before we work on Marathon Mode.
  • Gameplay art is about 90% complete. We're missing 3 bosses, the second one is being worked on
  • All story characters and their facial expressions are done. We'll most likely create some more facial expressions for each character as the story needs them
  • In terms of integration and the actual gameplay, we're still pretty far from being complete. What is shown in the demo is all we currently have. We'll be spending 6 months to push and polish content
  • We currently only have 4 CG completed 

Story Characters

Mindy

She's the main character for the game. Mindy is an ordinary girl with a bright personality. She loves to hang out with friends and go shopping. Her goal is to eventually become a Sakura Contractor and become as strong as her younger sister.

Minzy

Mindy's Sakura, also known as the number negative three. She has a tsundere personality mixed with Mindy's bright personality. She loves to joke around and tease Mindy, so it feels like they're always arguing. 

Seven

Mindy's friend. He tends to joke around and never takes anything seriously. He goes on and on about the most random thing that comes out of his mind.

Nina

Princess of Aletheia. Aletheia is one of the twelve nation that rules the modern fantasy world. She entered into a contract with two Sakuras: Ren and Nino.

Nino

Nina's Sakura. She's gullible, clueless most of the time and serves Nina as a maid. 

Ren

Nina's Sakura. If he's left alone, he's bound to cause trouble. He only respects his master and Nino. He has an extremely short temper.

Project visibility is my number one concern. It's difficult to let the world know about your project without spending money on marketing. We hope to overcome this obstacle with your help.
