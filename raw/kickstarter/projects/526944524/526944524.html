Test your reflexes and your will to survive as you escape the incoming Supernova! Do you have what it takes to NovaScape?

NovaScape is a mobile phone based video game application, in which the player will control a spaceship using the gyroscope within their phone. The player will tilt their phone from left to right in order to control the side to side flying motion of their spaceship.

The inspiration behind NovaScape came from a very simple game I used to love and play called Cube Runner. The purpose of the game is to achieve a high score based on flying distance, the longer you fly by avoiding the obstacles the more your score would increase.Â  What I want to do is take Cube Runner to an entirely new level, with much more intuitive controls and a greater user interactive experience.

That is when I thought of NovaScape! An endless action video game where the user would control a spaceship using the gyroscopic function built into their phone, to fly though space and avoid all obstacles in their path.

The game starts out with a brief video intro of a supernova explosion where the plane will be frantically flying away from.The video will smoothly transition into an over the shoulder game play view of the spaceship. The background will be moving and obstacles will be flying at the plane from the horizon.

The purpose of the game is to achieve a high score by flying the greatest distance possible while avoiding incoming asteroids and meteors. The longer the player stays alive by avoiding these impacts the more their score will increase and the harder the game play will get. Such increasing difficulties will included; meteor showers, black holes, comet strikes, etc.

The two main obstacles in the game so far are Asteroids and Meteors. My vision is to have many more obstacles developed to make this game truly blossom into the game I know it will become.

If the player crashes into such objects, their health bar will decrease and the Supernova will encroach ever closer. Better watch out! If you mess up too many times you will be engulfed within the fiery abyss to never see the universe and all its splendor again... Or you could just press replay and give it a second shot. Good Luck! Lets see if you got what it takes to NovaScape!

Once NovaScape loads you will be taken to the main screen. On this main screen your options will be to :

-Play the game

-Learn how to play the game

-Enter the Space Station to choose or upgrade your spaceship

-Check out the Global High Scores

-Edit your Settings ex.(Music, Gyroscope Sensitivity, Sound Effects)

-Connect with Facebook (Share stats, Records and High Scores)

There are 4 ship designs so far, my goal is to have over 10 completely unique and iconic ship designs! This will be possible if my pledge goals are reached with Kickstarter.

At randomized points throughout the game, a bright shooting star will fly over the ships wing and take a flying position in front of the player. If the player can catch up to the shooting star and collect it, the gameplay will be transitioned into a first person cockpit mode.

Within this cockpit mode the player will press a hyperspace button flashing on the screen in front of them and send them through hyperspace. During this hyperspace boost that will last 10 seconds, the spaceship will fly through a bunch of bonus stars.

When hyperspace ends, the spaceship will be launched into a new and colorful galaxy.

If the player fails to catch up to the shooing star in a set amount of time, the star will shoot off and you will have to wait until it comes around again. The incentive behind the bonus round is to:

  • Add a bonus score multiplierÂ 
  • Hyperspace to a new galaxy
  • Increase the players score

Within the game, there is the ability to share and log your stats and high scores on Facebook. There will also be a Global Leaderboard and the ability to look up users personal stats via a Usercard.
