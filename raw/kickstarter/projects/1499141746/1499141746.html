Breeding

Monsters are made of pure living energy, or magic, depending on who you talk to in the game world.Â  Breeding your monsters together is actually NOT "mating" but rather when 2 compatible monsters are happy they give off large amounts of positive energy/magic that can be harnessed by a Catalyst.Â  This energy or magic harnessed by the Catalyst then manifests into a new living monster!Â  The new monster created is dependent on its parents elemental type, but different elemental Catalysts will provide greater probability that the offspring will be the desired element.

Turn-Based / Real-Time

The battle system is a hybrid of turn-based and real-time elements and plays very similar to "Final Fantasy VII" ready/wait system.

Every monster has a "Speed Stat" that dictates how fast its turn gauge fills during the battle phase.Â  The monsters turn guages fill in real-time and once a monsters turn timer is full, the time clock stops to allow you to choose your attack and then select your target.Â  The action of choosing an ability to use and selecting your desired target, is turn-based.

Status effects that deal damage will calculate in real-time, allowing these abilities to impact different measurements of time and thus not limited to a monsters next turn.
