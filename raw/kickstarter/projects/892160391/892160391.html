Epic Art and Music

We have been working full time on the game with our own money. Better Game Engine, Dev Tools and more Game Content

We have the game prototype and concepts down but we need to switch to a more powerful engine that can handle huge levels, better graphics, cool special effects, the complete Viking squad and large groups of enemies with smart AI.
