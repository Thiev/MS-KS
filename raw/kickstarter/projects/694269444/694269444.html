What is the game about?

Beckii Cruel The Game is heavily influenced by games like Game Dev Story and Lemonade Stand and will take you on the journey of Beckii as she became famous in Japan.

Create viral videos in your bedroom, deciding on what genre and style they should be, then grow your kawaii empire and buy new things to make your next project bigger and better!

Increase your fanbase by purchasing items to help build your appeal, performance, charm and tech abilities, allowing you to create and unlock new types of entertainment including:

  • Internet Videos, just like Beckii started with!
  • Dance Videos as a group or a solo artist
  • Music Singles - branch out into the music industry
  • Concerts where you can meet fans and gain popularity
  • Clothing Lines where you can earn more money by designing kawaii lines!
  • And much more!

Only by mastering all four of your abilities will you be able to become a worldwide megastar, just like Beckii Cruel!

Buy equipment, gain hype, create productions, hire your enterage, attend awards. Beckii is definitely a busy bee! With your backing, you can support her career and gain a fun glimpse of what it is like too.

Risks and challenges Learn about accountability on Kickstarter

There really isn't much in the way of challenges for the project - Beckii has the idol factor, Sparkles produces epic music, the development team have worked on software for 10 years or more and the artwork that has already been produced is testament to the level of polish that will go into this title.

The main risk will be managing the budget for the game; if the Kickstarter scrapes through, we will be on a very limited budget so the timeframe will have to increase for delivery. We hope that with your help and pledges, our budget should be much greater, ensuring the project can be delivered to the highest standard possible, in the least possible time.

FAQ

  • Who is doing all the amazing artwork??

    The lead artist on the project is Kimberley, also known as KimmyManga! You can see more of her amazing artwork on her deviantArt page at http://kimmymanga.deviantart.com - drop by, say hi and give her a big hug!

    Last updated: Wed, Oct 16 2013 7:54 am EDT
  • Who is going to be developing the game?

    The development will be done a small team that approached me about the idea in July, and since then they have worked with Kimmy to come up with the concept art for the game, and did the site for it at http://www.beckiicruelthegame.com

    One of the guys used to work for Rockstar Games, and between them all they have over 20 years of development experience, so I trust them to turn the concept into a truly amazing game!

    Last updated: Wed, Oct 16 2013 8:48 am EDT
  • What is the game going to be developed in?

    The game will be developed in C# using the Unity framework (http://unity3d.com/) meaning it can be easily published to a number of platforms including iOS, Android, Windows, Mac and more.

    Last updated: Wed, Oct 16 2013 8:48 am EDT
  • £X for Y is sooo expensive? HOW CAN YOU ASK $Z FOR A GAME?! Etc etc..

    The price not only has to cover the cost of making the item(s) you are pledging for, they also cover the actual development and operational costs of the project too. It's somewhat the point of Kickstarter projects that you pledge the money for supporting the idea first and foremost, then the pledge goals are awesome extras that you may or may not want.

    If you just want to support and buy the game, you can do for £6-£10, which isn't a lot for a independently developed game which you will get on all devices and platforms. If you want some of the cool other things we are going to be doing to support the project, that's awesome too.

    Please note that all pledges stack, so by pledging at the higher amounts, you also get everything else before it, plus you help support the game for a higher amount which is always welcome!

    Last updated: Wed, Oct 16 2013 9:10 am EDT
  • Is the game solely about you?

    No. Beckii Cruel The Game is a working project title and although I will feature heavily in the game and have my own character line, it is geared more to be an Idol Simulator that has been endorsed by me rather than entirely about my own story.

    Last updated: Wed, Oct 16 2013 8:48 am EDT
  • Is £10 the price the game will be released at if it's successful?

    No. The price on the various market places and App Stores will vary per device and much less if you are buying it on a per device basis.

    We appreciate that £10 might seem like a lot, but at this stage it goes towards supporting an independent game including development, artwork, photography and music costs, and all the other things along the way.

    Plus anyone who supports via Kickstarter will get access to the game on every device we can release it on, not just one device too.

    Last updated: Wed, Oct 16 2013 9:10 am EDT
