GymNation - Eat, Train, Conquer. Customise your gym and compete against rival gyms to rule the leaderboards. Train to be the best!

GymNation is a sim management game on the edge of health/fitness and gaming. It’s a game that is fun to play, set in a unique environment with eye-pleasing visuals, whilst promoting a healthy way of living. This gives the game two sides, one of virtual training, and another that incorporates virtual training into real life training. So if you like how your character is getting on, why not head down to the local gym yourself and do the same workout.

The main objective of GymNation is to build up the players character or avatar, by feeding and training it in order reach a goal physique. This will aid in completing an extended variety of game tasks and activities in order to dominate the leader boards. As you progress, you can expand your gym and fill it with new items, from the usual weights and running machines, to spas and pools. Unlike many isometric games, GymNation is rendered in full 3D allowing you to zoom in and rotate your gym to see it from all angles. There are loads of features like traveling to other locations such as the Swimming Pool, World Gym and Boxing Club. Create or join a Crew with friends to reach the top spot on the leader boards and enter Rival Mode to destroy rival gyms around the world.

 GymNation has loads of features that will keep players hooked whilst also focusing on creating an urge for real life physical activities. It has been proven in many gaming platforms that players get attached to there game avatars. Take the Tamagotchi toy for example; Players would create a real emotional connection with the virtual pet, and feel real emotion if it was to die or get sick. For some people, seeing their virtual character growing unhealthy may an early wake up call before it has a chance to happen to the players themselves. This is what we are currently working on with GymNation, a way to promote a healthy lifestyle. As well as engaging with your avatar and its virtual life through your mobile phone, what if you could go to the gym, log your workout, and then have your avatar receive the same benefits. It will in term become a virtual image of yourself. GymNation will have such a deep social aspect that it will become motivation for players. Having your friends see your game avatar unhealthy and obese may push the player work harder on achieving results.

We have almost completed the first version of GymNation, but our aim is to keep development running as there are many features we have on the development plan and are aiming to update soon after initial release. These are some of the features:

  • Enter in powerlifting comps. This will be a mini game where you will interact to lift the weight. How much you can lift will also depend on how well you train your avatar. It will be a leader board type competition against the world and your crew. 
  • Hire a Coach/Personal trainer.
  • More specialty gyms to train at: Beach gym, Space gym etc.         
  • Crew battles. Join together as a crew and battle head to head with other crews. 
  • Incorporating real life workouts and diets into GymNation

That’s why every dollar of this funding will be going back into the game, primarily marketing. We have endless ideas for GymNation, it’s just a matter of having the time and money to create them. If we are lucky enough to get more funding then requested, you will see an extended amount of unmentioned features being added.
