Create your alter ego, enter in turn-based battle, team with friends, forge arch-enemies, collect and trade items in this online game.

If you have an affinity for anything comic book related then PLANET FULCRUM is the place for you. 

PLANET FULCRUM is an online game that allows you to create your own unique persona, enter in turn-based battle, team up with friends, forge arch enemies, as well as collect and trade your costumes, weapons and various upgrades with other players.

The overall style remains true to the two-dimensional illustrative comic book format, keeping it appealing to all ages will cater for all fan bases too. Whether you're into caped crusading hero’s, vampires, aliens or elvish warriors. No matter the genre, PLANET FULCRUM will give you the tools to create whoever you want to be.

It will also utilise current html5 technologies, making PLANET FULCRUM accessible with all the popular browser based devices.

Our experienced team of developers and creatives are already on the case and making this a reality. But we want to ensure we produce something truly engaging and diverse, and that’s where you, the Kickstarter community, can help.

The game thrives on each player’s creativity and we will produce a persona creation system that caters for all – if you can imagine it, we want you to be able to create it. This will require a huge team of illustrators, artworkers and designers to put together a wealth of persona skins, outfits and weapons. As well as a wide variety of battle scenes based on your preferred genres.

Your input into this will be vastly more than just financial investment. We encourage you to get involved on the PLANET FULCRUM forums, sign up for beta access and offer suggestions (constructive of course) as to what you would like to see in-game. If you are willing to pledge that little bit extra you get to play a key role in the creativity and design, collaborating with us to bring your ideas to life.

Our target for open beta launch is October 2013 at New York Comic Con (NYCC). We are also giving you the opportunity to join us and help celebrate the launch – check out our pledging options for more details.

Our heartfelt thanks to you,

The PLANET FULCRUM team

PLANET FULCRUM is a living organism whose energy comes from the life-force of its inhabitants. In return for playing host, the energy generated is shared with all, providing potentially unlimited power to the more dominant personas who reside there. 

PLANET FULCRUM calls to any who are in tune with their sense of adventure. Known as ‘the beckoning’, each individual will hear what their sense desires most, inviting them to PLANET FULCRUM to continue the Planet’s mobius of life.

Founding inhabitants

When the founding inhabitants discovered PLANET FULCRUM they soon learnt its potential for distributing unlimited power and opinions were immediately divided. Some wanted to use the Planet for their own megalomania, whilst others were desperate to protect the universe against such a threat. And so the Guardians and Overlords of PLANET FULCRUM were formed.

This story is told in the digital comic PLANET FULCRUM: Founding Divide (available in our pledging options).

Upon arrival at PLANET FULCRUM, you will be asked to choose your alignment and battle for control of the planet. Each of these alignments will have their own unique benefits and pitfalls, so choose wisely.

The Guardian is a protector of PLANET FULCRUM and all its precious resources. 

The Guardians promote a democratic view of PLANET FULCRUM, understanding that a harmonious planet means a harmonious universe. They believe the wealth of PLANET FULCRUM’s resources should be available to all those who share this ideal.

Every Overlord craves total dominance of PLANET FULCRUM and lusts after its power. For them, total control of PLANET FULCRUM is the key to ruling the universe. Overlords simply aren’t the sharing type. Only the strongest of Overlords reap the rewards, leaving little for the rest.

Perhaps heeding the call of PLANET FULCRUM more than others, the Nomad’s thirst for adventure is unparalleled. Without a care for preservation or domination of PLANET FULCRUM, the Nomad has only desire for battle and survival. With no regard for anything or anyone, the Nomad’s victory does not affect PLANET FULCRUM, since they lack any definable morality. They can, however, choose to temporarily side with the Guardians or the Overlords should the opportunity present itself and the rewards are to prove fruitful.

Control of PLANET FULCRUM - Dominance and Morality 

Dominance of PLANET FULCRUM is determined by the transference of power from one inhabitant to another, which can only occur through victory in battle. PLANET FULCRUM senses the victory and transfers power from the loser to the victor. 

Each victory is tallied and the leading faction will control PLANET FULCRUM. 

The moral beliefs of the dominant faction are reflected in the Planet’s landscape and further distribution of power, giving them the opportunity to grow in strength and continue with their domination.

And so goes the struggle for control of PLANET FULCRUM.

CREATE

Players are able to forge their unique persona by defining personality traits, skill attributes and customising their look from a vast resource of outfits and weaponry.

Visually, the persona creation system is largely based on the concept turnarounds used to develop comic book and animated characters, giving the player a real feel for the persona created.

When creating a persona, players will be able to choose their sex, build and faction (Guardian, Overlord or Nomad). They will also be given the opportunity to write their own persona bio and describe the origin of their power, adding even more depth to their created persona.

BATTLE

The battle section of the game is turn-based where you will pit your built up attributes against your opponent (like top trumps). The first player to win 5 rounds of battle will be the victor. The winner will strip the loser of a percentage of the last power used and this will convert to fulcra. In the event that both players reach 4 rounds each, no power is stripped and they are assigned as arch enemies until the next time they meet. When double powers are up for grabs.

Team battles are elimination based and comprise of 5 members each or less. As soon as an individual loses 5 rounds they're out! The team leader will call the strategy. From combination attacks to focussed attacks on powerful individual opponents the action will be thick and fast. With each elimination the stripped power will accumulate until only one team is left standing. The winning team then receives an equal share of accumulated power (converted to fulcra).

The personality traits will also aid you in battle. Carefully combining these with a power stat can make all the difference. For example matching a power with a strong "underhand" personality trait will catch your opponent off-guard... Unless they are more underhand than you! The strategy guide in the pledging options will reveal all the tricks.

The in-game style of the battle is heavily influenced on comic book art and the actual action will be more explosions and fast paced cut-scenes rather than full character animation attacks.

As battles between the two factions take place, the appearance of the planet shifts to represent the overall winner between the Guardians and Overlords.

Whichever faction has majority control over the planet receives benefits for its members such as discounted upgrades and exclusive outfit designs. This control makes resources easier to procure, thus enforcing the narrative that the faction is in current control of the planet.   

Before entering a battle, players will enter a 'battle ready' area where they can equip themselves with consumable items, modify their outfit, arm themselves and confirm they are ready to enter a battle.

Consumable items can provide temporary boosts to persona stats / attributes. However they can only be used once and the effect will only last for one round of battle.

TEAM

Once an Overlord or Guardian reaches a certain level of achievement they are able to create a team, which allows players to train together and fight teams from opposing factions. Players of any level can join teams to increase the social interaction in the game and allow groups of friends to play PLANET FULCRUM together.

The Nomad faction is unable to join or create a team; they have no bonuses from Planet control and no level structures. They can however be offered fulcra in exchange for temporarily siding with either Guardians or Overlords.

Players are allowed to change their faction (defect) once without spending any fulcra, however if the player wishes to change sides again they are required to pay a set fulcra fee. Any time a player changes their faction they are removed from any teams they currently belong to and their persona level of achievement is reset.

TRADE

Players will be able to purchase or sell consumables, outfits and weapons in the ‘trade dock’. All trading is completed in fulcra. Players are able to buy as many items as they like provided they have the fulcra available to make a purchase.

RANKING & REWARD DISTRIBUTION

The ranking and reward structure vary greatly between the Guardians and Overlords, keeping the Planet incentives in line with the respective morality type.
