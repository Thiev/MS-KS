An AMAZING smartphone game where the last person to say "Not iT" has to do the task. Get stuff done while having fun!

Hey Kickstarters! Are you ready for a social game that's a bit more...social? We thought so! Not iT is a smartphone app built on the rules to a game we've ALL played. You remember being a kid, right? An adult would haphazardly hand out out some task to a group of kids, and you'd all start chanting, "not it!" The last person say "not it" was "it." What a simple game! We're taking that concept from social contract to social media.

Why are we doing this? Because life should be more fun and rewarding! Most games are about escaping reality, but Not iT is about enriching reality. How many hours of Farmville and Candy Crush can you play anyways? And at what cost?

1. A Simple Game with a Simple Mission: Get Stuff Done While Having Fun

We're making task delegation fun and engaging. Get in a group with your friends, family or co-workers and start playing Not iT. You can create tasks that help accomplish group objectives, or you can choose from a dynamic list of task suggestions we provide. It's more about fun, creativity and silly embarrassments than real-world productivity. But we ultimately want to let our community make Not iT into the game you want it to be. Use Not iT to get your kids (or roommates) to do chores, or to settle office disputes over who has to clean the break room microwave. With friends, figure out who's going to be the designated driver. The possibilities are endless.

2. Full of Special Features

From tasks suggestions to achievements, there are several layers of game play to enjoy. We're even including an immunity system so that you can get out of being "it" and pin tasks on other players in your group! Volunteer to do a task, and you'll earn immunity. Achievements include titles like, "Biting the Bullet," "Mob Mentality," and "You did iT to Yourself." Did we mention you can take pictures of your completed tasks and post them to Facebook and Twitter?

3. Why We're Raising Funding

Not iT is going to be fun, and we want to include everyone. But, we all have that one friend who won't pay .99 cents for an app. You know, "that guy." He's the same person who can still shell out $5 bucks to grab a Starbucks coffee every morning...but we digress. When Not iT comes out, we want it to be FREE for all to play, so YOU don't have to worry about having to convince your friends to pay for it.

We're currently developing the iPhone version of Not iT. It's expected to be released during this Kickstarter campaign! Funding will help us make future versions of the app even better (read more awesome features). And if we raise enough money, we can bring the Not iT app to other platforms such as Android phones, iPads and other devices. The first $25k raised will go towards the iPhone version and adding new features.

You will, of course, be rewarded heartily for your contributions! We've done our best to come up with great incentives for your part in making all this possible. If you have other ideas of rewards that you'd appreciate, let us know (We'll add it to the list if feasible!). We aim to please! These campaigns are fluid, and we are very community focused! 

4. Speaking of Rewards

We have some interesting options and limited edition merchandise we're giving to our backers. Want a limited edition concept poster signed by the app designer? How about an achievement named in your honor? Check out all of our rewards. We're sure one is bound to entice you.

5. The Fun Starts Now

You don't have to wait until the app is released for the fun to start! Let's all get our creative juices flowing. "Like" or follow us on Facebook or Twitter, and then tag us in a post about a task you want to turn into a game of Not iT! For example:

A Vine or Instagram video of things for which you don't want to be it would be awesome too! Show off your creativity, you wonderfully awesome and talented community!

Risks and challenges Learn about accountability on Kickstarter

Our goal is to launch the app to the Apple App Store during the Kickstarter campaign. Hopefully we won't get rejected, as our team has loads of experience in app design and development. If we do get rejected, we'll fix whatever problems stand in our way of approval and get the app to market as soon as possible!
