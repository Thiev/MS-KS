The augmented reality App allows children to capture Christmas elves in their own home or anywhere they go!

It is a very cool and unique iOS augmented reality App for children!

The game allows children to virtually capture Christmas elves in their own home or anywhere they go!

The more elves children capture, the more points they accumulate and the more games and activities they unlock.

According to my extensive research, this one-of-a-kind project has never been done before. I am extremely proud to be presenting it to you on Kickstarter!

My mission on Christmas Eve is to go to all houses and make sure the kids are asleep before Santa arrives.

Last Christmas, my 7 year old son asked me to catch an elf! I said, "what"? He told me that all his friends at school were talking about catching elves in their own homes, and he wanted to catch one too!

To encourage Christmas spirit, parents were staging different scenarios where elves were being caught in the act of doing something.

My son believed that elves were smart and that a regular trap wouldn't work. He came up with the idea of an Elf Restaurant (Restaurant pour luntins in French). He made a wooden model with his grandfather (see picture below) and set it up in his bedroom.

The following morning, we had ourselves a genuine elf, and one very excited little boy! That Christmas is one I will never forget, as it was the beginning of our family's extraordinary journey with a mischievous elf.Â 

Moved by my son's idea, the elf fad that was hitting homes everywhere, as well as my own creative interests, I began looking for an App to catch elves.

I was both shocked and excited to discover that no such App existed on iOS or Android. There was nothing out there!

I am so lucky to be surrounded and supported by a great team. Artists, developers and writers are working to get this project up and running.
