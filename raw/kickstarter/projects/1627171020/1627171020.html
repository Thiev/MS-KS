Games Studios is an independent game company founded by Craig Walker in 2008 and the studios are locate in New York City. Phantom Games Studio focuses primarily on indie games.

The story is about evil a scientist named Professor Livers Dies who wants to rule the world by turning everyone into a zombie. The reason why Professor Livers Dies wants to rule the world is because he was bullied ever since he was a child.Professor Livers Dies created a device called “Turn Into A Zombie Ray" aka T.I.A.Z.R. Professor Livers Dies did not realize that you cannot control the walking dead. So Professor Livers Dies began to work on the most ingenious zombie control device ever in the mankind history of mankind. He also created another device so the "Zombie Can Recognize Their Leader Device" aka Z.C.R.T.L.D. He hacked the news station and present himself. Professor Livers Dies said I am going to turn everyone into zombies and rule the world and they will be my slaves. Professor Liver Dies don’t realize today was April Fools and everyone started to laugh at him!!! So Professor Livers Dies decided to kidnap a little boy named Mason Disick. He haced the news station again to tell his life story since he was a child. He used the T.A.R.Z and Z.C.R.T.L.D devices on Mason. Mason was turned into a zombie. So Professor Livers Dies said he was going to transform everyone else into a zombie and rule the world. Professor Livers Dies began to use the T.I.A.Z.R and Z.C.R.T.L.D and started to turn people into zombies. Calvin Johnson is a 25 years old police officer and he who is a gamer. He likes plays video games and he’s a momma’s boy. Calvin Johnson is the main Protagonist. He is the only hope can stop Professor Livers Dies.

Zombii Outbreak is a tower defense game. The game has many different levels. Zombii Outbreak levels are strip club, the farm, the light house, prison and many more. Each level tells a story. When you pick any level an intro will tell a story about the level. You must survive endless waves of attacking zombies, earning points from killing or damaging zombies. You kill the zombies by using weapons like freeze grenade, turret, grenade, nuke and many more. You drag the item/s on the screen and the item/s will activate and kill or damage the zombie/s in sight. Zombies enter the player – accessible area of the map via windows, climb out of a “dirt” surface, anywhere on the map; depends on the map, and each map is different. The strip club map the zombies will climb down the pole and starting to attack you.There is no limit to the number of rounds; the game will end when all player/s life bar run out; Zombies become stronger and faster upon the completion of each round, forcing players to make tactical decisions about point spending and progressing through the map. On occasion, zombies will drop power-ups and health such as cheeseburger, double points, first aid kit, hourglass, instant kill, pizza, and many more, making the round easier.
