A “Free to Play" online game in a crazy world full of action, eroticism and chaos with a funny touch.

Inspired by the city of Santander, a beautiful place in the region of Cantabria, northern Spain. Who has not wanted to go in a videogame, the streets where you walk every day!?

A new addition to the popular MMORPG genre, Capital Sander incorporates a city where players can interact with the world. By using an alter ego or avatar, you can choose different roles or professions so as to increase your world ranking through missions or mini-games, which are varied according to the role you play as.

What will you be? Gangster, Policeman, Escort, or perhaps a Politician, Celebrity or Yamakasi? ... or all of them at once!

Policeman:

You will take care to maintain order in the city. On your map you will see the progress of a crime and you will have to go to the area to neutralize it. You will start as a cadet and you can get promoted to the higher ranks , gaining better skills as well as having less to deal with in the city. You can even take some money under the table and be a corrupt cop!

Criminal :

Try to get respect from your enemies , steal money and buy luxuries with ease or fraudulently. Bribe cops , steal objects , extort money ... You'll get to be a real gangster!

Escort:

Win money and prestige attracting clients, attracting more girls for the business. Keep the opposition at bay, get a lot of money and get your club!

Capital Sander is a more complete, current, and adult experience , where anything goes. You can choose to be a good citizen or a neighborhood nightmare . It’s a crazy world full of action , eroticism and chaos, where the player can get everything they want.

An Indie Developing Project, having been self-funded for over 7 months and created entirely by residents of Cantabria and the Basque Country, each of which, specialist in one different area: design, animation, programming, sounds, etc.
