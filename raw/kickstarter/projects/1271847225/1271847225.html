Help fund our future merchandise and get some gamer gear in the process! Alaskan Gamers Unite!

My name is Cameron and I created 907 Gamers in Nov 2013. I wanted to create a free to join community of Alaskan Gamers. 

It all started with registering the domain name www.907gamers.com. A few of my gamer friends and I started inviting our gamer friends to a Facebook group. This group exploded in size. We had no idea how many gamers there are in Alaska! I'm writing this article on 4/04/2014 and we are currently at... 1041 members. We've expanded the group beyond Facebook into different networks. We have a Steam Group, Teamspeak Server, YouTube channel and Twitch.tv Channel. We continue to grow in size daily and the time has come to produce our own gamer gear.

We want to build up the community of gamers and people interested in gaming here in Alaska. This a platform for doing that. A central hub where gamers in Alaska can communicate and share ideas.

This project will allow us to get our members shirts and if we surpass our goal to even start funding our next merchandise project. If we meet our financial goal then we break even and provide shirts for our members at no cost.

If we surpass our goal, the rest of the donations spill over into creating more 907 Gamers gear such as hoodies, stickers, magnets, mouse pads, etc!

This is the beginning step towards growing into something bigger. If 907 Gamers had a budget and had funding beyond my own wallet, I think it could do big things. It's a dream of mine to eventually host the largest LAN the state of Alaska has ever seen.

    There will be several ways to pick up your shirt after this Kickstarter is completed.
