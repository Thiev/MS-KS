The Silver Tree is a standalone prequel to our acclaimed browser role-playing game Fallen London. You do not need to have played Fallen London to enjoy it - The Silver Tree is a self-contained story with its own beginning, choices and endings. We believe in telling mature, powerful stories driven by the choices that you, the player, make.

Its the 13th century, and the Mongol Empire covers half the world. Its capital is the city of Karakorum, at the heart of which stands the silver tree fountain - the envy of nations. But the incendiary relationships between the Khan, his ambitious daughter and a patriotic spy threaten to tear the city apart.

As an emissary to the Great Khans court, you will navigate a web of intrigue, love and betrayal. Choose your cause. Fight for what you believe. Spy. Investigate. Negotiate. Lie. Decide the fate of a city.

The Silver Tree will be designed and written by Yasmeen Khan - writer of many of Fallen Londons most beloved stories, including An Intimate of Devils and What the Thunder Said - and illustrated by Paul Arendt, our magnificent artist.

The Silver Tree is a non-linear RPG you can play in your web browser. Its a modern take on classic text adventure games, using words and images to fuel the biggest special effects budget in the world: your imagination. Well build it using StoryNexus, our own platform for creating story-based games. StoryNexus is a souped-up, tricked-out version of the engine we used to build Fallen London and The Night Circus, both of which have won awards and received accolades for the quality of their writing. If this Kickstarter is successful, we would intend to release The Silver Tree in October 2012.

How do I play?

You have a deck of cards. Each card represents a chunk of story. The contents of your deck are determined by the choices youve made so far. Clicking on a deck fills your hand from it, showing the stories available to you. To give you an idea, heres what Cabinet Noir, another game weve built on the same platform, looks like:

When you play a card from your hand, youre presented with an event and the choices you can make in response. What you choose will shape your character, the world around you, and the direction of your story. Heres how the story interface looks:

The Silver Tree will be a free-to-play game. While the core story can be played by everyone, additional stories can be purchased in-game, allowing you to delve into the worlds deepest secrets and interact with new characters who bring their own agendas and schemes to Karakorum. Our pledge rewards grant discounted access to these extra stories.

We like to tell sophisticated stories that stay with you stories that unfold over time, that you can immerse yourself in. To support that, our games are played sporadically, in short, meaningful chunks. You have a pool of actions, and spend them to draw or play cards. You get an action back every 10 minutes or you can purchase action refreshes if youd rather not wait. Our pledge rewards come bundled with heavily-discounted action refreshes to let you control the pace at which you play.

Failbetter make award-winning online games. We build strong worlds with real choices, not gimmicks. We make challenging, addictive stories which get into your dreams. The Night Circus and Fallen London showcase our storytelling skills. Give them a try or find out about our other projects here.

Items and pets include a story card dedicated to them - so theyre not just for show! With it in your possession you need never fear Death again - waltz from the Boatmans clutches and back to Fallen London in a single action! Are your friends suffering from wounds? The story will be about ten story-cards in length. Youll be able to replay it as many times as you like, trying different approaches. Fallen London stories will include optional branches that let you involve your friends in certain sections, so they can catch glimpses of your exploits!
