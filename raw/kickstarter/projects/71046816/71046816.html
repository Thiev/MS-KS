LIFE IS LIKE A GAME, SO WHY CAN’T A GAME BE LIKE...LIFE?


The Money Shift is a revolutionary game that takes real-life scenarios  and lets you play them out to discover what will happen if you take one path…or the other! Based on the ground-breaking board game “The Money Shift,” this video game version will be made available for your mobile devices so you can play it anywhere.  


WHO IS BEHIND THE MONEY SHIFT

The Money Shift was created by myself, Tom Palka, a Certified Financial Planner with decades of experience. In my career, I've encountered scores of people who are in need of financial education. But, like most things that are serious…learning about it wasn’t a lot of fun. Real life is a lot more serious than a game, and the situations people often found themselves in could have been prevented with some basic education. So I created the game to be a **blast** and still allow people to explore the options that life presents...in a way that doesn’t hurt you in real-life. 

The game is based on the board game of the same name, and was created by myself and produced by a known game company. We want to take the good parts from the game, and make it even better with a video game version you can play on your mobile devices!

86% of students would rather learn about money and gain financial knowledge in a class room than in real life. No wonder! Real life has some nasty consequences…why not simulate real life in an engaging and safe way? That’s where we come in.  


We have written narratives for characters and come up with many interesting scenarios! All of which fit in with eight phases of your life, which we will talk about below in Game Mechanics.

20% of Americans believe that playing the lottery is a practical strategy to get $100,000. That’s a scary thought to leave your future up to chance. Imagine if that 20% had played The Money Shift…they’d know that there are many practical strategies to make $100,000 or more! 


HOW TO PLAY THE GAME (GAME MECHANICS)  


The Game is played in 7 levels of life: Early Life, Teen Years, College/First Job, Young Adulthood, Mature Adulthood, Empty Nester, and Retirement. 

Each level has an infinite number of ways that it can be played. Players start by choosing a character (or having it chosen for you by the game, in a future “stretch goal”), then by shaking their device it rolls the dice. The players are then moved the appropriate number of spaces until they land on their next challenge.  

In each level, you’ll have to answer "Trivia" questions to move on. Questions like “What is the currency of China?” and multiple choice questions such as “Which of the following entities controls the printing of US currency” will need to be answered correctly in order for the player to move on. Players will also encounter “chance” and “life” scenarios and they will be asked to choose a path for the character.

“Chance” positions are random scenarios that can have a positive, or negative effect on your game play. What's going to happen? Well take the chance and find out!

“Life” questions are level-appropriate scenarios in which we present players choices like “Your roommate is a wild-child and likes to party a lot. You are moving in with them at the start of the next month. Should you buy renter’s insurance?”

Answering a set number of trivia questions per level will get you to the next phase of your life—and the next level in the game. These are randomized and players will rarely see the same set of questions and will likely never see the same order…so it’s fun to play multiple times! Want to play as a deadbeat and see what happens? Want to try to end up with the most money? Take the challenge!  


NOT JUST A GAME ABOUT MONEY  


Life isn’t just about money, and that’s a very important part of this game. Players will also have to balance their Health, Fun, Time, Success, and Spirit. (Why “spirit”? Well we all can acknowledge that there is more to life than we can see, so all religions and beliefs are respectfully honored...even not having a belief.  The concept of “spirit” in this game is about reflecting, focusing and finding an understanding and an inner power or strength.)  


Every player starts out with a perfect score and a blank slate…just like life! Through the game-play, the players will lose a certain amount of Money, Health, Fun, Time, Success or Spirit depending on the question’s slant and the scenario that is presented. As a player, you’ll have to make decisions based on what scarcity of resources you have, and the consequences some actions will have on your score.

Occasionally, “Chance” and “Life” questions may replenish your points, so be on the lookout for these. And, we foresee an in-game store where players can purchase more points to continue playing…though that is still to be determined. With these scoring components, it opens up the game to many different ways to play!  


The player who reaches the end of life with the most points in tact wins. Just like in life, actually having money doesn’t make for a happy and fulfilling life. A well-balanced person has to have other elements in their lives to be fulfilled. That’s what The Money Shift aims to replicate. And, through a video game available on your mobile devices it enables you to play wherever you want.  

The Money Shift is the real game of life. It has real-life scenarios, and presents real-life consequences and rewards for the decisions your character makes. Do I need life insurance? Play the game and find out!

You can make a difference by providing a game that teaches everyone how to win the game of their lives!   

We can’t sit back and let another generation succumb to high debts and bad financial decisions. We deserve better! The Money Shift may not be the ultimate solution, but it sure will help. That’s where you come in...
