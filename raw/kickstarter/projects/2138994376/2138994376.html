Treasure hunting through more than a million different mazes to find hidden objects. Avoid your friends zombies as you treasure hunt.

Mazhidden: Avoid your friends zombies as you play through millions of mazes, hidden objects and levels to become a master of Mazhidden.

Find your way through more than a million different mazes to find hidden treasures. Treasure hunt for a list of objects you must find to complete the level. To win you will need to avoid the zombie hordes your friends have sent at all costs.

Do you like playing Hidden Object Games?

Do you want credit for wandering a few thousand Mazes?

Do you posses the knack to solve Puzzle Games?

Do you have good Memory and Concentration?

Do you want to start a Zombie war with your friends?

That is what it is going to take to be a leader in the game of Mazhidden. We have more than a million mazes ready for you to explore. We have a million varieties of Hidden Objects to find. As you advance you may need to solve a few puzzles along the way to the next level. Can you remember where things are that you left behind? You will need to concentrate and focus to find your way to the next levels. What will do you with that zombie your friend sent into your maze? What are you going to do to your friends maze in retaliation?

Mazhidden is a 3D First Person maze game on Facebook that you play in your browser. For those not on Facebook there is also a version that can be played on our server once a user logs in with their persona. One of our stretch goals is to bring the game to mobile with a codeport. We currently have a mobile version working in our development environment, it is not as of yet deployed to the mobile stores.

Mazhidden is a 3D game in your browser on Facebook, the Web and Mobile. It is going to be free to play. Our stretch goal is to bring the game to mobile devicesin 3D! How many games have you seen like that? The game technology is cutting edge and only requires Flash-player that almost all Facebook players will already be familiar with. No other downloads. Just log in to Facebook or the web server to play the game in any browser that has flash player installed.

Mazhidden is a game of fantastic sky views of beautiful scenery. Players with tool access privileges will have the ability to customize the hidden objects in their own game. Designer players will be able to add their own sky box textures to customize their own games. The art savvy Designers can even add their own objects to customize their game. Approved sky boxes and objects will be made available to the community of players. These Designer created mazes; created for their own games can be approved for the community as well. There are all types of mazes including stonewalls, hedges and cornrows as well as custom made ones. Some mazes are outside and some are deep in labyrinths. Some are in out of this world places or on other worlds entirely. Only the custom mazes might become familiar to the hard core players. The in game non custom mazes are all different and no one player will ever play the same maze twice. The maze technology is completely randomized for maximum fun.

After completing each maze the player will gain a level anda coin on their way to becoming a leader of Mazhidden. The player can earn coins by completing mazes or from the Facebook store. When offering coins for purchase we are planning a high use economy where the player is getting a large number of coins for a few dollars. By playing or buying; the coins the player acquires can be used to purchase the power ups from the in game store.

The player will find that a truly challenging game is also a truly rewarding game. A game that is different every time is a challenging game that makes for more fun.

What are the game play aspects of Mazhidden?

Each time you enter a level the game will generate a completely random maze. This maze will be built and populated with hidden objects. The player must wander the maze to find these objects in hidden places. One by one the player journal will detail the object you must find next. If you find something that is not yet on the list the player will need to remember what it was and where it was so they can return to find it. Friends can power up your maze, detriment your maze or they can even send one of several zombie types into your maze.

Zombie Mimic: This is an avatar in the game where you can customize the look of the zombie with your own images and our simple tools. With our tools you will be able to program the functionality of the zombie as it wanders your friends maze. With a Zombie Mimic you can Zombify your friends games for the day or for the week. This Zombie Mimic will auto clean itself up eventually. As a player you may want to avoid the Zombie as it wanders the maze or you maybe the one foolish enough to challenge the Zombie Mimic. Those who approach the Zombie Mimic best be ware. These zombies cast all spells beneficial or not. The Zombie Mimic started as your friend so they may still be mostly helpful to you.

Perma Zombie: This zombie does not auto clean itself up. The Perma Zombie haunts the player until dispelled. The Perma Zombie is slightly more likely to casts harmful spells now that they feel stuck in your game.

Butt Faced Zombie: This Zombie harasses the player until removed. This zombie is just disgusting and a real pain the ass to have on the level. The Butt Faced Zombie only casts harmful spells

Higher level players can command more zombies to send against other friends. You will get updates from your zombie minions in your Facebook posts.

Zombie ups:

  • remove all zombie mimics
  • zombify a friend
  • zombify all friends
  • perma zombify a friend
  • perma zombify all friends
  • perma zombie removal
  • perma zombie secret protector
  • perma zombie apocalypse is to send one not cleanable zombie to one friend all day
  • send a butt faced zombie to a friend
  • send a butt faced zombie to all your friends
  • remove butt faced zombie

  • butt faced zombie apocalypse is to send one not cleanable bfz to one friend all day

Power ups:

  • speed up(level/day/week) - 2X speed
  • clear fog levels(once only) - clears fog densities
  • hot and cold ray(level) - blue/red indicator based on player facing
  • hint with hands(level/day/week) - hand pointing in front of the character
  • pop up objects(once only) pop ups the objects themselves located around the maze
  • pop up text(once only) - pop ups the search strings that you are looking for
  • detriment cleaner(level) - clean all detriments on level
  • swapper(once only) - swap the object looking for with the next one found
  • object finder(once only) - shows a path on the ground to one next object
  • path finder(level) - shows path to all next objects for the remainder of the level

Detriments:

  • back to the start(level) - takes player back to starting tile
  • slow down(level/day) - 1/2 speed
  • fog levels(level/day) - various densities
  • destroyer(level/day) - once found the object is destroyed. no more landmarks
  • mixer(once only) -hidden secrets gets swapped around while your in the middle
  • time warp(once only) - player journal will get reset
  • phase shifter(once only) - selectable objects are moved around
  • barrier of thorns(once only) thorny barrier to prevent access to a part of the maze for a few minutes
  • brain freeze(once only) - player will freeze when seeing the zombie for the first time that level
  • secret detriment anchor(level) - player can not clean detriments

That is a good start with what we are aiming for in the game play. Our stretch goals would allow us to design this out in greater detail from the start of the project. We think this is a good start and there is going to more fun bits for sure.

What are we trying to do with the funding from Kickstarter?

  • Create enough funding to sustain the project in the second half of development.
  • Staff a small team of programmers, artists and marketing free lancers to complete the game launch.
  • Stretch as possible to polish the game play and assets to make a more attractive launch title.
  • Deliver a game that is a new 3D paradigm for browser based hidden object and concentration games.
  • Market the game so that it is virally adopted by a large audience.
  • Stretch the game and technology into the mobile game space.

How will we complete this project with Kickstarter funds?

There are nearly two man years of code and development already completed on the game technology. The programming requirements are in a near ALPHA state withmore thanfifty percent of implementation completed. The next major phase is to finish that code and import more art assets. The game is currently populated by a limited number of low quality hidden objects. The art pipeline has now reached a mature enough state in coding that we can now grow the art and sound assets. The goal is to employ a small team of artists to create and purchase art for the game. We hope to leverage existing art assets that are game ready. These assets will be processed by the modeling and animation contracting to produce game ready assets. These new objects will be quickly added and introduced as content for the game.

We already have the complete working model in place and running. Minimal Mazhidden servers are up and running. These services include a homepage website, advertising, user management, database back end, remote objects and thegame code. The largest portion of the game technology is in place and all systems are working for a Facebook and a Mazhidden website version. With the foundation of the technology demonstrated there are still some big pieces inside the game that need to be implemented and hooked together. The servers can easily be scaled as required for wider user adoption. The game exists on our Mazhidden servers in a playable form about half way to completion. The majority of what is left to do is the hooking up what makes the game full featured as we would like it to be. The game isa fewmonths from BETA in July.

The programmers have yet to finish some game mechanics for playing levels. Many powerups arealready working in the game and the system allows us to easily add more. The Zombie Mimic is in the game with primitive AI stateslike Idle, Wander and Fight.The power up, zombie game play and store portions are next on the radar to improve upon. During this time we are looking to improving the feel of the game as well as the look by bringing more assets into the pipeline. The programmers also need to hook up and implement the UI and functionality for the in game tools as well as the tool systems themselves. Many parts of the UIare implemented including the in game store. Once they have a good user experience we will be near the end of the development cycle. Those details to finishwill include the evaluation for the in game coin system based on BETA player feedback.

The art contracting team needs to acquire and import more art assets in their efforts to polish the look of the game. This also include sound effects, music, textures and 3D models. Importing these higher quality objects will greatly add to the look of the game.

I am looking to offload the work I have been doing onto a few professionals. I will continue to have a major hand in managing and developing the project. My main focus will be on the immense programming left to do to finish the game play and tools. I will utilize outside resources to implement the areas of the project that are more well known implementations and techniques. Possibly in the disciplines of hosting, back end and UI.

We need less than six months to get the game to a BETA state. We are expecting a few months in BETA to iron out the issues. I fully expect the game to go viral and be an overnight success. In the case of a flatter adoption by players our stretch goal for funding the project is to acquire enough resources to see the project through its first two years of development and launch.
