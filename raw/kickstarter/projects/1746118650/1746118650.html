Turn-based artillery shooter game with real-time action elements for PC. Pilot mechanical units and battle against angry alien hordes.

We will be releasing on PC platform through Steam's Greenlight program. Please vote for us when we are there. You can also search Imagiro Studios on Facebook and Youtube for your news update and multimedia.

 IMPORTANT UPDATE:

After long heated discussions and feedbacks from many sources, our team has decided to initially launch the game as a Free to play PvP battle arena rather than a single player campaign. This does not mean that we are tossing out the idea of a story campaign, we are just delaying it until after the PvP release.

The decision has caused a lot of changes in the mechanics as well as design of MODUS. Firstly, we will be focusing on designing a wider selection of MODUS for players in PvP. Secondly, we must balance the current game mechanics to be suitable for PvP play. All this will cause a delay in the release of the story campaign, however, PvP mode will replace the date of release stated on Kickstarter.  

This decision was difficult to make as we must consider the fans that has already pledged to our project as the single player campaign. In such case, all incentives will remain the same and be delivered as such at the stated date with the exception of game delivery date ($10 reward). All future pledges in the $10 reward will grant the backer with the story campaign when released

An unknown alien meteor collided with the earth leaving human civilizations in ruins. The human survivors said that while the meteor somehow dug towards the Earth’s core, the meteors released alien genetic spores into the earth’s atmosphere and water system, eventually mutating the vegetation and animals as well as transforming climate and gravity. 

After the destruction of countless cities, humans built great protective settlements to shield against the polluted environment. Where nationality were used to differentiate people, name of human sanctuaries took its place; Vish-Gil of the desert, Nexkin of the sea, Sylvystana of the jungle, Caldarian of ice. 

A few enlightened human minds banded together to make scientific advancements in areas of energy, engineering, and biology which eventually lead to the creation of a fifth sanctuary - Tioria. 

A specialized project was initiated to reclaim the earth. A team of neurotically enhanced military personnel was formed along with the plan of creating war machines called MODUS. However, before the MODUS were completed, a horde of Excers attacked and destroyed Tioria, forcing the enhanced soldiers to be dispatched to other major sanctuaries for their safety. 

One of the MODUS was secretively completed, and as the story begins, you will play as it's pilot, a young soldier named Svat who was given the mission to complete the project.

MODUS is a skill-shot, artillery game that demands trajectory estimation and quick reaction from the player. We are planning to initially release on PC through Steam's Greenlight program. Some unique features include: 

Projectile parrying system: A core mechanic in MODUS that negates oncoming attacks. This is mechanic is vital to the survival of your MODUS as it is very fragile, so be ready to block the incoming projectiles aimed to destroy you. 

Vertical Gameplay: Jump up and down ledges and cliffs to get an advantageous position and navigate the unique terrain to find new ways of completing a mission and hidden items. 

Final armaments: Each MODUS is unique and have a “super” attack that takes time to charge. Regardless if it is a hyper cannon or lightning storm, you can be sure we will make it flashy and totally worth the wait. 

Play however you want: There are no restrictions on how you should play. Whether you like to rush in guns blazing or crafting strategy and takedowns from afar is completely up to you. How you complete a mission is in your hands.
