Bring one of the most popular MMOs in Asia to the English-speaking world.

Mixmaster Online is one of the most popular PC games in both Korea and Japan. We at Jango Games have been working on a French version of the game since 2010, and we receive requests from gamers every day begging us to make an English version as well. With help from Kickstarter and you, the loyal contributor, we will be able to bring this brilliant MMORPG to an entirely new audience.

Mixmaster online is a free-to-play MMORPG that lets the player create an army of monsters (Hench) at their disposal, and fight alongside them. It's been compared to Pokemon in its deep monster collection system, but rather than sitting back and shouting commands, the player is side-by-side with their monster army in Diablo-style action.

There are over 370 monsters to choose from in Mixmaster Online, each with a different set of skills and characteristics. Assemble your army and find a strategy that suits your play style the best. You could put together a group of fire monsters to leave your enemies in a pile of ash, or you can gather hulking ice Hench to squash everything in your path. All 370+ Hench vary in size, personality and stats, so you can get your army to look and play exactly the way you want them to, the choice is yours.

The customization doesn't end with the ability to pick and choose the Hench you're fighting with. If you want two Hench to combine powers, Mix Master Online features a system that does just that. With the ability to combine the powers of two Hench, players are given unprecedented customization over their playing experience. You can experiment with different combinations in search of the dozens of rare Hench that can be created.

Mix Master Online has a variety of features built around working with other players to attack, train, or even capture enemy monsters. Some enemy Hench are more difficult to catch than others, so you will need some help from your fellow Monster Masters to get them under your control. If you're looking for competition, Mix Master Online features an in-depth, squad vs. squad PvP system.

Mixing Hench together is a complicated process with a long list of variables. But, the basic premise is that certain categories of Hench don't mix with others, while other combinations can create rare, amazing powers.
We believe that Kickstarter is the best way for us to reach out to fans of Mix Master Online, and generate the kind of support we'll need to finish this project. Your donations will be covering the cost of Software licenses, hosting and developing the game itself, and paying our employees to design and translate Mix Master Online for an English-speaking audience. Kickstarter is our way of involving you, the player, in our development process to make sure that you get exactly the game that you want.

Jango Games is an independent video game publisher that was founded in 2008 by Cedric Toue. Our operation is very small at the moment, but we have always pride ourselves on our outstanding ability to offer a great game experience and helpful customer service. Our French version of Mix Master Online was a huge success, and we feel that this is our opportunity to answer the thousands of people asking us to open up an international server for this incredible, non-violent MMORPG.

There is risk in any development project, and this is no different. Our biggest challenge will be working with a new language, which is one that we are addressing by including native English speakers in our development process. The French version of Mix Master Online has been a huge success, and we are certain that we can bring the same level of quality to the international crowd. Bringing this fantastic game to more people would be an absolute dream come true, and your support will help us reach that goal. Thank you for your consideration, and we hope you share our vision and enthusiasm for Mix Master Online.
