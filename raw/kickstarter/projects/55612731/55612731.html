Hundreds of different monsters cover the world. Catch them to build the ultimate army!

Dragon Island is a deep RPG that borrows elements from old favorites like Final Fantasy, Dragon Quest and Pokemon and blends them with brand new gameplay ideas to create a unique iPhone\iPad\iPod touch gaming experience. Enjoy the thrill of hunting and catching hundreds of different monsters, and figuring out how to combine them or grow them into the most fierce fighting force available.

Combine, Capture, Conquer!

What Makes This Game Different?

An in-depth battle system that differs significantly from traditional RPG games in many ways including: 
  • Time instead of Mana. All actions use time as their resource. Stronger attacks can quickly dispatch enemies but leave your character waiting for a long time. Fast abilities have a much higher time to damage ratio.
  • Batting Order. To use a baseball analogy, your monster army can consist of more then 3 monsters (the limit that you can have in battle at one time). Once a monster dies it is replaced by the next monster in your batting order. Batting order size is determined by various factors. Plan an effective battle strategy that matches the enemies you expect to meet.
  • Strength In Numbers. The measure of your army's strength is its lasting power. Better monster armies will make it farther into a dungeon before having to run back to town. Towns completely heal your army but also reset all battles. There are many way-points so you can pick up where you last found one.

The World Map

  • The world map uses a "Points Of Interest" based exploration system. Each flag in the world is a town, tower, battle, forest etc that can be explored. You can find treasures, new monsters and more by being diligent.
  • Towns are laid out on the world map and there is a seem-less zoom in to let you visit the various buildings including shops, inns, the arena, and helpful villagers.

Dungeons

  • Randomly generated. No two visits are the same
  • Full of treasures and special monster battles
  • Our dynamic engine allows anything to be in a dungeon including other dungeons, towns, and more.

Hero

  • Combine monsters together to build new creature types.
  • Gain experience by increase the level of each monster type you have.
  • Equip items that increase the effectiveness of your monsters
  • Unlock larger batting orders by earning more powerful licenses in the arena

So Much To Do

  • Explore across a 3840x2560 world map that takes 16 iPhone screens to display
  • More then a dozen dungeons including an infinite dungeon that can challenge anyone and serves as an online leader board
  • Hundreds of creatures each with its own abilities
  • Both randomly created items and unique items

Why we need your support

Dragon Island is an indie made game. Everything to this point has been funded out of pocket and the game is about 75% complete. We made this game because we love RPGs and we like having them on our mobile device to play wherever we want.

We want to have as many monsters, dungeons, and items as we can at launch, and complement that with great artwork to seal the deal. With your help we can achieve that goal! Kickstarter funding is all or nothing. If we do not reach our goal, the project gets none of the funds raised.

Funding Plan

Depending on how much money is raised beyond the target goal, we will have funding to do the following:

  • Add new monsters, dungeons and items
  • Add more kickstart exclusives
  • Android Version
  • Continue to add new content as free updates after launch
  • Open up our xml based world\dungeon\monster\item data to community use to create either new titles, additions to the current game or possibly a "game builder" app. This is a stretch goal which would require significant success in either the funding or the app revenue itself.
