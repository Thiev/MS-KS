Zoogles is a community driven Facebook game that can be classified as a socially interactive, pet/creature simulator.

=What Zoogles Is And What We Are Doing=
We are creating a Facebook game, that is community based and free to play. The game can be classified as a interactive pet/creature simulator, but is much more than just that. Please watch part two of our video for more information.

=How We Will Use The Kick Starter Funding=
-We will buy development software (For programming, graphical design and music/sound effect creation)
-We will buy computer equipment to develop with (many of our developers are working with very limited equipment)
-We will get a better dedicated web server and domain (that will be able to handle large amounts of traffic)
-We will be compensating our developers for their time invested in Zoogles.
-Extra funding (over our funding goal) will be dedicated to allowing our developers to focus more on making Zoogles even better as well as for creating even more ground breaking Facebook games that will be focused on being social, interactive and user experience concerned.

=What You Can Do To Support Zoogles=
Make a pledge to the project, visit the Zoogles Facebook page, visit the Zoogles promo-site and spread the word, tell friends, family and others about Zoogles via Facebook.

All your interest and help is sincerely appreciated by us.
Thank you,
-The Zoogles Dev Team
Christian, Jason, John, Justin and Shane
