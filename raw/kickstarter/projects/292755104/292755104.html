Tiny Elementals - online multiplayer running game with unique graphics and amazing soundtrack for iOS and Android.

Long version of video:

Long time ago, no one exactly knows where, a group of children were playing the first runner game. The game consisted of old table and scrolls (as they didn't have mobiles or tablets, or any of such fancy stuff). Their father was a mage that used to study ancient knowledge of four elements. The children were playing runner in his laboratory and afterwards left the lab without cleaning it, nor closing the door or the windows.

That night a cat came through the open window and smoothly, with elegance, walked among the vials, potions and mixtures. But when he saw a mouse in a hole, he froze, laid down, raised his ears and prepared to attack. He jumped and accidentally knocked down some vials from the table, which fell down and mixed. The whole room was filled with smoke, light and fire! Suddenly, an explosion threw something away - that were little monsters!

The next day children found the labolatory in a mess, but they also found monsters that were hiding underneath scattered lab equipment. They got to know each other. And the monsters loved racing - they were racing with others for fun, to children's delight.

The game starts here. You are one of the children that have monsters on their disposal, and you are racing with your friends. Your goal is to race, collect coins and get ranking points. After each game your ranking will be updated and you will get coins, with the amount based on your final position.

You will race with other players in a single game. You will not only see them, but you'll be also able to affect them ie. throw something at them to slow them down.  Thus, wining will depend not only how you can avoid obstacles, but also how and when you'll use monster's powers and abilities. If you'll play more, you'll learn more, you'll achieve more and win with others. 

The whole game is divided into four elements: Fire, Water, Earth and Wind. Each character belongs to one of these elements. Red monster from the picture above is from fire element and he knows fire spells. He can throw fireballs, burn, leave flames etc. The blue one is from water world. He can create ice, freeze somone and cast frost nova. Every character has abilitities from his element plus global spells avaible to all. You'll be able to get each monster's abilities within race – you'll just have to collect block with a question mark. You will get one of spells and after that you'll be able to use it by pressing a button.

Gameplay modes

The game will contain two game modes: public and private. To join public game you will have to only press Quick Play button - the system will automatically find oponents with similar ranking. After finishing race your ranking will be updated based on other people rankings and your position. There is a global ranking for all players, continents, countries and friends. You'll be able to see your place in ranking and statistics view.

The second type of game is a private game. Here you'll have two options: normal game and a tournament. In normal game you'll race only once and that's it. In tournament mode you will play few maps (depending on the settings) and after each map you will get points which will sum up. After finishing all maps you will see tournament ranking and winners. This is the best option for playing with your friends.

Chat, friends and rooms

You can create a room and invite your friends and other players to it. As a owner of the room you can create private games and tournaments. All players from the room will get invitiations for a race. If you want to run in tournament with your friend, you only need to create a room, invite and select the game mode.The chat will help communicate with each other and choose maps which you want to play.

Achievements

The game will contain a long list of achievements. For  completing an achievement you will get rewards, which value will be based on achievement difficulty. For the simplest one you will get coins, for more difficult you will get extra stuff for your character, and the hardest one will unlock new monsters and abilities.

Celebrations

You can choose a variety of celebrations for your character. The first one, Warm-Up Celebration, will be played on a countdown to start the race. Your character will ie. do some push-ups or quickly run in place. You'll be able to choose this kind of warm-up in character screen.

The second one, In-Game Celebration, will be available while racing ie. when you leave other players behind, you can laugh evily or something else of your choice. As previously, you'll set up this celebration in character screen.

The two last ones are used after finishing race, where you and your oponents are on the podium - you can play happy or sad celebration. It depends on you which one you will celebrate. The purpose of these two is to show your feelings after the race.

Risks and challenges Learn about accountability on Kickstarter

Multiplayer game confronts software developers with a different challenge than the single player mode. When creating such a game the main problem is the delay in communication between players that results from the distance between them. Fortunately, we can learn from the experience of others and do not have to make the same mistakes. Therefore, in our game we use proven, tested and working solutions that will ensure the proper operation of the game.

Another risk is associated with a large number of players. Imagine that the game will be played by a few million people. For that you need a large number of computers, and the proper created software. Such software is called "scalable" and the idea is that as the number of players inceases, more servers are added that provide smooth gameplay and minimize the waiting time to start. Through our work with banking applications we have the appropriate knowledge and experience on how to design such systems so that the scalability problems did not occur.

However, we mostly fear that the game will not be liked by the players, but we will know this only when the game will see the light of day. Despite the fact that all our friends are very cheerful for us and we have information from them that we are doing a very nice thing which will be a hit, we will always have these fears. Therefore, Kickstarter is the ultimate test. If it can be funded and we get the appropriate support from the players, we will be 100% confident that we are moving in the right direction and that we meet their expectations.
