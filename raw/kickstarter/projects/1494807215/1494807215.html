From Sweden, NRD Studios is proud to bring you Champions of Demah. An RTS Action hybrid set in a rich and fantastic world. Champions of Demah is a multiplayer game, in which you are the Champions.

We have already come so far in making this game and need your help to finish it.

So we at NRD are asking you, Kickstarter to be our Champions and help us make one of the most exciting multiplayer games this year.

Development Diary:

Champions of Demah is an RTS Action Hybrid game set in a fantasy world, which we call Demah. The game will feature lots of multiplayer action, multiple modes of gameplay and hundreds of choices to be made in both character and player customisations.

Throughout the years there have been some attempts to create an RTS Action Hybrid game, but we feel they have all fallen short of the real potential for this genre. Champions of Demah promises to be the first of its kind, designed from the ground up in every aspect to fit this genre perfectly.

The world of Demah is rich in history and story. You play during the Times of Assent, where 2 brothers are vying for power to control the kingdom. Each of the brothers are now asking for champions to lead their armies and bring them victory. You are those Champions.

Champions of Demah hosts a wide range of features and content, and here is a short list of some of the main and exciting ones.

  • Up to 32 Players
  • Multiple Map Modes
  • Interactive Maps
  • Custom Characters
  • 9 Playable Classes
  • Hundreds of Skills and Abilities
  • Player Reputation

Character Classes

Champions of Demah features 9 classes at its current stage. The game features a system of Major Classes, with multiple sub classes that specialise in particular skill sets.

Warrior Class - The Warrior Class focuses on combat, with decent weapons such as throwing axes, swords and small to medium shields.

Berserker - Information coming in Video Update - Berserker
Guardian - Information coming in Video Update - Guardian


Scout Class - The Scout Class focuses on being a skilled hunter from range. The Scout uses short swords, bows and daggers. Scouts rarely have the options for shields.

Assassin - Information coming in Video Update - Assassin

Mage Class - The Mage Class focuses on mastery of the arcane arts. The base mage focuses on a wide range of magic areas, but specialising in none.

Skills & Abilities

All characters in the game have a set of unique abilities and skills at hand to define their style of play. A detailed system of skill combination and customisation will open up for deep strategic gameplay and team tactics.

Character progression will see games shift naturally during the course of play, battles pivoting on abilities becoming more potent and game-changing skills becoming unlocked.

Environments

The levels in Champions of Demah will be dynamic, familiar yet strategically fresh. The resources are randomly generated and never appear in the same place very often.

The levels feature cave systems, hidden passages and areas that can be affected by magic and other worldly objects.

Domains

The Domain system is your place to show off your achievements and character. All players start with a campsite, that features your banner and basic items as well as your character.

Throughout the game, DLC and earning achievements you will have more and more to display in your Domain. Which starts its life as a campsite, but can become a fully fledged castle with NPC characters, duelling arenas and much more.

Orders

Playing online is great, especially when you find a group of buddies to play with. When this happens you can form an Order.

Orders are our version of guilds and clans. Orders can fight in special game modes and even have their own special domain area, unique banners and symbols. When you play Battle of the Orders game mode you fight under your Order's colours and for your Order's glory.

Allegiance

Depending on how you play, will affect your allegiance. You have the good and evil.

More information on this will be released when we get closer to the stretch goal.

Commanders

Commanders play in the RTS view. Building the main base and commanding from the map room inside the fort. They can issue orders to players, create helpful routes and information updates to your team as well as use special skills and abilities.

Commanders can fight and defend themselves quite well, but its best left to your own team and NPC bodyguards to ensure your most important character is never at risk.

Reputation

Everyone loves to show off, and playing online and getting achievements is all well and good. But we got something better.

When you get achievements, you can show them in your domain as different options.

Kill a high level player? Well done, how about displaying a statue of him in your grand hall. Or maybe a head on a spike outside?

More detailed information on this system will be released soon.

When young, the dragon will be small and weak, easy prey for enemy players who climb to the top of the nest.

NRD Studios is a small studio located in Gävle, Sweden. We have a history in multiple industries and the 9 staff that make up our talented studio come from all manner of backgrounds and businesses. Collectively, the studio has over 50+ years of experience and skill in all areas of development.

In 2012 we decided to pool all of our skill and resources into finally realising a long awaited ambition to create a great game studio focused on gameplay, original design and original IP and so Champions of Demah was born.

Risks and challenges Learn about accountability on Kickstarter

Champions of Demah is an ambitious project, with many new design challenges.

Over the past months, we have faced such challenges and worked very hard to overcome this issues. Intense design sessions, analysis and feedback allows us to make better judgements.

With all game projects, comes risk. Champions of Demah is no different, but our passion for games, and this one in particular is unlimited. If you back this project, you not only get to fund a great game, but also a great studio with very talented team. One of the main risks we face is not having the finance needed to produce such a game.

With your support, for Kickstarter and Steam Greenlight and everywhere else across the web for Champions of Demah and NRD Studios, we are sure we will succeed.
