Pirate Software - Tap Adventure

Pirate Software - Tap Adventure is a fast paced action/adventure game, where you complete challenges and win Golden Skulls.  Each time you complete a challenge, you are awarded one Golden Skull.  To successfully progress through the story you must develop good reflexes and hand eye coordination.

Pirate Software was developed to be a fun, easy, pick-up and play sort of game, much like the WarioWare™ games on the Nintendo™ consoles.  You can play Pirate Software for as little or as long as you want.  Thanks to the auto-save feature, you can come back to Pirate Software at a later date and unlock even more challenges, or replay your favourites ones to beat your high score.

The Story

After the lead character Black Beard's town is burnt down by Red Beard's invading navy, he decides to assemble a group of angry townsmen to become Pirates, and plunder Gold from Red Beard to rebuild his town. 

The only "small" problem with Black Beard's plan...  Black Beard and his fellow ship-mates know nothing about Piracy!  Before Black Beard and his crew can become Pirates, they must first complete challenges set by The Queen of England and recover Golden Skulls scattered throughout the Caribbean.

Along the way, Red Beard and his Navy of Goons try to stop you from recovering skulls.  Before you plunder Red Beard's Gold and restore peace to your town, you must first prove you have the skills to be a real Pirate.

Playable Alpha Version

We have self-published playable but yet unfinished Alpha Versions of Pirate Software for several platforms (iOS, Android, Blackberry, PC, Mac and Web).  Currently, there are 8 playable challenges.  We released the Alpha with the hopes we would be able to do necessary fixes, graphical improvements, and add extra content along the way.  However, Pirate Software is going to require a lot of hard work and our full attention if its to be completed and polished to the standard we believe this game deserves.

Videos of Pirate Software (Alpha Version)

Our Goals

When Pirate Software is complete, it will have over 70+ playable challenges and hidden surprises.  We're always thinking of new ideas and exciting ways to engage through story telling and interaction.  To date, the PC Alpha version alone of Pirate Software has received over 100,000 downloads.  We need the money so we can dedicate our full attention in to developing the complete, polished version of Pirate Software.  We're aiming to create an exciting, colourful, fun, easy to control game.  A game where players can immerse themselves for 5 minutes or even hours and still have fun!

Risks and challenges Learn about accountability on Kickstarter

We'll be working on Pirate Software full-time to finish it. Our time will be spent developing the finest bug-free software we can produce, creating smooth animation and effects, and composing interesting melodies.

The biggest challenges facing Pirate Software are our multi-platform ambitions. Because Pirate Software - Tap Adventure is available across 6 platforms, we will release across each platform progressively. Our first release goal is to target the major platforms; these include: PC, Mac, Android and iOS. Shortly after, we will then release on Blackberry and Web.

With your support we can make Pirate Software - Tap Adventure in to an incredibly fun and challenging game for all to play!
