Historical Figures transformed into comic Superheroes and Villains YOU collect and battle with in story and multiplayer game modes!

History Fighter is an unexpected game about time-traveling, super-powered historical figures - fighting for the fate of the world! Inspired by a mix of comics, card games, science fiction, and historical fact, History Fighter is both a parody and celebration of all these things.

History Fighter is being built for iOS and Android. The first release will be for the iPad and we will optimize builds for other platforms one at a time, as quickly as possible from there. YES we plan an OUYA release!

See below for How to Play

Stuff we love: Bill & Ted, The Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy, Magic: The Gathering, Pokemon, Star Trek, Doctor Who, Stargate, The Matrix, MIB, Superfriends, South Park 

We are an independent team  - a mix of experienced game developers coming from companies including Midway and Ubisoft, working side by side with fresh new talent. The team has been working in spare time, and funding the bulk of this thing from our own pockets for a full year.

We have a working core game - and the talent is in place to execute on a production plan to finish History Fighter in about 8 weeks.

We want you to be part of it - to finish strong we need to focus as a team for the final push. Your pledge will help us to (1) finish and polish the game with your input, (2) control our own destiny (not sell it off) and (3) maintain the game properly post-launch.

But enough about us. Let's look at the game!

STORY FACTIONS

1. Order, Incorporated

2. The Time Defense League

3. Free Range Heroes (the non-faction faction)

History Fighter fuses meaningful card game game mechanics with a hilarious, choice-driven graphic adventure story! "Season 01" is written as 12 Episodes (story-driven mission packs) with room for some details yet to be decided, with YOU!

Yes, you will probably learn something while playing History Fighter. While History Fighter is not trying to be "educational", there is real-world knowledge fused to the DNA of this super fun universe. We keep the focus on the fun, while infusing lesser-known facts about everyone's favorite historical figures.

For example, did you know that Cleopatra took beauty baths in donkey milk? Or that Lincoln, the tallest-ever president, carried letters and papers in his big hat? Neat, huh!

How to Play The object is to reduce an enemy Agent or Hero health points (HP) to zero. All cards have an Action Point (AP) cost to play, indicated by the number in the top-right corner of the card - inside the blue sphere. AP is refreshed each round and can be boosted by items and/or abilities. Your available AP is indicated on the Agent Avatar card. Cards are played by sliding them from one’s hand into an open slot. Cards placed Attack (ATK) according to their stats and abilities. Cards placed Defend (DEF) according to their stats and abilities. When a card’s ATK value is greater than its target’s DEF value, damage is dealt. If no card is present in a targeted slot, damage is dealt to the enemy Agent or Hero. Moving a card onto an occupied slot replaces that card. Moving a card from one’s hand into the discard slot gives 1 AP. Playing a Use Item card opens the Agent Briefcase, where any items the agent has placed inside become selectable for use with instant effect.

The only dependencies in production we really have at this point are on our partners to follow through for hosting and networking multiplayer, which we feel good about.

We know the trails. Most of us have seen many cycles of game production so this is a straight forward situation:

We have a working prototype with a fun core game.
We have all the talent in place to realize our design.
We need only the space and time to get it all done.

You are funding a modest living wage for the real people making this game to produce the remaining designs, art, and animations needed for us to execute on our original vision and finish the game we have started. Your pledge also ensures we are able to maintain History Fighter properly (bug-fix and add stuff that you want) post-launch.

If you have specific questions about any of this, please don't hesitate to reach out and make contact with us!
