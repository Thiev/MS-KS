Â Â Â Â Â Â  In 1990, a geological survey found an underground lake on the outskirts of what was formerly Alaska's frozen tundra. Less than a year later, scientists, with the help of thousand ton drills, reached the lake and found extraordinary life forms from millions of years ago that had survived under the ground up until that moment. Little did they know, there was one life form not visible to the human eye. A parasite that invades the mind of every human it comes into contact with. Only one week after contact, newspapers started reporting a highly contagious illness that manifested itself in only one symptom. Insanity. Within the next month it had affected most of the world, transforming 98% of the population. We called them breakers. Though they could speak, plead and cry, we knew there was no human left. Just parasite... After a year, the breakers began mutating. They got stronger, faster, smarter... A whole new species sprang into existence. It was their species vs. ours. The human spirit lived on in the handful of survivors, in spite of the breakers control of the world -- scouring the ruins, hiding in the shadows, only hoping now, for god knows what. In order to prevail, you must face your fears. Fight for survival in a world overrun with monsters. The fate of the human race is in your hands.

Â Â Â Â Â Â  Forget everything you know about the survival horror genre and dive in to a new adrenaline-pumping nightmare. Explore a vast, open world and allow yourself to be captured by a setting filled with both beauty and dismay. Fight against foes that were once your neighbors, friends and family, but now thrive as a new existence -- human behavior severed by a neural parasite with only one goal -- to expand.

Â Â Â Â Â Â  Neural Break is a first person, survival horror taking place in an immersive, open world and set to deliver a new experience for the PC gamer. It will combine a realistic environment and atmosphere with the action packed, gun slinging, melee beat down you desire. Lose yourself in dilapidated urban environments, high-rises in every direction changing the skyline into a desolate parody of what it once was. Stand atop mountainous terrains overlooking enormous valleys and rolling hills. Hunt, or be hunted by wildlife, the only uninfected beings left on the planet. Most importantly, defend yourself from the ravenous horde and other corrupted throughout this cruel world.

Game features / Character Mechanics

In your face action -- feel the animation while you rip through your enemies... or they rip through you. First person only and expect to have all of the features of a current gen FPS.

  • Jump, Crouch, Prone, Lean, Aim down sight
  • Sprint, Walk, Swim
  • Vault, Pull up to ledges, clear fences and other obstacles
  • Downed State and be rescued from downed state

Choose your poison

  • Rifles
  • Handguns
  • Shotguns
  • One-Handed & Two-Handed melee
  • Environmental
  • Vehicles

Â Â Â Â Â Â  We will start with a map size of 100 square kilometers with plans to expand this terrain over time. All of our objects are modeled at a 1:1 scale. Our buildings are created with actual blueprints as often as possible. With the world scaled in this manner, it will actually feel like 100 square kilometers. The next map expansions are not planned to be separate servers from the first original world, but expanded areas of our original map. So, come patch time, you get into the action the same way you always have, but now there may be a whole new expanse of land to explore. Additionally, we will have walk through full scale indoor scenes such as shopping malls or supermarkets, police stations or fire houses.

Â Â Â Â Â Â Â  Multiple environments are planned. We are not holding a single actual terrain in mind. Each will have a look and feel different from the others. Our cities will feel greatly different than walking through a swamp. With rain, storms, and fog all spread uniquely through our environments, your experience will be greatly enhanced. Expect even more diversity with the additional map zones as they are released.

Â Â Â Â Â Â Â Â  You need to find supplies to survive. Use any means necessary to gather what you need. There are still many intact goods out there, but its the stuff that may seem like useless junk that will really pay off later...

Â Â Â Â Â Â Â  Scavenging is another way to equip your survivor. Combine items for something greater, mix herbs for heals and cures. Crafting is a required discipline. It is not something you will have to level up to be good at. If you have the tools and items necessary to craft items then you can craft them, finding the right combination is the key. Sometimes the best way to obtain rare items is to craft a homemade version from items that are easier to find. Imagine a world of looters, living off scavenging. Backpacks would be scarce after a short time, so it would be easier to craft a bag to hold your things than hope to find a backpack.
