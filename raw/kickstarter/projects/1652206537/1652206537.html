3 Dimensions to a 3rd WORLD Mission...

3rd World is an isometric 3D turn-based combat game, focusing on small squad tactics. In 3rd World, players take the role of the leader of a Mercenary Unity based in the Sprawl, the haggard, run down cyberpunk metropolis that may very well be the last bastion of mankind. Hired guns for corporations, the ruling elite of the Sprawl, the mercenary units act as security, shock troops, thieves or assassins â€“ whatever the corporations need doing "off the books", the mercenaries provide. As a new squad in the sprawl, players start with next to nothing, just their home base and the 3 units that form the core of the squad, but as players complete missions they gain fame, money and power. Gain enough power and security clearances can change, granting a squad the ability to control territory of its own and to hire more members or use better weapons.Â Â
