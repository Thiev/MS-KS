A 2D twitch puzzle shooter with a retro feel and a hard sci-fi plot. Can you defeat the Metacell and save the universe from destruction

Greetings internet! Here are the essential, need-to-know points about Metacell:

  • Metacell sort of already exists! It’s currently available in a light beta stage for the Windows platform only, and we plan to release it on Mac, Linux, web, tablet, and X-Box platforms as well. If we succeed by 300%, this will happen sooner rather than later.
  • Think of Metacell as a majestic, genetically enhanced octopus. Right now it can squirt ink and kinda swim around, but so far we’ve only been able to build it one tentacle. It’s not ready to swim around in the big ol’ ocean and catch little fishies with its handicap, looking like a grapefruit with one arm. We want to give our octopus a better quality of life; it needs more tentacles! We want to fix up Metacell with all of its tentacles and make it the best game ever. With your help, we’re going to do this by adding a ton of content: different game modes, selectable player characters, multiplayer capabilities, technology linking profiles and the mutable story throughout the game saga, and updating the engine to allow multiplatform porting to all those platforms mentioned in the point above.
  • The goal of this kickstarter is to raise the minimum amount of money it will take to 1) complete Metacell, the first game in the Metacell Saga, 2) fulfill rewards, and 3) deliver on the promises we are making within a reasonable length of time. The money will be used to turn the current beta (our jacked up octopus) into the game it is destined to become (a beautiful fully-fledged octopus with all of its appendages) by paying for the software, hardware, and talent to make that happen. 
  • Metacell is the first of a 5-game saga in which each game has a different gameplay experience corresponding to its place in the story. While Metacell is a 2-D twitch puzzle shooter, Metacell II: Beyond the Gate will be a 2.5-D shooter RPG. Metacell III: Adrift will be a 2.5-D puzzle RPG, Metacell IV: Ascension will be a 3-D action-adventure platformer RPG, and Metacell V: Origins will be a 3-D survival horror FPS RPG. The complexity of each consecutive Metacell game will increase throughout the saga. So supporting this game will lead to a whole pile of other, increasingly intricate, awesome games.
  • The other goal of this Kickstarter is getting the word out. We want to get people excited about the game, the Saga that the game will begin, and the universe that the story takes place in. We’re not just making a game; we’re telling a story and building an immersive universe just for you.

We have a lot of content for you to dig into, so check it out! If you’ve got questions and you can’t find the answers here, shoot us a question! There are also additional resources beyond this kickstarter campaign where you can discover more: the website link, link to dl the game manual and the beta itself.

What is Metacell?

Short Explanation:

Metacell, the first installment in an epic five-game saga, is an immersive 2D twitch puzzle shooter with a hard sci-fi plot. Each game in the saga has its own distinctive gameplay tailored to its position in the story arc. Our galactic hero and his crew fight to save the universe from devastation!

Story Explanation:

Metacell is set in an alternate reality in the year 2272. This reality is comprised of two sides: the primeverse, where existence is structured and logical, and the metaverse, where things simply are with no rhyme or reason. High Paladin Dominic Moore and his crew’s leave is cut short when he is called to answer a distress signal from Zion, a top secret research facility located within the metaverse. The distress call warns that the Genesis Project, a recent experiment that attempted to synthesize a hybrid creature with both meta- and prime-organic material, has gone horribly wrong. Moore must plunge his ship and his crew straight into the heart of danger to contain this creature, the Metacell, and prevent it from crossing over into the primeverse and tearing it to shreds.

What is the Metacell?

The Metacell is a creature created as a result of the Genesis Project that combines the unlimited potential of the unpredictable metaverse with the stability and constancy of primeverse organics. It was born a single-celled organism, but something went wrong and allowed it to breach its containment and expand in size at an alarming rate. Now, astronomically large and comprised of both prime- and meta-material, the Metacell is the equivalent of a god. As it nears maturity and the beginning of its reproductive cycle, the Metacell and its potential offspring pose a threat to the very existence of the primeverse.

What is the Judgment-Class Cruiser?

The Imperium's most powerful warships, Judgment-Class Cruisers are hand-designed and custom-tailored to their Paladins. Before space travel or even the age of machines, when the order of Paladins was just begun by Charlamagne, Paladins were always accompanied by their faithful steeds. Now, thousands of years into the future, this tradition has been carried through  the centuries to the current Paladins whose "steeds" have become the safest, smartest, most powerful mode of transportation in the empire. When a JCC is commissioned, the Paladin devotes 5 years to the process of hand-designing and building the JCC. During this time they are introduced to the navigator and soul of their ship, a technophile Spirit-born who integrates with the ship itself as well as forming a permanent empathic connection to the Paladin.

What's the current beta gameplay like?

Metacell is a twitch puzzle shooter, so it incorporates puzzles, traps, and shooting. The game is in the match-three genre, so players must make matches of three or more genes of the same color to remove them from the Metacell. In order to make these matches, players circumnavigate around the Metacell shooting randomly generated genes from their ship.

Core Gameplay Elements

  • Movement: the player's ship, the Judgement-Class Cruiser, is rotates both clockwise and counter-clockwise around the Metacell. This gives the player a full 360 degree range around the Metacell.
  • Ramping Difficulty: Every 10 matches increases the level of the Metacell. As the Metacell increases in level so does, the rate of gene-spawning increases and the chance of triggering a trap springing to life the Metacell's defenses.
  • Matching: in order to fight back the genes, the player must make matches of 3 or more like colors.
  • Score Multiplier: when you make matches quickly one after another you increase your score with a stacking multiplier.
  • Orphaning: when a gene loses connection with the center of the Metacell, the gene withers and dies.
  • Danger Zone: as the Metacell generates genes and the player fires genes into the Metacell to make matches, there is a risk of the Metacell filling up. Once that happens, the player will die if they can't make a match and clear enough space to keep battling the genes.
  • End Scenarios: The game ends if you run out of lives, score +1,000,000 points or beat level 99.

This isn’t your Mom’s game of Bejeweled though; we've added in some bonuses and challenges as you progress through the game.

Some of these bonuses include:

  • Bombs: destroy genes and projectiles from traps, the players gains an additional bomb every 10 levels.
  • Pick-ups [Xenomorphic]: shift color based on the color of the target reticle.
  • Pick-ups [Eugenic]: remove all genes of the same color when matched.
  • Pick-ups [Catalytic]: detonate after being matched, removing all genes and projectiles within the explosive radius.

Traps have the possibility of being triggered each time a player shoots an gene into the Metacell:

  • Spikes: projectiles that temporarily pin players to a random section of the board.
  • Orbs: shoot in random directions and must be avoided by the player.
  • Beams: lasers that the player must avoid.
  • Tentacles: tentacles attempt to grab the player and destroy them.

What are you planning on adding after the Kickstarter succeeds?

The current beta that everyone has access to is just the beginning! This Kickstarter will generate even cooler elements to the game. We covered these additions in the video, but here they are again in a fancy, expensive-looking list!

  • Selectable player characters, each with their own unique gameplay abilities.
  • Casual game mode that gives a relaxing twist to the gameplay as well as more relaxing space music to fit the tone.
  • Challenge mode that provides numerous single and multiplayer challenges to test and enhance the player's skills.
  • Multiplayer capabilities where up to 4 players can either compete against each other in several different battle modes or competitive challenge modes, or they can assist each other through the story mode or co-op challenges.
  • Updated technology allowing for smaller installers and better back-end tech to enhance the player's overall experience.
  • Expanded space metal and space music soundtrack.
  • Updated and additional movies to enhance the plot elements.
  • Linking profiles that will carry throughout the saga, allowing the player to shape their Metacell experience based on how well they do throughout each of the games within the Metacell Saga.
  • Mutable story mode that has many different storytelling paths, placing the story direction in the player's hands. What a player does within story mode will carry over within their profile to each consecutive story mode in the saga.
  • Updated engine to allow multiplatform porting to mac, linux, web, tablet, and xbox.

What makes Metacell a unique snowflake?

Worldbuilding! The world of the game is incredibly detailed. We’ve worked on this universe over the course of 8 years, so to say that it’s a fully-realized world is an understatement.

Gameplay! We’ve combined the classic simplicity of match-three games with challenging additions that transform the genre. And plan to add even more twist to the current beta.

Awesomeness! The game has a fantastic plot that works with our chosen game genre. The story is richer for the gameplay, and vice versa. It’s addictive! The speed of the game, combined with the story, is incredibly engaging for both casual and constant gamers.

Epic Space Metal! When you first listen to our completely original, completely awesome soundtrack for Metacell by the most excellent and talented Space Vikings, it may actually be physically painful for you to stop listening. It's that good.

Risks and challenges Learn about accountability on Kickstarter

So games are hard to make. Sometimes, when we are crying in the shower, we like to think about these things:

1) Have you ever seen an artist talk to a coder?

2) Adobe crashes more times then the Russians did in the space race.

3) We make everything from scratch, like Betty Crocker. Cooking was her only job because starting from scratch is REALLY difficult and time consuming.

4) What if our office catches fire and everything we’ve ever created is destroyed?

5) If this campaign crashes and burns, our self-confidence will drop into the negatives and we will all have to live in a cave and eat mushrooms forever.

6) Car crashes are 88.6% more likely when you’re sleep deprived from designing a game (statistics may or may not be based on wild conjecture).

7) Someone could snatch our baby game from its cradle, make off with it, and raise it as their own!

8) We could drown in the ever-growing sea of indie game developers and no one will even know we exist. Is anyone even reading this? Hello?!
