Using our partners at Activideo and their access to studio and talent we hope to produce a weekly news program and a month game program (the later will be Shillings to view).Â  Here is a quick taster of the weekly news.

Skills are important in EXISTENCE.Â  Each day you will be allocated skill points that you can assign to any skill you have learned.Â  You learn a skill by connecting a Skill Book to your Implant.

When you connect you get that skill at level 1, that means instantly you can use it!Â  But at level 1 your skill in using it is poor.

Take refining as an example, at level 1 you lose about 50% of the elements to waste, while at level 10 you lose nothing.Â  Weapons are similar, at level 1 a pistol will have a spread of, example, 10 degree's, that means the further you are away the hard it will be to hit someone, while at level 10 the spread can be 0.1 degree, so you should hit what your aiming at!Â  A final example is driving a van, at level 1 you will consume lots of fuel and find it hard to steer and brake, at level 10 it should be simple to drive.Â  However you may find at level 2 your happy with your own "finger" skills so don't need to train it any higher.

You gain skill points every day you log in.Â  You will be allocated a number of skill points that you can assign to different skills.Â  Some skills won't take that many points to get to level 10, some will take lots!

The in game currency is called Creds.Â  Creds are not real, you can't touch them, they are connected to the Tarian's mainframe.Â  They are used in game for trade, rewards and expenses.

There is a second currency, Shillings.Â  These are pieces of a rare Tarian elements.Â  You can purchase Creds for Shillings, but only if another player wants to trade their Shillings for Creds, at an exchange rate set by the player (all controlled by the market system buy/sell values).Â  Shillings can be used to purchase Skill Points but only a limited amount per day (50% extra to your daily amount of SP)

Shillings can be purchased by real world money in the EXISTENCE store.Â  But you can also use Shillings to purchase real world objects, Clothing, Games, Art Work, etc. You can also use shillings for cosmetic items in game.

There is no subscription, its free to play. You just purchase an account. Then its up to you if you want Shillings.Â
