A no holds barred rugby / american football sports game for the PC and Xbox 360 - with brutality and dismemberment!

Latest Video

www.bloodsportgame.co.uk

An PC and Xbox 360 Indie game which I am creating single-handedly. The aim of this Kick Starter project is to make a fast paced, futuristic, intense, brutal game and fun to play sports game with realistic graphics. The action will be fast paced and not for the faint hearted! 

The aim of Blood Sport is to win the match by scoring the most goals, or being the last team standing. Collect the ball and try and run past the opposing team, trying to avoid flying tackles and punches. Once you have the ball, you can throw to a teammate or kick the ball high into the air to clear it away from danger.

To score a goal, the player must throw or kick the ball into the opposition goal. The grass pitch is contained within netted barriers meaning the ball can't escape the play area, keeping the action intense and minimizing game stoppages. During the match, random power-ups will appear on the ground. These power-ups may increase a players speed, improve the players strength or give the player protection from attacks. The pitch will also have randomised weapons, for instance a sword might be thrown from the crowd into the play area.

The game is played over two five minute half's, with the length of each half is customisable in the options. Each team has one goalkeeper which will help guard the goal, although these goalkeepers can also be knocked out!

If funded, I expect this game to take about eight months, so it will be ready for a November 2013 release.

Three months are dedicated to creating all art assets for the game, improving 3D player models and textures, creating 3D models for the stadiums and all other graphics and textures that are needed.

One month is dedicated to programming game flow such as scoring and power up effects. Game modes, such as the tournament and player editor will then be added. Also, saving and loading of the options / player names needs to be implemented.

One month will be dedicated to the games AI ,creating a good path finder method so the opponents actively seek the ball and position awareness. I also wish to implement an advanced thought process for the AI; so if there are only a few minutes of game time left and only 1 player remaining the CPU will attempt to punch the head of that player rather than go for a goal.

Two months are set aside for sound effect and music creation. I want to create a moody atmosphere and hope to show this within the sounds.

The final month will be spent tidying, fixing bugs and bringing everything together.

Inspired by the classic Amiga game Brutal Sports Football (seen below), and also other games such as Speedball and the NFL Street series. I aim to combine the best bits from these games to create a unique 3D sports game for the Xbox Live Indie Games service. 

I've spent around a month so far on the game, creating some underlying game code and basic animation. Most of the player controls are completed, the players are controllable on the Xbox pad and the selection of the current player is easily changed. I'm currently implementing the rugby ball behavior along with the beginnings of the attack logic (such as punching). I've also added collision detection which allows me to begin adding game logic. 

If this Kick Starter project is successful and there is an audience which would like the see the completion of the game, I will create a new blog website and post regular screenshots and videos from the game as development evolves.

Random weather effects which modify the game-play (a rainy pitch means slower players and randomised ball movement). 

Weapons can be picked up from the pitch and used against the other team - a sure-fire way to put the odds back in your favor.

Headless fun - punch an opponent enough throughout the match and you just might see him lose his head, literally!

Beast of Friends - Compete against your friends in offline multi-player modes.

Power Ups - Grab power ups from the ground to improve your chances of survival. Power ups can be toggled on or off in the options.

Player editor - Edit the names of all players in the game

Goal celebrations - Control your player after you score

Season - Take your team to the top of the 'Blood Sport' league! Compete against the strongest and fastest teams across 3 leagues to be crowned champion. With unlockable rewards and weapons as you progress the action never gets old.
