About the Game

It is 1995 in Tanestown, a thriving city located where mountains meet the ocean. In this beautiful place the city has grown and attracted big companies and industries to go there. It has become a place where many people come to look for a new live or to add a new perspective to their lives.

Sitting at the back in a hardly illuminated room, a basement area which has been converted to a Jazz club and piano bar, a middle aged, well-dressed man combs through his hair, deeply buried in his thoughts. The club is almost empty, a rusty old jukebox near the back wall plays jazz tunes from the golden age of the 60's and the fuming Cuban cigar sitting on top of the ashtray on the table in front of Daniel adds even more to the misty feeling that already exists in that place. Before Daniel's eyes lie 5 Polaroid photos of 5 very different people. A note is written below every picture denoting some kind of a strong ability: Muscles, Brains, Computers, Explosives and Athletics.

Daniel, a retired white collar thief, has found his 5 pupils to train and to bring them to be the world's best team of thieves. He knows that he has set himself a big goal and that it will not be that easy. Nonetheless a smile appears on his face as he looks up into the dim light above his table and loses his thoughts in even deeper visions.

The Team

Aylin McNolan (26) has just arrived in Tanestown from Ireland. She has made a very hard decision to leave her family and friends but supposed connections to the IRA made her move unavoidable . Aylin has arrived in Tanestown's harbor in a large container ship, where she accepted a bad paid job as a ship electrician. After 6 months cruising through the world's ocean she finally set foot in Tanestwon where she placed another job application near a crappy harbor dinner, not knowing that one man has already caught sight of her. Aylin's skills in logic, electricity and lock picking make her very valuable for the team.

Linus Wells (22) has enrolled in the Computer Science Curriculum at Tanestown Institute of Technology. Though he is not very communicative, those close to him know that he holds the record of being the youngest hacker with only 12 years of age. Linus certainly knows his way around with computers, networks and encoding/decoding.

Milosh Nikov (48) is a Russian immigrant who has been spending most of his live in a shabby small garage where he tries to keep his head above water by repairing cars to ridiculous low prices. Being a strong fellow he is always available for rough things.

Kaito Yamamoto (42) of Japanese origin works in China Town where he owns a smallfireworkmarket stand. He is the hyperactive one, loves to play with fire and shutters anything he gets hold of.

Jared Warwick (33) the hot blooded Jamaican has been working part time as a post officer. He knows the city better than anybody else as his biggest hobby is parkouring wherever possible. Jared is the most agile one, runs fast, climbs up walls and leaps over obstacles as if they were not there.

Game Mechanics

You will start with Aylin after she has arrived in Tanestown and through Daniel your thief master, you get to know the other playable characters as well. The the first 4 missions are prepared for you with already assigned characters and gadgets. There you will learn how a mission is planned and how to make use of the different abilities the protagonists bring with them.The characters are set up that you have Aylin and Linus for the quiet stealth based gameplay, Milosh and Kaito for the harder action route and Jared for that in between. In a mission you will need to control all the team members, therfore we do have missions planned where you get to play with one or more specific characters or to put a team together freely with the possibility that multiple different team combinations can be used to reach a mission's target.But before you actually execute a job, you might need to investigate, take pictures, prepare gadgets or talk to people. Only then you can be certain that you can complete a mission successfully.

About Dream-Design Games

We are astart-upgame developer located in Lucerne, Switzerland. The team consists of 2 developers, 2 graphic artists, one sound engineer and one freelance story and mission writer. All of them graduated from Swiss universities and received a Bachelor Degree in either Computer Science, Game Design, Illustration, Music or Philosophy. Our team's passion coupled with the knowledge every team member has, enables us to create our games with the highest quality in mind.

Where we are and why we need you

We finished the ground work, the main characters are implemented, the city environment has been set up to be fully procedural (see below) and we already have the first 3 missions completed.

With your help we can expand our mission set to reach an estimated target duration of entertainment of about 5-6 hours. This consists of about 12 different missions including the possible additional play-throughs of the missions that allow alternate team & gadget combinations. Additional time of entertainment can be won through minigames of the open world setup. The amount of entertainment is tightly coupled with our stretch goals as well.

Please note that we prefer estimating the time of entertainment rather than a precise number of missions. This gives you the advantage to see directly what you will get from our product and how this reflects in the price we ask for a copy of the game. The time estimation is based on a normal play through: listening to the conversation, watching cut scenes, planning the mission and playing the mission in a normal speed.

Procedural, what does it mean?

Yes, the whole city is procedural, which means we can alter the look of a street or building by simply changing parameters. Most of the mission elements are parametric as well: Interactable objects, conversations, mission targets and the AI can be modified without touching any code.

In a first step we would like to open the city to a community. Those who want can contribute to the game by creating different kinds of buildings and enriching the environment. This can take off work from our shoulders and we can focus more on the main gameplay. And for you it is fun to be a part of creating a place.

As a second target, we would like to give you a mission editor which will let you create custom missions which then can be shared with the community.

What your money is being used for

Your money guarantees us that we can continue working on this awesome game. It helps us to acquire the necessary additional team members and lets us create the target product we are aiming at within a reasonabletime frame. Furthermore does it allow us to continue without the need to look for a publisher that limits us in our doing.

Because only a large enough crowd can make this campaign a success weautomatically get a large community that enables us to fit the game to your requirements and your vision.
