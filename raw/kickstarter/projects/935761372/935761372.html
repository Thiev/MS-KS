My name's Tanner! I have Autism and I love gaming! I want to go to game design camp and then make an FPS game funded by Kickstarter!

Hello everyone. My husband and I are submitting this for our son Tanner, who will be 15 next month, and desperately wants to go to a week long camp  at Arizona State University in Tempe, AZ (put on by Internal Drive™) this summer to learn how to make FPS (First Person Shooter) games.

The Game

The title of the game is "Insomniac: The Madness Within" and the tagline is, "Embrace the Madness."

You are a retired member of the USMC Force Reconnaissance .

The United States has made more enemies as years have gone on, as it has separated itself from the rest of the world and no longer sending any military aid or money to any other third world countries. The U.S. has also stopped inserting itself into other peoples' wars and conflicts. The new president, who is currently serving an unprecedented third term in office due to a complete overhaul in governmental regulations and constitutional bylaws, has almost created a bubble around the U.S. in that it no longer does anything for anyone and has completely shut its borders down to anyone who is not a natural born citizen. You get called in because the president has been keeping an eye on a top secret situation involving several high profile missing Americans, and many other high profile foreigners, that have gone missing over the last several months.

When you go into the briefing room, people you worked side by side with, as well as new people- and the president himself- are waiting for you. At this point they lay out the entire reason you have been called out of retirement to spearhead this mission.

It has come to the attention of the American government that over the last several months several third world terrorist organizations have kidnapped the top scientists from around the world and forced them to conduct experiments and research to unlock the full potential of the human mind. Now while at first the might not sound like a very big deal, word has gotten back to the President, the Joint Chiefs of Staff, the Department of Homeland Security, the Department of Justice, and as of late, the CIA and FBI, that each time more and more of the human brain is unlocked, significant amounts of paranormal abilities become available. (Think the movie Phenomenon with John Travolta only 100 times bigger.)

The reason we are so concerned about this is because there are three significant people who have survived the experiments and have had their minds 100% unlocked. There are unconfirmed reports of massive amounts of power, intelligence, scientific and mathematical knowledge, and abilities we have not even begun to understand, that these three people now wield. We are specifically working with the Russian and the German governments because the three people in particular are from Russia, Germany, and U.S.

The main idea of the experiment is this: There is medical evidence that indicates that those who stay awake for long periods of time hallucinate and their minds become weaker and more susceptible to manipulation. If this state persists for too long the person will slip into madness that we are not sure can be reversed. There is a small window of time between your mind operating normally and when you slip into madness that it will begin to unlock more and more of what the mind can do. These scientists have been working to permanently keep the minds of the people they have experimented on open so that they stay in this 100% unlocked capacity. The problem with this, besides the obvious, is that common sense, the ability to reason, the ability to determine right from wrong, the ability to be independent in any way, shape, or form, seems to be stripped from them. Therefore these people have these incredible abilities and are extremely vulnerable, and if they fall into the wrong hands, they themselves could be used as weapons. Given the abilities they have and the vast amounts of knowledge, computational and mathematical skills they possess, which is beyond the skills of the most sophisticated computer or scientific equipment currently in the government arsenal, they could create weapons of mass destruction the likes of which the world has never seen. We have no idea what has happened to the rest of the people whose experiments were unsuccessful. We don't know if they went mad or were killed. There's been no word, confirmed or otherwise. The goal of these terrorists is to bring the superpowers of the world to their knees, with the U.S. at the top of the list. They want us to give in to their demands- be it money, military, whatever- not only for themselves but to boost their countries so that they are no longer third world countries starving, desperate, and dependent on the charity of others for their very survival. One could almost admire what they want to do, if they weren't trying to annihilate the entire world to do it.

The reason we called you back is because you were the best of the best. You were part of the most elite force from the strongest military in the world. This situation is very delicate, time sensitive, and the highest priority of anything this country has ever faced. We need you to form a very small and elite group, the best of the best. You will have your pick from American, Russian, and German military. Choose the very best out of them. Make sure you would trust them with your life, because your life- and ours- depends on it.

This mission is going to take an incredible amount of stealth, skill, and extremely quick reflexes. You may encounter hand to hand combat. You may be in a position where you have to act as a sniper, and you will definitely have to use the best recon skills you have. Your objective is to go in without being detected, scope out the place, gather as much intel and evidence as you can, report back to us frequently with everything you find and see, rescue the three, and get out, making sure everyone is safe and preferably undetected. You will have to be very careful with what you say and what the three see. Mere suggestions could send them over the edge to catastrophic results. They are being held in Darfur with local guards as well as guards from Sudan, Rwanda, and the Congo. Expect the unexpected and be ready for anything. We don't really know what you'll find when you get there.

This game is a combination of military/government FPS, paranormal abilities, and plenty of horror and psychological twists to keep you on the edge of your seat and wondering what's going to happen next. Assume nothing and be ready for anything.

Concept Drawings

These are a few concept drawings from Tanner's notebooks.  These are just visions of what he would like to put into the game and are nowhere near the final product.

Tanner's dad is going to ASU Online to pursue his dream to be a game designer, and so his dream and goal right now is to do the same and make a game.  This is Tanner's start! The game design camp is hosted at ASU (Arizona State University) and put on by Internal Drive.  Both are very well respected in the community.

Our son is an amazing boy who has always been very bright, but he had to overcome a lot of obstacles due to his Autism and his ADHD. He is on disability, in a special school, and has had to deal with bullying, stress, meltdowns, and regression. Through the years, the one thing (besides his therapy cat) that has and continues to really help him calm down and relax is gaming. He is fascinated by not only the virtual world they inhabit, but he also loves the games themselves, as well as the processes for making them. He had the opportunity to take a game design class (a basic one) at school this year (he is in 9th grade) and it ignited a passion in him we've never seen before. He loves first person military type games and really wants to go to this camp so he can learn to make a game like this.

He has dreams of becoming a well-renowned game designer, and has decided to go follow in his dad's footsteps and go to college to make this happen. We’re so proud of him, and we know he can do this, but the first step is going to this week long camp. And sadly, we just can’t afford it. He has mentioned it time and time again, and I don’t have the heart to crush his hopes by saying the only reason he can’t go is because we don’t have the money. He doesn’t really understand the full concept of that, and to try and explain it using his camp as the catalyst for the conversation would really be to much for him.

My son doesn’t ask for much, so when he does ask for something, I do whatever I can to make it happen. He’s a sweet boy, funny, exceptionally smart, works hard at everything he does, and loves his little sister with all his heart (she’s 6 and he calls her the love of his life). He wants to make her, and us, proud. He just doesn’t understand that we are already so proud of him every single day.

I’m not one to normally come on a site like this and ask strangers for help. My husband and I are use to doing everything for our kids ourselves. But this is something that would help our son come out of his shell, learn something he loves, socialize with others his age, and get to be a “regular” kid for a few days. And we just cannot make it happen without help.

Risks and challenges Learn about accountability on Kickstarter

As far as risks go, there aren't really any to speak of. The camp is there to teach Tanner the ins and outs of the Unreal engine and the entire process of making an FPS game. The camp has small camper to teacher numbers, so it is not like a typical school setting. On top of that, once camp is over, Tanner's dad, who is tech savvy, will be helping him along the way in creating his game.

From the Internal Drive website under the camp description:

"About This Camp/ Featured Software

If you are looking to design professional 3D games that look and feel like the ones you play at home, then you don’t want to miss this 3D video game design camp for teens. Aspiring game designers will use the Unreal® Development Kit, the powerful pro-level engine behind Unreal Tournament® 3 and Gears of War®. Design 3D levels with custom geometry and employ a variety of textures, meshes, and dynamic lighting. Learn visual scripting with Kismet, changing gameplay conditions and event triggers. In our gaming camp, students work on individual projects and emulate the workflow of a professional studio to get an insider’s look at how the game design industry works.

We’ve substantially beefed up our coverage of Kismet, the visual scripting tool that will allow students to build custom objectives, and create custom scoring. If you enjoy playing games like Unreal Tournament 3, you’ll be amazed at what you can do to “make it your own!”
