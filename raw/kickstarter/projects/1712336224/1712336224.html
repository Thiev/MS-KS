NEWS FLASH: NOW ACCEPTING PAYPAL

Neo-SF, 2064.

You're a young journalist trying to hack it on your own in Neo-San Francisco. Its a tough city, but youre a tough nut. One day you receive a package containing a beta version of the latest ROM, an intelligent device that has replaced all personal mobile technology. With the ROM lay a mysterious note from a close friend, saying that he had to leave the country immediately. Weird.

In 2064, ROMs are as common as smartphones in 2014, but you've resisted getting one up to this point. You dont need fancy technology, and you dont care if you're old fashioned like that. But now that you have one, your life dramatically changes.

Your friend worked for Parallax, which is the leader in customer-side ROMs since 2045 and a company that youve been curious about for some time now.

As you progress through the story, you'll discover and solve dark mysteries surrounding your ROM and the L.I.P.S. OS it runs on, as well as one of the top producers of the machines, Parallax.

And remember, you will meet many different kinds of people during your journey through the feral San Francisco that has come to exist in 2064. It is up to you to decide who to trust, who to work with and who to challenge in this harrowing journey through Neo-SF.

Weve designed the gameplay to provide a fun, fast paced mix of adventure and puzzle solving.

Note: Please keep in mind that text speed and sound effects will be changeable and dialog is quick-skippable.

Be prepared for situations in which you make tough choices as you dive deeper into your investigation of Parallax and its ROMs. The game story consists of a prologue and will have five main acts that's LOTS of entertainment!

Gameplay consists of puzzle solving and dialogue choices that will open and close parts of the story. For example (spoiler alert!), when youre looking for a way into Parallax headquarters, you can choose to talk your way in, steal an ID card to sneak in, or convince a new acquaintance to help you hack through security.

Outside of the main plot, the game provides side quests and character arcs beyond the main investigation.

Our goal is to provide multiple paths and missed opportunities that will allow the game to be played multiple times as you explore the choices that you and your ROM make, and how they affect the world around you..

We plan to have a playable version of the game for you to experience at GaymerX2 in July 2014, and we're also offering a limited number of discounted badges to the convention, happening in (regular) San Francisco (July 11-13) as part of our reward levels!

Weve worked with a bevy of musical artists including 2 Mello, Radiofred, Virt, Aethernaut and other amazing musicians, to come up with some really awesome music that matches the game's dystopian future - check out a few preview tracks from the game.

Risks and challenges Learn about accountability on Kickstarter

Making games is a challenging endeavor and we've put considerable thought into mitigating potential risks. Our research shows that delays and unexpected costs hamper many game projects, and we've structured our project to avoid that mess.

We have a core team of passionate developers and designers that are dedicated to building this game. We're a small and lean team, and while we've gotten off to a great start we realize we'll need additional resources to make this game succeed. The core team will be supplemented by quality freelancers, ensuring a responsible and scalable production environment that is also extremely cost-effective and flexible.

We've decided to focus our skills on building out the game content and mechanics, and are using off-the-shelf technology solutions whenever possible. This means using the Unity engine which is a well-established technology platform for games. Unity provides independent developers, such as us, cutting-edge features while minimizing the technology risk.

Behind MidBoss Games, we have the experience of taking the GaymerX convention from a Kickstarter campaign (thanks to YOUR support!) to a reality. Over 2300 queer and straight gamers attended the 2-day convention in San Francisco at the Hotel Kabuki in August 2013. The convention was a huge success, and is coming back as GaymerX2 in July 2014! Putting together a convention and bringing an entire community together with extremely limited resources was an amazing experience, and you can bet that we'll kick ass again.

At the end of the day, we're a community-based organization and it's important for us to maintain clear and open communications with our community of supporters. We've set up reward levels that will help us engage with the audience, and will allow supporters to follow production progress through our social media channels.

FAQ

  • What if I want the Ouya AND Mac & PC versions? Or what if I want both the iOS and Android Versions?

    If you want to add a version of the game to your order, or you wish to gift a version of any kind (Ouya, iOS, Android, or PC/Mac), just add $5 to your order and then when we send out the survey indicate which version you want! :)

    Last updated: Wed, Nov 13 2013 5:28 am EST
  • You say that you "commit that this game will be an OUYA exclusive for a period of time," and then two sentences later you say that game "will be available on PC, Mac, Linux & Steam (if we hit stretch goals) and Ouya ON LAUNCH DAY." So which is it?

    That's the wording we had to put in the Kickstarter to be eligible for the Ouya #FreeTheFunds Bonus. Don't worry though, we checked and we're within the rules to release same day on Ouya, Mac and PC (and linux if we hit stretch goals). Yay!

    Last updated: Thu, Nov 14 2013 9:34 pm EST
  • I'd like a little more information on the protagonist - is the game played from a first, second or third person viewpoint? Is it more of a Monkey Island perspective, or something like Myst or Starship Titanic where you remain behind the screen?

    The game is played from the first person - you are playing you. You can choose your name and your ROM will ask you your preferred gender pronoun. Since the dialog choices will be your choosing, we hope that we can include a wide enough variety of choices to allow for your character to line up with your values - you can choose if you want to try to be deceptive, aggressive, or flirt with characters.Your sexuality and gender identity are your own and don't really effect the gameplay - It's more about if you can convince other characters why they would want to help you or if they like you as a person.

    The perspective of the game will be like myst/snatcher where the character is never explicitly seen, the point being that we want you to feel like you're actually in 2064 and going on this adventure, and the interactions that you have and choose to reveal and say to your ROM and the characters around you that make all the difference

    Last updated: Fri, Nov 15 2013 11:59 pm EST
  • Is Read Only Memories going to be DRM-free?

    Absolutely! We want our game to be as available and as easy to access as possible. We know that people may pirate our game, and we'd rather that the pay, but we'd rather not punish paying customers with DRM! :)
