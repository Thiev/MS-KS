Spy Wars is a single server, persistent world, espionage game that you play in the real world from your mobile device.

Do you remember playing cops and robbers? Cowboys and Indians? Just plain “guns” in the streets with your friends as a child? Do you remember the adrenalin and excitement of turning a corner and not knowing if the bad guy was there waiting for you?

Spy Wars will recapture that youthful spirit by combining video games with real world locations in an augmented reality adventure.Spy Wars doesn’t take place in a fictitious world with fictitious players; the game is populated by your friends - the battlegrounds are your very homes and neighborhoods…Together with people from around the world, three Factions fight for dominance on a global scale. Are you ready to join the fight?  Visit the website for more info!  www.spywarsgame.com

Player Dominated Persistent World

You’ll join one of three factions vying for control of local, regional and national spheres of influence culminating in a massive global score.The game doesn’t sleep when you go to bed; the fight goes on 24/7.

To help your Faction achieve its goals, you’ll need to contribute in a variety of functions and roles, both cooperative and individual.There will be data hacking missions, data pickup missions, player assassination missions and even missions to stop enemy factions from using WMDs on your home neighborhoods. Combat your enemies one or one or in groups; your skills and your dedication will directly contribute to your Faction’s global success. But the enemy is working against you at every turn…

Your Place In Augmented Reality

In Spy Wars, you will fight alongside your friends as a member of different classes.Each class will have its strengths and weaknesses when completing mission objectives, so choose your path carefully.

Missions start with simple investigative tasks that help grow your database of information on enemy activities, whereabouts or objectives.This will help to contribute to your database of enemy operatives and to thwart their devious plans.As you grow in rank and experience, you may find yourself tasked with tracking down a real player’s character or even to assassinate him or her yourself (virtually, of course!).

Advance Your Special Unit (Clan/Guild)

While you’ll be a member of a Global Faction, you may still want to form smaller Special Units with your friends.Your Special Units can work together for additional bonuses, share mission objectives and data and even wage ware on other Special Units.But be careful, the door swings both ways; become too successful and your Special Unit may be a target by one or both of the other Factions…

Interactive Community Events 

The game will also feature special community events moderated by staff that will have special combat tournaments and rewards that will help you, your Special Unit and Faction gain extra notoriety and experience.

Combine forces with another Faction to stop a WMD from going off on shared territory.Stop terrorist activities targeting innocent civilians.Just get together with other members of your Faction to get to know one another…. The game is competitive, but we emphasize fun and safe social interaction above all.

Discover the Greatest Secret in the History of Mankind 

What spy game would be completed without an intriguing story? It’s all about the information, Marty…By playing the game and participating in community events, you’ll have the chance to discover clues to unravel the greatest secret in the history of Mankind – or steal that information from an opposing Faction!

What will this look like in-game? If you’re familiar with PC-based MMORPGs, think end-game raids but in real life, with real people on a massive scale!

Risks and challenges Learn about accountability on Kickstarter

As with any project, defining the scope is critical. Our focus will remain on the achievable in the short term, with additional features added post-launch.

Technologically, this game is built on already existing platforms with proven records. We are professionals employed in software development and project management. We currently oversee projects with much higher budgets involving thousands of man hours per month of software and web development. We deal with project challenges and risks on a daily basis.
The most significant challenge to this project is time management. Many fan-created or hobby projects start with exciting goals but fall by the wayside due to a lack of proper management - or time period.

Funds raised from Kickstarter will allow us to focus our energies on accomplishing the goal and elevate it above a ‘hobby project’. Once funded, we will provide supporters with a detailed project plan and provide regular updates.

FAQ

  • I still don’t get it; what is Spy Wars?

    Spy Wars is a game that you play casually with your friends, family, co-workers.It works on a system like “checking in” on FourSquare; you go to real world locations and become part of the missions and story taking place there right now.You can play actively or passively, depending on the time you want to commit but still participate in the broader game world.

    The real world will be divided into grids and special locations that can be controlled by teams, factions or individuals.It’s something to do in the background while you carry on your lives, with the occasional near real-time engagement between players in your geography.

    Example:You are going to your favourite bar with a bunch of friends and you’re waiting for them to arrive.You could start a mission to seize control of that bar, in game, and get your friends to join.As you gain supporters, you win points without having to actively spend time doing anything other than being there.At the end, if you’re successful, the entire world can see that you currently control that bar.But beware, your enemies can take it back!

    Last updated: Fri, May 3 2013 12:06 am EDT
  • How do I get started?

    The game will be available on Android and iOS to begin with.Simply download the client and start playing for FREE.There will be tutorials and many automated features for the gamer who doesn’t want a complex time investment in learning the game.Our goal is to make this game simple and fun to play in your spare time with a gentle and short learning curve.

    Last updated: Fri, May 3 2013 12:05 am EDT
  • What happens if I want to play but there are no players in my area?

    No players in your area means you’ll have an advantage in performing NPC missions as well as capturing control points, as there will be no defense.This would be considered a PvE portion of the game. But be careful!Go on the offensive and some armchair commandos could try to remotely engage you with a cyber attack!

    Last updated: Fri, May 3 2013 12:06 am EDT
  • How do I recognize other players - am I supposed to?

    Part of the fun and intrigue of Spy Wars is that you may never know who is a player – maybe the woman in the red dress is actually an Agent.Anyone with a mobile device could be your ally or foe.

    Players will have callsigns that are independent of their other social media accounts, though, you can make yourself known, if you choose.This is similar to the concept to checking in with FourSquare with a photo of youself online.

    Last updated: Fri, May 3 2013 12:06 am EDT
  • How long is a typical interaction with another player?

    Interactions will depend on mission type.Passive missions will have no direct interaction whereas Active missions will be in the range of minutes rather than hours.

    Last updated: Fri, May 3 2013 12:06 am EDT
  • How would you describe a typical interaction with another player (examples)?

    Active Mission

    1.You go to a restaurant with your g/f

    2.You check in to the game and scan for missions

    3.You see an enemy faction is trying to take the control point with two players

    4.You select the defend open combat mission and ask your g/f to do the same

    5.Once grouped and the timer to join the mission has passed, you enter the combat phase

    6.Once every turn, you will be required to select an action.Turn length will range from 1-5 minutes, depending on the number of participants

    7.Once a mission is complete, you’ll be rewarded experience and reputation points and be able to check your stats against the offending players – keep a record of how many times you’ve defeated the same ones!

    Passive Mission

    1.You go to a restaurant with your g/f

    2.You check into the game and scan for missions

    3.You see an enemy faction is hacking your network

    4.You select the defend network mission and ask your g/f to do the same

    5.You enjoy a peaceful, romantic dinner, after which you check your phone for the mission results

    6.Once a mission is complete, you’ll be rewarded experience and reputation points and be able to check your stats against the other offending players!

    Last updated: Fri, May 3 2013 12:06 am EDT
