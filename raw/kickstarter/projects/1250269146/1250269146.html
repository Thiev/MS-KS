Crystal Arena is a strategy game coming to iOS, Android, and possibly PC and Mac too, inspired by League of Legends, DotA and Warcraft III. In Crystal Arena you control the Einherjar, powerful champions to fight alongside armies of human, dwarf, elf and mysterious creatures for the mighty Gods in order to save the nine universes from Ragnarok. In order to win an epic war, you need not only legendary heroes and a strong army, but must also devise brilliant strategies and make wise decisions. In Crystal Arena, you must gather resources, train your soldiers, upgrade their combat skills and spells. This is the challenge that sets this game apart from traditional MOBA games, not only must you have mastery over your Einherjar, you must also have full understanding of the units that will assist your Einherjar in battle. Different combination of units will have different effects with different Einherjar. Your real-time commanding on the battlefield is the key to victory in Crystal Arena.

The team has been focused on Crystal Arenas iOS version so far, but if you show us that you want and you show us your support, we can push it to PC and Mac too. Why on mobile? Imagine a LoL/DotA game with epic stories and legendary bosses from Norse Mythology, and you can play it anywhere at anytime you like!

People have been misunderstanding mobile games, most of them were just considered as cheap and small games to entertain people in the train or on the toilet. But why are indie heroes like Ironhide (Kingdom Rush) and big companies like EA - who have recently announced a remake of Dungeon Keeper for mobile - all turning to mobile now? Because there's great potential, the mobile games can be cool and awesome too! Crystal Arena is going to prove it and we need your help to make it happen!

  • Einherjar

Like the heroes in DotA and champions in LoL, the Einherjar are the souls of Crystal Arena. They come from different parts of the nine universes, each with a unique background and distinguishing personality. Every one of them is blessed with 4 powerful abilities, including 1 ultimate skill. Make the best use of the skills and your Einherjar will be unstoppable. At the debut of this game we are planning to start with 10 playable Einherjar! And we will keep adding more afterwards! By backing this project we will also develop 2 KICKSTARTER EXCLUSIVE Einherjar just for you! Remember to check them out.

We really want to make a game that you like! Vote for the Einherjar you would like to see in Crystal Arena and we will make the most popular one a playable Einherjar in the game! Check it out at the bottom of this page!

  • Strategy and Commanding

In Crystal Arena, you can assemble your own army! You decide the structure and deployment. Yeah, the existence of minions is no longer to feed your enemy but to be under your command and to die for you. Your troops will fight automatically when the battle has begun, and you will be controlling your Einherjar. Make sound strategies and lead your troops to victory!

  • Multiplayer

In story mode you can summon your friends Einherjar to aid you on the battlefield, if you had been focused on training a tank type Einherjar who focused on defense, you should consider teaming up with a friend who uses Einherjar who can deal higher damage.

Other than the story mode, players are encouraged to challenge each other in Crystal Arena. By winning PvP battles you can receive rank points, which give you higher ranks and bonus rewards, which you can use to arm yourself even stronger against evil.

Crystal Arena is a STRATEGY GAME, players will spend major part of the time building and upgrading, and fewer time on battling.

The battle part of most strategy games on mobile are fully automatic or even ignored, because controlling is hard to be realized on mobile devices. But we love controlling, who doesn't? We gotta make it happen! Crystal Arena is the first game that makes a perfect combination of automatic controlling and real-time controlling. The units fight automatically but you can decide their structure and deployment, which is also important parts of the strategy. And you can control your Einherjar, something which is easy to pick up but which also offers plenty of depth for hardcore players. If you are fed up with those trolls, feeders or afkers ruining your gameplay, you are gonna love Crystal Arena!

In the video I was controlling Solwyn the Great Mage, apparently I couldn't beat the boss with regular attack so I used the move-attack, a small trick that can often be seen in RTS games.

Einherjar, Spirit of warrior or the slain men, are portrayed as legendary heroes in Norse Myth. These warriors have died in battle and are brought to Valhalla by Valkyries. Every day they arm themselves and fight each other in the courtyard of Valhalla, and they fight with their lives. Those who fall will rise again in the evening, not only unharmed but also stronger and wiser, for they will not make the same mistakes. In this way they train themselves for the ultimate battle of Ragnarok.

I have found the Norse Myth a perfect carrier for a MOBA game, and this is where Crystal Arena was born. Heroes dont have to be killed to become Einherjar in the world of Crystal Arena, though some indeed are. Although the world is based on Norse Mythology, and this inspiration reflects on our main storyline, we've added a lot of our own twists, so you can expect the game to be much more fascinating beyond the Norse Myth itself.

  • Meet our Special Guest!

We're honored to introduce you our special guest - Mithos from Soul Saga!

In one of the nine universes exists a world called Oterra. A corruption called Ink spreads across Oterra and has forced the last of humanity to abandon their homeland on the surface to live in the sky island Medonia. Mithos is a young adventurer whose dream is to become the strongest Guild Master in the world and protect his home from corruption.

As Mithos traveled through the Tempest Reef, he came across a lost island that contained an ancient dimensional warp to Asgard. Upon inspection of the ruins, he activated them and was teleported to Asgard. The gods in Asgard are desperate for strong warriors, so they explained the threat of Ragnarok. Mithos realized that even Medonia could be endangered by Ragnarok, so he decided to stay and join the Einherjar until the threat was resolved.

We have another special guest, Dakura from Vacant Sky Awakening.

Robbed of his honor, his throne, and even his right to die, he now walks the earth, sowing the seeds of destruction in his wake. Dakura, once heralded as the savior of the ages, became the enemy of the people he once swore to protect. Despite his great betrayal, he could not turn his back on the threat of Ragnarok and drew up his sword one last time, to defend a world that no longer wants him.

Revobeing is a newly founded indie game company. Before Revobeing, we are Robekka Art Studio, a group of artists dedicated to provide high quality and affordable art support to games and comics. The studio was founded 6 years ago by Jason Chiu, when he was 18. In the past few years Robekka has worked for companies like Blizzard, Gameloft, Marvel (on the game projects), Spin Master, Peak Games and many other companies with big names, as well as assisting countless small companies and individuals. Weve spent all these years building games for others, and now we have a dream of creating a game of our own, Crystal Arena.

Crystal Arena has an international team, benefiting from our experience as an art studio, we've built a wide connection with professionals from different areas, all over the world. They've been assisting us in bringing Crystal Arena to life. The major part of Revobeing is based in a small city in China, Chengdu City. You probably havent ever heard about this place but its actually home of Pandas. Seriously, we got a LOT of Pandas here!

Though Robekka has worked for some big names in its client list, most of its clients are still individuals and start-ups with very limited budgets, depending on the situation we charge very little from them. We helped many make their demos and kickstart their projects here! With Robekkas humble income, the very basic version of Crystal Arena is almost 60% competed, why would we still need your help?

Making games can be expensive. The fact is that when our artists work on Crystal Arena, they wont be able to do their commercial works, which means we won't have any income. If we want to focus on the game, hiring more talented and creative people, accelerating the development and adding more content, more Einherjar and more music, we need this project to get funded. Every penny we receive from you will be spent on making the game a better game.

Another reason that brought us here is the great community of Kickstarter, here we can speak directly with you! We really want to know what you think about this game and what we can do to make it a game that you would love to play.

Kickstarter is an amazing place that really makes dreams into reality. We are very lucky to have worked on 2 successfully funded projects on Kickstarter already.

Their successes have greatly encouraged us and we hope that we can do it too! You are the awesome people that make me believe that we can!

Crystal Arena is going to be an online game, not a stand-alone. Being our Kickstarter backer is the only chance to get the very unique exclusive Einherjar, which is going to be one in a million! Think about you playing a unique hero in DotA or LoL that others have absolutely no way to get! It is that special!
