Amalos Overview

Amalos is a fictional universe set in the world of Minecraft. It extends and builds upon the survival aspect of Minecraft, by delving hardcore into the adventure style of gameplay. From PvP content, to dungeons & raids with friends, to even pure adventure, we have everything you'd find in a traditional MMO right here built upon the casual game mechanics of Minecraft.

We have spent an entire year building things in-game as "server content" and environment; just like another studio would do for a MMO. In that time we've also designed all aspects of an RPG into Minecraft, including leveling, quests, classes, and instances.

PvE content: In groups of 4 for dungeons, you can tackle dungeons to acquire loot to boost your class' stats, or in groups of 8 you can tackle the harder dungeons called raids for the best loot available.

PvP content: If all you like doing is fighting other players, we've got that in our "Battlegrounds".

Solo content: Discover the relics of the past, or master challenges from the mind of the Crazy Wizard Wandorf.

Survival: If none of that is up to par, see how long you can survive on the alternate plane of Existence before the plane collapses! 

Lore

There are two civilizations, unknown to each other since the dawn of time until just 3 years ago, the Valgarians, and the People of Amalos. 

The citizens of Amalos have always been a simple people – one of family and farming; while the Valgarians are that of industry - inventive, efficient, and business; always striving to find a better way for things to be done faster, easier, and for more reward.

Around three years Before the War, Valgarian explorers and miners sprawled so far east, that they encountered small villages on the outskirts of Amalos. Fearful of these new people who use strange technology, and overall were completely different, Amalosian militiamen attacked them at every opportunity. Word spread through both nations of brutality and fear, and each nation prepared for war.

At the start of the year before The Great War ended, Queen of the People of Amalos, Sephalon the 38th brought her generals together, and demanded they find any way to end this war. Her generals thought of a drastic plan, and started it in motion. They had troops dig underground, beneath the trenches of the Valgarians, and place the largest known stockpile of explosives known to humanity.

 Igniting it would prove to be the most catastrophic decision ever in time. The explosion worked as designed by the military leaders of Amalos, but it was underestimated. It ripped a hole down to the core of the earth, and unleashed some sort of ethereal essence into the world. 

The world itself had changed. Gravity seemed to stop working for the world, as dirt would stay where you put it, and stone would not fall.

The dead soon rose from the ground as zombies and skeletons, but the most terrifying were any of the soldiers that were killed in the explosion. They were mutated into these creeping creatures, and would explode upon contact with living people, chasing them to murder them.

This hole not only created necromantic monsters into life, but "demons" as well.

Both leaders had their armies retreat, and a mutual cease fire was called for. Through discussion, and pure infatuation, a treaty would prove to be unsuccessful in providing a lasting partnership, but a political wedding would ensure peace.

This new ethereal essence was later described as "magic" by both civilizations, and a research committee was put into motion by the Valgarian leader. 

Through their early weeks of studies, they learned that it opened not just a hole, but a doorway into several dimensions, including alternate planes of existence of Amalos. Not only did it open a doorway, but more could be created, and certain dimensions could be replicated flawlessly.

It is now 1 year later, that you join us on this journey to learn as much as we can about this "magic", and how to save our world from being overrun by the demons, by closing the Demon Rift.

Special Game Features

The game will support and incorporate every feature found in Minecraft that we feel fits with our features, along with everything listed below being developed and integrated by us. 

At launch, we will have 8 unique dungeons to experience while leveling, with 4 dungeons for level cap, and 1 raid. More dungeons & raids will be made as we see fit to patch in.

City Housing: To provide a persistent world, we've disabled building or destroying anything on the Prime Existence. We have spent time building houses in the cities as a convenience of players to quest and level on the Prime Existence for a fair in-game price.

Priority Log-In: If you are a Premium member, you will be guaranteed to log in versus a Free member. If the server is full, a Free member will be kicked in order for you to log on.

Chat Identifier: For Premium members we'll give you a special identifier in chat to let the other players know you're a Premium member.

"Heroic" Dungeons: These are dungeons that only level cap players can experience. They provide the best dungeon gear in-game, and prepare you for raids.

Raids: These extended length instances are meant only for teams of 8 players to tackle difficult bosses for the best armor and weapons in the game.

PTR Testing: As we develop patches and expansions, we'll allow Premium members to test new changes.

Dungeons: These instances are for players to experience truly unique content to tackle bosses as they level, and receive loot that is only found in dungeons with stats on them that boost your class' abilities.

PvP Content: We will have 3 different types of PvP "Battlegrounds", with 2 maps for each at launch - Team Deathmatch, Capture-The-Flag, and Infiltration. Infiltration is a Team Deathmatch mode, with the objective to kill the enemy's Commander. More match types and maps will be added as development goes (in patches, expansions, or before launch).

Solo Content: We've created a whole bunch of fun stuff to entice you to explore the world and discover neat things we've left scattered across the map.

Questing: We've planning to write and script about 500 quests for you to do to learn more about the lore, and to gain rewards such as gold, exp, and/or items and materials.

Classes: Our class system uses the generic "Holy Trinity" RPG system, of a Ranged DPS (using ranged weapons for more damage), Melee DPS (using melee weapons for more damage), Tanks (use higher armor valued gear to absorb more damage and keep enemies targeting you), and Healers (use special spells to heal your party/raid members). All classes have unique stats associated with them, and special abilities only players of the class can use.

NPC Shops: We've replaced the NPC trading system with our own, giving it the more traditional feel of an RPG. These NPCs are located practically everywhere, and are willing to give you anything you need for gold.

Resource World: This is the survival aspect of the game, located on another plane of existence, the alternate reality of Amalos. Players may use this place to build houses, and find all the resources they need to play. However, the plane is unstable, and may collapse at any time. Anyone and anything outside the "Safe Zone" will be lost in the atomic breakdown.

Kickstarter Rewards

All Kickstarter Backers will be the first to Beta test. We will have around 1-2 months of Friends & Family Alpha testing, then we'll open it up to Kickstarter Closed Beta testing for another 2-3 months, and then at last we'll open up to Open Beta for 2-4 weeks before launch.

As we apply development patches, characters will be maintained through beta, as to see the content. However, no characters will be carried through Closed Beta to launch until after the last reset in between opening up to Open Beta from Closed Beta.

Premium Membership vs. Free Membership

What is a Premium Player?

A player who has donated at least $50 a year to the development team.

For every $5 donated after reaching Premium status, you will earn 500 gold in-game.

What is a Free Player?

A player who has not donated at least $50 a year.

These donations help patches maintain speedy development cycles, and the on-going development of the game.

Future Development

Our plan is to produce regular patch content, adding new raids, dungeons, and quests. 

Our first patch, 1.1 will bring a new city into the picture, along with access to the Nether with new quests and content.

In future patches, we might open up other servers, so the player base can diversify and shrink some on each individual server.

Our ultimate goal is to have an expansion (or several), adding entire new dimensions with new quests, mobs, and PvE content, and lore.

Stretch Goals:

$5,000 - Everyone gets closed beta access, 3,000 gold in-game, and LIFETIME Premium membership!

Risks and challenges Learn about accountability on Kickstarter

Every project has its difficulties, and life sometimes just gets in the way of workers.

As we have road bumps along the production cycle, we will provide updates on what we're currently working on. We might adapt the "weekly snapshot" lifestyle for Amalos, providing weekly updates on what we've done throughout the week on Fridays.

Our project will not be updating with Mojang. When they release updates, we will be reviewing them to see if we want to include stuff ourselves, or if we want to modify them before we do. As we do this, it takes time to make sure our stuff is working, and so is theirs.

The only real potential challenge is the fact we don't receive enough support from the community to keep development happening. We only are able to work as much as you're willing to donate and support us.

Make sure to tweet this, share this, post it on Facebook/Google+/Tumblr/whatever to make sure the word gets out! We only survive to finish this with support from you!
