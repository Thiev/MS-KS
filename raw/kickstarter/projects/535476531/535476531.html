Are you funnier than your friends? PROVE IT with FigureOfSpeech - a mobile game for the dirty at heart.

There are two indisputable facts about the FigureOfSpeech team:

1. We love inappropriate jokes.

2. We love one-upping each other via text message.

Sound familiar? Do we have good news for you! FigureOfSpeech is a mobile game that allows you to compete with your friends while keeping your mind comfortably in the gutter.   On the go? Stuck in class? Need a readily accessible drinking game? FigureOfSpeech to the rescue! Just think of it as a steamy threesome with fill-in-the-blank trivia, your personal humor and turn based tactics.

Game play is never determined by your ability or inability to draw, find hidden words or connect three's.  Only your dirty interpretation of what's hilarious and fun.

Step 1 - The leader of each round draws a "fill in the blank" card.

Step 2 - Invited friends select their most hilarious "answer" card to complete the FigureOfSpeech.

Step 3 - None of our pregenerated "answer" cards quite do it for you? Type your own!  Inside jokes and private heckling strongly encouraged

Step 4 - The round leader blindly picks a winner. Finally settle who's the funniest OF THEM ALL!

Your hilarious answer can also be directly shared to Facebook (so your friends can judge you).

FigureOfSpeech presents a different kind of social gaming, but that's what makes it perfect for Kickstarter - a platform for connecting bold ideas with passionate people.

FigureOfspeech is still in it's early stages, so backers will join us for every phase of our development to keep it free from outside interference. FigureOfspeech's core systems are already in place and the biggest technological risks have been mitigated. Given that we want to add a lot of features to aid in your enjoyment, our risk is that the game will not be released on time.
