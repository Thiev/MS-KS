A arcade-RPG mech combat video game for iPhone and iPad. Power-ups, bonuses, and gameplay all dynamically suit your play-style.

Join Aspen in a life-or-death struggle to save the alien world Bawk, a place where warriors pilot massive metal titans called Omegamechs, ruling the battlefield with high tech weaponry and super-science, while political intrigue behind the scenes tips the galaxy ever closer to chaos.

Welcome to the video game Omegatech, a top-down, arcade-style mech shooter slated to be released in early July 2012. Set in a world torn apart by civil war, Omegatech puts you in the role of a young soldier, the only person left on the ground who can save the planet. The game is designed to adapt to your style of play so that, as you progress through the levels, your mech and missions become specialized to you. Our initial release will be for IOS, with later releases for Android, PC, and consoles.
