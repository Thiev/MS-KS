WHAT IS "PLANETS OF HOPE"?

Planets of Hope is a Sandbox MMORPG in which the main ingredients are crafting and evolution.

The game is played between 2 completely different planets, one being Earth and the other is a planet in space (Un-named as of yet, to be named by community).

Each planet will start off with only that of which nature has provided you with. On Earth this will be the basics such as water, trees, animals and minerals. The other planet will contain a lot of unknown materials, gases and other items that you will need figure out yourself ( Cruel we know, but fun!!!!!!!!!!)

Overtime as you excel in your crafting abilities you will find technology coming into play. The ability to create vehicles such as space craft to travel between planets will be possible. This will give a welcome boost to both Planets as some resources from the planets will obviously help boost civilization on your chosen planet.

UPON GAME LAUNCH

Upon the games launch you will be thrown into your chosen planet to start your life journey. You will have the ability to purchase your own plots of land to build whatever you want such as housing, shops, farms etc.... These plots of land will be cost real money ( Â£8 per plot), but you will be able to resell these for real money via our online store.

We have a limit to the amount of land you will be able to purchase, this is currently set at 6 pieces. During our Kickstarter project we will lift this limit, so you are free to buy as much as you like.

Each plot of land will be roughly big enough in size to hold a small house plus a small barn and farm.

FREE TO PLAY

Upon release the game will be FREE to play and this will never change. The only money that will ever pass from you to us is for plots of land. Even then you could resell and make a profit.

Buying land is not compulsory, you will still be able to play the game without owning land.

IN GAME FEATURES

Rather than give you a simple list we will give a you a brief description of what to expect from each one.

Crafting System

The crafting system is the most important part of the game. Without it, both worlds will be empty places. Everything in game other than what nature provides will be made by you.

You will need to hunt, kill, harvest, grow, breed and work as a community to create your world.

Auction System

Our auction system is in place for you to make a profit out of your initial investments. You will be able to sell you land for real money of which a 7% fee will be charged by us. This allows us to maintain a steady income for future developments regarding the game.

Player VS Player Battles

It seems after doing a lot of research that this is an area of which people are torn. We are currently working on this area of the game and will discuss it with the community to get the best results.

We are currently set at making only specific areas for PVP.

Player VS Environment Battles

With a physics based battle system mixed with natures ultimate beast's, you will be in a for a nice variety of combat.

The physics system will mean that you will need to be careful in what items and weapons you wonder around with. Obviously you will have a better chance of running from a wild animal with a smaller weapon or bag, but on the other hand a lion will not be killed with a pen knife !!!!!!!!!!!!!

Once you have defeated an animal you will be able to harvest parts of it to create items.

Maybe you could capture the animal instead. Horses would better alive than dead for example.

Dynamic Weather System

Rain, snow, twisters, thunder and lighting will await you in game.Â  We do not want to release full details of this yet as some things are better left as surprises.

All we would recommend is for those with animals...........

Lock them in a barn during a twister!!!!!!!!

Evolution

As with everything in life, things need to evolve. For instance, bring a animal from outer space back to earth or some plants and see what effect it has on Earths wildlife.

You may also find some natural disasters coming into play to balance out the worlds. This is something that is only in the discussions and idea phase so the community will play a major role in how this develops

The crafting and skill systems will be closely connected to the evolution process.

We will be sharing more information regarding this in the near future.

In game currency

The in game currency will be a mixture of precious stones and gold.

You will need currency to purchase things such as player crafted goods and also your NPC's for jobs they do.

World NPC's

NPC's will randomly spawn across the planets and will go about their daily business until they are hired to do work for you. They will not have the ability to own housing or any other items except for what they spawn with.

NPC's are an area of the game in which we will be working with the community closely on.

Villages and Towns

As the land around you starts being purchased you will soon find that their is a small village/town suddenly evolving around you. Over time this could end up becoming a full blown city with it's own blossoming community.

Working together could be the key to success.

Mining and Cave System

This is how you will gain the items needed to purchase player crafted items and also find rare items to help you make better products.

Finding these items will be no easy task though as you will need to master the ability of mining. Take a look at real miners and you will see it is no easy task and requires skill and luck.

A guide to mining will be available to Backers from a particular level.


ART ASSETS IN GAME

The assets used in the video was not created by anyone on our team. We purchased them for creating prototypes and to give some idea of what the in game assets would be like. We will be using some of these purchased assets as they are simply amazing, plus it makes financial sense for any indie developers to utilize these low cost pieces.

Doing this allows us to spend finances on other aspects of the game. Although we will be creating atleast 85% of all in game assets and artwork.

We know that a lot of people see this as a negative thing but the budgets that the big companies have are a world apart from those in the Indie world. Plus this will only be for the initial launch phase, we intend to use 100% of our assets shortly after launch.

BACKER REWARDS

The dates given for each tier are based upon the reward within the tier that will take the longest time to present to you guys. We will try our best to actually release rewards before the estimated date listed where possible but we have to be realistic therefore we have used the given dates to make sure we have enough time to sort out any unexpected problems that may arise.

During the course of of Kickstarter Project we will be adding to a few of the reward tiers also. We also understand that some backers like the options to purchase some add-ons so this is something else we will be working on throughout the month.


A FEW SPECIAL THINGS PLANNED DURING DEVELOPMENT FOR OUR BACKERS

One of the key aspects to this game's development is the ability to engage with the community during the development process. One way of thanking you for supporting us is by running a few competitions for Kickstarter Backers.

One of the prizes that will be up for grabs is a trip to our studio to see exactly what we do, plus have a gaming session with the team.

There will be many thank you competitions running during the course of development

WHY KICKSTARTER?

We have come to kickstarter for a couple of different reasons.

1: The money raised will help us to grow our team of developers and artists to get the game moving along faster and to a higher standard. Our current team are all part-time and funding will allow them to take their roles on full-time plus bring in a few more.

2: With the ever growing costs of Engine licenses it is very hard for any indie development team to create a game of huge proportions. Basically, the bigger the game - the more money that these companies like from you.

We will also be participating in the "Kicking It Forward" scheme which means if we are successful in our funding 5% will be thrown back into other projects on Kickstarter

Risks and challenges Learn about accountability on Kickstarter

With all things in life there will be unexpected challenges. We are ready and prepared to take on any problems that will show their ugly faces along the way.

There will be no reason for this game not to be completed, if we fail to achieve our Kickstarter goal it will simply just take a while longer to complete.

We are at a stage in development where we have the base combat system, loot drops and some mining/harvesting running (video is only a few days away from being shown).

Our team of Devs is a fantastic combination of experience and young fresh minds. We will be adding more experienced devs to take this game to level you expect.

The only issues we will have without kickstarter funds is the time scale in which the game is developed and the overall scale of the game.
