Persona Wars is a free-to-play online collectable card game built around your GamesonaÂ Personas. Players will start with a selection of 15 companions in addition to a single persona card and a variety of booster cards. From these, players can create a deck of 6 cards, which is used to battle other players.

As you gain experience through battles, you will gain access to a wide additional variety of companion cards each with it's own strengths and weaknesses. As your Personas level up and grow you will also eventually unlock additional Persona slots within your deck, allowing additional Personas from yourÂ Gamesona profile to champion for you on the battlefield!

ONLINE

When playing online, players determine the deployment order of their starting three companion cards as well as which cards from their hand will replace defeated companions on the field. Battles are turn based, but combat is calculated behind the scenes. This replacement mechanic allows decks to be constructed tactically in order to complement a specific card or strategy.

OFFLINE

Before going offline, players can set up a defensive or counter-centric deck and then specify the order at which cards deploy to the field. If that player is attacked when offline, the game will simulate the battle between online and offline players and determine the winner. Losing to an offline player results in a considerable penalty to the attacker. Similarly, winning against an online player while offline results in substantially increased rewards.

STRATEGY, VARIETY and CUSTOMIZATION

The presence of Personas on the battlefield is what makes each Persona Wars deck truly unique. By having determined where the stat points from your Personas goes, you will have customized a unique champion that fits your play style and strategy. Even before battle has started, the difference in player's Persona cards will have already set them apart! Companion cards then add a further layer of strategy and should be selected based on the synergies they create with your Persona.

In addition to companion cards, players will also be able to radically alter the flow of battle by deploying 'booster' and 'ability' cards, which might have either temporary or sustained effects on the battlefield. You can equip booster cards to both your Personas and companion cards to either boost theirÂ strengths or make up for their shortfalls. Persona cards will unlock additional booster slots as they grow so you can stackÂ complementaryÂ boosters.

Booster and ability cards are earned by defeating other players in battle. Beating higher level players will increase your chance of receiving higher level reward cards. You will also be able to unlock booster cards through the store.
