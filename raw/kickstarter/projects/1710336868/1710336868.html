Magic Meisters is an Action Role Playing Game that can be played as a single player or co-op experience ( Up to 4 Players ) for Desktop / PSNset in the open world of Espiria . Here is the backstory of the main story arc :

A hundred years ago, a talented Meister ( Master of Magic / Mage ) with a name long forgotten, trained and mastered all the elemental forces of the world and sought ultimate control of the lands. He put a plan in motion that started a great war and during this conflict he absorbed the souls of all the soldiers to cast an ultimate spell, and fabricated out of pure elemental energy toweringTitans and creatures that were completely under his control.

During this time, other Meisters from around the world gathered together and though they suffered heavy casualties, broke through and managed to destroy him and seal his creatures away..until now. The Elemental Titans and their minions have been appearing over the world once more, and as a Meister you are tasked with finding out why and destroying them once and for all.

Magic Meisters is an expansive RPG experience with an overarching main quest-lineand plenty of repeatable adventures and side-quests to keep you busy for hours on end with some amazing features :

The game starts off with customization choices giving birth to a new Meister of magic to the world, You!We're setting the game on equal ground here making it a feature that any gender can have any hairstyle/clothing combination they choose!

At the start of the game, you will have your own home and the beginning of the story-line will pick up, but it's up to you how you want to proceed from there. Either continuing the main story arc or leaving it, whether that be by foot, by magic, or by mount!

Three types of questswill keep the game flowing from your departure from your once quiet life in Espria,

  • AREA QUESTS : Quests that are activated by entering a certain area or zone. Leaving the zone will drop the area quest.
  • SIDE QUESTS : At certain times of day or night things may happen in the world of Espria where NPCs may require help!
  • STORY QUESTS : These quests follow you along the main plot of the game as described from our backstory!

In addition to exploring and questing, their will be many side-quests, achievements and progression points that you can earn in the game using other unique abilities all Meisters have. Collecting and crafting play a major part in the game as well, allowing you special abilities from the very start such as Cooking, Fishing, Brewing and Imbuing.

Our combat system is based in real time, allowing the players to act and react instantly to any abilities or maneuvers the enemy is using. This system allows us to tinker around with enemy tactics and a.i while still giving the player to see 'warning signs' when an enemy is about to attack.

Some enemies attack faster, stronger or.. stranger then others so the warnings may not always be precise! Learning attack patterns, how to dodgedamage and counter-attack are very key parts of combat in the game!

Our spell system is built on the worlds 5 basic elemental magics :

The player is free to specialize or to mix and matchthe 70+ abilities and spells available to create their perfect Meister. Meisters can also customize Ability points to increase a physical or special attribute of their character.

Spell Points and Ability points are earned as you level up through the game by defeating enemies, discovering new areas, or by collecting and crafting.

The Character Creator is your first stop to customize your very own Meister! All the general character customization options are available to you including race, skin color, eye color and shape and more. A special thing to notice is your ElementalAffinity. This unique feature allows you to pick what element you think you will specialize in giving you a slight bonus and learning it's first spell. This can also be changed at anytime in-game.

Clothing and Weapons can be tested with the preview option in the character creator screen, but you will always start off your character with the default clothing set. Clothing in the game does not effect your stats so you're free to customize your clothing to match what suits you best! As in other games, clothing stores will sell different clothes every day and some clothing can only be found or gathered! Any gender can wear any type of clothing or hairstyle, so customize to your hearts content!

To alter your characters ablities and stats we opted for an entirely new system that we created with items we are calling,Spirit Orbs.

Each character can equip 3 or more orbs on them at one time. These orbs have modifiers that can effect your stats, spells, and abilities as you progress throughout the game and eventually can be crafted by your Meister.

Along with the orbs, you can of course assignSpell and Ability points. Together,you'll be able to tinker with both your out of combat and in combat abilities and spells tounlock unique gear and items useful to you as you play!

One of the defining features we are building Magic Meisters with is the ability to play completely offline in single player at anytime, and then take your single player items, progress, and equipment online at anytime.

  • SINGLE PLAYER : The single player version of the game retains all your character details, items, and equipment for you to take into an online world at anytime. The single player experience NEVER needs to be connected online.
  • MULTIPLAYER : Create a new mutliplayer worldor Joina server ( 4 Player servers ) with or without a password and play together with the ability to do everything you can in the single player experience expanded with emotes, chat, and if we can fit it in with your help, a great assortment of PVP COMBAT.The more players that join through multiplayer, the more difficult the enemies become. However as a bonus,higher quality loot also drops!
So, what exactly makes Magic Meisters different then youraverage MMORPG fare? It's not only the ability to play offline, but the intense focus of your characters importance in this world. Meisters are rare magical beingsthat aren't common and townsfolk will react to you, some may acknowledge quests you've performed and even give you new quests based on that, special items or knowledge about the world that will come into use as the days turn into nights and hidden areas unlock!

MAKINGYOU, the player, feel like every quest you take in has an effect on the world and it's inhabitants is a very important part of the magic put into Magic Meisters!

Stretch goals are definitely something we hope to fund but we'd like to state that even if we don't meet these EXTRA goals you will still be getting the full Magic Meisters game experience with full open world exploration, quests, abilities and more! These are simply extra features we'd truly love to include at our launch with your support!

Our small team ( Two Full Time) have a great amount of experience in our different fields and have developed small indie mobile titles ( Adrenaline Outlaws, Epic Pets, UFO Hunt, Etc ) and have work experience for a wide variety of indie game companies, with this project we're trying to be more ambitious and bring all our creativeness out to create and expand a game world that players will want to stay in and explore for hours at a time.

Big Credit also goes out to the following people who helped us develop the game idea, concepts, and graphics for the alpha : Exquicyst, Adam, Nervene, Nic, Michelle, Jason, Yamer, Tair and Arrowroot.

Magic Meisters was inspired by games like Skyrim, Animal Crossing, and Zelda,brought together with hard work and passion from our small creatives here. We've been working on getting the bones of the game together and with your help, we can complete Magic Meisters to a high quality standard with better gameplay, graphics, and features!

Risks and challenges Learn about accountability on Kickstarter

The biggest risk with any game production is a delay in production. With any project comes setbacks and features that need more tweaking or adjustments and we acknowledge that certain game tweaks may take a bit longer then we think. However, we've given our best guess at a release time at late Q3/Q4 2014 for Desktop players.

The game has been, and still is in progress, and base features that required some of the most work ( NPC Interaction, Combat, Questing, Character Creator ) are near completion already. The Kickstarter's purpose is to add, refine, and complete features for the game and make a complete package available to everyone for the PC, Vita and other platforms! All funds go 100% towards game development.

If you feel that you still have some questions do not hesitate to give us a message at anytime and we will try to answer it as best as possible!
