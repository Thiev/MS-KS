GAME OVERVIEW

Hello Backers!

We are Humaliens USA and welcome to our Kickstarter Campaign. What is Humaliens Academy or HA as we like to call it? Well... with enough Pre-orders from all of you Backers out there, HA can be where everyone plays the online multiplayer hobby game called Humaliens Superbeings Digital Chip Game. Players will take on the role of Gamer Students called Simulists, whose prime focus will be to win with all the chips on route to graduating each and every year at HA.

What makes the Humaliens Superbeings Digital Chip Game cool? As veteran hobby game designers we did not want to create another collectible card game that would cast Humaliens in that dubious shadow of Pokemon, Yugioh or Magic. We had already experienced that fate in 2004. Instead, we wanted to make an online hobby game that everyone can identify, because it’s like nothing else out there. The media will call it (poker without gambling) for the new digital generation. Kids, teens, adults and everyone who plays will call it… HA!

Moreover, HumaliensAcademy.com will be known for its choices of fast pace battle games that will showcase futuristic game rooms where players will use their Simulist Avatars to control the table action that combines elements of chess, checkers, war, trading card and minature games. Each game, Knockout, Super and Ultimate will have something for everyone but it will be the collecting and winning with all the chips that powers a player forward to achieving the highest honors at HA.

That said, HA will draw from an immersive futuristic setting called the Humaliens Universe with beautifully depicted characters called Superbeings. And with our Humaliens backstory in place, the skies the limit to the imagination we can present to everyone who plays. Our goal as story tellers will be to entertain you through beautifully crafted chip sets until the end of time.

WHAT IS THE WINNER TAKE ALL CONCEPT ABOUT?
Well many of us are card players, particularly those who play Poker, and we love all those games too, but we also specialize in hobby games for all age groups. That said, one thing was clear to us, we wanted to make digital chip games that appealed to EVERYONE, to give HA a mass appeal. So think back how it was for kids back in the 90's with pogs when players played for keeps. It's a defining feature in hobby game history and it's a feature we will be expanding on with our winner-take-all setting that can also be toggled off by parents. That said, the end result is a socially cool way to compete for other players digital chips.

So there you have it... the plan is for this feature to only be accessible when you reach a certain level and will require Membership. This will put an emphasis on playing the games for the intended goal of graduating. And trust us when we say kids of today 7+ will hang right with us and give you a run for your chips.

That said, don't hesitate to pledge, you will see our inspiration in game sites like PKR.com the worlds best 3D Avatar driven Online Poker site from England, or Urban-Rivals.com, an Online TCG that keeps on gaming with daily tournaments and everything community from France. And of course, one day, HA will fill the gap between what is NOT GAMBLING and what is SOCIALIZING for all players looking for chip action that makes kids out of us all once again. Ultra cool and fun for everyone!

HUMALIENS ACADEMY YEARLY GRADUATION SCHEDULE
Each year at HA will take 1.5 real years, or 18 months. This coincides with the release of 3 Sets of the Humaliens Superbeings Digital Chip Game per Academy Year. That's a new set released every 6 months, starting with:)

Set 1: Rise of the Simulists (ROTS - 300 Total Chips) - Base Set (255 Chips) - Emphasis on five of the six Humaliens Factions (Aliens, Humans, Androids, Beasts, Clones). ROTS Promo Set (45 Chips).


Set 2: Super Alliances (SUAL - 150 Total Chips) - Main Set (140 Chips) - Emphasis on Team Strategy. SUAL Promo Set (10 Chips).

Set 3: The Paranormal Extra Terrestrials (TPET - 150 Total Chips) - Main Set (140 Chips) - Emphasis on the sixth Humaliens Faction, the Xpets. TPET Promo Set (10 Chips).

In short, Simulists seeking graduation will have to win with all the chips from each of the 3 Sets for Year 1 and accomplish all the goals to be honored as a Simulist Superbeing. Also worth mentioning a Simulist must get "punched" to graduate and that's something you'll just have to learn about later...

HOW WILL WE DO IT

As veteran hobby game designers getting it right with limited financial resources remains our biggest challenge. Remember, we’re not building the next great 3D MMO, because that costs millions of dollars and a pile of resources we just don’t have. Instead we have day jobs, and that’s not going to change unless we can raise the money needed so we can work on HA full time in 2012. That said, what we do have going for us is momentum! Humaliens has matured over the years, and we now have an entire Humaliens Universe and Game Strategy in place that our talented team can execute with the following funding goals:

1) Raise 25K and we can quit our day jobs to work on HA full time in 2012 and launch in 3D by fall-winter of 2012.
2) Raise 50K and we can guarantee a 3D launch of Humaliens Academy by fall-winter of 2012. Moreover, we’ll develop the 3D Digital Chip Games we really want to do! With 100's of 3D Avatars and intense table action that will make playing digital chip games an experience as cool as PKR.com (the world's finest 3D Online Poker site) without the real gambling. That said, the overall game plan is to complete a 2D/3D launch of Humaliens Academy by the fall-winter of 2012 through the Idea Fabrik publisher/developer HeroCloud program. We are currently signed up and on board to execute under this program. So with enough backing, this is the platform we will publish through. In addition, Idea Fabrik plans to support a Facebook platform in 2012 as well, so logically we could port over our 3D games to Facebook sometime in 2013. So sorry we don't have any 3D work to show but we're swimming in 2D right now and our entire Player Management System is in 2D that's integral to the site  - which is where everyone will buy, sell, manage, and build their chip collections, not to mention the many other Academy related modules we've completed.
