Dodge Wars: Battle for Mt. Olympus is a fast paced arcade style dodge ball game set in Ancient Greece. The Gods and Titans are squaring off in an epic dodge ball tournament for control of Mount Olympus. The game features fast paced 3v3 game play, a full single player mode, online match made multiplayer, and a leveling system with unlockable armors and unique customizable special abilities! Each of the arenas features a unique environmental hazard that requires the teams to stay alert and with practice use it their advantage!  Dodge Wars is a spiritual successor to the Super Dodge Ball series and Sega Soccer Slam!

Dodge Wars features an epic story depictingt the battle between the Gods and Titans as they compete in the Dodge Wars Tournament. The young deities find that new arenas have been created for the tournament more dangerous than any before; it seems the Prime God, Genos, is acting very strangely. The story is told in a comic book style, each character has their own ending and beating the game with each character will unlock a special armor set!

Dodge Wars also features an extensive library of custom armors, specials, and artwork that is unlocked while you play. During game play you will earn medals based on your performance that give you bonus xp at the end of each match in addition to the normal win/lose xp you receive. There are over 15 different medals that can be earned multiple times per match for huge xp bonuses at the end! After leveling, you can visit the xp store and buy armor and unlock specials for your characters! (These screens feature the newly remodeled Titans before and after they equip some of the armors)

Dodge Wars has a full matchmaking system making online play quick and easy! Take your custom armor sets and offensive/defensive specials online and see how you fare against the rest of the world. Who knows you might even be playing against some of the 8 Red Pixels dev team!

Risks and challenges Learn about accountability on Kickstarter

There are risks with any venture. The video game industry is filled with stories of games stuck in development hell that never see the light of day. Where 8 Red Pixels situation is different is that we have been diligently working away for 3 years learning and creating; only waiting until we had a good grasp of the technology and design before going public with this campaign. You will not be waiting around years wondering when you will see Dodge Wars.  

We are only months away from finishing things up, final testing, and getting this to market.  With your help through Kickstarter, Dodge Wars will be out for the PS4 and PC in the first half of 2014.

So now we come to today, it’s time for Dodge Wars to get out of the basement and into the living room on a shiny new PS4. We decided the best way to make this goal was by reaching out to the public through Kickstarter to help raise funds to bring Dodge Wars to the masses. 

Dodge Wars is shaping up nicely, the characters are growing by leaps and bounds, with new animations, moves, and voices being added weekly. The environments are being expanded and refined as we learn.  Single player’s story is written with the comics being drawn and full audio being added for the characters.  Dodge Wars will be a fully realized product that will shine on the PS4 and PC. 

All we need now is $50000.  This will cover the remaining development expenses to complete and test Dodge Wars. With any additional funds, we will expand the Dodge Wars universe through the addition of characters, levels, and even multiplayer modes.
