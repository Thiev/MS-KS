Our Vision

The classic action adventure game, reborn. The fun at and excitement of not knowing what is going to happen next, solving puzzles, being the hero of the hour. Racing against evil to save your friends and keep your gold. Sonic meets Megaman with a touch of Fright Night. As you will never know when the banshee will attack you, you village, your gold, or kidnap one of your friends. Run Paddy run combines  speed running games, with adventure, shooting, strategy.

So lets do this lets bring old style fun to a new era of gamers.  We love games that make you laugh, jump with fright  frustrate you and make you want more and more. 

This is a stand alone game for PC, Mac, IOS, Android and windows. With a twist, we are maximising the potential of each platform, so you get teh BEST gaming experience for the device you are using. Gameplay will be the same, however the experience will be different. 

With Run Paddy Run you'll get: 

  • Excitement and adventure with the occasional scare thrown in.
  • Get The Game on all devices.
  • Amazing graphics with smooth gameplay. 

The Story

Paddy is the king of the leprechauns, mostly joyful but when the banshees attack he just gets mad. The banshees attack Paddy looking for gold. They believe its hidden in Paddys Castle, if they cannot get Gold they kidnap other leprechauns and hold them to ransom. Paddy must do two things. Protect his gold and rescue his friends. Paddy must also defend his castle from attack by the banshess creature army. 

He rescues his friends by entering the Banshee Castle 

Once paddy has his gold he has magical powers, but he losses his magic if the banshees have the gold, paddy will die without it.

So Lets Talk GAME

Our adventure begins with Paddy the king of the leprechauns chilling at his village with his fellow leprechauns. 

When the evil banshees attack. 

Paddy must protect his pot of gold, but he does not realise that the banshees are out to capture leprechauns as well.  As this tricky leprechaun uses his skills and tricks, like 

  • Exploding cow dung
  • his potato gun
  • and many other traps to slow down the banshees.

escape if you can with his gold. 

paddy can escape back to his castle. But he may not be safe there. 

If the banshees get the gold and Paddy gets his gold back, he breaks the spell and 

However some of his fellow leprechauns are captured. Now here is one of the challenges. How do you protect your gold and save your friends. Because if you gold is captured, you must get it back. Or paddys magic will fade and he will die. So you have to balance between keeping your gold, your source of magic and remember you need gold to upgrade your items.

But be warned you will never know where the banshees will attack or from which direction they will come for. 

Here is a video of some of the gameplay. 

You can hide your pot of gold in different places. You must keep it safe, however the banshees are hunting for it. What will you do? protect your gold or save your friends. If you friends are gone from the village too long then you will lose gold as well. 

However if the banshee gets your gold you are transported to the dark woods, where the rules change. You cannot use any items except speed, your items only work as long as you have your pot of gold kept safe. 

Lay traps for the banshees, slow them down

Who will win in the race against Evil.

Beat the Queen Banshee

She is the most evil and the most powerful. You will encounter her many times each battle she comes back stronger. 

These boss sections are also randomised, so you don't know when she is coming to get you.
