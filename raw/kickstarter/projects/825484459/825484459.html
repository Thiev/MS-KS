Gameplay Features Explained

Core Gameplay Loop

The core gameplay of Eden Star is simple: establish a base, expand and defend it whilst exploring the surrounding environment for the remnants of the lost colony. Upgrade your armament and deploy additional Eden Kits to expand your outpost, inviting friends to join you in attacking large alien nests for greater reward.

Â Dynamic Day and Night Cycles

Drawn to the Teslinium that powers your technology, the native inhabitants of Pharus 7 are most aggressive at night. Pioneers must ensure that their base and Eden Kit are adequately fortified during the day or risk losing everything they have worked to create.

During the daylight hours you will need to explore, excavate, build and upgrade your equipment. You will still encounter enemies in the daytime, though in smaller numbers, and some of the more deadly creatures will only venture out at night.

Upgrade and bolster defences using multiple build materials to strengthen your structures and utilise new minerals discovered during the day. When the battle is over, rebuild your structures, improve your designs, and prepare to face the next attack.

Fight in your own way with free-flowing, physics-based combat

Enemy engagement is unique within the 1st-person genre, blending physics based combat with tactical execution.

Crush, smash and blast enemies around the destructible environment, combining different RMD actions, attacks and abilities in order to survive against multiple enemies and environmental hazards. The RMD Tool attached to your forearm hosts an extremely advanced AI system that learns from every combative action. Each successfully executed move and attack boosting the Toolâs power.

The full vision (not present in the Pre-Alpha Tech Demo) of the physics based combat enables the player to build a flow of attacks. As you string together attacks without taking damage, further attacks and destructive finishing moves become available to execute.

In one-on-one combat a Pioneerâs finesse in combat and skill with RMD weaponry is unmatched, against crowds of advisories you can manage your enemies by using basic attacks to build momentum, working up to powerful executions and focus attacks. Alternatively you can deploy gun turrets and defenses to take care of the battle for you!

Â Explore, cultivate and decimate the ultra-interactive environments

Eden Star rewards ingenuity and presents both combat and environmental challenges that can be resolved and traversed in numerous ways. With an intuitive control system in place, Eden Star gives you the ability to manipulate your surroundings in a real-time physics environment; there are no pre-scripted answers, so adaptability is the key to success.

Build with the CornerStone construction system

The construction in Eden Star is unique, championing a freeform build mechanic that allows you to place an object in any orientation without it having to be snapped to a grid.

The CornerStone mechanic allows you to use any placeable component in Eden Star as a ârootâ. Each Cornerstone is not tied to a world grid and can be placed on the ground almost anywhereÂ with no predefined horizontal rotation. When the initial root is placed it creates a CornerStone grid, allowing additional components to then snap to it for easy and fast modular creation of structures. When you want a new structure in a different orientation you can choose to place additional components as CornerStones at any time and then switch between their respective grids with ease.

Â Excavate resources from the surrounding environment

Dematerialise whole minerals and structures to gain resources, or alternatively break them down into smaller chunks with the tools in your arsenal in order to absorb them more rapidly.

Pharus 7 holds multiple minerals that will allow you to convert and create structures and mechanical components with varying properties and attributes that will augment your defenses. Certain minerals may enable you to build walls that are strong against the majority of attacks but weak against others, whilst rarer minerals will be able to be combined to create complex mechanical structures with intriguing properties...Â 

Deploy, defend and upgrade your Eden Kit

The Eden Kit is the most important piece of equipment at a Pioneerâs disposal.

It acts as a beacon, spawn-point, power outlet, compact factory and is the epicentre for your whole base of operations, creating a protective energy field that shields you from the corrosive atmosphere of Pharus 7 as it terraforms the area around it. Your must defend your Eden Kit(s) at all cost as its primary role is to provide you with the ability to convert natural minerals into buildable resources or into credits for upgrades. As with the RMD Tool, the Eden Kit is upgradable, and if you have the credits, you may request deployment of further Eden Kits to expand your area of exploration.

When you die your Eden Kit will attempt to recreate you, therefore you must protect it from any enemies drawn to the Teslinium energy contained within. Alternatively, use gathered resources to create buildings, traps, or automated defenses to protect yourself and your new home.

Realtime Damage and Deformation

Watch as objects, enemies, and environments deform and take damage in real time using the latest PhysX and Apex destruction around the world. Almost every surface and structure can be destroyed, mined into and then recreated at will.

Eden Star offers a wide range of play styles, allowing you to focus on the aspect of the game that you most enjoy. Whether it be actively seeking out your enemies in the world or focusing on the creation aspects of the game, Eden Star encourages you to experiment and develop your own class of character. Eden Star does not use a âcookie cutterâ approach to creating character builds, rather it will offer a wide variety of modules and upgrades for you at your Eden Kit, providing the ability to swap between loadouts based on your current scenario.
