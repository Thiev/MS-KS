CREEPS is a FPS-TD game that lets the player control squad members, deploy equipment and embark on side missions.

When a Canadian research team working at Trevain Industries opens an inter-dimensional portal known as the Hellgate, the city of Calgary and all of North America is thrown into complete darkness. C.R.E.E.P.S an elite military contractor led by Sgt. Jake Muldoon is deployed into the scene to restore power and close the gate.

Atmosphere

Darkness and fog reign everywhere.  Visibility is short and the atmosphere is eerily. Screams and growls are heard everywhere.

Exploration Phase - You must reach  locations where the lights can be turned on.  Be careful there are unknown creatures roaming around.

Game Action Phase - In one word: Mayhem.  Enemies hate the light. Wave of enemies will came after you, your squad and your equipment.  You need to think on your feet to keep yourself and squad members a live or from being kidnapped.  


Tactical

You play as Sgt. Jake Muldoon, fearless leader of C.R.E.E.P.S select which squad members to take on each mission. Give real-time commands to each of your squad members during missions.  Select the equipment to take on each mission.  For example, for a mission with many  flying enemies you can choose surface to air missiles.  For a mission with supper fast enemies you can place a lot of frost mines or barricades to slow them down.

 Working Prototype Game Play

 

  • Immersive Graphics: AAA-quality graphics give players a detailed world to play in.
  • Squad members can be controlled by the player and have unique weapons and skills.
  • Player can choose from many different weapons, from Sniper Rifles to Chainsaws.
  • Player can setup special equipment to slowdown or kill enemies.
  • Unpredictable enemies, different types have unique AI.
  • Kidnapping system make the game more intense.

Risks and challenges Learn about accountability on Kickstarter

Making a game is never an easy undertaking, and developers have to deal with a variety of risks. Here's how we are dealing with each of them:

FINANCIAL RISK: We are a responsible. Our core team has been together for more than 2 years. We have invested in C.R.E.E.P.S ourselves, and are fully prepared to do whatever is necessary to reach its completion. As you can see from the multimedia we have shown in our website and facebook page https://www.facebook.com/Brink3D we are dead serious about this game. We wouldn't be asking for your support if we didn't already have our own skin in the game. We can finish it but we need your help!

SCHEDULE RISK: The completion date for the game is an estimate based upon our experience. Should some features take longer than expected to implement, there is a chance that the completion date will slip. If we feel this is necessary for the good of the game, we promise to be transparent about any date changes and keep you, the backers, informed along the way.

GAME PLAY RISK: Sometimes, features end up being less fun than anticipated. This is why we have invested so much time and effort in C.R.E.E.P.S prior to bringing it to Kickstarter. We wanted to make sure we had something that could be great before asking for your hard-earned money.
