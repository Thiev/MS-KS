A MMORPG, with skills to train and monsters to fight. Has weather, Day&Night, HD Graphics, and FPS movement.

http://img207.imageshack.us/img207/8784/vortexscreenshot2.png

Welcome to Vortex. A MMORPG in current development.  Vortex Features the following.

Some of the game is a remake of other games, and with our own twist.  The skills are based generaly on Runescape, but have many more additions and alot more interactive gameplay. Like fishing, you can cast, reel in, weild your fishing pole, upgrade your pole, with alot of other features too.  The problem with most RPG's is the combat system, it's rather based on pure luck with dice rolls, where you can have the same stats as the next guy but only win a fight 3 out of 10 times, or something that hits the same on every monster, no matter what they have on.  We have/are developing the combat system so that it "slides on a scale" with the opponents defensive armour stats and add alot more realistic gameplay.  The game has FPS like combat, sorta like Elder Scrolls, but better, creating a game thats realistic, fun to play, and a great place to talk to friends.

We are in need of hiring the Programmer and Artists full time and we need this funding to do so.  Any donation amount is appreciated.

20+ Skills to train in interactive ways - Agility would require running and jumping over many different obsticles, farming can include gathering from random bushes to creating your own farm, including plowing the fields, to raising and caring for farm animals.

FPS-like combat

Weather + Day & Night

Environments based off real places

Quests & Random Tasks
