The Little Runaway is a 3D Mobile Action Runner Game for Apple and Android Smartphones with great stretch goals.

Little Runaway is a 3D mobile action Runner Game in the spirit of Sonic the Hedgehog, Subway Surfer or Temple Run titles you know and love!

Our little Runaway just escaped from his parents into the big City and is ready to get into trouble and to experience lots of adventures. 

The game world is designed to have ongoing fun with an unlimited number of levels and objects. The mobile App will be released Late 2013 on iPhone/iPad and Android and is built with Unity3D and the 3D objects with 3ds Max. 

Depending on the level of funding we also have plans for disclose the Source Code templates and 3D objects into open source and also publish the game for Windows and Mac Desktop as well as Windows phone.

(Technology) 

From our previous experience with the Unity Engine, we could look no further. Its flexibility, ease of use, and quick turnaround on development means less barriers between the creators and the creation. 

Its robust scripting system and one click approach to deployment allows us to simultaneously develop releases for iPhone/iPad and Android, as well as Windows and Mac. 

The whole gameplay and landscape is created in Unity3D. This allow later to publish to different mobile and desktop platforms like iPhone and Android or even the PlayStation. 

The 3D objects and characters are made with 3ds Max and can easily modified. 

The whole endless game landscape is divided into segments which can modified separately. The system adds one after one so that for the player it appears as one long endless run. 

Changing the look and feel of the 3D objects is quite easy by applying a new texture onto the 3D structure. 

So using Unity3D and modeling all objects as 3D Objects gives us the most flexible setup. And this means for you guys, using our Code and framework most of the work is already done. Implementing new objects or changing the look of the already integrated objects is not a big task.

Gameplay

Our little Runaway just escaped from his parents into the big City and is ready to get into trouble and experience lots of adventures – well his parents wouldn't call it that way ;) 

 Little Runaway is single player endless runner game in the spirit of Sonic the Hedgehog, Subway Surfer or Temple Run. You as the player collect milk bottles and avoid collisions with obstacles. As a reward you collect points for covered distances. 

It’s a good idea not to run or jump into the cars, trucks, barriers and pillars. Instead you can jump on some of them or dive under them. Ramps to cars and trucks help you also to come on top of them. 

We created different mission sets which when solved reward with Coins, Boosts and increase of the multiplier. And to train the player to come back daily we also installed daily challenges which reward with coins when achieved. 

Next we have boosts, which increase your skills and extend your opportunities: 

  • 2x multiplier for gathering points
  • Magic baby rattle with force of gravity to milk bottles
  • Magic mode: speed up collection of bottles without obstacles
  • Sleep mode to decrease speed
  • protection shield
     

These entire boosts are time restricted, so hurry up when using them!

Sure we have social media integrated. For IPhone/iPad we access Apple Gamecenter. You can login with your email or Facebook account, so you can share your score with your friends later and we can keep you updated when a friend of you passes you in the rankings. 

You can always play our latest preview here. 

Beware: This is not the final mobile app, it's a version you can test play in your web-browser to get a first impression. It's a daily build and it’s not yet polished. So especially in the look and design we will have lots of improvements once we go into the polish phase.   

Monetization: 

We are using a virtual currency in the app for max results. The milk bottles are our ‘virtual coins’. You can collect them while running and then buy virtual goods and power ups in the integrated shop. You can also exchange your real bucks into the virtual currency as usual. All the boosts and power ups described before will be paid with this virtual coins. Also Rewards are paid out with that. 


Beside that we use Chart Boost ads for best monetization with lowest impact. We are using them very carefully, the players sees them only when he comes back into the main menu, so we don’t want to over-use them. For those of you who wanna build their own Game with our code it’s easy to change this and show them more often or not at all. Beside that the player can watch Videos in exchange for coins. That’s perfect for guys not able to convert real money into virtual coins.

 IN Game Ads: (Billboard)

When we reach this stretch goal, we will add code templates and 3D object examples so that you can integrate Advertising into the actual gameplay. Selling this In Game Adspace to Advertisers is another way to monetize the game. Or just add funny content there – it’s up to you guys!
