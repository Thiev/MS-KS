Sanga is Mandarin for "Three Of". This free two player puzzle app is a simple game of connect three. (Think Tic Tac Toe combined with Connect Four). The first player to place a piece that forms three in a row will have "Sanga"! However, there are aspects that make this game much more exciting than just placing Xs and Os.

  • There are a total of NINE pieces to choose from.

NOTE: The images below are NOT actual game screenshots. These are concept arts. They are simply tools to illustrate the game play. They may not be the actual game pieces.

  • The pieces are not unique to any player. This means that anyone can play any piece.(don't forget, anyone can play any piece!)
  • There is a limit to the number of times a piece can be played per game.
  • Each of the nine pieces will have 2 characteristics: color (red, green, and blue) and shape (block, sphere, and donut). A player has "Sanga" when all 3 pieces in a row match any one of the characteristics.

So how do I win at this game?

Prior to starting the match, the creator of the match decides on the win condition: one Sanga, three Sanga, or play to the end.

That's it?

Yup, this game is very simple to pick up, but a lot of fun to play! Kids love it because it's colorful, and educational. Adults love it for the challenge, and the strategies!Â

The reason why you see some photos of an actual Qoob is because at first, I considered making Sanga into a board game. However, I felt that making Sanga into an app is the way to go because of three simple reasons:

  • 3D!
  • 3D!
  • 3D! (yes, it's all three reasons)

This game uses a 3D board, but to really see every playable position, one must pick up the Qoob and rotate it. If done on a physical Qoob, the game pieces on top would fly everywhere. I LOVE PUZZLES!

My name is Chi Ni (pronounced Chee Nee), and I am a math instructor. I love all types of puzzles; math puzzles, logic puzzles, online puzzles, paper-and-pencil puzzles, Tangrams, even jigsaw puzzles. I have played all types of puzzle games from Bejeweled, Sudoku, Tetris, Ken-Ken, Candy Crush, Angry Birds, Bust A Move, and even Rubik's Cube (yes, I can solve the entire cube, not just do the trigger move), and I love them all. In fact, I love puzzles so much, I include them in my lesson plans for my students. I feel puzzles are a fun way to bring out and hone our problem solving abilities.

While teaching geometry, I wanted to use a puzzle to explain the difference between 2D and 3D. While there were a few puzzles that contained 3D elements, it was not sufficient in explaining what I wanted, nor was it fun. I began to think of ways to make a 3D puzzle more challenging and interesting. The end result was Sanga.

A more detailed reponse:Â 

In the beginning, I thought I could get the entire project done by hiring an inexpensive overseas designer/programmer. However, as time progressed, I realized that this game was bigger than I initially thought. I was not satisfied with the work I was receiving (you can see what few results I was getting in the PDF book under the Historian back reward). Added on with the fact that we were having communication problems, I chose to terminate the contract. This is why the art is not finalized. So I created a few images on my own, but realized that I am NOT an artist. (You can also see all of my "unique" designs in the PDF book under the Historian Backer reward)

Ultimately, it was a costly lesson; there's no I in "team", but there's no team in "I" either. I needed capable individuals who can perform the type of quality work I am looking for. Fortunately, I found a team that is up for the challenge: AlwaysPlayer2 (https://www.facebook.com/OrionsRun).

I chose this team because they have experience in creating games. In addition, they are local to me. Therefore, I can have better communications with them as well as apply accountability to the group.

I am not a gaming company, but I am a puzzle lover who has a great idea for a game fun for everyone. I feel deeply about the potential of this game, the 3D aspect I believe is going to change how puzzle games are developed in the future. This is what my focus will be, and why I am dedicated to use all the funds to make this game work. I may not have a lot of money at my disposal, but with your help, I know we can create a revolutionary game that will not only look amazing, but play even better.

The first step is to get the game build for the iOS. This will mean:

  • Design and build menus
  • Design and build 3D Qoob + User Interface
  • Design and build 4 sets of game pieces for 4 different skins (Take Out, Toodee, Qem, Haq)
  • Design and build scoring system
  • Design and build premium pieces
  • Design and build a tutorial
  • Design and build local play (At initial launch, if the stretch goal is not met, Sanga will be available with Hotseat Play or Connection thru Bluetooth.)

along with

  • 5% Kickstarter fees
  • 5% Amazon fees
  • 20% tax after receiving funds
  • Kickstarter rewards fulfillment

First stage can be complete with $25,000

Estimated Completion Time = 50-60 days (This is a conservative estimate. I would rather be ahead or on schedule, instead being late on schedule)

I know a lot of people feel that music and sfx is an integral part of the game. That's why it's the first thing I want to add.

This is the next major milestone. The online play aspect will make the game available to a larger population. You will be able to find more opponents over the internet. In addition, this will allow for tournament play, and game center integration. Rankings will show who is the best at Sanga in the world.

Sanga is designed to be a 2 player game. I believe the key to a good one player mode is to have an intelligent AI. We will focus on creating a fun one player experience. It will also require some testers to make sure the everything runs smoothly.

What's a puzzle game without having puzzles? I have already some puzzles that will make you say "Cool!", as well as ones that will challenge even the most avid puzzle gamers.

The game will start on iOS. However, reaching this final level will allow us to transfer to other platforms.
