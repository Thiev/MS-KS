Final Quest

Long ago the peaceful world was ravaged by a fierce conflict. King Edward, Sepcom Emperor torn apart by envy and bitter hatred, battled to decide the fate of the world. Ralph and his team are mercenary's true epic warrior's who in the past was quest hunters, look to seek out the magical Gem Power and for those who are trained to use it. But a time of change is coming. One small boy, the last of a long line of Gem Warrior's, will soon discover his magic, power and leadership and his heritage. In so doing, he will embark upon a Final quest to free the people and to find his long lost father.

Final Quest Part II The Rise of Dormai

Action Packed game follow Ralph and his friends on an epic voyage to find his lost father. Have you got what it takes to confront the Dormai Empire with alternate endings and what YOU do through out the game can determines the ending which path will you choose?

You don't have to play Final Quest to play Final Quest Part II but it is recommended as the story follows on from Final Quest, by completing Final Quest will give you a better understanding on what is going on in Final Quest Part II.
