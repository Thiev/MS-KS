Do you know what critters dream about? Flying! Really! And you can help them fly in our new game 4 iPhone/iPad game - Flying Critters.

About this project:

Just what are Flying Critters anyway?

Flying Critters is an iPhone/iPad game based on an idea I came up day one day while trying to decide what my puppy was dreaming about as he snoozed so sweetly on the floor right next to me. Then that got me to thinking - just what do critters dream about anyway? Especially the earth bound ones that can't fly.

And there you have it, the seed idea for the game Flying Critters was born that very day. Basically, Flying Critters is an unfinished iPhone game where otherwise earth bound critters (or animals, if you prefer) get a chance to fly. And here's the thing, since they're not skilled at flying already, they need all the practice they can get.

Will you help them?

So here's how the game works - you get to play as the character of your choice, in the land of your choice, and all you do is you just take a running leap right off the first slope you come to .... and, Taa Daahh!

... You're flying, or sliding, or maybe you just crashed and burned.....

... but that's okay, because now you can do it over and over again. And if you don't want to be a flying pig, maybe you might do better as a flying bison ... or a flying wolf... or maybe even a flying crocodile! Your choice completely.

So if you find that you do better as the sea turtle, then you can play as the sea turtle over and over again. Or maybe the panda is your favorite. Or perhaps the beaver or raccoon is your critter of choice.

You make the decision - and now YOU have that opportunity. And if you're feeling really wild - mix it up. That's what I like to do!

Because our Flying Critters game does not make you have to be only one character the entire game like most of the other flying type games already out there! We give you a choice and an opportunity to change around as much as you would like.

And speaking of critters, here is our cast of critters for you to choose from:

A mighty Wolf,
An orange Kitty,
A cute little Panda,
My favorite Piggy,
A sweet little Crocodile,
My buddy the Bison,
Our little bandit, the Raccoon,
My favorite Beaver,
and last but not least, a Sea Turtle.

Playing our Flying Critters game is very easy (however it does require some finess to get just the "right touch" to play as good as you can). But that's where practice comes in right?! But don't worry, there are some tutorials built into the game too to help out newbie "critter pilots".

Here's how you play - you just touch the screen when going downhill (to build up speed), then release to go up the hill and fly away. Do this over and over as you travel across deserts, mountains and oceans and fly as far as you can in the time that you have. Then, simply earn as many coins as you can to get even more points to get a higher score.

Our artwork has been created with the user in mind too. Not only are the images beautiful, but they were created while trying to keep in mind the users perspective from high above the earth. And I think my attention to detail in the art shows through.

From the care put into each and every critter - right down to the kitty's collar, and the bison's ruff - to each and every beautiful background painting, heck, even to the objects at the bottom of the screen you see passing by as you fly through the air, the utmost care has gone into creating each and every image found within the Flying Critters game.

Where does the money go?

Up until this point, all the funding for this game has been entirely out of my own pockets. And now my pockets are running on empty. But with your help, the kickstarter community funding will allow me to complete this game with all sorts of tweaks to the point of crazy "jedi" quality.

Plus, I think will be completely awesome for you, because hopefully if you donate, you'll soon be a fan who will play, and hopefully love and enjoy, the Flying Critters game too!
