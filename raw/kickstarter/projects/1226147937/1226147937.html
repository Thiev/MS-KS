Gigi & Lily is an iPad app featuring a child & her thinking hat. Together they learn how to count to 10 as they explore their world.

My Story Put your thinking hat on and discover a new world of possibilities! Honestly, the inspiration behind Gigi & Lily is an inquisitive 4-year-old girl with an infectious smile, whom happens to be my niece. Likewise, both my niece and Gigi share the same name as well as a love of learning.

Inspired, I set out to create a kid friendly character that would encourage children to place their thinking hats on each day. Thus, helping them to think differently about the world around them while learning.

Using critical thinking skills Gigi and her special thinking hat Lily will teach kids how to count, read and solve simple math problems by thinking about their environment differently. Learning is fun when you put on your thinking hat each day!

"DREAM BIG AND SMILE DAILY!"

Before I continue I would like to introduce my self. To begin, my name is Dale Robert Kilgore, Jr and I am a digital artist with a BFA in digital arts form Bowling Green State University (BGSU). "Go Falcons!"

Additionally, I also happen to be the chief artist, animator and programmer for the Gigi & Lily learning app. Honestly, I could be considered a one-man studio, sort of like a jack-of-all-trades! Presently, I have a new iPhone app within the iTunes store, which can be viewed here. Also, I am the artist, animator and programmer for both apps. 

In sum, I have a passion for story telling and game design. In fact my chief goal is to create captivating games that entertain and enlighten players of all ages.

Gigi & Lily's super-duper number adventure.  

Gigi has a big problem. She has lost her number buddies and needs to collect them. Join Gigi and her super-duper thinking hat Lily as they learn how to count to ten while exploring the world around them.

In this Interactive iPad app users manipulate as well as count objects on screen in order to capture hidden numbers. Once captured each number must be placed inside a special number jar. 

 Gigi and her number buddies!

For example, players may be asked to swipe a web causing a spider to revel it’s self. With the spider on screen players are asked to count it’s legs aloud, with the aid of a friendly narrator. When the narrator and player have finished counting a hidden number becomes visible. This continues with various objects on screen until all ten number buddies are found. 

Gigi & Lily’s Super Duper Tree House. This is where their learning adventures begin.

App Design 

Briefly, the images below are mock screen shots and are the best representation of the interface design for the iPad application. However the design may change somewhat as I explore and test other designs throughout the production process. 

 Additionally, all elements on screen will react to the users touch. Simply put if it is visible on screen than players can manipulate it. To illustrate, users will be required to perform various actions throughout the game such as swiping, dragging, and tapping objects on screen. The goal is to create an interactive world that players can manipulate as they learn. 

In addition players will be given positive reinforcement as they progress throughout the game. This reinforcement will be given with a special list of animated words that appear on screen as well as phrases said by the in-game narrator. 

Honestly, while working on this project I have had the pleasure of working with a talented musician as well as a gifted voice actress.

The official theme song for Gigi & Lily was designed to capture the characters inquisitive and playful personality. This was captured perfectly by the artist ShiftyPop! 

Additionally players of the app will be aided in their adventure by the narration of the talented voice actress Adoxographist. Her kid friendly voice is a perfect fit for this learning app. 

You can have a big impact. 

Put your thinking hat on and discover a world of learning with limitless boundaries and potential. Admittedly, while we often take the simple act of thinking for granted it’s significance should not be lost upon us.

For example, early humans mastery of fire and Thomas Edison’s creation of the first commercially useful light bulb, took us out of the darkness and ushered in a new world full of possibilities.

What caused such discoveries to come to fruition? Well, much like Gigi & Lily early humans and Thomas Edison had to put on their thinking hats before their discoveries. Simply put they had to prepare themselves to think about the world around them differently.

You can help usher in a brave new world by creating the next generation of big thinkers. By encouraging children to put on their thinking hats they may also learn how to thinking differently about our world. Honestly, I believe that the way in which we think about the world around us reverberates throughout our daily lives. You can help change the world that we live in one thinking hat at a time. 

Honestly, game development can be somewhat costly. Actually, cost can skyrocket rather quickly! This is in large part because of various elements like, equipment cost, developer fees, software costs as well as labor costs.

In fact, this is why I am seeking additional production funds. Presently I have already invested thousands of dollars on equipment upgrades, like software as well as a new computer powerful enough to render out the complex animations needed to develop the game.  

Unfortunately, purchasing the much-needed computer and software diminished my production budget greatly. Consequently, I am unable to pay for other production costs such as, developer fees, labor costs associated with hiring musicians, voice actors and an additional programmer. Alsohardware costs for things like storage devices and additional computing power are presently out of my reach.  

In sum the money requested will go directly towards these costs. Sadly without these funds production of the Gigi & Lily Learning App will soon come to a halt. Presently, production has slowed down significantly as I attempt to work around the budget short fall. 

 (Lastly, in the event that the funding goal is not reached production will continue and I will work hard to ensure that all backers receive their proper rewards. I am committed to finishing this project!)

General Submission rules

No Submission may contain any material that is abusive, vulgar, threatening, harassing, libelous, defamatory, obscene, invades a person’s privacy, violates any law, any intellectual property or other property or other rights, or is known to be false.

Furthermore no submission can contain bullying, harassing, obscene or pornographic, sexually oriented or explicit themes. The promotion of hate, personal injury, death, damage or destruction to property is not allowed as well.

Any submission violating any of the guidelines will not be allowed. 

Name & Audio Submissions

Name & Audio submissions share the same rules as those listed in the General submission section. Also, while all name requests following submission rules will be honored there is limited space on in-game plagues. 

Consequently, when submitting a name we respectfully ask that you keep them at a reasonable length. We consider a reasonable length to be between 1 to 25 characters with spaces. 

Even so, if problems occur because of the length of a name that you submitted we will work with you in order to find a solution. 

Which platform will the Gigi & Lily learning app be released on?

Presently, Gigi & Lily will be released for the iOS platform, more specifically the iPad.

How long will it take to complete the app?

Truthfully, a safe estimation for competition of the application is approximately 10 months. This estimation accounts for production, and testing phases as well as any unforeseen production delays or creative changes. The typical completion time for my previous iOS gameshas been between 2 to 10 months depending on the complexity of the game. 

How can I follow the production process?

Weekly production updates will be posted each Friday morning through the official twitter, and Facebook, pages for Gigi & Lily. Updates will include a production photo and or artwork as well as a brief summary of key production goals that have been achieved for the week.

How and when do I get my perks?

As soon as the campaign ends all backers will be contacted though email and thanked for their contribution. Additionally, a detail explanation will be provided as to how perks will be given out. Also backers will be provided with a production timetable as well as details on how they can follow the production process from start to completion.

Typically all perks will be given out on launch day and will be in the form of a code that can be redeemed through the iTunes store. Also other digital rewards will be sent through email or be redeemable through a special site to be named at a later date.  

(Lastly, please feel free to ask any question in the commentaries, as they will be answered promptly.)
