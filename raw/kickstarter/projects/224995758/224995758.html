The Scoop

This project has been a personal passion for some time. The idea was hatched after endless hours of gaming myself, and not finding one game that filled my needs. In lue of finding one, I decide "Let's make one!", and here I am. I am already neck deep into the project, but having some funds to add some spiff to it all and make the timeline realistic are always welcome.

Mortifera Incident is a text based (With graphics and sounds), online multiplayer RPG that incorporates many elements. Players will start as a sole survivor with a varying background of the players choosing, and will start by merely surviving. As they progress they will ascertain skills and items that will help them in staying alive. Work even harder and they will acquire NPC's below them who can help them with barricading buildings, starting patrols to look for survivors, and more. There is no limit to the scale of the game, as the world will be dynamically generated as players move through it.

This project has been a dream in my eye for a long while before I dived into it, and now that I'm deeply involved, I'm reaching out to the community to help make it all possible by giving it a real budget.

Features Will Include

- Unlimited Game Size, (Grid Style 1sq. Mile Areas That Dynamically Generate), game will literally span 100's of thousands of miles.

- Ability to barricade buildings and deal with survival needs such as food, equipment, fuel, etc.

- Ability to team up with NPC's to form raiding parties and patrols.

- Choose your own path, be a raider, a mercenary, or just a survivor.

- Use yourself or your NPC's and allies to take control of equipment such as vehicles, helicopters, boats, and more.

- Ally with other players to form powerful coalitions and help you maintain a large "undead free" zone.

- In depth story that is total sandbox, allowing the player to discover clues to the outbreak while letting them have the freedom to do what they wish, whilst interacting with other players.

- Always progressing, never charging more. The game will be free to play with the option of being a donator that will allow some perks like more time in a day, etc. as a thank you for helping support the further progress and expansion of the game. I will never charge anyone to play this, as I want it to be an enjoyable experience, and if I accomplish that, a little food in the bowl to let me continue to expand the project will always be humbly accepted.

- Awards and accomplishments. There will be accomplishments that will be given as game awards for achieving things such as your first barricaded building, first patrol, etc. Some will be limited edition items for performing certain tasks at certain times, and much more to keep the game vibrant and give those achievement mongers something to always strive for :)

Development Phases

1) Pre-Alpha: In this phase I will be going through the game concept and idea board to develop the rough outlined code to make sure that all of the basic functions of the game work properly. Things like moving, communicating, and your account preferences.

2.) Alpha testing phase will consist of a small group of dedicated testers working on testing the basic features of the games looking for any bugs, and listing them, so they can be eliminated based on a priority of how much they affect the game.

3.) Pre-Beta phase will consist of implementing more advance features of the game such as NPC interaction, group formation, conquering and barricading, etc. During this phase our testers will help test out these more advance portions of the game, and we will again correct issues as they arise based on how much those issues affect the game.

4.) Beta phase will be when we open up the game to a much larger testing audience to ensure that everything is working properly. We will have most major issues addressed at this point and most bugs found should be minor, bearing any unforeseen bugs due to the higher load on the server from the higher population.

5.) Public release. This phase is straight forward, the end goal, the public launch. We will open the game to the general public, and then we will begin commencing work on continual bug fixes, game updates, and new features!

The timeline for the currently planned features is approximately 8-12 months before we are ready for beta, and 12-14 months before we are ready for full public release. Help from Kickstarter funding will allow the development team to dedicate more time to this project and with any luck, decrease this production time. As it is now, without an actual budget, we are at the mercy of our regular jobs, and this can cause production delays.

I am looking forward to meeting the many wonderful members of this community, and myself, as well as the rest of the team here are really hoping this works out for everyone, and we have some stretch goals and rewards already planned if things go well!

FAQ

  • Will the game be persistent forever, or will it reset? If it doesn't reset, how do you balance?

    An excellent question! Yes, the game will be persistent, and will not reset. However, the world is also "limitless" in that it continually generates as players move through it. Once the game has expanded to a certain point, we have already planned out "Starter" areas that are "Safe Zones" for players who are under a certain age in game, and these Starter areas will also be generated on the fringes of explored territory, keeping them out of the center of the fray so to speak.

    Also, with the planned structure of the game, there will be no benefits for long-time veteran players to attack young players. They will be busy performing much more advanced tasks and taking on larger enemies that will benefit them. Think about it like this. If you had 100 survivors under your control are you more likely to go after the guy with a shopping cart, or the food and fuel convoy of another player who has 75 guys under him/her? :)
