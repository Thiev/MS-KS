This game will merge both Egyptian and biblical testaments into a story driven third person shooter.

IN ONE SENTENCE

Journey through the life of Jericho Fox as you the player experience Jericho’s life from his past, his present, and his future as he fights to defeat Seth the Egyptian God of Chaos before he releases Armageddon.

GAME SUMMARY

“Revelations: The Chronicles of Jericho Fox,” is a third person shooter/ action adventure game that tells of Armageddon from the New Testament Revelations brought by the Egyptian god deity of Chaos and Storms Seth .This game will merge both Egyptian and biblical testaments into a story driving third person shooter that will fulfill the player’s knowledge as well as intense action in the mythological world of the Egyptian gods through different downloadable episodes.

You play as Jericho Fox a French archeologist and well known theft to most, who is cursed with immortality by the god deity of the Sky Horus for bringing the world’s end by releasing Seth from the Urns of Ra in London, England on January 1, 1912. Now Jericho has become the weapon of Horus, and with the aid of the warriors of the Heru Temple known as the, “Wadjet of Ra,” He will have to continue the war that has split Egypt into two nations since the beginning of the 1st dynasty. You will go through Jericho’s past, present, and future as he fights, and searches for knowledge to destroy Seth before he merges the three dimensions of Heaven, Earth, and Hell to bring the Apocalypse to fruition.His cursed path for redemption is the world’s only chance for salvation.

GAME PLAY

Jericho was given special powers by Horus besides the curse of eternal life. His right eye is the eye of Horus and his right arm is the powerful weapon of the Egyptian God of the Sky. With the eye of Horus, Jericho is able to see what item, weapon or tool would make a suitable weapon for conversion, He is also able to use the eye as a waypoint to know which direction to go and use his right arm to convert any item he touches into the weapons of Horus at will. Each weapon he uses has its own special ability that makes it unique and powerful.

Jericho’s controls are the standard third person shooter controls with third person view/free camera to rotate around Jericho’s body as well see the world around him. His attacks will change for every weapon he is holding - from long range to close range.In addition he is able to use a variety of guns and melee weapons each there own strengths and weaknesses.

Battle in hostile environments as you are pitted against Seth’s creatures including Possessed Human Bodies, Demon Priest, Desert Scared Harpies, and Gigantic Mummy Titans.  Carefully manage your weapons and ammo because Jericho will only be able to carry four types of weapons at a time.

Research and solve puzzles to unlock areas to progress in the game, or to unlocked special items to view such as art work, videos, and educational information on Egyptian Mythology.

WHAT MAKES REVELATIONS THE CHRONICLES OF JERICHO FOX SPECIAL?

- The Story: This game will be a series consisting of different episodes, which tell the life of a man damned to fight a war between Egyptian gods that has lasted since the 19th dynasty. You the player will see the world I’ve imagined, balancing beautiful visuals and game play. I will pull you into a new world of adventure, horror, action, and the experience of gaining knowledge on the history of Egyptian Mythology. I’m hoping to expand your knowledge with each episode we create and hope to get feedback, opinions, and knowledgeable arguments to improve and expand the game experience for the player.

- A Variety of Weapons that hold the Power of a God: Almost any item or weapon the eye of Horus sees as a suitable weapon the player will be able to transform it into a weapon of Horus, and each weapon (long or close range) will have a very powerful special ability that can take out multiple or single enemies. You will be able to see my interpretation of how I believe our modern day conventional weapons - even items such as tools - will look and work if they held the power of a god. I’m always thinking of new items to transform into weapons and can’t wait to show off what we have and what we are going to bring to the table.

- Using the Power of U.D.K to create a Unique Game: A lot of projects small and big use epic’s powerful engine to help them bring out stunning and superb graphics that bring their imagination to life. Using the power of the Unreal Engine 3 we are able to make sure our visuals for our Environment, Characters, and Assets are rendered on a next gen level. Using our current knowledge as well as what we are learning on our personal time we are able to improve and keep improving on the project.

- Replay and challenges: We will also pack in many different types of surprises to keep you the players entertain with time challenges, as well as rewards.

- Original Music Scores: A superb sound track written by Scott Graves that captures the mood and intensity of the game.

Game Level Setup

Nigel's Corporation Building is the first episode of Revelations: The Chronicles of Jericho Fox, taking place in 1912 London. Jericho Fox (the player) has arrived to Nigel's office on the 20th floor to give the urn of Osiris and the urn of Horus, which he has stolen from the temple of Horus to his employer Nigel Waitfeild. After Jericho and Nigel have made an agreement for the jewel to be his payment instead of money, Nigel accidentally reads the magical incantation on the back of the urn of Osiris and releases Seth Egyptian god of Chaos from his eternal prison. Jericho must now escape the building before Seth's essence consumes the entire building, the people inside, and him along with it.

Primary Player Focus

The primary focus of the first playable is for the player to escape the building while evading dangerous obstacles, fighting off human corpses that are possessed and avoiding Seth's essence, which is consuming the entire building and everyone inside. The player must go through six floors (10th Floor to the 5th Floor) and adapt to the changing environment that is occurring on each floor. The player will have a crowbar as a weapon, no accessories, and a few health-packs to aid them. They only have Jericho Fox's caution and paranoia to give hints and directions to progress through each floor.

Risks and challenges Learn about accountability on Kickstarter

One of the main risks we are worried about in the development of the project is time, we need to try and schedule ourselves to have the game ready for packaging by the scheduled date. Seeing that everybody on the team has a regular day to day job, we work on this when and where we can and put in all we can in that time we have - We are making sure to add in more time to focus non-stop on the project.

Another risk for us is data loss, we have a backup system and an upload server that we keep multiple up-to-date backups of our code and assets. Another is maintaining the high level of polish that we have set for this project. This means that content will be more costly to produce, however the work will require less polishing at a later stage allowing more time to create content.

Our last challenge is people management. This is an interesting thing, especially for our project. You don't want to bring people in too soon or their enthusiasm will wane with nothing to do. What I try to do is get a good, clear overview of our project's lifespan, and building from there. Since I’m the director it’s my responsibility to find people and let them know what I’m relying on them to do to make the project work.
