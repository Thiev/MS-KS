                        HOSTILE SHORES ONLINE (MMORPG)

Our Core Product that we will be Developing is the Massively Multiplayer Online Role Play Game (MMORPG) Hostile Shores Online
This is a Ragnarok Online Free Private Server Style Engine with the Hostile Shores A Year And A Day Storyline, and is going to be Fully Customizable by our Users (everything from Items to Maps to Events)

  • Free to Play PC Game

  • Story Driven with Open World PVP (On/Off)

  • Fully Customizable (and allows for Character Transfer between 
    Site and Social Games)

The setting for the story takes place in and around islands which are surrounded by mile-high waterfalls that render it isolated from the outside world. Life in this valley (the inner world) has existed and flourished for so long separated from all outside effects and technology, that they are no longer aware of it's presence.

The inner world is inhabited by seven culturally and ethnically different peoples, each created as references from history. The style of each culture represents an individual iconic warrior. Each peoples' culture, constitute a province (Mecca) and have its' own elder.

The elders visit the inner world as spirits via each Main City's Temple and are available for spiritual guidance in times of peril, once the warrior allows a single personality (of their many) to reach out. These cultures are in constant conflict and shifting alliances, however they all share a fear of the unknown, as described via their links to a Karma driven Collective Consciousness.

The main character in the novel is Reyer Jolnir “Shidden” Katil, called the Oat God. He is of the Celtic / Viking leaning culture with overtones of Pagan traditions, in a quest to unite the seven cultures within the inner world against his rival. Opposing Reyer is his nemesis Ian “Burning Shield” Mikhail of the same culture, who has his own quest to take over the world, even if it means contacting the outer world to utilize the advanced technologies.

We will be using the funds to develop the game (purchase the servers, maintenance, minimal marketing via youtube / game sites, hire additional programmers / artists) and are planning to have our Beta Launch by the end of the year !

MMORPG Design Specs:

Basic Gameplay

Ragnarok Online Free Private Server, inspired Engined and Gamplay, a fully customizable MMORPG where you create a character and develop his story within the Hostile Shores World. Theme Art Style is Japanese Painting (traditional rather than Anime) Theme Music Style is Celtic themed Heavy MetalAn Open World PVP warzone where as much focus is given to wars as is given to character details, both in story as well as in “looks.” Leveling consists of a Main Level which his shared across characters (personalities) and a Job / Secondary Level which is specific to that personality's class.

Example:

Shidden

1) WAR / Swordsman

2) HEL / Spearman

3) SIN / Bowman

The player takes control of their character and their small army will follow. In Battles and Wars the player must attempt to defeat the enemy commander NPC. The player must make their way through the map defeating soldiers and enemy officers while trying to keep their commander alive, as well as their Main Camp and other important strategy based constructs. Survival is key throughout game play. A player will combat hundreds of NPCs and Players alike, and in most cases it will be difficult to discern friend from foe, player from NPC. The entire world is PVP and the players are always attack-able even by allies. The officers and commanders have superhuman abilities and develop them as they level up. Generally a high level will be able to defeat a low level but more than one low level will be able to defeat a single higher level character, thus making NPCs very important.

When a Player dies they are able to use their Psychic powers to teleport-save themselves and appear to the closest allied Healers. They will be an inch from death and usually very weak. On defeat a player loses a percent of Gold and all non linked weapons and armors. If a player has refined their weapons and armor, or defeated enough enemies, thus leveling the items up, or created the items in the item design / editor, than the selected items will teleport with the player on defeat. In a War setting if the player is the leader the battle is over, and if the Supply Camp has been destroyed the player will not be able to teleport-save back in to the War. This is also the case in some Events, but not the case in most aspects of the Online World. 

Players must utilize teamwork because often Puzzles must be completed in order to complete Quests.

Leveling is primarily from killing enemies, but gold and drops are mainly given as Quest Rewards. 

Story Synopsis:

Players will chose to follow the path of a Hero or a Bandit for everyday life
Either way will have access to both Reyer and Ian's clans and will chose to follow one of them
They will embark on journeys / quests in order to increase their level and rank
Once they are prepared they will advance to higher level missions and must make choices
If they choose to disobey Reyer, or follow Ian beyong the waterfalls, then the advanced story begins
If they do not then until then they can help Reyer stop other players from advancing
Once they leave the waterfalls though, they are captured, tortured, and unable to escape
Reyer, and Nikulus are busy on quests of their own so cannot come to their aid
The outer world is filled with corruption and the people rely on technology rather than auras
Stripped of their power they are rescued in an anarchist raid on the facilities
Not long after Ian's armies will move to rescue, and destroy as well
The player can choose to join Ian, or aid the Anarchists
If they return with Ian they will find the inner world no longer a hospitable place for them
Eventually will need to work with Anarchists in order to either supplement their powers with Tech
Or find a way to reconnect with the Collective Conciousness and the spirit of their Elder
At any time players can choose an alternative storyline and follow either, The Bandit Kings, The Paganic Witches, The Bronze Blade Brotherhood or Hunters, The Bronze Blade Tamers and Living Weapons, or Event NPCs ranging from One World Order Uprisings, to Town Guard Commanders
Top Players and Developers will be able to add their own missions, quests, and full storylines as the Game progresses further, and becomes more and more customizable

FAQ

  • What will the Game Look Like ?

    It will have the feel of a free online server, 2D Sprites in a 3D environment - a throw back to original MMORPG gameplay, but as far as the graphics / visuals are concerned - we are going for a Japanese Painting Style (pre-anime or traditional)

    Last updated: Wed, May 2 2012 3:49 pm EDT
  • What is the Game going to have for Music ?

    The music will be done initially by a select few Bands, with a Celtic - Metal theme. After launch we will allow players to upload music they they own the rights to, and the game will evolve in this section, just as it will with items / quests / maps.

    Last updated: Wed, May 2 2012 3:58 pm EDT
  • How will Guilds Work ?

    Guilds are setup to be like Kingdoms. While they may start as a Party or a Guild or a Group, as they grow in numbers - so too will the guild. A % of exp from each guild member will go to the guild, leveling it (as can be done with items such as your sword). The guild will grow in to a Kingdom and there will be 1 Leader, and a 2nd in Command. As the Guild Grows in to an Empire, it will allow for more players, more bonuses, control over more territories, and additional leader positions. A guild will help each other and share between them. They will have allies and enemies, and will move up the ranks of power through War.

    Last updated: Wed, May 2 2012 3:58 pm EDT
  • How does the War work then ?

    A war is 24/7 but is not instant - a guild will have time to prepare for battle. Why I am saying 24/7 is because at any time a Guild can Declare War on another Guild or a Town or Bandit Enclave etc. Then there is a waiting period, then the battle, then a cool down period after each battle. The battle consists of a selected area, 2 guilds (in some cases there allies as well) and 3 goals. The WAR class has the ability to destroy the Main Camp - which will end the Battle, and the SIN class has a bonus when attacking the NPC Commander / Leader, which also will end the battle. The HEL though, has the ability to destroy the Supply Depot - which will stop all NPCs / Players / Allies from returning to battle, or joining in as allies. A Guild of a high rank will have additional Skills / Powers / Benefits, from NPC Guards to Traps to Siege Weapons.

    Last updated: Wed, May 2 2012 3:58 pm EDT
