The Game

Son of Nor is an award winning and challenging 3rd person action adventure game for Windows, Mac and Linux that can be played in single player or with up to 4 players cooperatively online or via split-screen.

On their mission to stop the impending attack of the Sarahul army the players discover Noshrac's vast desert world. Faced with wild creatures and Sarahul warriors, they will stand their ground in heated battles. These fights are not to be underestimated! Your magic has to be used creatively in order to succeed.

Your abilities, though, can be used as more than just weapons in a fight. As you discover the long-forgotten temples that are protected by clever mechanisms, you'll have to rely on your powers as well to successfully proceed.

These mechanisms are the source of power that many mages in Noshrac are looking for. Succeeding in opening them, frees the magic held within the temple, granting you the additional ability to control the elements of the world and increase your odds against the Sarahul.

Son of Nor consists of fights, puzzles, exploration of the temples and the world. All the while enhancing the player's experience by the diverse story and original lore.

Player Powers in Son of Nor

As a Son of Nor you control the following abilities:

  • Telekinesis: Use your environment as a weapon. Throw rocks and other objects with the power of your mind to deal damage or to trigger carefully prepared traps or mechanisms.

  • Terraforming: This power allows you to manipulate and shape desert sand. Use it to overcome obstacles with ease, build paths where there are none, raise up protective barriers or slow down incoming enemies.

  • Elemental Magic: Use the power of the elements in your surroundings. Cast powerful spells made of fire, wind, or essence and combine them with each other to conjure even more powerful effects.

Think and use these powers wisely. Some enemies can only be defeated with strategy and a clever combination of your abilities and not by just raw power.

Immersion

The game is being designed in a way to have as little HUD and UI as possible. There will be no on-screen icons you have to click to trigger magic, or life bars that show how many hit points a player has left. As much information as possible is delivered using natural interaction, visual effects, animation and sound.

The Edge, the last refuge of the human race is located in the northern mountains of the desert world Noshrac. It is home to mankind after their defeat in The Great War against the Sarahul Empire.

With the intention to exterminate the human race, the refuge is soon discovered by the Sarahul Empire whose armies are being readied for the attack.

You are a Son of Nor, a human blessed by Nor, the goddess of the night. You possess the power of telekinesis and the ability to shape the sands of Noshrac. It is your purpose to protect what's left of your race and to save it from extinction.

On your quest to save the human race, you’ll uncover long forgotten secrets, learn magic old as time and stand your ground against attacks and ambushes of different enemies. But be careful whom you trust! Dangers lure everywhere and even time itself might prove to plot against you...

Exploring the temples and robbing the magic held there, enables you to control the elements fire, wind and essence. A very valuable gift needed during the impending attack of the Sarahul. But remember, magic always comes with a high price...

Depart on a fantastic quest that will not only alter your destiny, but also the one of the whole continent.

Action packed fights are one of the main elements of Son of Nor's gameplay.

Levitate rocks above the battlefield and release them to bury your enemies underneath as soon as they pass. Raise up protective barriers using the desert sand surrounding you to shield you from ranged attacks or to hide from enemy patrols. Take advantage of every loose object, set carrots on fire, conjure earthquakes or gale-force gusts of wind to stand your ground in fights.

Make sure to choose your battle ground carefully. Explore the diverse environment and exploit tactical advantages nature prepared for you... or prepare them yourself!

Son of Nor will have several interesting physics-based puzzles that can only be solved by using a clever combination of the player's abilities (telekinesis, terraforming, magic and its combinations).

Cast a wind spell to fill sails, cast a fire spell on desert sand to turn it into glass. Use terraforming to lower sand below an impenetrable wall and make it crumble. Use telekinesis to trigger distant pressure plates, traps or block enemy troops.

Son of Nor has an extensive cooperative multiplayer mode in which up to 4 players can join forces against the Sarahul Empire. You will encounter several situations and puzzles when you play the game cooperatively that differ from the single player experience. These special areas have been designed to be solvable by multiple players only.

Combine your telekinetic powers with other players to move a huge rock you cannot lift by yourself and solve puzzles cooperatively that require several actions to be executed simultaneously.

Fights in multiplayer mode add a lot to the game. Though preparing traps and combining spells is possible in single player, it is a whole new experience in cooperative mode. The feeling of success is incomparable when crafty plans forged by a whole team with an impressive choreography of actions grant you the awaited victory.

For example: one player absorbs a fire element and enhances several rocks with his/her magic. Then, he/she levitates them in a narrow space where enemy patrols pass by regularly. Another player waits for the patrol to pass again, having smaller stones ready. As soon as the patrol shows up, the second player shoots them onto the levitating blazing rocks, which then explode getting rid of the patrol in one go.

Figuring out puzzles in Noshrac's lost temples is even more fun with friends!

Son of Nor should become a fully featured action-adventure game that can be played in single-player or cooperative mutliplayer mode. The final game is going to consist of about 50% fights and action and 50% puzzles and exploration. We aim for about 8 hours of gameplay that is subdivided into 6 story chapters with various challenge maps in between. There is a substantial re-play value when you play Son of Nor in singleplayer or multiplayer mode, as you can access new areas with friends.

Challenge maps are small levels in which players need to complete a single task. For instance pass a canyon full of Sarahul warriors quickly. There will be leaderboards and high-scores for these challenges, which can also be played in co-op multiplayer mode and replayed as many times as you want to improve your ranking.

As the story progresses, players visit new areas, cities and temples. Every area of the story is going to be concluded with a puzzle-boss-fight. These are more powerful enemies with special abilities that need a strategy to be defeated. Players need to find their weakness by using different abilities. This concept is going to be implemented in other important enemy types as well.

In addition to your telekinesis and terraforming powers there will be 3 magical elements you will learn to control during the game: wind, fire and essence. Each of which you can use in two different ways. Cast a magical element directly into the environment or enhance objects that you pick up with it. These elements react with each other by creating a new spell when combined, there will be a total of 7 spells and additionally 7 enhancements you have control over. These, together with telekinesis and terraforming comprise your vast powers!

Another really wonderful feature is the possibility to invite your friends to continue a game together, you started alone in single player. Or vice versa, you can continue a multiplayer save game in single player mode!

$170.000 ~ Additional Spells

You are going to be able to choose between different variations of an elemental spell, called Specializations. So for example with fire magic: choose to have a long range fire spell, or maybe a broad short range fire cone or even a very directed fire burst that deals a lot of damage which is harder to land in return.

You can choose a Specialization for each of the elements, this way you’ll be able to customize your character according to your way of playing and get the most out of it. This basically grants you a total of 3 different fire spells, 3 different wind spells and 3 different essence spells to choose from, which triples the total amount of available spells in the game.

Risks and challenges Learn about accountability on Kickstarter

The biggest challenge we have is that we are a distributed team. As we are dispersed all around the globe we have to deal with things like language barriers, cultural differences and of course time zones.

Fortunately we are learning to use this to our advantage. At any time around the world, people are continuously working on Son of Nor. When a team member wakes up in Austria, he'll find new work done by colleges in the US or Ecuador. We have been working like this for over a year now and as you can see in our weekly developer diaries, we are able to achieve an excellent and continuous progress!

Creating video games is not a piece of cake. It's hard work. It takes many people of different trades, many iterations, lots of testing and lots and lots of time, which means that it's quite possible that we hit a setback.

But don't panic! We are a fully committed team which is also fully committed to bringing the game to you. We'll be constantly updating and posting news about the status of this project through social networks and regular e-mail updates. We also invite and encourage you to comment or contact us, we are eager to get feedback and get to know every single one of you!
