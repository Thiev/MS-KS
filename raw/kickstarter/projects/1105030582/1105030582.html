Over at PawByte we're pretty big Otakus and wanted to wonder what would be the result of a lovechild of Dungeon Keeper, Code Geass and Death Note. Fans are awaiting sequel/reboot for each, so why not make our own imagery of how that lovechild would look if destiny somehow allowed it. With that you have Underground Throne.

Adbarlia is an unstable nation. After the government passed a law to harvest necessary organs from people who skipped on their bounty, you discover there is an entire new country underground living in anarchy. The Adbarlian government does not wish to get involved in regulating the literal underground world, so they simply pass a law to succeed it from the nation. What this means is that the underground world is literally it's own nation, but who will rule and reign over this new found nation?

Start your own nation and reign as Supreme Ruler

As a young arrogant person you intend to become the ruler of this new nation. After receiving the power to teleport objects and organisms by a suspicious mystical creature your form an army to set his goal into a reality. As of now you have yet to tell your family from fear they will be in danger or attempt to talk you out of it, but your definition of justice is being the supreme ruler of your world. There are other people with your same ambitions, but you do not believe in partnerships only for people to obey you.

Aside from hiding this secret from your family, the Adbarlian governments and agencies are noticing evidence of your power and are attempting to track down the personal responsible for your actions. In a bizarre twist you find yourself constantly sneaking back to your Adbarlian home to divert attention from yourself. How long can this last and what do your friends think you are doing ?

Key Gameplay mechanics

Dynamic Missions

-Instead of just kill your rival or the heroes, missions will be based also in exploration

Minion Merger Room

-This key feature will allow you to splice separate minions into one minion. This will be a crucial requirement on certain levels

Different abilities per character.

-Most of the powers aka spells are different except for the core 5 spells

--More info coming as they are built

Active Dungeon Master

 

-One big change is that you yourself will be able to play within the game

-You will have your own abilities and can not be placed or drop

--Mechanically when DM is killed the game is lost

--Mechanically a creature that can only be self-possessed and not a DM

-Mechanically the DM defends your dungeon throne when not selected

-In online play you can change your DM.

Heavy Drama

-Energy will help to level the playing field when you are broke or visa versa.

--When running out of energy/gold and carrying creatures they drop from your hand.

-Procedural generated levels

-Mod support

-Ranked Multiplayer matches

-My special dungeon mode

--This is a continuous dungeon you manage which is connected to the PBU world. Be careful as your dungeon can suffer constant invasion from trolls. :D

Online and Local Offline Multiplayer

I quit my job to go full time indie back in December 2013. I do not have the funds to pay for the staff needed to make this game, thus I'm asking the KickStarter universe to join me as we build this game.

Game Development costs money. It is that simple. Our goal is not to become rich from this franchise, but simply give gamers the experience they've been yearning for. 

The purpose of this campaign is to pay for artists to upgrade the placeholder art you see in this campaign and video. 

We have a reasonable and responsible budget to pay our team for graphics and audio as well as licensing and convention costs. 

KickStarter has never failed us and it is the perfect platform to unite gamers under one banner.

Risks and challenges Learn about accountability on Kickstarter

Our greatest challenge will be graphic support. We have created things on shoestring budgets, but never in 3D before. When you play the game, we do not expect you to say it is the best looking game you've ever played; but boy will the graphics match and present the atmosphere of the game we wish to give you.

As with most video game projects, we can not properly forsee the future and the amount of delays that may arise while making this game. We have prepared for that and will release the game through iterations via the Alpha Prime service.

PawByte is also working on another project titled Agriduel (aka Fields of Fresh). Agriduel is a 2D RPG title that is nearing it's completion. PawByte's staff has more than doubled since the beginning of starting Agriduel and is able to create multiple projects at the same time. Due to the Agriduel being a 2D game and Underground Throne being made in 3D team members are being split into separate projects; with the exception of programming, HUD art and voice acting.

FAQ

  • Is $8,000 too much for this project?

    By no means. Nathan(the lead programmer) will be eating Ramen noodles while haggling his artists and musicians to contribute lovely art for the game. $8,000 will get the game nicely done 3D models, textures, HUD graphics as well as some upcoming voice actors.

    Last updated: Mon, Mar 10 2014 1:02 pm EDT
  • Is $8,000 too low for this project?

    No, this goal is set to pay our dedicated team to create 3D models, concept art as well as GUI graphics. We are also participating in OUYA's Free-The-Games Fund which will double our game's funding once it is released onto the OUYA.

    Last updated: Sat, Mar 15 2014 7:01 am EDT
  • What's up with those graphics?

    The main purpose of this KickStarter is to pay our artist to change them. In previous projects we had them work on things with a huge wait until payment; but it is not the best practice for developing a game. They are living people and it requires large amounts of time for them to design great art. We will prefer to pay them as soon as their tasks are done; not months afterwords. Life is tough, why make it tougher on them?

    Last updated: Mon, Mar 10 2014 5:23 pm EDT
  • Will there be bacon in the game?

    Yes, the cafeteria area will serve your minions bacon.

    Last updated: Sat, Mar 15 2014 7:55 am EDT
  • What do I do in the game?

    You explore, expand your dominion and combat other dungeon masters along the way. There are also neutral hostiles that will need to be eliminated and custom missions that do not revolve around combat.

    In AlphaPrime Version 0.25, the combat system is unavailable; but V0.26 will include combat(slated for released on Thurs 3/20/2014). Back the campaign and watch it evolve.

    Last updated: Tue, Mar 18 2014 7:48 pm EDT
  • What does Peter Molyneux think of this game?

    On twitter he wished us good luck, we're still trying to reach out and get a deeper conversation with him.

    Last updated: Tue, Mar 18 2014 7:48 pm EDT
