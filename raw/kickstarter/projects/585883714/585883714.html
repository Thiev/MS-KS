Me and my Little Company (start small, dream big!)

So, why have I decided to start up my own games company?

Well, I've faced three redundancies in three years. That sucks. Secondly, I'm a great fan of storytelling, and I feel that games are a fantastic interactive medium for telling stories, especially when there are branching storylines.

I do this because I love it. I'm never going to get rich from making 2D RPGs, of that I am certain. However it would be incredible if I could build a big enough fan base to be able to do this full-time. I am humbled by the fact that anyone wants to play my games, in all honesty.

My brainchild; the #data//hacker series is a three-parter, and is based around an online game called 'Online World.' I draw a lot of inspiration from games such as Final Fantasy and the .hack series, but am sure to make this project my own.

Initiation follows Jay as he seeks revenge on the Administrators of Online World, who cruelly banished him from the beloved fantasy realm after a case of mistaken identity. Jay soon becomes entangled in the mess of corruption and deceit led by the infamous corporation.Â 

I expect to be releasing the final version of Initiation in March 2013; the project is coming along steadily.Â 

Corruption continues Jay's story after a big moral decision, which effectively makes this title two complete games in one. Players can also transfer their save file from Initiation to keep their party, inventory and achievements intact! However it can be said that Corruption will be playable without an Initiation save file, and without priorÂ knowledgeÂ of the game multiverse or lore.

Reboot - I can't really reveal too much about this title on account of spoiling the first two, however I can tell you that the plan is to develop this title in 3D, perhaps in the Unity engine.

What about The Real World? - 2015 saw the worldâs economy crash with an almighty roar. One-by-one, countries and their governments fell into disarray as the people lost faith in politics. Soon, it became anarchy, with every man, woman and child left to fend for themselves. It was not long before destitute countries began invading each other for what little resources were still left in the world, and by 2017 much of the worldâs population had declined as a result of these conflicts.

While the game projects cover what happens within Online World, I also hope to be filming a couple of short films to help supplement this post-apocalyptic feel. These films will be directed by a good friend of mine, whom I have known for some years, and will be centred around people living in this desolate cityscape image we have created, as the inhabitants alternate between menial, almost pointless jobs, and playing Online World.

There are also potential plans to produce a supplemental graphic novel or strip, which is one of my stretch goals.

Initiation is the bulk of the project at the moment. Once Initiation is complete, the framework will also be present for Corruption (and perhaps pt1 of Reboot). Core features of Initiation are as follows:

A Branching, Immersive Storyline with a wide range of memorable characters that you can interact with on many levels

Continue your save file from Initiation into Corruption

An estimated 20+ hours of core plot content, with optional content making up many, many more hours.

Dungeon-based exploration, with each and every dungeon feeling unique and different from the last (no copy-paste dungeons like in .hack)

Dynamic world that changes based on your actions whilst playing

Recruitment system that allows you to make significant tactical choices

300+ Items of equipment, with over 30 classes to wear them, each with their own unique skillset.Â 

Generic classes mature depending on your actions into unique classes such as the Noireknight, Dracokin, Bladedancer or Astromancer (and many more)

Lots of optional content including locales, bosses, dungeons, real estate, DodoBaird racing and a featured Card game named Trioarch!

I've already put my own money into the project, but my resources are limited (I have children to think about). That's why I'm here: to connect with gamers that can be as excited about this series as I am!

Risks and challenges Learn about accountability on Kickstarter

The only risk here is the scope of the project affecting the release date. It's pretty big! But there's always help on hand, and I remain confident that I can hit my March 2013 launch. I would never release an unfinished game, because I would personally hate to receive one myself, so if I do have to push back the release, rest assured that it is simply because I refuse to release a sub-standard product.

On the other hand, you all have access to the BETAs upon release, so you can see for yourselves how the project is coming along.

The main challenge I have to overcome is fitting the project around my family life. I'm only working part-time at the moment so that gives me plenty of free time to work on the project; every free minute I get.

I have a large network of graduates and friends are are willing to help me should I stumble at any point. I have a fantastic wife who spurs me on at every moment and ensures that I keep to the work schedule. I also have massive self-motivation to make this dream a reality! I want this finished as much as you guys!
