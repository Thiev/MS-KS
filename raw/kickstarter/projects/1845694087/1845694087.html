In Colonisation: Moonbase you will build and grow a colony on the lunar surface in this construction and management game for Android

Please note that all game footage and screenshots are of the game in its ALPHA stage - this means that what you see is not final and lots of improvements will be made before release!

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

This is an indie game, made by myself with input from a couple of other people.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Have you ever looked up in to the night sky and wondered if you could colonise the moon? Could you manage and sustain a human population on humanities next frontier?

Colonisation: Moonbase is a 3D mobile game for Smart Phones and Tablets running the Android operating system, presented in glorious full HD.

Go where few have gone and do what no one has done before.

Build your base on the lunar surface to support and grow a colony of humans.

Feed them, keep them warm, give them oxygen and in return they will support you in your goal of creating advanced technology and mining the Moons resources for use back on Earth?

Can you make a successful base and become rich beyond your wildest dreams or will you find out the hard way that humans need more oxygen than you thought? Find out how well you can handle life on the next frontier with Colonisation: Moon Base!

Do you remember those classic games like Theme Park and SimCity?
Colonisation: Moonbase takes what these games where and puts them into a futuristic setting with an update visual style and feel.

Apart from the visuals the game has some very unique features that differ from the games mentioned above. Unlike most city building games Moonbase is set in a completely new location with new challenges.

You won't have the old classic grid placement, instead the game will allow you to place buildings anywhere you like so long as there is enough room! - in turn making each map look unique and amazing, allowing you to be completely flexible in your design - it also adds to the challenge of finding or reserving space for larger buildings!

The game will also have a much stronger focus on strategy and resource management whilst also making it accessible to casual gamers with a varied amount of difficulty as you progress!

Plan your space well and prioritise what you need or you could find yourself out of space and unable to complete your objectives.

In addition, you will have access to over 70 buildings and 10 real lunar locations to build your base and grow your colony.

By exploring the moon in 'flight mode' you can take control of the camera and fly around your colony. Take in the breathtaking scenery of the lunar surface; highly accurate and mapped to real locations on the moon!

We have design Colonisation: Moonbase to be playable in long or short bursts. There is always something to do, such as building more structures, unlocking new buildings or growing your population, Alternatively you could sit back and let your resources build up!

And if that is not enough you could always go fly around your colony and admire all your hard work.

The game also has an auto-save feature that will save your progress regularly, so if you want to stop after 10 minutes you can always come back and continue where you left off!

The game also includes a tutorial to help you learn the basics, but we are sure you will pick it up in no time.

In short, NO the game is not pay to win!

With all those different options for backing, what does the standard game come with and what do the extras do?

The game will be free on the google play store, with in-game purchases and in-game advertisements, where you can then purchase the resources and buy add free.

You will be able to play the full game with no limitations although it might take you longer to complete and maybe a bit more difficult with limiting resources. We want everyone to enjoy the game and have fun - but we can't make it completely free!

The £2 Kickstarter pledge will have no in-app advertising but will have to pay for any resources you may wish to purchase. (no compulsory purchases to complete the game!)

Cheat system?
Exclusive to Kickstarter! For being an early bird and getting in on the action before the game is finished we are offering a cheat system. Instead of paying in-game for resources, you get unlimited resources forever! It will then be up to you if you decide to cheat or if you would play the game as normal - The option will be there to change as you like. You will still have to make sure you have enough resources to support and grow your colony - but you can add them for free.

Sandbox mode!
Unlike the cheat system you won't ever have to worry about your resources and keeping them up to maintain your colony, this version comes with everything costing 0 to purchase and 0 to maintain. - you will have the choice to turn this mode on or off at the beginning of a new career. (you can always choose to use the cheat system if you disable sandbox mode)

Alpha and Beta?
Can't wait until the game is complete? or if you simply want to give some early feedback and help shape the game into something bigger, then you can get early access. Again this is EXCLUSIVE to Kickstarter!
The alpha will be given out immediately after a successful Kickstarter project.
Both of these will be in the form of an emailed .APK file for your android device to install.



As the current build is in alpha there are some things missing or incomplete, one of the major things that will be improved is the building detail, a few of them are complete whilst others do not have any textures (you was wondering about those purple buildings right?)

All buildings will have lights/windows on them making them feel like they are occupied and also looking cool at night time!

UI Text - We are currently trying to make the UI text readable on all screen sizes as this is proving difficult with the amount of information being shown at any given time. Currently some of the text is too long as there have been recent changes to the font.

UI Help - We are also still working on some aspects of the UI including more feedback help such as warnings when resources are low and coloured text so you can easily spot any issues your base may have.
Currently there are some help systems in place - semi included in the video but would like to point them out.

Clicking on your resources: - When you click on your resources a menu will appear showing you the total in/out as well as the top 5 producers of that resource and the bottom 5 producers of that resource. Clicking on these will then send you to that building to help you identify it.

Purchasing buildings:- When purchasing buildings your finger may be covering the building when placing it, at the top there is a secondary indicator to show you if the current location is a stable place to build.

You may of also noticed that when your placing the building the costs appear below the resource, showing if you have enough, this helps to identify when you have enough and reminds you the cost so you do not have to go back to the menu to look

Missions - These will be a set of tasks and objectives to complete where you will receive rewards once completed, there will also be a story plot within some of the missions, can you keep the peace, what will you discover along the way? is it possible to fully colonise the moon?

Tutorial - The tutorial is mostly complete but was excluded from the video as the purpose of the video was to demonstrate the current state of the game. - rest assured that the game will be easy to grasp with discovery of useful features throughout the game, making it accessible to anyone who would like to play the game.

Transport ships & Trade - These will allow you to trade with earth to buy or sell extra resources, you will see the ships take off and land, adding some activity to the game.

Civilian ships - These will not have a function, but instead will be like the people and cars from other city building games - giving the feeling that there is now life on the lunar surface.

Post screenshots to Facebook - You will be able to post your screenshots from the game directly to your Facebook account, where you can do what you wish, maybe save them and make it a desktop background? or just to show your friends your awesome moonbase design!

From the video and screenshots you may of seen that there are a lot of the same buildings (especially those water mining outposts!) when the game goes into beta we will be testing the balance of the game and gathering feedback, our aim is to balancing the resources so its still a challenge but so you don't struggle and have to build the same things over and over - because that’s boring!

You will be able to build over 70 different buildings - over 50 of which will be completely different and unique to each other.

As this is a HD 3D game we want to cater for all devices, even those that would normally struggle with high end games such as this so we have implemented some graphics options to allow more people to enjoy the game.

Currently the minimum requirements is an android device with touch screen capabilities running android 2.2 Froyo or higher.

As for device specification for memory and CPU, we are not 100% sure at this point, though on the lowest settings the game should be playable on a device with 256mb of ram our current recommended is 512mb of ram.

We will update all Kickstarter backers with the system requirements if they change.

Risks and challenges Learn about accountability on Kickstarter

Android devices come in all shapes and sizes from phones to tablets - some cost £50 and some cost £500, unfortunately we can not guarantee compatibility with all devices.
That said we are trying our best to make sure the game will run on as many android devices as possible. We currently have a select handful of varying devices for testing purposes, ranging from the HTC desire to the Sony Xperia S.

As the game is a demanding 3D HD game there are several graphical options in the games settings to try and cater for all devices big and small.

We are also always looking into ways to improve performance and do everything we can to keep the requirements as low as possible.

Please note that all game footage and screenshots are of the game in its ALPHA stage - this means that what you see is not final and lots of improvements will be made before release!
