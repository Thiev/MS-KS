THE FOLLOWING IS A CONDENSED VERSION OF THE OFFICIAL BUSINESS PLAN FOR THE DEVELOPMENT OF GOLDEN DRAGON GAME ZONE. SHOULD ANYONE HAVE AN APPROVED NEED TO REVIEW A COMPLETE VERSION, A FORMAL REQUEST CAN BE MADE BY E-MAIL.

Mission statement

Golden Dragon Game Zone will be a unique, one-of-a-kind family entertainment facility aimed toward encouraging quality family interaction by utilizing current and past gaming trends set in a safe, awesome and intriguing atmosphere.

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Most businesses stem from a need. When many individuals can be noted for saying, “I wish there was a (particular type of business) in our town” someone, consequently, will use this as an incentive to start such a business. Whenever this, proposed, business is explained to someone, they become extremely excited. Why do so many individuals become so excited? Because everyone is a gamer of sorts and like to have a good time. In addition, times are hard and gaming is the number one mode of release and escape from the stress and frustrations of everyday life. We plan to aid individuals find relief from that stress.

Golden Dragon Game Zone is not just a business idea; it is a labor of love. Families need affordable, safe and fun ways to spend time with their loved ones, especially their children. This is why Golden Dragon Game Zone (GZ) will be a one-of-a-kind, family gaming/entertainment center that will astound and thoroughly please all who enter. An example of this is how patrons will be greeted by a life-size, smoke-snorting Golden Dragon (Which represents honorable behavior, justice and promotes goodness).

This establishment will consist of several different gaming areas or “Zones”. An all inclusive Arcade Zone will mesmerize patrons while the Collectable Card Game Zone (CCG) and other one-on-one table game areas will appeal to the more relaxed and strategic minded individual.The Computer Gaming room will be equipped with state-of-the-art P.C.’s and Gaming Pods featuring current and vintage games. Additional “Zones” will facilitate Laser Tag, billiards, Air Hockey, Magic the Gathering card game tournaments, a role-playing game room (RPG) for D&D and other role-playing games, a rock climbing wall, as well as a Food and a Retail Zone.

Future plans include a track for radio-controlled cars, a go-cart track, bumper cars, an extreme play area for young children (Kid Zone), Paint Ball, an indoor park and much more. (See Section 7 for details.)

A concrete and definite reason for such a business is because Gaming is the business of the future!According to Research Associate Peter D. Hart, more than 1/3 (35%) of parents play computer and video games and 93% of these parents have children who also play them. So, who exactly are the gamers? Everyone! “According to data compiled by the NPD Group, a global market research company, and released by the Entertainment Software Association, the computer and video game industry sold 273million units back in 2009 leading to an astounding $10.5 billion in revenue” (http://www.esrb.org/about/video-game-industry-statistics.jsp).

No one can dispute that industry trends for electronic entertainment and gaming are UP.In essence, gaming and electronic related entertainment is the business of the future and it’s not about to slow down. Venues offered by GZ will add to and enhance affordable, community entertainment opportunities, utilizing this fast growing industry.

WhileNathaniel Young will serve as Proprietary Owner it is important to note the facility will be primarily, co-managed and operated by family members. We feel this gives us a distinct advantage in that since we operate as a close family we can easily and enthusiastically furnish a family atmosphere and services unmatched by other gaming facilities.

Perspective customersinclude individuals of all age, sex, and cultures. Anthropology reveals gaming to be about as old as humans themselves. Individuals work hard to maintain an existence but playtime is necessary to balance out the emotional needs of an individual. All work and no play can create emotional disorders and depression.

Ancient civilizations went to great lengths to facilitate gaming activities and modern people are no different. All that has changed is the technology. The drive to play is the same but advanced technology now provides endless ways to have fun. In short, everyone is a potential customer.

An analysis of competitors has revealed that there is not one established facility that will incorporate as many gaming choices as will Golden Dragon Game Zone, nor have any created an atmosphere such as the one Golden Dragon Game Zone purposes to create.After looking up gaming stores or parlors, in as many ways possible on the internet, not one has been found to come close to matching the variety, ambiance or excitement Golden Dragon Game Zone (GZ) promises to produce.

It is not our intention to specifically “compete”with other businesses but rather to cooperate with other business of our community. A detailed and comprehensive plan has been assembled whereby we will endeavor to join with other local businesses to aid with mutual promotion. We feel the more we get to know our business neighbors the easier it will be to make contact with future patrons. Involvement in local charity will also be a way for GZ to make contact with the public. This is an area we are very excited and anxious to participate in. We will particularly sponsor and aid in child related organizations.

One aspect of our Marketing Plan, to reach potential customers, will include use of any and all free representation we can obtain. We have found this to be an area we are very successful in. On staff is a seasoned writer experienced in developing press releases and human interest stories for publication. There are ways to secure free coverage on local T.V. and radio as well. Michigan is a unique place where festivals, parades, and other special events provide local businesses places to show off their wares and services. We plan to make ourselves quite visible in all local regions and will distribute thousands of fliers with coupons. After a combined 44 years of business experience we know how significant and successful fliers can be; they are far more effective then radio advertising and far less expensive.

Our unique management team has had many years of successful, self-employed business experience dating back to 1979. We possess tried-and-true skills acquired both in the field and College/University classroom. Between our immediate staff we have acquired 5 Associate degrees, 4 Bachelor degrees and 2 Master Degrees. We have paid our dues scholastically and within the proprietary workplace. We are proud to proclaim, all the businesses our family had established, over the years, were successful and profitable.

Projected costs/expenses for the initial set-up of Golden Dragon Game Zone, within this document, have been established to be high-end figures. Calculations indicate the facility would have to bring in around 12K a month, or $3,000 per week, to cover monthly expenses. Daily, this would break down to (depending on whether we are open 6 or 7 days a week) about $428.00 to $500.00 a day to cover expenses. We are aiming for an initial $250,000.00 budget to begin this project. With leeway provided, this would allow for a cushion of about $86,000.00 for unexpected expenses and expansion.

Key “Zones” will bring in the most revenue. These would include the large Arcade, Laser Tag, and Computer Game Zones. Ideally, the other “Zones” would provide the profit needed to cover unexpected expenses and to put toward future growth. Additional revenue will be generated from the Food and Retail Zones and from unique events including special appearances from well-known gaming celebrities, gaming competitions (competitors pay entry fees), and game related conventions (Detail of such events are discussed in Section 4). For a facility such as this, ideas for, revenue generating, events are limitless.

We don’t just imagine such a facility, we feel it! Many told Walt Disney he was crazy to build an amusement park. Look what his vision led to. There are an endless number of events, which can be related to the Gaming industry. Future plans include moving GZ into a much larger, custom built, facility to accommodate such events. This center will be an incredible, all-enclosed, year-round, family oriented facility that will have something special for children and adults of all ages.

BACKGROUND OF BUSINESS PRINCIPLES (Origin of Business Idea)

As he assisted with management duties of the family business in the 90’s, Nathaniel Young could see a need for a safe place for young people to go to, for entertainment. Our store was a one-of-a-kind establishment in the middle of a little Arizona cow town. Desert Emporium carried a full line of animals, pet foods, supplies, and after six months developed a sizable music sales department plus Nathan took a small area of the store to sell used games and gaming systems he purchased at swap meets. He had a Sega and Super Nintendo system set up so kids could try out the games before purchasing them. He also began to sell comic books. This is when he realized that a Game Zone type-of-business would be a good venture. Gaming was in its infancy and the concept of paint-ball was just getting started. Now, the gaming industry is a multi-billion dollar industry. This was back in 1993 but the concept of a Game Zone continued to brew within him.

With the technology available now and the fact Nathan is highly proficient in computers and all gaming venues, he has a distinct advantage toward creating and managing the Game Zone he has envisioned. He, and his brother Jason, know what gamers want and need.

All family members involved with this project have experienced working for others and for themselves. There exists a long history of entrepreneur success and at some point in time, at different age levels; all have experienced and exhibited superior business management and excel in customer service techniques.

Independently, older brother Nathan and younger brother Jason had both come up with the idea of a gaming parlor. Nathan was currently living in and working for the State of Delaware but when they learned both wanted to build such a business a partnership was born. Their goal is to develop and produce the ultimate and most amazing, family oriented, gaming/entertainment facility ever experienced in West Michigan.

This is not just a business. It is a labor of love. Families need affordable and safe ways to spend time with their loved ones, especially their children. The goal of Golden Dragon Game Zone is to promote and facilitate family connections and involvement in order to help preserve and improve family structure. It is well documented that children with involved parents perform better in school and have a more positively developed sense of self-esteem and self-efficacy. An establishment such as Golden Dragon Game Zone will facilitate this process. Additional employment opportunities will also be provided for locals.

The establishment of such a facility will also provide our family resources to aid local food banks, the Michigan Special Olympics, sponsorship in local sport, Big Brother/Sister programs, High School Work Internship programs, and other charities in this region. We are very passionate toward giving back to our community.

DESCRIPTION OF THE BUSINESS (Resumed)

Golden Dragon Game Zone will be a one-of-a-kind family gaming/entertainment center that will astound all who enter. It will possess an atmosphere of amazement, excitement and pure awesomeness! As previously mentioned, all who enter will be greeted by the life-size, smoke-snorting Golden Dragon and patrons will feel as though they have stepped into another realm.Specific considerations will be given to lighting effects and décor to produce an atmosphere that will transcend each designated zone into a catalyst of excitement, fellowship and imagination.

To recap or reiterate, Golden Dragon Game Zone will consist of several different gaming facilities or “Zones”. The all inclusive Arcade Zone will mesmerize patrons while the Collectable Card Game Zone (CCG) and other one-on-one table game areas will appeal to the more relaxed and strategic minded individual. The Computer Gaming room will be equipped with state-of-the-art P.C.’s and gaming pods featuring current and some well-liked, vintage games. Additional “Zones” will facilitate billiards, Air Hockey, Magic the Gathering card games and tournaments, role-playing game room (RPG) for D&D and other role-playing games, rock climbing walls, a food court, retail items, a track for radio-controlled cars, a go-cart track, bumper cars, an extreme play area for young children (Kid Zone), Laser Tag, Paint Ball, and a Zone for an assortment of family oriented board games (all provided). Tickets acquired from games, designed to reward players, can be used to redeem prizes in the “Ticket Redemption Zone” area.

Future plans include miniature golf, and a lavish indoor “Park Zone” with a Carousel, goldfish pond with a bridge, gaming character sculptures and unique playground systems. The Chess/Checkers Den Zone will be secluded with a lush, old world style décor.

Golden Dragon Game Zone will also be a site for special events. Events we hope to present would include but would not be limited to, Robotic Competitions (such as thru the Robot Fighting League of Peoria, Ill. and other supporters), gaming conventions, Pin Ball Expos, Wizards of the Coast sponsored CCG tournaments, various performances, Cosplay and other conventions, yearly charitable costume balls, special holiday events and much, much more.

By creating a fun-filled environment for all ages, the goal is to aid individuals with affordable (and many free) activities that can facilitate closer family ties.

Figures obtained for 2009 with regards to how the gaming industry has boomed. According to the Entertainment Software Rating Board the gaming industry took in $10.5 BILLION in revenue. By 2013 a considerable increase can be logically expected. In retrospect, gaming and electronic related entertainment is the business of the future and it’s not about to slow down. PRODUCTS/SERVICES TO BE OFFERED

Our management/planning committee has comprised a 3-ring binder 4 inches thick to contain the multitude of ideas and detailed plans for each individual “Zone”. Plans for current and future operations have been carefully and thoroughly documented to allow for expansion of GZ. Initial, “Zones” to be established will include the following.

Computer Repair/Service (The “Repair Bay”)

Managed/operatedby seasoned and college graduate, computer technician Jason Young, this division of GZ will provide complete diagnostics, repairs, upgrades, building of custom computers, sales of supplies and more including a home entertainment system set-up service .Only fully trained/educated, insured and competent computer techs will be employed. A select number of professionally trained individuals, known for their expertise, have been selected to allow GZ Computer Repair service to be the best in Montcalm County. At this point there are no known professionally established and dependable computer repair/service businesses in this region. GZ will set the standard for customer satisfaction and service.

Gaming Zones, Arcades, Interactive Activities

State-of-the-art arcade games and game simulator pods will be utilized along with many beloved vintage gaming models. It is the goal to provide an assortment of games which will appeal to most individuals from Sci-Fi, racing, sports, fantasy and any other venue one could imagine.

Interactive games include activities that range from (CCG) Collectable Card Games (Magic the Gathering, Yu-Gi-Oh, Pokemon etc…), (RPG) Role-Playing games( D&D, Star Wars, Warhammer ), , board games (chess/checkers, Monopoly, Children’s games , Battleship, and many more ) computer gaming (w/ LAN capabilities), radio controlled vehicles, Laser Tag, Robot Combat (College level and professional league competition) and more.

Very few gaming-oriented business are making an attempt to do what GZ plans to create and all fall very short when it comes to the totality of gaming, proposed by this business plan. We will provide the environment and atmosphere needed to make each “Zone” distinctively special, inviting and exciting.

Retail Zone

Game related items will be featured in this zone. An inventory of T-Shirts and other apparel, featuring gaming related icons/sayings will be carried. Also, games, dice, dice bags, Magic the Gathering paraphernalia, fantasy items, action figures, music (CD’s), gaming books, miniatures, card sheaths, artwork, toys, and so on will be retailed. (The list is far too long to include here)

Special Events

There is a lack of viable and exciting live entertainment in which an entire family may experience. It is the goal of GZ to accommodate the community with monthly, special events that will appeal to and supply a unique, fun-filled day or evening, for families. Special events (many for charitable purposes) to be planned will include, costume balls, costume contests, live entertainment, celebrity showcasing, pinball conventions, showcasing of local talent, Robot League competitions, Gaming Olympics, Chess and Magic the Gathering tournaments, Cosplay events, various game related conventions, and so much more. Throughout the year, holiday related activities will be sponsored. Again, our ideas, on paper, are far too numerous to list in this report. Trust us, the ideas we have are awesome!

Food Court (Zone)

It is the choice of GZ’s management to provide foods and drinks in a primarily, self-serve type of cafeteria venue. This way, costs can be kept to a minimum and will make it less expense for a family to take a food break. The atmosphere and décor, however, will be exceptional and very inviting to patrons.Foods GZ will provide include popcorn, drinks, chips, candy, pre-made sandwiches, pizza, hot dogs, chili dogs, chili fries, nachos…all provided in a calm and comfortable dining environment for all. We will not decorate the Food Zone in bright reds, oranges or yellow colors because these are fast food colors to psychologically encourage patrons to hurry up and get out.

Community Support

Activities and participation in community affairs and organizations had been important aspects of family business enterprises of the past. Many years of support and leadership activity within the many levels of Scouting and the 4-H organization was performed. As a close-knit family the awareness for support for children in need weights heavy in our minds. Golden Dragon Game Zone is committed to aid and support organizations such as, soft ball leagues, toy collection projects for the holidays, mentoring programs, children’s medical support activities/fund raisers, food banks, and if possible, hopes to initiate local Internship/Apprentice employment programs with High Schools, and other local employment opportunity programs.

SECTION 9: BIBLIOGRAPHY

Target Market and Location

Surprising results were obtained by searching population and regional growth records via www.point2homes.com/US/.../MI/Greenville-Demographics.htm, with regards to choosing a location for Golden Dragon Game Zone. By searching the demographics of the Montcalm, Michigan area, population comparisons were made between Greenville (the proposed site), Big Rapids, Ionia, Belding, and Alma Michigan. Surprisingly, it was found Greenville has the fastest, steady growing population in Michigan. This is fortunate in that Greenville is only a few miles from where we live, and we know it well. It is a very nice community.

Expansion Plans

It is pointed out in Section 2, how future plans include moving GZ into a much larger, all-enclosed facility which will provide incredible opportunities for outrageous fun. Enhanced plans include the indoor park with a Carousel, gold fish pond and bridge, Bumper cars, go-carts, larger arcade area, gaming convention facility, Robot League competition arena and grandstand, performance/entertainment venue, radio controlled vehicle tracks, Kid Play Zone, Laser Tag, Paint Ball, Gaming Museum, Theatre, Rock Climbing Zone, and upgradedRetail and Food Court Zones.

Unique holiday and other special events will be added to provide greater variety of fun and affordable entertainment for families. Again, examples include gaming conventions, Cosplay events, guest entertainers and personalities, unique Winter Holiday events, costume balls for Halloween, with annual New Year’s, Valentine’s Day and Spring Festival dances. In addition, we would like to participate in sponsoring a local Scrabble competition in conjunction with the National Scrabble Association.

Electronic Entertainment Expos (E3’s) are increasing across the country and we would like to host one as well. Conventions of this sort draw thousands of participants from all over the world.We hope to build GZ in size and reputation to the point where we can host a major Expo each year for Mid Michigan. Additional popular conventions include Comicons, Dragoncon, Gamescon, and Mojang. (Actually new events are being created all over the Country, each year)

Gaming and all its many, many aspects is the business of the future. This industry is taking off like a rocket and we plan to strap in and head for the stars.

Management Operations and Family Involvement

Unique and diverse artistic abilities abide in our family. All possess experience in communication, leadership and entrepreneurship. Signs, designs, planning, decorating needs, construction, bookkeeping, art work, promotional needs, advertising, web design and maintenance, computer programming and upkeep, purchasing, customer relations, legal matters and more will be dealt with by family members. Most of our family members are descendents of 17th century, Scottish Highlanders (The Youngs) and operate under clannish style principles. We are all highly independent and strong willed yet draw strength from one another regularly because we are motivated and upheld by family strength. We work well and collectively together yet do not hesitate to offer different ideas to one another for discussion.

Even though our region was hit hard by the relocation of 3 major businesses to Mexico a few years ago, our region in particular has bounced back and has showed definite and steady growth of businesses and population. The recent building of new businesses, restaurants, and banks indicates there is enough population to support new major development. We plan to be a part of this new growth.

Our family has been successful in producing profitable businesses during the past 3 decades and will apply what has been learned toward this endeavor as well. It is important to employ low cost/free tactics for advertising and to only purchase what is needed to move forward. A business may be started successfully by only purchasing what is absolutely needed. Waste of any resources in connection to a business is unacceptable. Strict budgets need to be made and adhered to but spare funds need to be available in case of emergencies or other unexpected and unavoidable expenses. Profits will go toward improving and upgrading Game Zone and, when possible, will be applied toward the needs of local organizations and programs GZ becomes involved with.

Weekly management meetings with “Zone” supervisors and monthly employee meetings will be held to upgrade procedures and customer service. It will be important GZ maintains a grasp of what our public wants and that their needs are reasonably supplied by upgrading and conforming to new and better standards and service.
