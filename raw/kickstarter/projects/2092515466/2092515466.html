Welcome to the Medieval Future. Play an immersive fine art video game that leaves the player in a daze of future glow.

globHAMMER is a video game focused art collective based in Brooklyn, New York. We seek to push the horizon of what video games can express by creating a beautiful, never ending game. These ideas have driven us to design our latest fine art video game, SRC 1515.

The Human Touch

 SRC 1515 utilizes traditional art techniques to create beautiful imagery. Nothing is left to chance except the outcome of the challenge.

High Octane Visuals 

Graphics render best at 1080p on Windows, Macintosh, and Linux computer screens. For best results, play on a modern desktop or laptop with a graphics card.

Immersive Experience

globHammer removes all icons, buttons, or status bars that distract the viewer/player from enjoying the visual pleasure of the game’s immersive graphics.

Color Palettes

SRC 1515 is made out of extremely bright neon colors. We have no shame when it comes to using a glow tool. You can't miss it!!!

Imaginative Retro-Future Environment

In a world where blades are fashioned by thought energy, the Medieval Future World is set in a sci-fi realm filled with relics of the past and techno advancements of the future.

Puzzles

The game contains 15 different puzzles. How do you summon the Worm God to protect Minrva from the relentless C.A.R.E.TAKER? Can you train your reflexes so you never connect with the falling and fatal LoggerNogs? Is there enough Hyper-Glide Wet to keep the Psycho-Punks from following you to the Cigbacacco Trees?

Mature Themes

Love. Death. Drugs. Greed. Source. In the Medieval Future nothing, is off limits.

Built on Open Internet Technologies

SRC 1515 is made out of the Internet itself. It is built out of javascript code and HTML. As long as the Internet exists you will be able to play the game over and over.

If you want to play the game from beginning to end, get serious and refrain from dying.

If you prefer to skip around the story, die often.

The goal of the game is to solve the puzzles. With each puzzle resolved, a new layer of Minrva’s story is revealed. Puzzle-solving may require the use of physics, world cosmology, or Minrva’s reflexes. The environment itself might also provide answers or advance the story.

Sometimes, dying is a puzzle itself.

Headlines on Royalty Reality TVC read: Odd Future Child of Royalty, Born With Mystical, Fleshless Arm.

At birth, Minrva narrowly escapes the wrath of her father King's Psycho-Blade. Unable to end her life before it begins, King Trueheal banishes his daughter to a cell for the first years of her life. After years of boredom, brimming with resentment at the injustice of her captivity, she finally puts together a plan and escapes imprisonment. 

After days of dazed walking, Minrva finds herself in the middle of an enchanted forest. There, the hero makes new friends, and frees enemies from their own prisons of fear. Minrva's acute survival instincts and courage enable her to undertake the deep exploration of a mysterious, unknown universe. Along the way, fate provides the right tools at the right time to aid in cosmic solutions. Destined since birth for a life that's anything but ordinary, Minerva embarks on a long, exciting, and arduous adventure!

 In the year 1515 AS (After Source), on the barren plains of Aberral, a 5 tier mega center bustled into the Medieval Future. On the Day of Names, Sky Benders roared over the Tower of Gith. The city was ablaze with rumors that an enchanted Future Queen had been born. It was smeared all over Royalty Reality TVC, but who knew what to believe these days. The New News was just as bad. Wars were just anivision and thought-energy was the weapon of choice. These strange days even Cyberpunks got high on Hyper-glide Wet. Clothing Belts to give the appearance of nudity were in high fashion. Glowing Grills were also a big thing, but most people found them to be extremely distracting in conversation. As long as Flip Landers did their jobs, and there were no more Land Surges, why would anyone ever leave the Tower? Everything one could ever need was all here, inside.

 SSSSSSSkkkkRRRRREeeeeeee!!!! What was that?!!! Oh snaps! That was Misero the demonic, bat eared, grump with the mouse purse... His cries of torment can be heard throughout the forest. The laughter of mice & rats drive his large sensitive ears mad with anguish. His vice & greatest pleasure is to collect them into his smelly bag; and with his sappy ultra sticky saliva, he licks their feet and suctions them to trees where they slowly die. This drowns out their laughter briefly, but only until other happy mice pop up out of the woodwork. It’s a Sisyphean task, but some demented creature has to do it.

The Royal Truheal family is known for their wealth, and their savage tyranny. Vargus Truheal (whom we know as the present King Truheal) was the youngest runt brother of three older brothers. As a young, skinny, street smart punk, Vargas believed his true destiny was to become a professional rapper. This was not to be, as his three gangster brothers met their deaths young, leaving only Vargus to become King. 

Under the new, young, and misguided King Truheal, unfathomable acts of mass genocide occurred. If young Truheal was to be miserable in his new post, he would make sure everyone around him felt the same way.

 One of King Truheal’s favorite pastimes is raiding peaceful villages, especially those where Abberal’s Giants live. He rounds up the megalithic masses by binding them together with energy chains. Truheal then ships them back to his twisted megaplex at The Tower of Gith, where the giants are imprisoned. They endure years of soul-breaking solitude in darkness. Many perish. The ones who do make it all but lose their minds. Eventually, Truheal is able to remove any vestiges of a sense of self. The Giants are then turned into C.A.R.E.TAKERS (Cybornetic Attack Reconnaissance Enforcement TAKERS) and equipped with the knowledge required to use thought weapons. The giants who make it through the final trial – Truheal’s Death Maze -- then become his enslaved muscle and earn the full-fledged title of C.A.R.E.TAKER.

 globHAMMER seeks to create a gaming experience that delivers the player into a fine art environment. We’re concerned, firstly, with the visual and emotional experience we give each player. We work backward from there and use computer technology to present our vision to the world.

Currently, the crew is comprised of two artists. (We think two can be considered a crew.) Those two artists are Minister of Shapes, Ryan Ford and Digital Champion, Brad Henderson.

 Bringing SRC1515 to Life

For more than a year, we’ve worked hard to grow globHAMMER. We envision SRC as a game that never ends. With your help, we’ll be able to spend another year focused on taking the game to even greater places, and to add to our team.

Innovating Technology

Bringing traditional art mediums into a gaming environment has been a fun challenge. Along the way, we’ve built a few custom tools to enable our work. Digital Champion's favorite innovation was a touch screen networked scanning station to digitize the painted imagery.

What We’ve Accomplished So Far

  • The Minister of Shapes has animated the first Cut-Scene, "The Origin Story of Minrva's Birth," where it all begins.
  • Digital Champion has created the foundation for solving puzzles using physics or combat.
  • Together, we have taught Minrva how to walk, run, jump, punch, die, and be reborn.
  • We also took a great deal of time giving The Future World its color. This process involved painting and designing six different stages for Minrva to play inside.
  • Finally, Digital Champion learned how to package the game so that it may be easily enjoyed on Linux, Windows, or Macintosh desktops or laptops. 

What We’ve Learned

We’ve each had to level up our skills in traditional drawing and painting, as well as in Javascript and Photoshop. But, more importantly, we’ve learned a great deal about working symbiotically in a creative partnership. These learning experiences have made us better communicators, and we’re a stronger team thanks to both our challenges, and our commitment to the project, to one another, and to our collective creative vision.

Now, at last, we’re ready to launch this Kickstarter Project!

We want you to get as amped as we are!!!

globHAMMER appreciates that your sponsorship is an act of faith and mutual trust, and we thank you for your faith in us.

The funds we seek to raise will enable us to move this mind blowing game forward. Digital Champion and The Minister of Shapes will continue to devote their creative output to bringing this playable work of art to life. 

The First 10K Funds The Heros

A successful campaign will allow us to advance the game for another year. During that time we will paint on canvas, code in the dark, and create. At the end, an amazing piece of art will exist for you to play!

An Extra 2K Funds the Power-Ups

At this stage, we’ll include power-ups to make globHAMMER stronger. We could use a few extra tools, including a large format scanner, a microphone, and sound design software to improve the quality of the game’s audio. Digital Champion would like to have both a Linux and Mac computer to make sure gameplay is seamless on both platforms. We’ve also been interested in attending local conferences to bring SRC and globHAMMER to the rest of the gaming community.All-Pro Painter could improve the quality of his paints and paper.

An Extra 15K Funds Extra Lives

Living in conscious harmony with our community is a main priority of globHAMMER. We take social responsibility seriously. Nothing will make us happier than to support other fellow artists as they play along side us doing what they love! Because of this we have set aside a stretch goal that will enable us to grow, because the bigger the glob the better the glob. At this stage, we’ll formally include two additional artists to work alongside us. We have our eye on two such collaborators who we believe bring the perfect mix of imagination and execution.

An Extra 25K Funds Stage 1: Unleash the globHAMMER

At this stage, we’d plan to rent a very large studio with the fastest Internet possible for the four of us in Brooklyn. Anyone interested in our progress would be welcome to visit globHammer to play the latest prototypes, learn about our process, and stay informed on our most current progress! Digital Champion dislikes programming alone. :(

THANKS FOR YOUR SUPPORT

We hope you’ll be among the first to nurture our creative team as we go after what we believe were meant to achieve. Please join us, and experience the awesome in-depth world of SRC in our exploration of fine art gaming.

 A Great globTastic Thanks to JJ Walker, Gypsy, Kristin Black, Jan-Luc VanDamme, Joey, Melissa, Molly Sampadian, and Mr. Pierre for all your help.
