A multi-player video game inspired by the Shaman Don Miguel Ruiz (The Four Agreements) set at the peak of the Mayan civilization.

MAYA 2012 -Portal to the New Humanity

ABOUT THE GAME

This game is about a warrior during the Golden Age of Mayan culture who overcomes daunting challenges, and becomes a shaman. The game begins with our hero, Balam, who wakes up to find his village being pillaged and burned.

Alone in the jungle, he must find his way to survive. Success for Balam (means Jaguar) depends not only on energy and courage, but also on insight and wisdom.

WHY IS THIS GAME SO SPECIAL?

MAYA 2012 is a breakthrough online video game, one that can change lives.
A high-adventure video game that feeds the soul?

What audacity! But were excited by this challenge, and invite you to participate.

We all know how video games have economically greatly surpassed all motion picture productions, but the vast majority of that success focuses on mindless violence and destruction as entertainment. Meanwhile, education is at a crisis.
What can we do to provide another option for our youth? And for ourselves?

How can we help to expand hearts and minds if so many of us are receiving our news and education through the digital world such as Internet gaming?
Our project addresses these questions pointblank. Whether you play video games or not, MAYA 2012 provides high adventure filled with wisdom. These insights will not only help one to advance in the game, but to also meet challenges in daily life.

With your support of MAYA 2012, you will be contributing to the education and culture of our youth.

ENDORSEMENT

The wisdom in the game has been endorsed by one of the most influential shamans in the world today, Don Miguel Ruiz of the Toltec and Mayan traditions, author of the best-selling The Four Agreements, a N.Y. Times Bestseller for years. It has inspired millions with a new way of experiencing life.

MAYA 2012 is a mirror ofus. Our choices and actions at each step of the game provide a great opportunity for warrior training in the great adventure of our lives. - Don Miguel Ruiz

WHY KICKSTARTER?


We need your help because traditional producers of video games, so far, have not been able to embrace this kind of vision. So, we are appealing directly to you, the people. Even $25 will help. Whats more, with each pledge, you get great rewards, plus the satisfaction of being part of an exciting, groundbreaking video game!

THE GRAND ADVENTURE

MAYA 2012 takes place around 650 A.D during the golden age of the Mayans. Art, science, and technology have blossomed with the creation of huge pyramid complexes, great city-states, and a sophisticated calendar thats still impacting the world today with its auspicious date of 2012.

The Story

As mentioned above, the game opens with your village being pillaged and burned in a brutal surprise attack by an enemy tribe. With everyone being slaughtered or enslaved, you are thrown into the first task of the game, Flee and Survive.

After eluding your enemies, you, as Balam, find yourself all alone immersed in a lush jungle with underground rivers, dramatic highlands, wild cats, peccaries, and crocodiles. Drawing on your courage, inventiveness, and stamina, your main task at this point is for basic survival.

In the next level of the game, after a tribe adopts you, you face new challenges that come from the rich complexity of village lifeinteraction with others, personal ambition, and competition. This demands facing daunting
challenges on the physical

social

and spirit realms:

Finally, in the highest reaches of the game when your special destiny is revealed, you are chosen as a candidate to become a shaman. You will now be challenged as a leader in the Tribal Council as well as in war.

Will you have the courage and insight to embrace your destiny?

IN CLOSING

To create a breakthrough browser game like this normally costs $300,000. But in the last year and a half, the MAYA 2012 team has already created $200,000 worth of assets including research, game design, story development, and all the key artwork.

Now we are ready for the final stage of completion. The $100,000 were seeking will be used for game coding, building enrichments, and final artwork.

If we are able to raise more than $100,000, we have the ability to launch faster, and expand the game to take you deeper into the Mayan mysteries. This would be amazing and together we will open up an entire new genre in gaming.

ABOUT US

The MAYA 2012 team has worked with Don Miguel teachings and members of his teaching team to create a new type of shamanic game. The main members of the team bring not only industry experience but long years of study in the shamanic traditions.

David Welch, Producer

David has an extensive background in shamanic studies and brings years of experience as a Producer to the project. His background in front of and behind the camera in production of Film, TV, and Commercials has been as an Actor, Stuntman, Assistant Director, Casting Director BEING THERE,and Producer of the award winning filmPEACEFUL WARRIOR - whichembodies many of Don Miguel's teachings and can be seen as a modern version of MAYA 2012.

Fu-Ding Cheng, Writer-Director

Fu-Ding is an artist, author, and visionary filmmaker. His series of prize-winning films, Zen-Tales for the Urban Explorer, was presented at the Hammer Museum in a special retrospective, and his Music Video for the Jefferson Starship, We Built This City was nominated Best Video of the Year. Dream-House, his childrens book was published by Hampton Roads, and his album cover for Hearts Dog and Butterfly was featured on billboards on the Sunset Strip. While teaching film, he integrated his spiritual discipline and craft by creating a groundbreaking new curriculum, Shamanic Tools for the Filmmaker.

Since break-through experiences in 1995 as apprentice to Don Miguel Ruiz, Fu-Ding has been leading shamanic workshops distilled from a life-long quest including many Meso-American wisdom traditions.

Fu-Ding is a master shaman, and one of the best Toltec teachers I know.

----Don Miguel Ruiz (author ofThe Four Agreements)

Christiaan Webb, Associate Producer. Music Supervisor

His background in MMOG includes years of hands-on experience with Internet gaming. Through the years, he has researched and written on the gaming industry and has worked as a game developer and advisor.
