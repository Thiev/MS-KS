During SHUFFLE MODE the song/level you choose will play through once, then you will "shuffle" to a random new song/level. You keep your current health and will continue shuffling to new random song/levels for as long as you can survive! Bonuses will be given for each successful level completion.

DubWars is a music game that combines the mechanics of a top-down shooter with the power of dubstep and electronica music. Your weapons are synced to the music creating times of intense weapon load outs and calm moments where your survival skills are tested.

  • Current Beta in OUYA's "Featured" list
  • Showcased at E3 by NYKO Technologies

We love the intensity, anticipation, and especially THE DROP of a really good dubstep song. What we wanted to do is create a game that would capture that experience, amplify it, customize it to each unique song, and make it all interactive.

  • Destroy waves of enemies to the power of Dubstep!
  • Unique mechanics combine twin-stick shooter with music-based gameplay!
  • Minimum of 10 custom-built levels with amazing visuals and effects!
  • Discover and support both independent musicians, and your favorite established artists!
  • Weapon Upgrades -Upgrade your weapons using hard earned coins to take your weapons to the next level! Every weapon in the game will be upgradable. 100+ upgrades will be available!
  • Difficultly Modes - Play in Light, Medium, Hard or Endless!
  • Bosses - Selected stages will also have this "Difficulty Mode" where you will face off against a boss! In these fierce fights the music will endlessly play till you take down the giant monstrosities!
  • Missions, achievements, upgradables, unlockables, and MORE!

Risks and challenges Learn about accountability on Kickstarter

We've been getting a lot of support from both the gaming and dubstep community. Releasing any game is not easy, there is a lot of optimization to make sure it runs on all the platforms we want to release on, there are a lot of unique gameplay problems as well. Each level is individually tailored to the song, which is a lot of man hours. It means hand animating the triggers and creating each weapon from scratch. We've already built four levels, and we keep getting better and better at it. Depending on which songs we get, making sure each level is unique and entertaining while still fitting in the DubWars mold AND the overall feel of the song will be quite the artistic balancing act.
