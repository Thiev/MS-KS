FAQ

  • Will you guys be on Steam / Can I get a Steam key?

    We are absolutely investigating Steam as a distribution platform. The only thing that is stopping us from launching on Steam Greenlight immediately is the fact that there have been some snafus with other indie games getting on Steam Greenlight, then getting a proper publishing deal, but the fact that they were on Steam Greenlight prohibited that publishing deal from properly launching on Steam. But the fact remains that we very much want to be on Steam, and should that work out, you will absolutely be able to choose to get a Steam key.

  • What about controller support?

    We've been considering/designing control schemes to support dual-thumbstick controls from day one. Upon launch we promise you that at the very least USB-Xbox 360 controllers will work. Obviously we're going to work hard to expand controller support beyond that, but USB-360 controllers are an easy given at this point in time.
