Through Knowledge You'll Seek. With Wisdom You'll Endure. Beside Belief You'll Conquer.

      Welcome to the birth of a dream. There is a lot of knowledge in this world, and many who fear it. Our belief is a bit more ancient. Knowledge is a huge factor in everyday life, but somehow it's application seems all to often  to be looked over. We intend to show just how important knowing and understanding one's world is.

     The game project is inspired largely by games like Final Fantasy(VII, X), Kingdom Hearts, King’s Field, Dark Souls, Jak and Daxter, Spyro, Morrowind, Skyrim, Dragon Age, and The Witcher. The gameplay is a mix between a JRPG Style story and atmosphere, a skill system seen in more western RPG’s, such as Dragon Age, and a player movement system seen in classic and modern platformers. This, combined with a global survival based A.I.**, will make each encounter different, where any condition can vary, just like in real life. The player will traverse the open world equipped with basic skills that allow such things as a stamina-based climbing ability, which can be done on most surfaces given the right tools are at the players disposal. There will be moving platforms, small beams, hanging ropes, and a variety of ways for one to travel through an area. The battle system will be real time, and will be a combination of basic melee striking combos and a large assortment of mapped skills, from magic to special combos. The leveling system mainly governs your base stats, such as vitality, stamina, and magic, whereas all other stats affect these base stats as well as how your character behaves when doing tasks like dashing, climbing, jumping, alchemy, crafting, cooking, and so on. As the player explores their world around them, they’ll be presented with quests to help others for a reward, which will include things like experience, weapons, armor, items, and crystals. Crystals in the game serve a variety of purposes; they help channel energy for nearly all tasks, they allow the player to use special abilities, they embroider weapons and armor (making them incredibly powerful,) they bring forth great beasts to aid you in your journey, and they even can even save the world. As you explore the world, you will encounter some very dark crystals, but you also have the ability to cleanse them and turn them back into their original form. This results in very rare and powerful crystals, and a special jade colored crystal. Collecting the jade crystals is the main goal in the game, and by collecting these, you stand a chance when the end comes. They have an ability to fuse together whenever they touch, allowing the character to place them where they once laid so that the seal is once again complete. They are found everywhere around the world, dark enemies drop them frequently, and are sometimes found on people who will trade them, but usually not cheaply. All crystals exist more frequently in certain areas around the world, and there are stories of vast amounts of the planet’s energy condensing and forming pure crystals. There are many legendary creatures, figures, and locations hidden all over, and it’s up to you to learn more about them and see if you can catch a glimpse of these myths. The player will be given a pet as part of the story, and this pet will level along with you, learn its own skills, and help you fight back the darkness. There is a relationship dynamic between the player and the pet as well, which affects how the two react out in the world when facing different situations. There will be many puzzles spread throughout the entire world which can be found in ruins, caves, dungeons, and other places to be explored. Exploring is just one of the aspects of the game. There are also the interactions amongst the NPC’s found around the world. Some will have quests, some will speak of myths, and some will simply not wish to communicate. There is also a game that is very popular (story-wise) played between two people; it is a game based on a classic Tactical RPG engine. The player will find small items representing different creatures that can be used as playing pieces. The board is projected onto the ground, and each player takes turns similar to any of the Tactical RPG’s found today. However, there is still much to be fleshed out with this feature. As the game develops everything will go through the tweaking processes, so keep in mind these things are basic descriptions of the full engine, and will be adapted as necessary.

**Global Survival Based Artificial Intelligence: a system to be designed as a foundation for the entire game engine that uses many different survival oriented variables that each “living” entity is spawned with, plus an overall survival level of the planet which causes the game to become more difficult on every level due to the enemies becoming more fearful for their survival, increasing things like their aggressiveness and courage, or even causing things like plant life becoming extinct. Just as there is death and danger, so is there life and abundance, and these aspects follow the same survival rules in the game. The plants will provide food for the vast amounts of creatures and NPC’s, and in return will have their pollen spread across the area. Nature will also follow various traits, which will allow for cross pollination and hybrids when combined with the simple and effective growing/harvesting system. This system goes hand in hand with the alchemy, which will allow the player (or other NPC’s) to make potions, herbal mixes, projectile weapons, poisons, and more. All enemies will have weaknesses and strengths, and they will vary based on how they see fit to beat you. As you level, they too evolve to survive you. This allows for more interesting battles, as enemies will evolve in either offensive ways, defensive, or sometimes even in both. Bosses have the same type of evolution aspect, but they will also have certain larger weaknesses that will vary based on their strengths and weaknesses. This system makes the world the player visits very unique each time he/she plays. Another aspect we plan to implement that actually alters the feel of the game in its entirety is doing away with the traditional HUD in favor of displaying the necessary information using aspects of the universe (for example, displaying chakras lining the character’s spine for mana) and an instant loading feature that allows the player to pick up exactly where you left off when resuming gameplay. The idea behind this is to completely immerse the player in the world, and stray away from anything that takes away from this.

      The project will be worked on every day, being shown as much attention as physically possible until completion. It will be made using a combination of platforms including, but not limited to, Cryengine 3, Maya, Photoshop, and FLStudio. There will be a minimum of three highly dedicated developers working on this from start to finish, each with a unique vision being shaped into an ever developing world. By the end of the year all assests and basic engine will be developed, leaving the demo completely playable, at which point the main game world will become the focus. The remainder of the time will be focused on perfecting the visual aspects of the world, defining each of the micro-stats of the different game entities, (i.e. the various affects each of the different plants hold, their dominant and recessive genes, color of the petals, type of fruit (if it has fruit at all.) This, by the way, would work perfectly with the ability to farm/plant, and crossbreed to find the perfect plant for your adventure.). This will also be the time when a lot of the extra gameplay mechanics are brought forth, or perfected, (i.e. a climbing system to add a refreshing dynamic of exploration currently not seen in many games). All aspects will be worked on throughout the entire project’s life; these are the planned overall shifts of focus.

      We also have a mobile extension of the game planned that will allow the player to experience side adventures; these will tell backstories, uncover tales of special secret legends, reward special crystals, extra experience, rare mounts, and plenty more. These rewards can all then be transferred to your console game and used the same as any other item in game. This, however, is most likely going to be considered if the project reaches a stretch goal, and more time can be dedicated as well as a couple more machines and team members.

      The reward system set in place follows certain guidelines. While the dream worlds that are offered as a reward are up to you to create, they must not be offensive to others, and still follow basic game structure. Everything found in the dream world doesn’t necessarily have to make sense with the storyline, and are entirely up to your imagination; they can be crazy and misshaped. You are allowed to define everything from how the ground looks, to what color the sky is, to what the enemies look and behave like. This reward system is designed to give the community a direct hand in making a game, to help us produce a good amount of in-game extras, and for everyone (backers, players, and even us) to have some fun as the game nears its end in the development process. The Legendary set that is given to all backers $50 and over will be designed a bit later, but rest assured, it will be well worth it, and most likely will have a few variations so it better fits your play style.

     Thank you for your time and interest in this project, and we hope you enjoy getting to see a game grow from a seed of an idea into an experience of a lifetime. All footage comes from a sub-par computer system, and therefor has vast potential for improvement, especially in the workflow area. Again, Thank you and we love you.

     Here are some screenshots of current progress. If you would like to keep up to date with the project and participate in contests, check out our Facebook page at www.facebook.com/aprojectinharmony

Risks and challenges Learn about accountability on Kickstarter

One of the biggest challenges faced in the development of this game, besides license and equipment funding, will be a massive loss to game data, resulting in the potential loss of month and months of work.Thankfully, even being our biggest upset, it is easily fixable with the installation of a RAID setup on each of the computers that will constantly back up any and all data. Along side this factor goes any other equipment failures, which will only be a temporary upset, and most likely will only be one of the machines at a time. At this point backup hardware would be found and installed. Should the circumstance of all computers being lost arise, then starting at ground zero will be nonexistent since the RAID drives will still have the files stored, and can be accessed on any computer. Other than this it is simply a matter of time until this finished project sees the light of day and deserving adventure seekers around the world.
