Captain and manage your ship along with your crew in this deep space adventure! (PC/Linux/Mac)

The Game

  • Beyond Black Space is a real time space strategy game focused on the ability to control the entire inner workings of your ship and crew. You are the captain of the last surviving ship from earth. 
  • You decide on how to advance the ship, how to equip it, appoint officers to each role and assign crew members to repair specific systems. 
  •  Your officers will be unique and irreplaceable should they die. They will learn and advance in their job role as time goes on becoming more efficient at what they do giving the ship stronger abilities. 
  •  For repairs you will have a resource system used to assign crew members to specific repairs. Your general crew also advances in repairs as time goes on should they survive.
  •  Escape an unknown force seeking to destroy you while exploring the galaxy, meeting new races and discovering alien technology to improve your own ship.

Features:

  • Amazing Story, in fact we are writing a book about it!
  • Randomly Generated Galaxy for a different adventure every time!
  • Over 1000+ Random Story Scenarios/Missions with random choices and random outcomes giving every player a unique experience every single time.
  • Multiple methods to advance/upgrade your character/ship based on your play style. Engage massive fleets and be rewarded, find advanced vessels, destroy and extract parts from them or find and mine asteroid belts to craft your own upgrades.
  • Officers who improve their selected job roles but be careful for death is permanent here.
  • Engineering - Assign specific repair orders, choose priority of what needs to be repaired but be warned, if shields are down and that section gets hit again, your team will be space dust.
  • Interactive battle choices, decide on your style by becoming a war ship equipped with the latest and greatest weapons, becomes the fastest and hardest ship to hit, a carrier with thousands of fighters to deploy or something completely different. The choice is yours!
  • Advanced Character Improvements, as you progress and complete events you will gain experience tokens that you can use to improve your leadership skills by unlocking new abilities, buffs and more.
  • It's just you out here, you are alone in space with no place to repair. You must manage repair on specific systems and your overall health/hull. If your hull collapses its game over, no check points here. Repairs have to be done in real time whenever you get a chance.
  • Meet new and unique races throughout the galaxy. Become allies and gain great benefits or declare war on them. Every choice you make will have ripple effects throughout the galaxy. Becoming allies with one race may automatically make you an enemy with others so choose carefully.
  • Customize and choose a captain portrait and then choose and assign officer portraits! (Choose yourself if you contributed a certain level!)
  • Map Timers, every section you travel to will give you a limited amount of time to conduct repair before having to move on

Combat

  • Combat will be real time strategic action. You will have to target enemy systems to try and disable or destroy them before they do so to you.
  • But that isn't all, as your ship takes damage you will have to send repair teams  to get damaged systems back up and running.
  • The game will feature many different types of weapons, shields and advanced technologies. Will you go with one type or mix and match different technology to be prepared for anything space has to throw at you?
  • In combat 3 choices will play a major role. First you need to choose your style, weapons r'us, fleet carrier, big shield modules and hull upgrades or become a ship so fast that no one will see you coming. Maybe a balance between all of the above? It all depends on your choices and how you wish to play. The ship awaits your equipment and upgrade choices. 
  • Second, you will have to choose how you will attack, this can greatly depend on how you fit your ship. You can disable weapons and leave the enemy sitting ducks as you slowly chew away their hull. Maybe disable their super fast regenerating shields first? What you target is up to you.
  • And finally, you must simultaneously manage your ship repairs. Lets say your Engines, Shields and Hull all take damage, what do you repair first? Shields to prevent any further damage? Engines so you can make a quick escape? Or your hull so you don't blow up? You have many more systems to repair on your ship and a resource system to assign repairs throughout your ship. The repairs portion of Beyond Black Space plays a huge role on your overall success and provides a very unique experience that is both fun and gives a sense of total control.

Harvesting

  • Asteroid Mining is the name of the game here. But in Beyond Black Space you wont fly into an asteroid belt, be greeted by some cheesy text and have resources appear in your inventory. Oh no...
  • Here you will have to work for your catch starting with finding an unoccupied asteroid belt(unless you want to conquer one).
  • Harvesting will be done by first scanning the asteroid belt to see which resources it contains, how rich they are and how stable the asteroids is. Keep in mind that the accuracy of this scan depends on your scanning ability and equipment. 
  • Once done you will choose the richest and most stable asteroid to mine. But do not get too greedy as over mining it will cause it to collapse and destroy everything you cut out.
  • Finally you will send out a recovery shuttle to collect the resources.
  • There will be many different asteroid belt types each containing its own unique resources with different properties.
  • Keep in mind for those who do not enjoy this type of game play other methods of collecting upgrades has been establishes such as war zones, missions and strip mining enemy vessels.

Crafting

  • Crafting will be completed with resources gathered from the asteroid belts. Basic ship equipment upgrades can always be worked on and rare schematics can be found throughout the galaxy allowing very powerful ship equipment, weapons and other upgrades to be researched.
  • This will also be a very involved process but yield great rewards for those who enjoy this game style. There is a fine balance to keep crafting fun without being too simple. And as with mining this will be an optional path to follow for those who enjoy it.

Factions System

  • The game will have a very basic faction system that keeps track of all interactions with alien species you may encounter.
  • Since every play-through is randomly generated, you may not always encounter specific races.
  • The game will randomly pick from the list of aliens and spawn them into your game, randomly generating alliances and wars between them. So you will never know in games which alliance may upset other races.
  • Forming alliances has several benefits in the game. You will be able to access restricted asteroid belts, receive backup and pass blockades that allies have setup. While in ally space you will also have a longer timer per map.
System Requirements:

The requirements are all subject to change. 

  • OS: Windows (XP/Vista/7/8) Linux or Mac
  • Processor: 2ghz Core 2 Duo
  • Memory: 512 MB 
  • Graphics: OpenGL 2.0 Support, 256 MB (dedicated card preferred)
  • Minimum Resolution: 1280 x 720

So why Kickstarter?

Developing a game in your spare time is great, but once you realize how much potential it has to be a fun game, some additional Vespene gas may be required.

This started off as a small idea to work on but quickly became a project that excited everyone involved. After showing it to friends and families they all had the same question "When?".

But we also realize that it may take a while to complete unless we all focus 100% on it. Right now everyone has been working on this for many months without receiving any sort of benefits or salary, and well it takes money to run a game studio.

To continue to work on this and make a great game that everyone can enjoy we will need some help.

We also have big plans for this including expansion packs, sequels and even multiplayer modes.

So needless to say this isn't a one time project, this is a dream and the beginning of a small developers company that makes games for the community. At heart we are all gamers and we believe in one thing above all, fun comes first.

So if we are able to get funded for this project you can expect lots of updates for Beyond Black Space and some other fun games for PC, Mac and apps. We even hope to release our games on networks such as xbox and psn.

Risks and challenges Learn about accountability on Kickstarter

When we first started this project we all wanted to have fun and release a simple game. However as time progressed we started to realize that in order to make it fun and feel complete we had to keep adding more depth to our game play elements.

We do not want to cut these out of the game at this point. The game is far along and with a little help from our supporters should hit the finish line in about 11 months.

Our greatest risk and challenge is running out of time to complete this project while distracted with other obstacles and jobs in our lives.

We will overcome these challenges with your support and switch our focus 100% on this to make it the best it can be!
We have the tools, the time, the experience, the people and the passion to complete this project, now we just need your support to help deliver a fun game to our fellow gamers!
