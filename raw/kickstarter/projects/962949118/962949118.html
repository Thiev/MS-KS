A Full HD action-RPG platformer by Game Atelier: Newton is back and is now a keen swordfighter with transformation capabilities.

Update: Kickstarter Campaign is canceled, FDG Entertainment joins the adventure!

Check out the project updates to know more!

Video: You can watch the project video in 1080p ON YOUTUBE.

Project overview:Flying Hamster II is an ACTION-ADVENTURE GAME with PLATFORMING and RPG design elements (some may call itMetroidvania). It is the direct sequel from the game Flying Hamster (2010) and is made by the same passionate team of professionals.

Why this project:

  • Flying Hamster (2010) was downloaded 1.7M+ times on PS Vita, PSP and iOS, including both free and paid versions.
  • We received many messages from fans asking for a SEQUEL.
  • But most importantly: many people are craving for a new WONDER BOY / MONSTER WORLD game.

Flying Hamster II is our answer to both Monster World AND Flying Hamster fans, and more largely to any action-adventure games lover.

Why we need you:With your support you can help us make Flying Hamster II happen, and target new platforms!

Platforms:Flying Hamster II is developed for WINDOWS (Steam Key + DRM-free download), and depending on Stretch Goals achieved our goal is a multi-platform release on PLAYSTATION 4, PLAYSTATION VITA, XBOX ONE, NINTENDO 3DS and WII U.

Estimated release: Mid-2015.

Flying Hamster II is a 2D sidescrolling Action-RPG with hand-drawn 1080p Full HD graphics (note: resources are all in 4k resolution, just in case we require to use them one day), top notch animations and unique Flying Hamster humour signature!

Our main inspirations are the Wonder Boy / Monster World, Zelda series and Secret Of Mana, and along with our vision of what a 2D platformer RPG should be, we are ready to deliver the best gaming experience ever.

  • EXPLORE the world, loot and slash many different enemies with your best weapons. Find new equipment and unique transformations granting you access to tons of secret passages and hideouts.
  • DISCOVER the beautiful hand-drawn 2D environments in Full HD, talk and fight with smoothly animated characters or huge bosses.
  • LIVE the amazing adventures of Newton the Flying Hamster, dive into this fantastic video game with its epic story and catchy soundtrack.
  • Transformations: Newton can change into several different animals, each one with its unique capabilities: while the BEAR is strong and can destroy almost everything, the SNAKE can sneak into small gaps and climb walls, the FROG may use his tongue to grab almost anything, and the PIG whilst cute is pretty useless. These transformations are all complementary but of course the only one with flying capabilities is the HAMSTER.
  • Primary weapons: Newton is equipped with a wide variety of weapons from the regular gladius sword to the whip and even a morning star. He can also be equipped with more advancedmagicalweaponry that is capable of more than just hitting foes (i.e: theIce Sword can create ice blocks when used on water).
  • Spitting: Newton has the unique ability to stock various stuff in his jowls and SPIT it at his enemies. He is also capable of charging and releasing SPECIAL ATTACKS. Each type of ammo has a different special attack, and if Newton runs out of ammo, he still can use his own spit unlimitedly!
  • Equipment: Interacting with your environment in Flying Hamster II is closely related to the equipment at your disposal. ITEMS, HELMETS, SHIELDS, ARMOR and BOOTS are more than just classic helpful stat modifiers. They help to access many hidden areas (i.e: Want to walk on the bottom of the ocean? Use the Heavy Boots. Or maybe on a lava lake? Just equip yourself with the Ice Boots).
  • Puzzle solving: The World of Flying Hamster II contains some simple puzzles that will require a bit more than sword dexterity. One of the most exciting puzzles is the possibility to ROTATE THE WORLD by using the tongue of Newton in Frog guise. This is a very fun feature but it can get very confusing to find your way out of the lost temple maze.

The story is a direct follow up to the first Flying Hamster (2010).

After deafeating the Nerdy Alien Boss, Newton finally got back his tasty GOLDEN SEED and ate it at once. But as it turns out this was not a great idea: as soon as he finishes his delicious seed our fearless hamster feels sick and quickly transforms into a PIG, thus removing the capacity to wear any clothes and, even worse, losing his flying ability.

Newton has no other choice but to find a way to CURE HIMSELF. Fortunately hell quickly discover that other very RARE LEGENDARY SEEDS exist in the World and eating one of them may turn him back to a Hamster. But finding them wont be so easy as plenty of monsters and other evil creatures will try to stop him.

During his quest Newton will eventually stumble upon a VERY STRANGE TOWN full of weird unknown beings but hiding a great unexpected SECRET.

Here starts Newtons journey to recover his original form as the one and only FLYING HAMSTER.
