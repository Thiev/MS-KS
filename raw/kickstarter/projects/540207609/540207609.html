Update 1: Stretch Goals and Chucklefish Games!

At Heart Forth, Alicia's core is an ode to the classics. Remember the electrifying gameplay of Castlevania: Symphony of the Night, the expansive worlds of the Zelda games, and the rich, engaging story of Xenogears? This game is crafted in the spirit of those great adventures of the '90s.

The story unfolds amidst the repercussions of centuries worth of struggle, strife, and bloodshed between cultures, races and worldviews. But above all else, it's an epic Metroidvania RPG that combines timeless 16- and 32-bit gameplay, modern mechanics, and a set of unique, intertwining stories to take you on a fantastic journey.

  • Master devastating and versatile magic spells to annihilate your enemies and solve creative puzzles.
  • Engage in fast, agile melee combat with an enchanted wizard's whip, over 20 special attacks and upgrades, and a wide array of mobility skills.
  • Adventure through a vast, intricately detailed world filled with hidden wonders and puzzles, dastardly platforming, lethal dungeons, and much, much more.
  • Play with powerful RPG elements that include all the gear, loot, sidequests, upgrades, crafting, and gameplay depth that make for a classic RPG experience.
  • Discover 5 secret fairy companions to crush, shock, freeze, and burn your enemies, or save you at your most dire moment of need.
  • Experience a deep and immersive story that takes place within a much larger history of events and cultures, and starring a cast of fascinating, memorable characters.

There’s an island in the sky named Auster, and on it, there’s a small wizard village with the name of Brenia. Centuries ago, a great star struck the Earth: the mountains crumbled, and land and sea poured into a great Void that now lies hidden beneath the clouds. This island and its sister, Quilth, are the last surviving remnants of the Old World.

Half a century ago, the wizards took the power of the Island for themselves. And so, Elthëlian, the Spirit of Light, cursed them never to have children again. 

Today begins the Trial of the last two wizards of the bloodline: Alicia and Höstra.

They are taken to the Hillside to reach the Clearing on their own. But their ceremony to come of age is stopped. One of the wizards has been killed by Elthëlian. That night, the children run away in search for help, but Höstra becomes entranced by the Spirit, and he throws Alicia off the edge of the island.

Alicia awakens in a strange land beneath the clouds: all along, the Old World has remained here, concealed beneath the clouds. Fearing for the safety of her homeland, and for her friend Höstra, Alicia sets off to find a way back and stop the Spirit.

Along with Alicia’s adventure comes another story: that of The Woman. Told through live-action footage, her story will offer you a different perspective of Heart Forth, Alicia. Depending on how you end up interpreting things, the universe of the game might suddenly come to mean something different.

The universe of Heart Forth, Alicia expands though centuries worth of history, hundreds of timeline events, dozens of characters (many of which, while playing an important historical role, might not be seen or mentioned in the game), numerous cultures, and plenty of twists and turns to keep you wondering about what's really happening.

Heart Forth, Alicia remains a Metroidvania, and although the story takes hold and plays out when reaching certain milestones, the game is designed to let the gameplay take the spotlight for the most part, and let the story influence things from the background.

Combat

Rather than being a stereotypical spellcaster, Alicia is a warrior-wizard skilled with both weapons and magic similar to Gandalf in the Tolkien universe. Thanks to the Wizard's Lace, her enchanted whip, she doesn't always have to rely on magic to get the job done.

Throughout the game, you'll be able to dramatically improve your abilities with the Lace and deliver progressively more devastating attacks as you acquire upgrades to increase its damage, range, apply further enchantments, unlock mighty special attacks, and more.

Of course, magic is an essential part of any wizard's life, and here it provides Alicia with a potent arsenal of destruction. As she acquires spells over the course of the game, she'll grow in strength from a novice wizard to a master of magic, capable of facing any enemy that stands in her way.

Each spell is distinct, multi-purpose, and can upgraded to become even more powerful (if you can figure out how!). More than that, as with special abilities in any well-crafted Metroidvania, spells are also integral to solving puzzles, discovering secrets, and progressing through obstacles.

Alicia won't be the only one using magic, either. The fairy companions that she acquires throughout the game have their own magical talents to help her through nearly any situation and against any foe. They can dish out damage of any element, temporarily provide an impenetrable shield, help you gather unreachable items, replenish your energy when you need it most, and more.

Exploration

Whether Metroidvania or RPG, a great game in either of these genres requires a great world. Designed in the interconnective spirit of Super Metroid and The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time, Heart Forth, Alicia is set in the Valley of Éliadin. We've done our utmost to make it an exciting and memorable setting, filled with twisting paths and unforeseen peril.

Within the Valley you'll adventure through 12 vast and different regions, from the great Forests of Verílion to the desolate Áurin Desert, or from the ruins of Elenia to the bustling cities of Blivek or Ynnebluïn, and from the tallest mountains to the most entrenched abysses of the world. This Valley is a land teeming with hidden secrets, long-forgotten dungeons, powerful artifacts, and lethal surprises.

The denizens that populate a world are just as important as the features that define it, and the Valley's inhabitants run the full range of friendly to ferocious. Monsters and beasts like ecleckers, sandmen, golems and skeletons roam the wilderness, and long-forgotten wraths and necromancers have lurked deep in the dungeons. 

The human world, however, is often just as dangerous to an outsider like Alicia. She'll have to stop the dark kingdom of Blákatha which seeks to overtake the Valley, and help Oznin overthrow his corrupted uncle Ahriman from the throne of Blivek as she searches for a way to save her homeland. The only person she'd be wise to trust is herself.

RPG Elements

Another goal we’ve had with Heart Forth, Alicia has been to make a game with depth that harkens to the great role-playing games of the '90s. Our aim in implementing RPG elements is to give players more customization, control, and flexibility when it comes to their experience with the game.

We've built a number of systems to achieve this goal. The game features a leveling system to maintain a stiff challenge throughout your adventure. You'll have access to a detailed map system to keep your bearings straight and help you better explore the world, and a scheduling screen to keep track of sidequests that happen at different times of the day. And, of course, the game will have a tremendous amount of loot, equipment, and the corresponding stats to keep even the most die-hard RPG fan happy. 

Those are the just some of the systems working behind the curtain though, and we want to create a world that brings players back to classic RPGs in a more overt way. That's why we've set our sights on adding in sidequests both tragic and tendering, cute minigames, and exciting challenges to both strengthen yourself and round out the world if you're a completionist. 

You'll help bring an oppressed town back to prosperity: help people reopen their family shops and businesses, and you'll reap the rewards. You'll prove that you're the quickest wizard to ever wield a Lace. You'll spark romance between lovers, salvage your old gear to craft shiny new loot, and win prizes while breaking records in timed challenges both custom-built and secretly hidden throughout the world, among the many, many other diversions as you adventure through the Valley. Heart Forth, Alicia will be filled with little details like these waiting for players to discover them and realize the full RPG experience.

Why Kickstarter?

You might be wondering why I decided to take the game to Kickstarter. The reason is very simple: developing this game on my own is incredibly slow. I started this game 7 years ago, and though I’ve cumulatively worked on it for more than four (the last two, full-time), I simply don’t see myself finishing this game in a reasonable amount of time without bringing other people into the project.

Fortunately, I’ve been lucky enough to get a bunch of talented people interested in the game and ready to jump in. However, professionals like them will hardly want to jump into the boat with you if you don’t have funds to pay them with.

Aside from the costs of physical rewards, almost all of the money raised in the campaign will exclusively be used to hire the people to help with game development (namely graphics, music and code), aside from getting a small fraction for my own living expenses, and another one for taxes and fees. Any extra money will be used to fund stretch goals such as porting the game to Mac/Linux, getting it to certain consoles, developing extensions to bring more features to the game, and maybe even add extra areas or content to the game.

I would never give the game up even if things don’t work out as planned, but without outside support I'd have to get a part-time job to cover my living costs, and it'd take me a few more years to finish the game. Kickstarter is basically the only viable option, and it's a really good one if we can make it happen.

Any support you can provide to this project will be invaluable to me, and I thank you in advance for helping me finish Heart Forth, Alicia.

- Alonso

Risks and challenges Learn about accountability on Kickstarter

As with any large undertaking, there are potential problems that could arise during Heart Forth, Alicia's development, but we’ve already done and will continue to do our best to minimize, if not negate, anything that could jeopardize the game's timely completion.

Heart Forth, Alicia has been in full-time development for a long time, and one of the benefits of all of that time is a clear and refined vision for the game. Along with the support of the fantastic group of people at Chucklefish to help us along the way, we're confident that we can live up to our backers' inevitably high expectations not just in quality, but punctuality.

Confidence, however, isn't certainty. We can't promise you a development process with no bumps in the road. Anyone who says otherwise is outright lying, because there isn't a person on this planet who can predict all the twists and turns that life will send your way. What we can promise you, however, is that we've done everything possible to be ready for anything that may come our way. And, if all of our planning and preparation leads to a development cycle as smooth as we expect, then we should all have a great time being part of a community together and have a fantastic game in our hands in the not-so-distant future.

Heart Forth, Alicia is our dream, and we would never let it fail. We do, however, need your help to make it the best it can be. Stick with us, and we'll bring you a game you'll never forget.
