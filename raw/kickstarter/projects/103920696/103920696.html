QUESTIONS THAT YOU, THE POSSIBLE FUNDER, MAY HAVE:

WHAT IS THIS GAME? WHY IS THERE A PARROT TELLING ME WHERE TO GO? DID I OFFEND IT?

Seeing-Eye Parrot is a game with a simple premise, and hilarious results. You must lead MURRAY (an old, blind man) around various obstacles in many different levels in this fun arcade-style game. How do you do that? Why, you control his SEEING-EYE PARROT, of course!! 

(What... That isn't... normal?... ... Just go with it and see if anyone says anything...) 

It features simple controls, with only two buttons (or taps) needed to control the game. You aim at the spot you want Murray to walk to and press the "TALK" button, and it will make the parrot speak, sending Murray stumbling off in that direction. But he won't stop once he gets there! He'll keep walking until you tell him to stop with the "STOP" button. (WHAT?!? You have to press TWO DIFFERENT BUTTONS?!? That's TWICE as many as ONE BUTTON!! I dunno about this...) You must lead him around obstacles to get him to the end of the level. If you feel daring, you can try to lead him around to collect various STARS on each level to gain a higher score! (Nothing like using senior citizens to pick up junk laying on the ground.)

With controls that are simple to use, we hope that everyone should be able to pick up and play this game!

Meet MURRAY and his SEEING-EYE PARROT... What? You've never heard of a Seeing-Eye Parrot? Bet you want one now though, huh?

WHAT SYSTEMS WILL THE GAME BE AVAILABLE ON? SERIOUSLY, DID I UPSET THAT BIRD SOMEHOW!? HE SEEMS REALLY UPSET AT ME! 

Well, that depends on the funding of the game, and the availability of the software. We plan on releasing it for certain on PC and MAC as a digital download, and we want to create a version for FACEBOOK. If possible, we plan to release it on iOS (iPhones and iPads), as well as Android systems. Any other systems are uncertain at this time.

WHEN WILL THIS GAME BE FINISHED AND RELEASED?

Right now, We are shooting for a date of DECEMBER, 2012 as the latest, possible date. That is being generous with the time, to make sure as much time and effort possible is being put in to create the best game that we can! If we can be done earlier, and still deliver a fantastic game, then we will let you know!

WHAT IS MY FUNDING GOING TOWARDS?

The funding is supplying the software, hardware, and manpower to create this game. We will have one person working with the game engine and programming it, someone creating the artwork for it, and someone creating the music and sound effects.

WHAT IF YOU GET MORE FUNDING THAN YOU ASKED FOR.... SERIOUSLY!!! WHAT IS THAT BIRD'S PROBLEM?!?!?

(Ignore him. He just wants attention.)

If we get more funding than we asked for, it will go straight into making the game even better. We will create more levels to play in, maybe even hire another person to help with one of the above mentioned fields.

If possible, we would like to hire an animator to create short, funny videos for the game's intro, outro, and the intro of each level pack. We have sent out emails to such animators like EGORAPTOR, but it just depends on how much they need and if they have the time. If any of reply with a price, we will update this page to let you know! The baseline funding limit won't change, but maybe the right animator would encourage everyone to donate even more to make sure we can hire them!

This game will be alot of fun to play, but for people to be able to play it, we will need your help! So please, help us fund this great game and make it possible for everyone to get to experience this.
