While exploring planets and territories, use devastating bombs from spaceships you control to destroy everything you come across.

You start your journey with a lightweight spacecraft on a nearby planet. Your goal is to explore as much of the territory as possible while staying alive. Your travels will bring you close to death, encountering dangerous obstacles such as black holes, life-sucking danger zones, airspace mines and anti-aircraft warfare. To make the fight fair, we’ve given you the best part: the ability to blow up everything you encounter!

Each ship is equipped with a set of bombs differing by caliber, count per shot and reload time. Bombs capable of causing severe explosions, turning the ground into a fountain of dirt and objects into millions of tiny shattered pieces. That’s the good news!

The bad news is your ship is not invulnerable, capable of taking on damage and possibly running out of fuel. So another challenge you will face is to refuel by landing on fuel stations and repairing your ship by picking up armor kits.

As you proceed deeper into each level, you will come across energy spheres and underground minerals. These are a valuable resource, allowing you to travel to distant planets, unlock upgrades for your ship, and buy new ships as well.

Controls are unique but at the same time fairly simple to pick up. Each engine within a ship is controlled by a separate joystick, making it possible to do some crazy maneuvering. If you prefer an easier method, we developed a synchronization mode, which enables you to control both engines with a single joystick. This is useful when you need a smooth and stabilized flying experience. Other controls include buttons to drop/launch bombs and use your landing gear.

Don’t be fooled, the game is not solely dependent on your pilot skills; you’ll need strategic thinking as well. As you acquire more ships, you’ll have to decide which ship is best suited for specific planets and levels.

REQUIRED BUDGET

Art & Effects – 25%

The main design and art is already completed but we still have some work to do with planets and levels. We want the content of the game to be as visually appealing as possible to maximize the game-playing experience.

Sound and Music – 20%

The sound and music we use in the game needs to be fine tuned to be perfectly looped, have a few volume levels and overall be glitch free.

Optimization – 25%

The game currently has huge deformable levels, tons of interactive objects and a real physics engine, requiring a lot of computational resources. While Demolition Lander runs smooth on top Apple devices, we still need to tweak a few things to work on old devices with decent fps.

Quality Assurance – 30%

We want to deliver the game in the best possible quality. That’s why we are putting a lot of effort and resources into quality assurance. Demolition Lander already has the following awesome features and is a step away from being released. But we need your help to make it happen.

REAL PHYSICS ENGINE

The game features a real physics engine where every object behaves as it would in real world. All objects have their own mass, friction and elasticity, making the interaction between them as life like as possible. There is also a weather system in the game: wind will move your ship off course, while rain and snow will make the ground slippery, causing landing of the ship to be a challenge.

IMMENSE LEVELS

Every level is fairly large, as it takes up to 20 minutes to explore each. To speak in figures, imagine a picture with 65536 x 16384 pixels in dimensions, and all this vastness is filled with interactive game objects.

DEMOLITION

Everything in the game is destructible. Use your bombs to cause severe explosions making the objects shatter into millions of pieces and the ground to become a fountain of dirt. Here’s a small example: if you want to enter an underground cave – no problem, just blast a hole in the ground and fly in!

NATURAL HAZARDS

There are a number of natural hazards like black holes, radiation and low temperature zones. Black holes will suck you in and hold you until you’re out of fuel. Danger zones will damage your ship every second, slowly bringing you closer to death.

DEADLY TRAPS

You will encounter deadly traps on your way, such as airspace mines and anti-aircraft warfare. Airspace mines are simple, yet deadly enough to kill you, as they will explode if you touch them. Anti-aircraft warfare is an example of old civilization defenses. Their primitive construction allows them to launch a bunch of highly charged cannon balls in a small area, and if you linger near one of them your ship will be blasted into little pieces.

LOTS OF SHIPS

We have constructed 10 ships, where each has a unique base specification such as engine power, agility, and weapon caliber. We will add a new ship every month. Each ship in Demolition Lander is like a person with its own character and behavior, some are heavy and have lots of fire power, some are fast and agile, but you will always find the right one for each level.

SHIP UPGRADES

Each ship is upgradable where almost every part of a ship can be improved.

SOCIAL INTEGRATION

Let your friends see your achievements with Demolition Lander – share your results via Facebook or Twitter.

NO IN-APP PURCHASES

There will be no in-app purchases. The energy you collect on a planet will be used to upgrade your ship, buy new ones and unlock new planets.

MULTIPLAYER MODE

You will be able to play Demolition Lander with your friends over WiFi. Get ready for some fearsome battles! Up to 6 players can fight on the same level in deathmatch, team cooperation or capture the flag modes.

ENEMY BOTS

Enemy bots will be added to single campaign and to multiplayer mode. Bots are good in flying and battle tactics, so you will be challenged to break through.

MORE WEAPON TYPES

Following weapon types will be added:

Cluster bomb – shatters into a cloud of smaller bombs before detonation.

Boomerang bomb – this one has a modified falling trajectory, allowing you to hit targets that are not only directly below you.

Napalm bomb – pours gallons of napalm before hitting the ground.

Penetrating shell – it can deeply penetrate the ground before causing an underground explosion.

Vertical laser – powerful laser beam burning everything beneath your ship.

Terra-maker bomb – it creates a pile of soil in the hit spot, thus entrapping your unlucky enemies 

Danger-zone bomb – it creates a temporary danger zone in the hit spot that will slowly kill your opponents.

MORE PLANETS

Additional 10 planets each having 5 levels will be added.

GLOBAL MASS MULTIPLAYER - THE ARENA

Mass online multiplayer mode will be added – the Arena. Every planet in the game will have its own Arena battleground, an enlarged level where up to 20-30 players from all over the world can play simultaneously.

Every player will have their own Rank – it is a measure of player battle experience. Be assured that no money can buy you more rank, as you’ll only earn it by your victories!

Every Arena battleground will host only players with similar rank, thus regulating it to a fair fight.

Following modes will be supported: deathmatch, team cooperation and capture the flag.

Every month we will organize online championships with special prizes.

PUBLIC LEVEL EDITOR

We will create a public level editor with open ratings system. Best levels will be added to the game every month.

PORT TO OTHER PLATFORMS

The game will be ported to Android / Windows Phone / OUYA / PC / Mac platforms.
