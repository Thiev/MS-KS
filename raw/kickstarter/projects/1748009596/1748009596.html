Project: Red Experience a world and story unlike any other! With a new form of RPFPS (Role Playing First Person Shooter). A fully immersive 3D experience with unique gameplay. This is a passion project made with a deep love for games. For Gamers, By Gamers.

What is Project: Red? 

Project: Red is a role playing first person shooter a pioneer project that has both a dynamic world and a complex battle system that takes many features from current shooters with a new spin. 

The world is ravaged by a global war in the year 2057 with three massive countries throwing all they have at each other. In this war you are a United Nations soldier attempting to thwart the attacks by the Asian super powers on the American coast of the Pacific. 

The game will be a multi-platform game that runs off the brilliantly made Unreal Engine which allows for great gameplay and stunning graphics. 

Project Red will be designed to grab the player emotionally by the dynamic storyline as well as mentally by forcing the player to think before acting in order to defeat the endless waves of invaders. 

The weapons and armor will be highly customizable allowing for different personalities to feel the game fits them best. Also due to the futuristic setting, there will be mechs, yes MECHS! Full body suits that allow for devastating mayhem as you rampage through the streets with guns and rockets. The suits will enhance strength, damage, speed, and health, making for a destructive scenario. 

 The scenery will contain urban and countryside, indoor and outdoor, and old and new. The world will be remodeled to set the mood of the future that we have envisioned the world to come to, having some familiar sights as well as new building and designs never before seen. 

This is a game that will appease many groups, Hardcore Gamers looking for a new way to shoot up bad guys as well as the Casual Gamers looking for a fun time and a deep story. 

...And yes for all you fanatics out there, there will be a multiplayer mode where you can take your new skills learned in the single player story to kick butt online either working together to live in the survival mode or by teaming up against fellow players in traditional and new game modes like team death matches. Multiplayer would available shortly after launch if Project: Red is OVER FUNDED!

Key Features 

  •  Single player role playing first person shooter a combination between both categories
  •  A massive array of guns to choose from, snipers, assault rifles, SMGs, LMGs, shotguns and more!! 
  •  Over 20 different attachments and buffs for your guns to sweeten up the fun. 
  •  Construction tables allowing the player to design and personalize a gun.  
  • Cover based system allowing for the player to take cover while under fire. 
  •  Many unique abilities such as bio vision to give the player an edge and set this game apart from other current RPFPS games. 
  •  A bundle of different areas to fight in from urban to countryside, indoor to outdoor, and old to new. 
  •  A complex world that has interact-able objects. 
  •  A solid physics engine, props to Unreal Technologies. 
  •  A 12 song original soundtrack. Including this demo track.
  •  A deep storyline that captures the player through great animation and voice acting. 
  •  And hordes of enemy soldiers to gun down!!! 

If OVER FUNDED

  • Multiplayer Game Modes including Players Vs. Players team death-matches or Players Vs. Computers survival rounds.

Why KickStarter?

Project: Red has been in the process of creation for two years now, but only recently has the idea of turning it into a game become the path for this idea. If Project: Red becomes funded the reality of turning an idea and story into a game would be accomplished much more quickly. 

Our experience with crowd funding and personally witnessing success stories has been wonderfully positive for us at Vertigo Games and has opened our eyes to the power of a direct relationship with the gamers. We want to create RPFPSs in a fashion and style that helped to make the true classics. And along these lines we've abandoned the typical developer/publisher model and placed our future in the hands of gamers. We've embraced the crowd in every aspect. YOU are our publisher...and you are the best publisher we've ever worked with! 

 This process allows us to create the games exactly the way you want to see them. There is a purity of development when we can spend 100% of our efforts making the game with an open dialogue throughout the process. We'll start with a strong vision document that details our sensibilities, and from there we'll stay in sync with you, from concept through the execution of the ideas. 

Who is behind this project?

We are the game developers without all the fancy and excessive title. What we can offer however is the drive and ability to work with you to bring you the most engaging role-playing first person shooter experience we can. We are the people who want to make the game people want to enjoy, and we are willing to put our best foot forward. 

Project: Red is being developed by Vertigo Games, an indie team lead by John Vislisel. 

John is a special effect, 3D artist, as well as programmer with several years of production experience. His résumé includes 3D architectural construction, 2D and 3D animated films. And a mix of personal project games. He spends the rest of his waking moments being inspired by games, obsessing over sci-fi films & TV series and working on small video projects. 

Jake Schott (the project manager) have been friends for years. Project: Red is a culmination of their passion for games and 3D Art.

 Vertigo Games hopes to create many games for you to explore, but first need to bring Project: Red to life. 

Why Support Us? 

Video Games are made on many different platforms, with many different publishers and with many different licenses, but the bottom line is, the only way to be able to put creative decisions before commercial ones is to develop a game independently, and we are asking for your support to help us do just that. The reason we brought this project to Kickstarter is for a dream to become a reality! Help us believe in you by believing in us! 

The minimum goal for our campaign is $50,000 and if this goal is reached, Project: Red will definitely be a reality sooner rather than later. Sadly, if after a month, the campaign does not reach this minimum fee, all contributions will be refunded. 

Where Does it All Go? 

Money can often be complicated and as supporters you would only want to support something you can be confident that your money is going to help without being wasted. That is why we want to be completely transparent and the money we are trying to raise here is completely for the licenses, software, and hardware necessary to complete the game. None of the money is going to be used for one of us to make a profit. All the money that comes in is thrown directly into the project. 

Above our Goal 

This is the best part, really. “Project: Red” has been designed with expansion in mind, and through stretch goals, we can expand the vision and scale of the game almost seamlessly. 

  • Our first step after being fully funded is to fund the ability to take our game into a multiplayer experience so you can take the fun you’ve had and share it with your friends. 
  •  Also our team is very focused but minimal with extra funds we could expand the ability to have other qualified individuals jump on board and enrich the game experience. 

Now we don’t want to share all of our secrets! But we also want you to be able to share in the confidence that we have for this game! In order to do that, we are offering weekly updates on progress and monumental steps as they come to you, our funders, with any amount of support!
