KORK - Kingdom of Royal Knights is a new CRPG under development by Noorcon Inc.

INTRODUCTION:

First I would like to take the time and from the start say that I am thankful and grateful for KickStarter and more importantly YOU! Yes, you are and will be responsible for the success of KORK! So thank you for all of your support!

We have achieved quite a bit of milestones set internally, and we are going to share those achievements with you on this page, and throughout the life of the project.

We have set a target goal of $66,333.00 dollars. This is an extremely conservative approach for a project of this type and caliber. Of course we would like to see our funding to hit between $250,000.00 and $550,000.00. This would be more realistic for any independent studio who wants to create and publish an RPG.

Our goal is to have the initial release be ported to the following platforms:

(1)Microsoft Windows

(2)Mac Computers

(3)Linux

If we do get more funding from the community, our goal is to also provide the game on the following mobile platform:

(1)iOS for the iPhone and iPAD

(2)Android

(3)Windows Phone

Again, I would like to thank you for your time and support!

Sincerely,

As of March 2013:

The team has been hard at work in bringing together the story and game play another step closer for the completion of the project. The following video will give you a sneak peek at the starter level and also demo some of the particle FX that are currently under development.

As of February 2013:

KORK has been in development since December of 2012. We have been working hard to bring our ideas into reality by designing and developing the internal working of the game components from the software side of the game and from the graphics side of the game!

We have been working hard to identify and create the basics of the Artificial Intelligence (AI) code which will be used to control and manipulate all of the characters in the game. The software mechanics which will control the user interaction with in game objects, and the code which will glue everything together!

We have had great progress is the level design and level creation of the game. Bringing together great tools and artists who together we were able to generate nice looking terrains and great looking graphics for the time and budget which was allocated internally by Noorcon Inc.

We are now seeking more funding to be able to concentrate on the 3D modeling/animation for the characters under development for the game. As well as the other 3D objects which will be part of the world. We are also going to need the funding to create better code (software) that will actually work to bring into life everything in the world! Basically the brains behind everything!

We are really excited about the amount of work which we have achieved in such a short amount of time, and we are looking forward in doing more as we progress throughout the development life-cycle.

Risks and challenges Learn about accountability on Kickstarter

As all great discoveries and projects in the world, there are some risks and challenges to our project as well. However, the risks and challenges for our project are not as complex as some of the more serious risks and challenges that say a project that is going to send humans to Mars! Obviously there are larger risks and challenges facing for a project of that size and ambition!

Having said this, we still do face some risks and challenges, albeit in smaller factors!

Creating a SCOPE CREEP! Sometime, teams get excited and keep adding new missions and objectives to their project which causes what is called a Scope Creep! A never ending project! We are going to make sure that we do not get into this mode! We have set simple and doable objective for our game, this will ensure that we can focus on the first release of the game. Any new ideas or additions can always be added in a sequel!

BUDGET: Yes, we need to be able to feed our families, and hence it is important for us to be able to financially survive and work on the project. In order for us to be able to deliver better graphics and game play we do need a ROBUST BUDGET. This will ensure that we can provide for our families while we are working on delivering the game!

The good news is that, we have all of the TALENT to be able to achieve the goals we have set for our project! The challenge we are facing is the financial resource necessary to bring our goals and ideas into the world!

Here is where your HELP and CONTRIBUTION will really dictate the faith of our project.

We and thankful and grateful for each and every pledge. We will be working very hard to make sure that we design and develop a really great game.

Thank you in advance.
The Development Team
