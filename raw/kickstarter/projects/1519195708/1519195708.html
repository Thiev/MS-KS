Immersive is a multiplayer exclusive fighter with the power to create your own unique character & fully control your destiny.

11/23/2013 Update: Dark Anime Studios is now officially supporting PayPal!

Immersive is an all-new, real time, open arena, multiplayer exclusive. Immersive resurrects that incredible feeling of being in control. Allowing players to truly be involved with their characters destiny. Unlike typical fighters Immersive allows the player to dive deeper into their characters progression.

 

Immersive is an entirely new property, delivered using one of the most powerful game development technologies available today. Immersive is a fighting game for the new millennium that goes beyond the concepts that made the genre great.

At the base funding level, Immersive will be developed for Windows with a targeted release of mid-to-late 2015.

The game is being designed with a flexible scope in mind, as the budget grows, the game grows. More maps; more weapons; more easter eggs; more complexity; more adventure!

 Immersive's story revolves around the player's creation. Simply put Immersive has no story. Players will use their "Story Book" to create their own fantasy adventures or join in on adventures created by the community.

Aside from using various map to create a truly compelling story, players will direct their own cut scenes using Immersive's "Story Book Director". Initiate or terminate battles with the press of button. and change camera angles to insure spectators receive a good show, along with the ability to choose who is allowed to enter your "Story Book" . With no story, Immersive allows it's players to create their own anime or fantasy adventure using their dream character!

 Immersive has a few game design elements to insure the player is in control and not the other way around. DAS's basic primary design is very straightforward and simple:

• Build every element of the experience to make you the player, feel like this virtual world and character has become your world and character.
• The sound and music will be balanced; they will add emotion but not become distracting or increasingly annoying.
• The interface should be intuitive, simple and transparent.
 

 Environments play a big role in Immersive. By utilizing Epic Games' powerful UDK engine, Immersive will deliver visually stunning environments that will immerse it's players. UDK is an amazing resource that enables us to build a world that players can completely lose themselves in for hours or even days on end. For more information on UDK click here.

 We'd be excited to reach our target goal and to create an amazing experience for each and every individual, but we're also anticipating what it means to move beyond that goal. Achieving a Kickstarter funding over the base level would be used to benefit Immersive to become bigger and better - adding such stretch goals as new places to explore, additional platforms, localization, and much more.

 

 It's simple: This is what we love ♥

As hardcore gamers ourselves we want to deliver a game that focuses primarily on you the player. Unlike traditional mainstreamed and commercialized games' purposes that are only to recieve monetary gain, we intend to successfully launch immersive with numerous amounts of content while still keeping a Free-To-Play model. We love giving the individual player full control. We are doing this for those who like the idea of telling their own stories and for those who love fighters but hate that you are unable to use a character of your original creation.

 Currently, we are independent, and it feels good. Money has come and gone, but we've managed to stay alive as a small, independent studio. Indie developers and studios are making a universal comeback thanks to some interesting new publishing platforms and models - such as Kickstarter.

Kickstarter is a way for us to deliver a stunning experience that doesn't empty your pocket, while keeping us independent enough to make choices that a publisher might not understand or abide to. In addition to regular Kickstarter updates, we will be making our development process transparent and accessible to our supporters in the form of exclusive backer forums. You'll be able to interact directly with the creators, read behind-the-scenes blog posts about the ins and outs of the game's development, and stay current with our overall progress.

 The funding will be spent solely on Immersive. It's very straightforward: we'll spend the funds that you invest on making Immersive the best possible experience.

We'll be adding to the team after we get funding, so we can finalize our design and then get started on all the new art, modeling, texturing, sounds, music, lighting, characters, interface, technology, and more.

Risks and challenges Learn about accountability on Kickstarter

Risk 1: The Servers

With all multiplayer themed games there are bound to be some server issues. Our goal is to launch Immersive with servers running as smooth as possible.

Risk 2: A Big Project

What we’re attempting here is kind of ambitious. We want to create the feeling that the player is actually visiting another world full of unique and well developed characters. This involves combining gameplay elements from a few different genres with a lot of handmade content, including a great deal of unique character animation and environment detail. There will be a fair amount of experimentation in our approach.

Risk 3: Not Enough Content

With Immersive we want to create a world where no two characters are alike. This requires a great amount of content. Failing here will limit the player's creation process, throwing immersive into the "Typical Multiplayer Game" section.
