Risks and challenges Learn about accountability on Kickstarter

First off: The project will indeed be perfectly available to all audiences, be it Windows, Mac OSX, iOS, Android, and even Linux!

Making video games is really cumbersome if you don't know what you are doing, it's just the touch of heart that begs the perfection, and courage to obtain the final goal- a successful release.

Only when you put your mind into it, shall you truly extract your dreaming fantasy and cause the sea of dreams in which you have imagined to flourish and splash onto your naked shores of reality.

We are currently targeting all iOS devices from 2010 onward (which specifically leaves out iPhone 3GS or earlier), and iOS 5.1 and above. Depending on what Apple does with iOS, we may also need to trim our list of supported devices and versions.

There are risks of compatibility issues on some devices.
Android devices have an even broader range of performance than iOS devices. Our current minimum Android version is 2.3, but even within 2.3, some devices with older graphics chips or lower RAM may not be supportable.

Android tend to have different compatibility issues in the phone market. if you go through some of the games of today in googleplay, It's a nightmare down there in the comments section: some people complain that a certain game isn't working or freezes because of certain android device.

On both Android and iOS, we utilize Unity3D version 4.1 as our development platform. If Unity drops support for or has irreversible bugs on a particular OS/device combination, we have no means to bypass that issue.

also the App store are controlled by the guys at apple, they tend to not like an app that isn't part of their "Quality" of things, this depends upon the principle of customer satisfaction and the principle of the app.

We also need time to set up the server basics for high-scores, this will take awhile, so once the servers are fully up and running will you be able to see your user-name and high-scores.

Your generous support will minimize all these risks, thank you very very very much!
