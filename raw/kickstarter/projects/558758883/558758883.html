Story based puzzle solving adventure. Gameplay rewards are not just virtual prizes or level ups - they are real!

 

Dream Song’s family friendly gameplay evolves and progresses in conjunction with its strong storyline. All actions and decisions unlock something. Whilst primarily it is an adventure puzzle game, it includes elements not normally associated with this kind of genre, such as real time 3D flight and a climatic action combat scene (Quick Time Event). We want to keep your mind active and your reflexes keen.Will you be the one to help Michael and his grandpa solve the mystery of the dream world and discover the Lost Song? If you are the first to find it you will win a hard earned reward: The Lost Treasure (as featured in the story) – a real ceramic chalice glazed with gold and bound with a gold rim.

When you have read what follows you will be some way to understanding our passion to get Dream Song finished and released. We took an idea and through commitment brought it to where it is now - we are proud of what we have done so far. This is our first project. We don’t have a large company to support us or to pay us a wage. 

We really do need KICKSTARTER and we really do need YOU!

If Dream Song and all it offers appeals to you, then please get behind us, give it your support and we will finish a unique game that we can all be proud of. If you are in the credits, then it is credit to you also.

Thank you.
Bern & Sean

Six chapters of intrigue and mystery which through game play draws you ever deeper into a fairytale adventure, where your thoughts and actions are crucial to bring all its story threads and features toward a tantalising cliffhanger – but will you manage it? There are many different puzzle types, with riddles to keep you thinking day and night. Solving these will entitle you to unlock digital content, such as a virtual pocket watch, a stand-alone puzzle game, and specially designed wallpapers. You will also gain a chance to earn through skill and merit a real reward every month for six months.

Another six chapters of mystery and venture, but this time heightening the experience through plot and story with more intensifying puzzles and game play. With the unlocking of each chapter you are driven progressively to reveal and solve Dream Song’s hidden secrets, drawing you towards its master riddle and climatic finish.

All registered owners of Dream Song Part 1 and 2 will, on the twelfth month, be entitled to download the ‘Dream Crystal’. This essential download is intrinsic to the solution of Dream Song’s master riddle. Through detective work, diligence and persistence throughout all twelve chapters the skilled puzzle solver could earn Dream Song’s lost treasure: a beautifully hand crafted ceramic and gold chalice ~ all you have to do is find the Lost Song!

But don’t think for one minute that’s the end. For after one year (from Dream Song’s release) whether the Lost Song is found or not, a clue to a SECOND MASTER RIDDLE will be revealed, offering the chance to claim another unique treasure. When you buy the full game of Dream Song, you buy into a minimum of two years' experience of monthly ongoing riddles and puzzles, not just to keep you on your toes, but to earn you a chance of virtual and real rewards.

Real rewards Include beautiful full colour Dream Song books, an original 1912 Edison Bell 78 'The Song of the Thrush' (as featured in the story) and 3D printed miniature polished gold steel and polished nickel steel chalices (approximately 4.5cm high).
See below for other precious Dream Song crafted objects, available from our Shapeways DS 3D store if we reach our second stretch goal.

However, you don’t have to play Dream Song just for the rewards, the game alone with its immersive story line and puzzles is complete in itself. Each chapter of text and every puzzle solved leads you toward a satisfying and climatic gaming conclusion.

Part of Dream Song’s uniqueness involves discovering half-hidden items in the virtual world that can be produced, fully revealed, in the real world via a printer. It is through this, with the clear evidence you will hold in your possession, that your actions and thinking in the real world become crucial. Further, because Dream Song does not tie you to the computer with all its game play, you can easily take its printed out elements to school, college, work, pub, or club, or even a specially organised Dream Song meeting. Share it with friends, family and other Dreamers. Collaboration may bring about answers you never thought of. We want Dream Song not just to be immersive in your virtual world, but also to offer you a totally immersive experience in your real life with real people – hence real solutions deserve real prizes!

Dream Song’s first game play happens when you least expect it (we're not divulging that little surprise here), but soon after its introduction you are led systematically and intuitively through the main game controls. To aid your engagement in Dream Song we have avoided using typical computer function terminology, such as ‘point’, ‘click’, ‘press’, ‘save’, and ‘load’.  To start the game and to exit is a simple matter of lighting or dousing the candle that burns brightly on your desktop. Even the game’s function buttons, in the form of a tipped bag of runes, are intrinsic to the scene they represent. The story, puzzles, riddles, rewards and the lost treasure are all linked and play their part in bringing together the fullness and entire experience that is Dream Song. As the red book in Dream Song says, “You are about to discover a world of synchronicity, not just in the pages of this book, but in your very life.” We at Felstaf don’t want anything to detract you from that!

Dream Song is not all work and no play - we believe there is a place and time for everything. So, when you enter Grandpa’s study you can, if you wish, take time out and chill, for his room allows you to try out many things. Stand alone puzzles and mini-games are a feature here and some allow you to practice the very skills you need to use in the main game itself. You can piece together and repair old gramophone records and, if you have found the lost gramophone needle, they will even play. There are all sorts of weird paraphernalia just waiting to be prodded, poked or picked up. You will find shelves of old books from ‘Mad Inventors’ to ‘Dreamers’, and some relevant to Kickstarter backers (see pledges).

The main object in Grandpa’s room though, is not the gramophone or the working telescope that points you to something strange, but ‘The Stone Whisperer’. Will you be able to acquire all its missing parts and piece it together to enable it to function? You will if you can find the missing blueprints that illustrate the nine crackpot inventions that you have to assemble and operate (see Pledge 17 CRACKPOT INVENTIONS). The Stone Whisperer is an integral and fascinating part to working out the master riddle.

All of Dream Song’s main game play is based on its twelve chapters of story and the Tablets of Living Clay that accompany them. Throughout the game, among other things, you will obtain special postcards. How observant are you? Can you answer the questions they ask? If you do you will bring to life the twelve clay tablets. Each tablet holds a sequence of puzzles that unlock many features as well as opening up a portal that will enable you to enter Azmad’s Realm.

Will you be as experienced as Michael, flying swiftly in this Dream World, to follow the elusive Song Thrush? Will you be able to soar, dive and swoop your way through the dark tangled forest in a dramatic chase? Will you make it to the Lonely Tower? Even if you do, what is the secret behind the Magical Feathers needed to assist you in unlocking the mysterious boxes within? Every time you complete the puzzle that waits inside these boxes you unlock a clay picture for printing and a further chapter to read and to print out – what clues will they reveal that were not seen on screen?

As you progress, unravelling the mystery that is Dream Song, key puzzles solved unlock the doors and drawers of The Collector’s Box. What secrets await you there? Will you find another page from Grandpa’s book or another scribbled note? Will you be able to print them out to reveal yet more truths? What about the eight strange objects you collected and have you found the gems yet? What is their significance?

Your wits as a sleuth and as a treasure hunter will sharpen with every turn. With each correct move it will soon become clear which is the right path and which is the wrong. As you successfully progress through certain key puzzles you may notice the mysterious pictures that at first seemed void of illustration begin to reveal their purpose. Through a keen eye and persistence the truth begins to dawn and you will know you are getting close to discovering the Lost Song. What is more, because Dream Song offers you a totally immersive experience, you won’t just know it; you will feel it!

Perhaps at this stage of the game you may wonder why the little Butterfly Box does not open and what is there left to do? If you have been diligent and have completed all the tasks, then you are prepared. If you have succeeded in helping Michael and his grandpa defeat the Wizard-king Azmad, then you are ready. All you need now is to download the Dream Crystal to help you solve The Riddle of the Two Brothers. This is the final part of the puzzle that will enable you to solve the Master Riddle, find the Lost Song and gain the chance to win a hard earned reward: The Lost Treasure – a ceramic chalice glazed with gold and bound with a golden rim.

If you believe yourself to be hardworking, diligent and determined, then you could be the one to earn this special reward. In this world the truth is ours to claim. All we have to do is walk towards it – when all is put together and understood, the truth is simple.
