The first multimedia video game that was ever made is reborn! Starring Christopher Lee.

DEUS EX MACHINA 2

Deus Ex Machina 2 is the reimagination of the classic 1984 Game Of The Year, Deus Ex Machina, the first true multimedia video game ever made.

Written by Mel Croucher, creator of the original game, published by Automata, the first video games company in the UK, this brand new version is a darkly comic cradle-to-grave roller coaster of your entire life. It takes you on a surreal journey from before your own birth, all the way through the temptations and dangers of your life, and beyond your own death. And the music's great too!

  • PC, Mac and Linux versions developed using Unity
  • words translated from English into 18 more languages
  • soundtrack album released
  • book ready for publication

WHY YOUR MONEY IS NEEDED

We want to get you involved, join in the fun, become part of Deus Ex Machina 2, and help us fund the following vital elements.

  • We need £24,500 to perfect the user-options, gameplay and scoring with your help, before we release the final PC and Mac versions.
  • £23,000 to complete the prototypes for the iOS and Android mobile versions.
  • £8,500 to customize and personalize the opening and closing sequences for all versions with the names and images of our Kickstarter backers.
  • £4,500 to remix the ultimate in-your-head soundtrack for all versions.
  • And of course we'll need to pay Kickstarter, and some fees, and some minimal packaging costs for your lovely rewards, around £3,500.

So, OUR GOAL to achieve all of that is to raise £64,000 with your help.

STRETCH GOALS beyond £64,000

£84,000
We want to make the game as mind-blowing as we can, so a virtual reality version is high on our target list. We'll produce Deus Ex Machina 2 The Director's Cut, for the Oculus Rift virtual reality headset if you can help us hit this stretch goal.

£112,000
Our aim is to reach out and entertain people all over the planet, which is why we have already translated the game from English into Catalan, Chinese, Czech, Danish, Dutch, French, German, Greek, Hungarian, Italian, Japanese, Norwegian, Polish, Portuguese, Russian, Spanish, Swedish and Welsh. If we can achieve this stretch goal then as well as the 19 languages already built in to the game, we'll invest this additional backing to add another 10 translations to cover the rest of the game-playing planet.

£128,000
If we hit double our target we'll celebrate by sending every Kickstarter backer a code to download Deus Ex Machina 2 for your choice of iOS or Android format, on the day of publication, no matter how little or how much you have pledged. And we'll stop planning and get ready to start shooting Deus Ex Machina: The Movie, by Anthony and Nicola Caulfield (producers of movies on "War Of The Worlds", "Sonic The Hedgehog", and the history of video gaming documentary "From Bedrooms To Billions"). And of course every funder will receive a code to download their copy of the finished movie.

THE GAME

Imagine if your life was nothing more than some electronic game. Imagine if you knew then what you know now. Imagine if you could replay your little life all over again.

Battle your way to the womb, fertilize that egg, navigate the birth canal to get born, then focus, crawl, stand, balance, run, jump, learn and obey! Battle the hairs and zits of adolescence. Discover pleasure, love, pain, guilt and betrayal. Mess around, run away, hide out, suffer under, heal up, sell off, struggle through, knuckle down, tip over, cave in, dance on! Exploit your power, peddle your guilt, bust your body, embrace your decline, swallow your pills, lose your mind, dream your dreams and fly!

Sperms, cops, killer ducks, naughty bits, zimmer frames, hallicinogenic eyewear, dead mice, dementia, hermaphrodites, land mines, war crimes, neurons, morons, rock stars, and the greatest voice on the planet as your Narrator.

OUR SUPERSTAR VOICES

Sir Christopher Lee is The Narrator. The greatest voice on the planet. Star of Dracula, James Bond, Star Wars, Lord Of The Rings and The Hobbit. Become part of his incredible career by joining us on Kickstarter, and gain exclusive access to Christopher Lee interviews, outtakes and behind-the-scenes videos and images.

Ian Dury is The Fertilizer, remixed and remastered from his original brilliant performance. Kickstarter funders can enjoy bonus alternate takes by the legendary star of Hit Me With Your Rhythm Stick and Sex And Drugs And Rock'n'Roll.

Chyna Whyne is The Machine. Her haunting, soulful voice has earned her a place in rock history as favourite session singer with Eric Clapton, Bob Dylan and The Who. Help us fund the next stage of the project and gain access to her exclusive gallery.

Chris Madin is The Defect. One of the best young voices in pop, and star vocalist on X Factor. His performance, taking over Mel Croucher's original role, is outstanding.

Joaquim de Almeida is The Defect Police. One of the all-time great baddies, Golden Globe winner and star of hit productions including Desperado, 24, Missing, Batman and The West Wing. Join us and enjoy exclusive interviews and outtakes.

Mary Carew is The Teacher. She has an amazing operatic range and her global hits include Adiemus, one of the most popular classical tracks of all time.

Sulene Fleming is The Night Nurse. A brilliant performer and star of Ministry of Sound, Sulene's talents as a songwriter shine in all her compositions for her role in our project.

The great Jonathan Cook is our audio producer, and other musical contributors include Chris Blackwell, Ray Davies, Johnny Lithium, Rick Eastman, Alidor Nzinga, the Crouch End Festival Chorus and the Royal Philharmonic Orchestra, with orchestral music by Debbie Wiseman.

Risks and challenges Learn about accountability on Kickstarter

All human activity involves risks and challenges, and games development is no exception. Developers can run out of time, producers can run out of money, key personnel can run out the door to join the circus, early ambitions can turn out to be impractical, and there's the situation where everything goes to plan but the plan stinks.

Well, I don't think we're going to run out of time, because all the hard slog has been completed and we're now into perfection phase. I don't think we're going to run out of money, with your help, because the development, the music, the translations and the artwork are all paid for, and this funding is about knocking everything into shape. And our team is stable, experienced and besides we've already joined the circus.

Since I started the first ever video games company in the UK, my team has never failed to deliver on time every time. This time it's no different and I believe the risk of failing to complete the project is very low indeed. If I happen to drop dead, go mad or disappear, then my colleagues will take over seamlessly.

Essentially, this is the game I have wanted to make since 1983, ever since I ran up against the technical limitations to my future-vision first time round. All I had to do was wait 30 years for the technology to catch up, and for Kickstarter to put me in touch with you. I want to make this game not for myself, but for all of us. So, maybe the only real risk for you to consider is that Deus Ex Machina 2 stinks.

Right at the start, thirty years ago, I took the risk of defying the norms and busting the templates of conventional gaming, by producing Deus Ex Machina as an interactive movie, played in real time to a perfectly synchronized rock music soundtrack. And now I pledge to do everything I can to turn that original vision into reality at last.

My heartfelt thanks to you for taking the time to read this. If you're considering backing the project then I hope you enjoy the ride and I look forward to welcoming you aboard with open arms. Keep smiling.

Mel Croucher

  • Is Deus Ex Machina 2 DRM-free?

    Yes. There will be no DRM restrictions on Deus Ex Machina 2. We do not shackle our titles with Digital Rights Management (DRM) for the following reasons:

    We trust you, our backers and our players, not to pirate Deus Ex Machina 2. DRM does not stop the illegal use of files, it just adds hassle for paying players. We do not want to waste our money and our backers' money on DRM costs. DRM-free content will play on tomorrow's platforms as well as today's. DRM would fundamentally change who is in control of Deus Ex Machina 2, and we want to share that control with you.

    Last updated: Wed, May 29 2013 7:00 am EDT
  • What are the player options and game content of Deus Ex Machina 2?

    The in-game player options are ...

    LANGUAGE select from your choice of English, Portuguese, Spanish, Catalan, French, Italian, Greek, German, Russian, Chinese, Japanese, Dutch, Danish, Swedish, Norwegian, Polish, Czech, Hungarian and Welsh. SUBTITLES toggle on/off. BIRTHPLACE planting of your homing-beacon. PAUSE your life, to freeze the action at any spot. RESUME the life from the moment it was paused. REBIRTH to restart your life from the beginning. OPTIONS to control the playing experience, including audio controls. QUIT. But why would you?
    Here's a complete rundown of the DEUS EX MACHINA 2 experience. Remember, this is no conventional game, it's an interactive movie that takes exactly one hour from start to finish, so here goes ...

    PRELUDE: make choices that will affect the whole of your life, before you navigate your roller-coaster journey through your own existence. You may encounter, touch, enjoy and exploit all the good things in your life. Or you may avoid, reject, overcome and defeat all the bad things in your life. Or to hell with the rules ... you may chose to do the opposite!
    FERTLIZER: outwit the rival sperms and their comedy football formation tactics to fertilize that tempting egg.
    BIRTH: navigate the pulsing slalem to the outside world and dodge the squid-cam spy probes of the Defect Police.
    INFANCY: learn to crawl, stand, balance, walk, run and jump your way through your earliest experiences, as forces more powerful than you try to control your mind and condition your behaviour.
    SCHOOL: run your own race and resist all the propaganda to obey, as the teacher from hell force-feeds you the rules of the institution.
    ADOLESCENCE: battle those sprouting hairs and erupting zits, and navigate a hormone-fuelled pathway of teen-dreams.
    LOVE: accept those tempting lips and get jiggy aboard that yellow submarine. You know you want to.
    BETRAYAL: reject the spotlight of publicity and manipulate your privacy shell through the catwalk, because the war-mongers have plans for you.
    SOLDIER: this is no place for heros. Run, dodge and flee the urban battlefield to survive.
    CRIPPLE: your body lies shattered in a nightmare hospital, but your mind is free to navigate and heal.
    WAR CRIMES: face your trial and evade entrapment in the courtroom of your worst nightmare.
    DANCE: send in the clones and have a ball, as you party the night away in the ultimate club scene.
    GUILT: your pathway is through the heart of the global trading world, where every investment you make will affect the lives of millions.
    POWER: the lonliest road of all to navigate, with a supercity under your control, but is there anyone left to command?
    DECLINE: now you battle the enemy within, through your own veins and arteries, hunted by white killer cells as you try and bust the blood clots of a massive heart attack.
    DANGER: a race against time, with your brain under attack and its neural pathways acting like a rogue pinball machine.
    OLD AGE: your geriatric home looks like a comedy clockwork hallucination, and every time you pop a pill or swap your eyeware your world mutates into something even weirder.
    SENILITY: your pathway is through a dreamscape of false memories and real events from your incredibly long life. Can you even walk unaided?
    WONDER: but hey ... that futuristic buggy provides a mean ride towards the ultimate portal.
    DEATH: well, what did you expect ... multiple endings?! We all come to it, and it's nearly game over.
    TRANSFIGURATION: except for one final surprise, involving a wheelchair, some wings, an entire morphing planet and a free ride home.
    THANKS: actually, two final surprises if you happen to spot your own face in there!
