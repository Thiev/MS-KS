Nightmare Zombies is the first Oculus Rift Only immersive zombie simulator in the Post-Apocalypse urban environment of New York City.

Nightmare Zombies brings a new way into horror that no game offers, all players will have a chance to see the true first person perspective.

Urban Planning
We did an overlay of the map with blender to show proof of concept on the game design, we will go 1 on 1 scale with streets, the city blocks will contain 1-4 buildings each.

There will be multiple revisions on the development of Central Park until its adequate in quality & style.

Creative Inspiration is based on Neo-Toyko from Anime of Akira, and Freedom Project, including Blade Runner.

Self-Aware Zombies + Lots of Zombies

Gameplay (Single Player, Persistent or 24 hour story mode)

P.A.D.D Air Drone (Built-in 3D Printer/Manufacturing Robot)

P.A.D.D will contain a 3D printing system that will create anywhere from Military weapons to explosives on demand, due to the Fluid Nano Chambers within the drone.

Access to Military Grade Weapons, Medical Equipment, Including Bio Tissue Repair.

Raw Matter can be deposited into the drone.

  • 5 Massive Underground Networks
  • Level 1: Only 10% of lights are on, and this sub-floor is the deadliest, zombies are roaming and there is a fair share of items scattered that will be attainable just walking around with a flashlight. Most of the stores will be closed (breaking & entering), some will be opened.
  • Level 2: Most of the lights work, but there are still zombies in the region, safer to travel but your odds of finding items are rare in this area. (mostly stolen and damaged)
  • Level 3: no zombies exist in this floor, mercenaries and militia will be roaming since its a fast travel across the map, including thieves and armed robbers will be present.

  • Level 4: Security is present in this area, no weapons are allowed within the area (items will be stored & safe), this is a central trading hub, you can chill with your friends or negotiate on your next move.

  • Level 5: Private Sector Floor (Classified)

    Massive Subways are planned and will added, these subways will have access to multiple massive unique Government Storage Areas

Nightmare Zombies is the first to bring Zombie Post-Apocalypse to the Oculus Rift. The year is 2094 and the outbreak has started in downtown Manhattan New York, you walk outside to see what you can do to survive, its the fall of humanity, your destiny lies within you. 

We know every zombie game out there, its been there done that, that's why we are taking a different approach, the rules are simple, if its not original we wont implement it!

True Anarchy Environments, its the fall of humanity. The Rules are simple, they are no rules, or government that exist to enforce justice, you can come together or fight for resources. you and your friends decide the faith of others. 

Why you will love this game

Other than being chased by a horde, The game will offer state of the art Military Units including updated FA-18 Hornet Jet, Tanks, Vehicles, and also a T-7500 Apache.

T-7500 Information:

Still under Heavy Development is the T-7500 model, we are working with the special rotor system animation and the Cockpit display system, including its Auto Hover stability system (Optional: easy flying) This unit is used in combat warfare, military grade, the governments usage is for riot control under martial law. Payload will include 2000 rounds of ammo, including 6 missiles, supports 1 pilot and 4 passengers. Equipment: 2 Aviation landing lights, Thermal Vision & Target Acquisition

FAQ

  • What are the System Requirements ?

    Minimum System Requirements
    Hardware: Oculus Rift VR
    OS: Windows 7 64-Bit / Windows 8 64-Bit / Linux 64-Bit
    CPU: AMD 8 Core 4.0 GHZ or better
    RAM: 16 GB RAM
    HDD: 80 GB HD space
    Video: NVIDIA GeForce Titan or better
    Sound: DirectX Compatible Sound Card
    DirectX: 11
    Internet: Broadband Internet connection and Steam and Online Multiplayer

    Minimum Hardware specifications are for optimal +60FPS, SLI highly recommended

    Recommended System Requirements
    Hardware: Oculus Rift VR
    OS: Windows 7 64-Bit / Windows 8 64-Bit / Linux 64-Bit
    CPU: AMD 8 Core 5.0 GHZ or better
    RAM: 32 GB RAM
    HDD: 80 GB HD space
    Video: 3-way SLI NVIDIA GeForce Titans
    Sound: DirectX Compatible Sound Card
    DirectX: 11
    Internet: Broadband Internet connection and Steam and Online Multiplayer
