Picture Back At the Barnyard meets Call of Duty. Play as humanized animals who work for an underground military organization, F.A.R.M.

We are a small, independent company just trying to get started.  We decided to try raising the money ourselves, rather than trying to go through a publisher.  By doing so, we can make the games the way we picture them.  This allows us to put more focus on aspects of the game such as game play mechanics, graphic quality, and storyline without the outside influence of a publisher.  It is our goal to make F.A.R.M. and any future titles with our fans in mind and give ourselves the control to make the games we want the way we want to make them.

*NOTE  We are aware that some viewers are experiencing lag with the video.  We are not having this issue on our systems, so we appreciate you bringing it to our attention.  We are in the process of fixing this issue and should have a replacement video in the next day or two. 

Double Our Fun(ds)! We're participating in OUYA's #FreeTheGames Fund, created by OUYA to support developers making new and creative console games. OUYA will match your pledge dollar-for-dollar up to your goal amount (and a max of $250,000), so help us get there and make a great game for everyone! To be eligible for this match, we commit that this game will be an OUYA exclusive for a period of time. To learn more about OUYA, go to www.OUYA.tv.

 In the command center, you will have access to security monitors.  These monitors are live streaming, running constantly in real time.  These give you the ability to zoom in on areas and spy on the different things going on in the world.  Valuable intel can be obtained with this method of intelligence.  As you progress through the game, more areas and cameras become available, eventurally giving you access to every place withing the game world.

 Take some time out and hone your skills at shooting and melee combat in our hi-tech shooting gallery. It is equipped with many of our unique weapons, such as corn gattling guns, pea shooters, potato guns, mushroom cap grenades, and many more.

 In F.A.R.M., you will play as many different characters with different styles of both movement and abilities.  You will be able to fly as our duck fighter pilot and carrier pigeon.  You can sneak in and out of small spaces, climb walls and ceilings, as well as shoot webs to place new cameras using our spy-der.  Fight melee as our fox who was trained by a former ninja.  And shoot, sneak, and fight your way through areas with Swinehaur.

You will also visit many different areas, including a beaver dam which is risking explosion, a full city, outskirting farms, and even aboard ships!

 In one of these many buildings is a hidden entrance to a F.A.R.M. research lab.  The top level part of this mission is to sneak around undetected and find the secret entrance.

 We are offering permanent advertising with the game world.  Your ad can be for your business, a memorium, or really anything you want put on the sign.  Ads must comply to our rating restrictions as explained in the FAQ below.  Your billboard will appear somewhere within the game

Risks and challenges Learn about accountability on Kickstarter

With any project, some things happen that are out of our control. We may experience delays in production due to the size of our team, but we are all very focused and motivated to create this game within the next year. If there are delays, backers will be updated via the backer blog.
