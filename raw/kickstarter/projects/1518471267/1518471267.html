About Phoenix Revival

Phoenix Revival is an exciting new shoot 'em up (SHMUP) inspired by the great classics such as Phoenix, Astroids, and Galaga combined with a cooperative play style pioneered by games like League of Legends, World of Warcraft, and Diablo 3. We're launching Phoenix Revival on the OUYA initially and hope to bring it to other platforms in the near future.

Gameplay

We've put together this short gameplay video to give you a taste of what the game is all about. And if you've got an OUYA, head over to the storefront and play the game today.

The Ships

One of the best features of Phoenix Revival is the variety of gameplay options available through different ship types. The game will launch with four free ships, with additional free and purchasable ships available each month.

  • Tasadia (tank type)Â is built tough. This ship is great for drawing enemy fire and has a number of abilities to absorb and deflect enemy attacks. Play as Tasadia if you love being the core of your team.
  • Altheda (healer type)Â has a range of spells that provide defense and healing to the whole team. Play as Altheda if you love being self-sufficient in battle and a great ally.
  • Fireax (caster type)Â brings explosive firepower to any battle. Play as Fireax if you enjoy annihilating waves of enemies with screen filling destruction.
  • Silverspark (carry type) can break through even the toughest enemy defenses with powerful attacks. Play as Silverspark if you enjoy taking down dangerous opponents in head-to-head combat.

Risks and challenges Learn about accountability on Kickstarter

Phoenix Revival has been under development for three months and is currently available in the OUYA store, so we're very confident in our ability to use the funds raised from this Kickstarter to further improve the game.

The biggest challenge we face is improving the art and sound in the game. Syraca Studios is a one-man indie studio with strong support from close friends and industry associates. Progress could slow down significantly if we lose our current artists and collaborators. Having the additional resources from this Kickstarter will give us more options for hiring out various art and production tasks to improve the game faster.

We're relying on external companies to produce the physical rewards at a few backer levels and we're aware that supplier issues can slow down or impede delivery of these types of rewards. We have identified alternate suppliers for each physical reward and are prepared to switch things up if necessary to get the rewards out the door. We promise to communicate regularly about the various rewards and any issues we may face.

We're committed to the success of Phoenix Revival. We plan to develop new content for the game for several years and would love to eventually see it available on many different platforms and introduce new styles of play. Come join us in making this dream a reality.
