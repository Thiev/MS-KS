Digit Math (name TBA) is designed with the ability to have well over 500+ Levels to help young kids actually be interested in/do Math.

Our goal is to use our partial AI (Artificial Intelligence) and Math functions/routines/arrays to create a specialized Math App that is designed to help kids to young adults learn how and keep interested in doing Math repetitively as we've done tons of research and continue to but the current way Math is taught in the public sector, it's proving to be an awful and unsuccessful mixture of methods. Everyone learns differently.

With our current design and behind the scene works of mathematical thinking that works based on the users score (right vs wrong) and actually determine what numbers (high, low, even, odd) he/she is having issues with and throw in automatically different mixes to help strengthen ones ability to do Math just a little better, we know we can help keep you and the young ones on the right path to success. What a long sentence but how do we keep you tuned in though, what platform of learning?

As the Mobile Phone world has more Smart Devices then Computers in general reaching well over 2 Billion yearly, this shows us gaming on a hand-held platform is the future but why not actually learn and get better at something, more like Math. As you know, everyone once said, "I will never need that type of Math when I am older"... Each and everyone of us are kicking ourselves as Adults now, especially us, as our careers depend on all forms of Math, especially the type we KNEW we weren't ever going to need.

The truth is, in some states not even 12% of students graduate High School and even less make it and a lot of it has to do with Teachers and School Districts who can't supply the time and dedication each individual needs and as horrible as it is, it's a fact and has yet to change.

Sure you can get a child a tutor but more and more that is a luxury that just isn't in the cards for most families with both parents out of work. However, beyond the Teacher factor, classrooms are noisy, annoying, sometimes smelly places kids are stuck for 1-2 hours per class and surrounded by friends or bullies but doesn't matter which is which as it is a distraction and making it more of an impossibility to learn or want to.

We're here to develop for a young persons mind, we know they enjoy being alone or away from Adults but they really love playing games and being in comfort but shooting the heads off of mythical creatures shouldn't be the only mathematic learning skill, "2 head shots gets 20 points and 1 body with 5 body shots is 10 points". Well, already we know 3 bodies have to exist to make 30 points but see the kids automatically generate this function and is technically great but the problem is, the way they're learning it, it is so quick and as good as that is, it has no real learning value they're going to recall or use in the real world but having 2 trees with 20 apples and 1 tree with 5 apples together is 25 apples in all, is a more likely real life situation and something they can possibly remember.

Studies show visuals are a MUST and we're here to prove it.If we don't successfully make this game with your or anyone help, one way or another it's coming out and going to prove our point but we really just want to help and as that goal will never change, we will continue to do EVERYTHING to get the Artwork and Audio+Video we need to enhance our App to keep it visually stunning and attractive to teach todays and tomorrows youth.

Thank you for your time, and check back as our Project Manager: Lance Seidman will be putting out his video shortly.

FAQ

  • Who is this really for?

    The answer is not so simple as we're designing this to be used by EVERYONE. However, in reality... We assume young children to young adults (5-18) will be our 65% of the real users. Currently we figure a big chunk of 19-38 year olds will be our 25% and obviously the other 10% is the older group but we sure do hope our stats are wrong and a good mix of everyone will be using it!

    Last updated: Thu, Oct 27 2011 3:21 pm EDT
  • Are you charging for this? If yes, why should I help?

    If we can get successfully funded, we will NOT charge for this Mobile App and actually freely distribute and take our long hard earned work and donate to the App for the cause but only if we can get the right funding...

    Last updated: Thu, Oct 27 2011 8:58 pm EDT
  • How are you really making a difference?

    Unlike most Math App's that are created, we're taking years and years of public research and converting it to a condensed all-in-one smart artificial intelligent tool for anyone to be able to pick up our app and eventually basically memorize answers to help learn the equations and the answers with it but also to really dig and plant the knowledge in the users brain.

    Last updated: Thu, Oct 27 2011 9:02 pm EDT
  • Why do you need over $8,000?

    Unfortunately we've gone to many, many different professional gaming studios to speaking with gaming Artists and Movie makers and unfortunately, around $6,700 is the cheapest quote we got but that is just for Artwork and another $3,000 for animating and modifying as well as getting unique royalty free audio and what have you. Which we are putting over $3,500 of our OWN money in too!
