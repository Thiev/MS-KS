Apollyonic is a new breed zombie shooter mixed with exploration, crafting/building, and super powers!

Apollyonic is keeping the classics alive by introducing an arcade style zombie shooter; blending action, adventure, open world exploration, and survival elements into one classic gaming experience.

Unlimited possibilities! The game will also feature random loot and a random gun generator, making the total amount of guns/loot available in game pretty much endless.

An experimental super soldier with unknown abilities. Sent in by the Government in a last hope attempt of regaining civilization. Apollyonic's character objective is to exterminate, find survivors, and adapt survival skills using various resources and techniques. Skills acquired through crafting weapons, objects for safe areas, and medicine.

Apollyonic game play tasks; locating objects throughout the cities used to build and create necessary items. Build safe areas, construct new items and weapons, earn kill-streaks, and much more!

Survival Elements focus on stealth, finding survivors and constructing safe areas. Apollyonic allows for full open world exploration to obtain needed items, materials and survivors, unlimited access into each house and building. Unlimited possibilities!

 The game will also feature random loot and a random gun generator, making the total amount of guns/loot available in game pretty much endless. There will also be various stats that will come into play. Take for instance your speed, if your carrying a bunch of heavy items in your inventory you will slow down making it harder to run from zombies.

Multiplayer! This game wouldn't be complete if we didn't have multiplayer. This game will allow you to play online four people max, and you will be able to work together to build, explore, trade, and even battle with with each other. 

It has been just my over worked / non-paid graphics artist who lives all the way in New Zealand, and myself(USA). After few months of hard work, we finally have something to show for our efforts.

The current state of the game is good enough to release as another arcade zombie shooter, but we want unique and fresh game-play for this project. With your help we can afford to work full time on this game. Maybe if we are lucky even hire in a fresh mind to help extend and make our ideas even better. With your help we will be able to implement our crafting and building ideas which would lead to endless possibilities for you as a player. We would also be able to make the open world even larger, more places to visit, more items to find, more things the player can create. With your help the random gun/loot generator will be made. With the help from you we can finally make the ultimate zombie sandbox game everyone has been waiting for.

How we will use this money

With the money you all so generously donate, we will use it as listed below.

Engine: Game Maker Studio - Master - $1000 per copy +
licensing fees for each platform.

This will run us roughly $1000 per copy. We are looking at 2-3 copies of this engine. The reason we chose to go with game maker is simple. The interface is easy on the eyes and the performance is outstanding. We will be able to release on PC, Mac, Linux, Android, iOS. All from one code base, we won't have to write tons of extra code to get them working properly on all devices.

Testing devices - Estimated $3000 

Let's face it quality control is a must, we don't want to ship out our game not knowing first hand how well it runs on different devices. So we estimated that it would cost around $3000 to obtain these. We would acquire a decent Mac(We need this alone to export to OS X / iOS), Android phone/tablet, iPad/iPhone. We are so serious about bugs that we don't even want the betas to have them.

Music / Sound- estimated - $3000

With your help we can afford to pay some of our extremely talented friends to write bone chilling music for the game. As well as purchase some high quality sound effects, and or have them custom made to our needs. 
Here's a example of a song that will be on the soundtrack

Miscellaneous: $11,000

This will cover any extra software we may need. It will also help with any extra assets that may be needed. We will also use this money to cover all physical rewards, and any other hidden expense. This money will also help with our living expenses. Making games isn't cheap, and it's extremely hard to do so without any money to help with living expenses. This money will ensure we can stay fully dedicated to this project.

Risks and challenges Learn about accountability on Kickstarter

Our biggest risk would be not getting the funds to do what we have planned. If for whatever reason we don't meet our goal we will slim this game down and release it as a simplified version of our vision.

Another issue could be the time frame people are expecting this game to be released. We have already put in many hours on this project and have a good amount to show for our efforts. We know there is still several hours of hard work ahead of us to reach this goal, and we are more then ready for it.
