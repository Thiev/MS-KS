BasketWars is an insane, challenging basketball game set in WWII coming first to iOS in March 2014.

WE NEED YOUR HELP!

We are JABB Interactive, an indie games company based out of Los Angeles, CA. Our goal is to create the most beautiful, addicting, and fun games the world has ever seen. We do this by attempting to differentiate our games from the rest of the market by thinking outside-the-box, and by fusing several genres into one awesome game. We are coming to Kickstarter for some help. We have been developing our game, BasketWars - World at War, for the last year and a half and we are simply out of funding to finish this truly awesome gameBecause we don't like waiting for stuff ourselves, we will submit whatever we have done to the App Store so you can play the game right away. The beauty of mobile games is that even though we are only 60% done with developing the game, we can implement the other 40% in future updates. The other 40% that you are helping us make includes some of the most exciting aspects of the game - meaning more levels, weapons, bosses, modes (multiplayer and Zombie mode) and features. A huge reason we're launching this Kickstarter now is to make sure when the campaign ends, you get to play, at the very least, the minimum viable product. We've had to make some concessions to make that possible, but at least you'll have the core game immediately, rather than waiting months to receive what was promised. If you are an Android user, this is even more reason for you to back our project as a portion of these funds will be used to make BasketWars for Android!!!

Help an indie game make it big, and get some cool stuff for doing it.

As with any basketball game, the core goal is to throw the basketball into the hoop. In BasketWars, however, theres a lot more to it - these arent ordinary balls and baskets. In our alternate (and crazy) history, the Axis forces have set up power stations at each major battle site that look oddly like basketball hoops.

The Allies have found that disabling these stations render the enemy forces defenses useless and allow the Allied forces to inch closer and closer to victory. The catch is they can only be disabled by interrupting the power flow of the stations net, which causes a power surge and blows up anything nearby. Thus, the Allies came together to create fearless fighting basketballs that will brave certain death and launch themselves (literally) into the war effort in order to take down these key strategic outposts.

Along the way, the Axis forces will notice your efforts and send in heavy firepower in the form of bosses grenadiers, tanks, planes, mortars, turrets, and more to block your path and take you down! Activate multiple power surges to destroy these beastly machinations and claim victory over the enemy!

Drag with your finger to aim, release to throw, tap while in the air to make the basketball shoot at anything in its way with one of 4 weapons. Dodge incoming missiles, bullets, mines and other obstacles in order to destroy those power stations and help the Allies claim victory!

Risks and challenges Learn about accountability on Kickstarter

As out first Kickstarter project, there are sure to be some hurdles along the way in regards to coordinating and shipping rewards. Weve done our best to prepare for these potential challenges and are committed to making sure our backers are taken care of. We hope youll bear with us if there are some hiccups in reward redemption, but if all goes well, patience wont be necessary! Were totally open to questions and concern, so send us a message here or talk to us on Facebook to get issues resolved.

As for the game, we have an internal deadline of around March 20 for the launch of the game, but even that is dependent on Apple approving the app. No matter what, we will make sure our backers are some of the first to know when there are any updates on the status of the game, and overall our goal is transparency in every aspect of our campaign.
