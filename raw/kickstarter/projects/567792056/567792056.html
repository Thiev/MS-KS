Zodiac-inspired Fantasy Fighting Game

 
We are initially seeking funds ($10,000) to launch the DEMO version of Zodiac Wars! (pledge $5-$10 to have access to the Alpha, Beta and Demo when they become available). All extra funding will go towards Revamping the Overall Game Experience (Graphics, Gameplay, Physics, etc.)

*** The $20 Pledge for a Copy of the Complete Finished game will be available after the $100K Strech Goal has been reached. We have a SPECIAL $15 pledge that will get you the final version of the game when it is completed (limited to first 1,000 backers only).

 
 Zodiac Wars         is a Next-Generation one-on-one Fantasy Fighting Game, where all its main characters are inspired by the 12 signs of the zodiac . The Fighters will compete against each other in an Arcade-style fantasy one-on-one  arrangement. The game's fighting system will be a mix between popular fighting game styles like Mortal Kombat and Soul Calibur. All characters will have unique fighting moves, combined with Special and Magic attacks. Each character will resemble and include specific traits from each respective zodiac sign.

 Our team tossed around a few ideas on the type of game we wanted to create & the platform where it was going to be launched initially, and finally decided to work on the genre we are most passionate about, which is also a genre where there are not many developers, Fighting Games.

We wanted to create a fighting game where players would identify themselves (or at least a tiny part of) immediately with a character before actually playing the game. The idea of creating a character for each zodiac sign was a total WIN. Our gamer friends' feedback was tremendous and everyone got really excited about the concept.

One-on-one (versus style) Fighting games have always been fun to play and master, and there's a lot of room for innovation in the genre.  Zodiac Wars will combine popular fighting game styles with new industry-leading technology.

The Demo versions will be available first for Windows/MAC platforms. The Finished Game will initially be launched for the OUYA/Android platform (if $100K stretch goal is met), and if further stretch goals are met, on other new platforms like XboxOne, Sony PS4, and Windows/MAC/Linux.

Also, we plan on releasing a high-end Tablet version (with easy finger tapping action!)

 •12 playable characters to choose from, plus hidden characters to unlock.

• Play Modes: Arcade Mode, Player vs Player Mode (LAN and Online),   Multiplayer Tournament Mode (LAN and Online)

• Attacks: Special moves, magic attacks, hit combos, finishing moves, etc.

• Graphics: HD Stunning visuals, interactive scenery and vivid atmospheres (many stages to play in). Zodiac Wars will be powered by the Unity 4 Engine*.

• Sound: Zodiac Wars will feature digitally mastered HQ music from international orchestras and music producers.

• Controller support (We'll be supporting Xbox™ controllers, pc/mac/linux compatible controllers / arcade-style joysticks, etc.)

• Platforms/Consoles:  The game will be launched initially to the OUYA™/Android™ platforms, and if specific stretch goals are achieved, on XboxOne™, Sony PS4™, Mac OS X , Windows and possibly Linux.

 We simply won't go to publishers to release our game, we want to work with you, the players! We're using Kickstarter because we see it as an amazing opportunity to get players involved in the development of the game and offer their feedback while the game is being developed; to be able to deliver, meet and go beyond the player's expectations of the game. With Kickstarter, players become part of the game.

Each one of us at Black Monks Studios have a full-time job and families to take care of. We have spent many off-work hours creating the concept of this game. Zodiac Wars is our dream, it really is what we are passionate about. We are getting ready to move our Studio to the next level by developing this game, and we need the financial support to continue it's development Full time. We need to hire more talents to join our team, we need to expand our working space, obtain more hardware and acquire essential software licenses (which can add up very quickly).

The initial budget of $10,000 may not seem like enough to make a game like this, and it is certainly not enough, but we will develop the Demo Version with this initial goal, and if specific stretch goals are achieved, the game will get developed completely to it's final stage. 

How funds will be used:

- 10% Programming, Testing (debugging) & Deployment- 10% Artwork (Illustrations, Concept Art, assets)  - 40% 3D Characters & levels design, in-game Mechanics, Graphics Engine, Software Licenses, etc.- 10% Audio (Music, SFX) - 10% Kickstarter & Amazon fees - 20% Reward Fulfillment (Creation & Shipping)

We have the experience to keep costs minimal (we'll do our best at it). If we're able to surpass our initial goal, we'll use all the excess funding to polish the game even further to the last detail and also aim at launching it to new console gaming platforms like XboxOne™ and Sony PS4™. Also, we have a few stretch goals that will definitely get the game to the next level and add a few extras to the Zodiac Wars experience. We need your support to make it happen!

Risks and challenges Learn about accountability on Kickstarter

Currently we are a small team trying to make a very complex and extensive fighting game (many characters, many unique moves, skills, special attacks, traits, complex physics, etc.) Highly-skilled programmers, designers and industry professionals are required to create such an amazing game like this one. Our team will have to hire a few additional talents to be able to reach our goal of delivering a polished AAA fighting game experience. Our accumulated wealth of knowledge over the years has taught us that you don't need a 50+ man team to make an awesome game.

Financial support is crucial for us to be able to finish the game and release it. Our team members have extensive experience in each field and are confident we can make it work with your support. Our past experiences will help us oversee future problems and keep delays at a minimum, but naturally, we are not exempt from them. Like any other game or software, testing is very time-consuming and is one of the most important phases in the development of the game. Our vast knowledge, combined with your help, will keep the project moving forward.
