A Fun, Professional and realistic Flight Simulation Add-on for FSX and the rewarding experience of flying.

Learn to Fly with a Boeing 717.

I want to create a professional Add-on for MS Flight Simulator X about the Boeing 717 and the basic principles of flying. Learning to fly is easier than it looks, and by following our tutorial you should be able to perform a basic flight from the beginning to the end. Also the purpose is to have fun with a High-end Quality designed simulation model that is professional, full of realism, and specially keeping in mind those mature and skilled simmers that love the rewarding experience of excellence, attention to detail and overall fun.

Almost every single jet has been reproduced as a replica under the MS Flight Simulator X environment by different publishers and developers, products like Level-D 767, iFly 737NG, 747 Queen of the Skies, 767 Captain, and from the Boeing 727 to the 777, Airbus Line, Embraer aircraft, and so many more; All of these different models and their variations have been professionally designed by a few, and these developers preach only one model of business: High-end Quality Products; Unfortunately for the Boeing 717 and the MD-90 EFD this is a problem: the same level of high-end quality, professionalism, excellence, fun and realism is very scarce, and I really want this to be the end of this problem. Hopefully with Kickstarter and your help we can change this.

Here is a list of some details the final product will include:

  • Flight Operations and Step by step Manual fully detailed and an optional Video tutorial.
  • Perfect and Highly accurate replica of the aircraft Boeing 717, including high quality photoreal textures inside and outside. The 3D model will have more than 60,000 polygons. Interior Cabin highly detailed with passengers and live sounds.
  • Accurate simulated model and animations: Ailerons, elevators, spoilers, landing gear, stabilizer movement, all doors, forward stairs, cockpit windows, rotating engine fans, turning wheels, wipers, animated thrust reversers, etc, basically all details accurately modeled and animated. Every detail of the aircraft will be an accurate model without affecting the frame rates.
  • Ground Support: Catering Truck, Belt Loader, Baggage Train, Loader, Fuel Truck and Pushback tug, Complete set of different external views, including Take Off and Landing from different runway spots. Parking and Maintenance animations and sounds all managed from FMC Options.
  • Virtual cockpit with high quality textures, functional, animated, zooming capabilities and cockpit sounds, high quality lightning, flood panels, fully detailed flight deck, animated features for windows, and even flight deck door, every single knob and switch will be FULLY operational so that you can perform a complete flight just within the Virtual Cockpit. Every single procedure starting from “Cold and Dark” all the way to Engine shut down on destination parking.
  • Fully Operational Panels, Optional Widescreen Panel, Captain and First Officer, Fully detailed and Operational AFT Overhead Panel and FORWARD Overhead Panel, Glare shield and Outboard Console, FMC, FORWARD Pedestal and AFT Pedestal, Outboard Console, every detailed of SISP Panel, EIS Control Panel, etc, every panel in full detail animated and operational in 3D Virtual Cockpit as mentioned previously.
  • Fully operational fuel system, Crossfeed, Air conditioning and pressurization, communications radios, transponders.
  • Full simulated Electrical, etc. Simulated hydraulic system, Pumps and Instruments. Fully simulated Pneumatic system, pneumatic indicators.
  • Fire detection system, fire loops, fire Handles, warning sounds, etc.
  • GPWS, warning and Caution sounds, Take off configuration warnings and sounds.

FAQ

  • will your 717 be as system rich as the PMDG MD11 and will it have an accurately modeled MCDU with LNAV, VNAV, FIX, HOLD, Derated Thrust, Offsets and able to load SIDs and STARs and build a realistic flight plan from within the MCDU?

    Absolutely Yes and more! I have no intentions at all to build a "lite" version of the 717. The project I am working is rich, realistic and accurate and the final goal is to make it worthy to compete with what PMDG did with the MD-11. I have been thinking to build an "Easier than Complex" version for the 717 but ONLY as an option for beginners, but the focus is still them SAME: Accurate model, all systems as an exact replica as well as animations perfectly identical to the real 717. It makes no sense for me to do a "newer version" of PF 717, specially thinking of simmers like you and me is the reason I have been working on this project, I am not going to waste money and time when something brings no passion for me or our community.

    Last updated: Wed, Apr 11 2012 5:27 pm EDT
  • Will this be available for FS9 as well?

    Yes, but only for those who pledge $100 or higher, and it will require more time on delivery, estimated around 6 months.

  • Do you have a team?

    Yes I do have a team, is almost ready to be in place, they are under contract right now, I cannot take them out of their current legal obligations but those obligations are getting close to be fulfilled, if this project gets successful and funded we will be able to get the team together and start on this asap.

    Last updated: Wed, Apr 11 2012 5:24 pm EDT
  • You are asking for $12,000.00 but isn't this a little bit too much?

    The project is asking $12,000.00 and believe me when I say this, IT IS A VERY LOW REQUEST, specially if the final goal is achieving a completely professional software capable to compete with PMDG software. Here is an idea of what requires, just on Man-hour total cost takes more than $65,000, I have asked other friendly investors to help me with this project, they don't think this can be done, but If I am able to get the $12,000 I am asking they will support me with additional $60,000.
