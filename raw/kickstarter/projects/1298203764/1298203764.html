Gameplay

You start each game trapped in a random room that is slowly overcome with Zombies. Â Each room has three game play types that randomly occur, Standard, Hazard, and Boss Battle.

Â From this point, you 5 seconds to venture into another room or stay in the current room.
  • HazardÂ is the same asÂ Standard, except each room has a unique hazard. Â For example, what is normally just a grandfather clock, now turns into a terrifying buzz saw that can slice through you as well as the undead.

When you terminate zombies you will need to gather their gibs/chunks (remains) which is what you use to purchase weapons from the armory.
