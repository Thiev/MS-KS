The story behind Vengeance

Set in the near future, several years after a devastating world war; fighters have now become a commodity sought after by those who are now in power. Tournaments are held to determine the worth and price of each professional fighter. Combat has become a way of life for most people. Now a special tournament is being held where the worlds most deadliest and powerful fighters have been gathered, but this tournament isn't to set or increase their individual value as a fighter, this tournament promises to please a higher desire that each of the fighters involved have. They each desire Vengeance!

Who is behind No Limit Games
  • William Brevard and Kevin Loza are the brains behind No Limit Games. Both have a passion for games, and a passion to make a fantastic product. 
  • This campaign is meant to raise capital that is needed in order to help us complete our current project and get the ball rolling on being able to start working on the next game. 
  • The contributions that we receive from here, will allow No Limit Games to obtain the necessary middle-ware and other licenses that we will need in order to complete our game.

What the funding will be used for, and your rewards:

We are seeking $35,000 in funding. The breakdown of this funding need is as follows: 

  • $26,900 Will be used for middle-ware and licenses 
  • $6,100 Will be used to pay our employees for their continued hard work on this project from its beginning 
  • By simply pitching in $2 you are already supporting us, and for that we thank you by putting your name in the credits as a supporter. 
  • With a $10 pledge, you deserve to see the beautiful artwork, and concept designs that our artist worked on over the first year of development
  •  $15 gives you access to our beta test, where your input will help this title launch with minimum to no bugs. 
  • At $20 you deserve to get a copy of the game yourself! 
  • With a $80 pledge, you can proudly show your support of Vengeance and No Limit Games with a Vengeance T-shirt. Just send us your size requirements!! 
  • For those that offer up a $150 pledge, you also receive a custom printed CD. Signed by the Co-Founders of No Limit Games LLC Time for you to hop in and cheer for your favorite fighters. 
  • With a $1500 pledge we will create a character of you and place them in the cheering crowd on each level..
  • If you want to go one step further and actually be in the game, then a $3000 will make that happen. We'll take your image create the character and add it to the game. 
If we do not reach our goal, no money will be collected from anyone that pitched in.

Your help and our future

Vengeance is the launchpad for No Limit Games LLC. With this game, we will announce ourselves to the gaming community as a new entry into the industry. From here, we will add more games under our belt, each one better then the last; learning from our mistakes, and improving on them. We will use the success of our games to form new partnerships with other companies that we are already in contact with such as Konami. Our aim is to continue to move forward becoming a much stronger team as we get closer to beginning work on our flagship game. 

Once we have moved beyond being a start-up company we will begin to focus on building games that run true with our primary mission statement of building games that break the limitations. Such as massive cross platform games. Our primary goal is to bring the gaming community fresh new games never done before successfully. But this all starts with Vengeance. Our contributors will take part in helping a small no name company make a name for itself and step foot into the game development arena. You will be what helps us get there.
