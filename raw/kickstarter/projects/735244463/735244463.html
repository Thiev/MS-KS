Before we go on to talk about the exciting part, a little about us here at Mobilehead:

Mobilehead has been around the since 2008. We were primarily a work-for-hire development studio, and experienced growing pains and ups and downs as a game studio. In 2010, between projects, we started to do our own publishing. That was a rough go! We didn't have enough time to create a great game and didn't have enough marketing dollars to drive downloads for the games we were creating. So we moved back into work-for-hire projects to regroup and earn a steady paycheck.

...Which brings us to "Galactic Wars"

"Galactic Wars" was conceived as a "be-all" project, something we really wanted to build in 2008--one of the awesome projects that a newly-minted studio like Mobilehead dreams of creating. It was going to be our grand flagship and lead to a universe of great games. And then the economy crumbled, reality hit, and things got shoved onto the back burner while we scrambled to stay alive. Well...we're alive! We survived the crash and in the process we developed even more experience in game design and creation.Â  --including beefing up the original Galactic Wars story, adding new technology, gadgets and gizmos, and spiffing it all up in some really cool new ways (sometimes waiting makes things better!). Now what we need is time. And time = $

That's where we need your help, Kickstarters! We need 6 months to finish this project! 6 months, so we can hold off on taking new projects and concentrate on bringing "Galactic Wars" to life!

Genre and Target Platforms: Galactic Wars will be a real time fleet battle game for the PC Game. Our stretch goals will also include iOS/Android/Web versions as well.

"Galactic Wars" is a space fleet game where the player controls their own planetary faction and builds up a fleet, recruits and trains heroes, and battles against other factions for galactic dominance. Our focus is mainly fleet battles using real-time strategy to control your ships and attack your enemies.

Outside of battle, you'll be using strategy on a galactic scale to position fleets, reinforce strongholds,Â  upgrade your fleet in varying ways to utilize different strategies and weapon configurations, send diplomatic ambassadors to forge alliances with other planetary systems, or invade and blockade other players systems and dominate them!

So what is Galactic Wars? Think of the detailed, rich universe of "Wing Commander," with the galaxy management and strategy of "Star Wars: Empire at War," mixed with the real-time strategy battle game play of "Homeworld."

The Story, in a Nutshell: "Galactic Wars" is a story about the Hylarian Federations advancement and colonization of space in the nearby star systems, Saris, Tanaius and Epsilon. It has been 200 years since the first colonies were established, and since then the Hylarian Federation has prospered from a golden-age of interstellar trade and commerce.Â  This golden-age though is short-lived, as the planetary Magistrate governments desire for power and control spark the fires of war, and all the systems in the Hylarian Federation are thrown into a civil war.

As a player, you are the Magistrate of your own world in the civil war torn Hylarian Federation.Â  You must choose your allies carefully, and conquer your enemies swiftly, before an armada blockades your planet and claims your world for their own.

One of the unique things we are trying out is that the game you play and the faction you choose will determine the outcome of the next story arc. So you, the players, control the life and the story of the entire game series.
