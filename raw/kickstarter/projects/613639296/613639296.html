An App that turns your Daily Life into a Video Game and prepares you for the coming Zombie Apocalypse.

UPDATE 

The Zombies are coming and Limbic Thinking wants help you survive the Apocalypse this October with ZPREP. The app that helps you to achieve your personal goals by kicking some Zombie ass. ZPREP uses the premise of a Zombie Apocalypse to motivate and challenge players to achieve their personal goals (all the while becoming a total bad ass). You are assigned a character based on your daily activities; who is then given a series of real life tasks that would need to be completed to help you reach your personal goal. Along the way you can share the fun with your friends by completing missions together, to help motivate each other.

Limbic Thinking is a newly formed start up. Our company was set up to take our learning and extensive experience in working with high risk and challenging young people and fuse this understanding of behaviour with technology. The entertainment arm of our company is heavily influenced by Gamification.  Currently we are looking for exciting ways to help people achieve real life goals with the use of gamification. Gamification is the integration of game dynamics in every day real life. We want to use functions that are currently used in every day gaming to help people achieve their personal goals. We are a small team but passionate and driven to see our products made. We want ZPREP to exist even if we are the only people who play it.

In-Short

In-Depth

ZPREP has three (3) main areas of interaction; levelling and role progression, mini-game system, and social play areas involving Groups and Leaderboard Systems. The Game is about preparing people through a video game experience that uses their real world to earn XP, Complete Missions and Take on other Players.

WHY ZPREP?

When we create goals or attempt to change behaviours one of the first key things is to monitor, motivate and prioritise. This can be seen as a arduous task and many lose motivation. ZPREP aims to provide the novelty required to keep you engaged and motivated to fulfil your goals.

THE SETTING

Games are normally creative, they allow us to act in situations that may be foreign to our everyday lives. By creating a constructive environment, in this instance a zombie apocalypse, it allows the player to challenge the way they think of their goals. It enables them to be creative with their short-term and long-term goals. Also Zombies are way cool and if there was a Zombie Apocalypse wouldn't you want to be at your best? 

HOW IT WORKS?

Most RPGs incorporate a class system, however ZPREP follows a different system for identifying the player’s Role. Rather than selecting a class, such as ‘commando’ and then having activities and goals allocated, ZPREP goes backwards and allocates your role after finding out about you. Allocation of Roles depends on the Focuses and sub-Focuses that the player chooses for the gameplay.

For example studying medicine at university would be SKILLS, ORGANIC. Going for a long run would be FITNESS – ENDURANCE. Growing a Potato would be SELF-SUFFICENCY – FOOD PRODUCTION. 

LEVEL PROGRESSION AND REWARDS

Like most RPGs there is a level progression system. To Level in ZPREP you need XP that is earned by doing three different things:

  •  Completing Tasks prompted by ZPREP, such as Role Mission-Lines, Rush Missions.

  • Grinding XP with your focus areas,

  • Achieving in the in app Mini-Game.

 

The first release of ZPREP has a level cap at Level 50. Player’s also receive in-app items and features while progressing through the game. As players progress through the App they are given Boss Fight Challenges at every level 10 bracket to provide a true challenge and accountability measure. To provide added motivation if you are using ZPREP to achieve goals players have the function to nominate real life rewards that they can receive after completing the Boss Fight Challenge.

Some examples of Boss Fight Challenges are:

Fitness: Run to your Workplace with a 15kg pack.                               

Self-Sufficiency: Go hunting and then prepare the animal for consumption. Sorry Vegans deal with it. 

Skills: turn an item worth $5.00 into $100 or more within 3 days. 

ROLES

Roles provide you with an in-game character for you to play and learn to prepare for. There are 5 roles currently planned for ZPREP with possible additions like most RPG style games each role represents different abilities and skills. You role is determined by your focus selection.

Roles act as an interface between ZPREP and your Real Life. Each of the 5 Roles is given a unique mission-line that you progress through as you level and is intentionally designed to allow you to experience similar challenges and tasks you would be involved in in the Zombie World to come

MISSIONS

Are based around core skills associated to the Role you have been assigned. The Missions provide an opportunity to see the story behind your Role and equip you for your role to play in the coming Zombie world. They are designed to really test how the player would experience a zombie world; a Scout needs to prove that they can be sneaky, a Leader needs to demonstrate leadership abilities. After completing a mission the player is awarded items that cannot be purchased in game, such as pets, mounts and Role specific weapons and items. 

Example Missions:

Scout: Supply Run - run to your local store and acquire 3 long-life items.

Survivalist: Sweat Shop - Make an item of clothing

Merchant: Money Maker - Build/Create something and then sell at a profit. 

Honesty Systems

There are always those who will try and take the easier road. The high level of autonomy that ZPREP allows may seem as though there is a great risk of Cheating. Here are some of the techniques we will use to keep players honest:

  • TESTED - through the Boss Fight Challenges players have to complete advanced tasks. A player cannot progress to the next level bracket unless they complete the boss fight. this occurs every 10 levels.
  • EVIDENCE REQUIRED - Players may be required to submit photos or link videos when completing missions, and/or entering focus areas. 
  • ITEMS PRICING - If you could purchase the item you really wanted from the in-app store at a reasonable price why waste hours spamming XP submissions? 
  • FLAG TRACKING - ZPREP will be programmed to flag excessive XP increases within unreasonable timeframes. 
  • PLAYER FLAG PLAYER - There will also be an option to flag concern about the integrity of other players through their profile. This flagging will then be validated or rejected. 


MINI-GAME

The mini-game feature is included to give players an outlet for all of their Zombie frustrations. You are given a time limit and a horde of Zombies to kill, with difficult increasing as the player levels. 

After level 20 players will have access to a Pet. If things aren’t looking good in the mini-game a prompt will enable RUSH ATTACK which will see the pet enter the mini-game temporarily.

LEADERBOARD SYSTEM

The Leaderboard system allows you to test yourself against other ZPREPPERs, and try to become the best Scout, Leader, Militia, Survivalist, or Merchant. Players send challenges to other players and are then rewarded after a successful win or penalised after a loss. The loser of the challenge will be docked In-Game Currency Credits and move below the challenger on the Leaderboard, the winner is awarded the In-Game Currency Credits and move up the Leaderboard. The Leaderboard System is only available to gamers at End Game after reaching level 50. The challenges which the two players undertake are linked to the 3 Core Focus Areas.

When challenges are accepted there is a 24-38 hour period in which proof must be submitted, this allows for players across Time-Zones to challenge one another. After uploading evidence challenger and defender are asked to choose who won. If they are unable to decide, ZPREP can invite a third player to make the decision    

RUSH MISSIONS AND GROUPS

J Join a group and prepare together with others. You will need a core group of fellow likeminded people to survive in the coming Zombie World. Share your ZPREP experience with friends or meet new ones through the group system. The group system is similar to a clan or other collective groups in RPGs, however ZPREP’s groups are limited to 5 members. Players are able to Find, Join, or Create a group. Group rush missions take the form of push notifications, advising of small challenges that can be completed within a 5-10min timeframe. As with all missions players will receive rewards upon completion    . 

If all of our Stretch Goals are met the Group System will become a larger focus of ZPREP with Group PVP. Each group will need to find a balance in the Roles each member of the team has, as this will affect the outcome of battling other teams.

Some examples of Rush Missions are:

  • Run to your car within 1.5 mins.
  • Sneak past 3 people.
  • Get to the drop zone (a random location allocated by ZPREP computers) and collect supplies.
